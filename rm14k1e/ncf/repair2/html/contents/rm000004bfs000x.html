FUEL LID OPENER SYSTEM > DETAILS



FUNCTION OF MAIN COMPONENTS

  1. The fuel lid opener system consists of the following components:

    Component
    		Function
    Fuel Lid Opener Switch
    		Pressing the fuel lid opener switch located on the driver side instrument panel causes the fuel lid to unlatch.
    Fuel Lid Lock Assembly
    1. The fuel lid lock assembly consists of a built-in motor that opens the fuel lid via a link.
    2. If the fuel lid lock assembly malfunctions, the fuel lid can be opened manually by pulling on the emergency opener that is provided on the fuel lid lock assembly.
