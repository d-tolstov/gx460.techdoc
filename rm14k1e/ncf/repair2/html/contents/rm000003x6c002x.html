CLIMATE CONTROL SEAT SYSTEM > GENERAL



OUTLINE

  1. A climate control seat system is provided in the driver and front passenger seats.

  2. The climate control seat system creates comfortable front seats by using the seat heaters and blower units.

  3. The seat heaters are on the front seats and are controlled by the seat heater control sub-assemblies.

  4. The air conditioning amplifier assembly controls the ventilation function.

  5. The heating function and the ventilation function cannot be operated at the same time.

  6. For the ventilation function, only air in the cabin is used. Heated or cooled air is not used.
