WIPER AND WASHER SYSTEM > GENERAL



OUTLINE

  1. Smoothly and smartly designed front wiper arms and blades are used to emphasize the body design.

  2. The washer nozzles are located under the engine hood to ensure good appearance. Spray type nozzles are used. These nozzles can spray windshield washer fluid in a wide range, thus achieving excellent wiping performance.

  3. The headlight cleaner system sprays washer fluid on the headlight lenses to clean them.
