FUNCTION OF MAIN COMPONENTS

  1. Seat Heater System

    Component
    		Function
    Seat Heater
    		The seat heater is sewn into the internal surfaces of the seat cushion and the seat back. It warms up the seat surfaces using the heat caused by electrical resistance.
    Seat Heater Control Sub-assembly
    		Controls the system based on the signal from the thermistor and the seat heater control switch.
    Seat Heater Switch
    		The operation signals of the seat heater switch are input in the seat heater control sub-assembly in order to adjust the seat heater temperature.
    Thermistor
    		Detects the surface temperature of the seat cushion.


FUNCTION

  1. Seat Heater System

    1. The seat heater system is operated by pressing the seat heater switch. The temperature can be adjusted between two stages by pressing the HI switch or LO switch of the seat heater switch.

    2. A thermistor detects the temperature of the seat surface and the seat heater control sub-assembly controls it.
