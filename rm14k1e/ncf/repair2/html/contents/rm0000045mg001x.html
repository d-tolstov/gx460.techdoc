THEFT DETERRENT > GENERAL



OUTLINE

  1. The theft deterrent system is controlled by the main body ECU. The theft deterrent system will operate when an attempt is made to forcibly enter the vehicle or open the engine hood, when the battery terminals are removed and reconnected.

  2. The system consists of door lock control system parts, entry and start system parts and a security indicator light.


SPECIFICATION

  1. The warning specifications of this system are listed below:

    Warning Method
    		Hazard Warning Light
    		Flashes
    Vehicle Horn
    		Sounds a warning at approx. 0.4-second cycles
    Security Horn
    		Sounds a warning at approx. 0.4-second cycles
    Door Lock Motor
    		Locks
    Warning Time
    		Approx. 27.5 seconds
