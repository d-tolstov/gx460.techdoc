METER / GAUGE SYSTEM > GENERAL



OUTLINE

  1. An Optitron display type combination meter assembly is used.

  2. The Optitron display type combination meter assembly achieves excellent visibility through the use of smoke acrylic in the protective panel, and bright Light Emitting Diodes (LEDs) that have high contrast to illuminate the indicator and the dial.

  3. A meter ECU and a multi buzzer are enclosed in the combination meter assembly. This ECU maintains communication with other ECUs through the Controller Area Network (CAN).

  4. A front seat belt warning function is provided. If the front seat belt is not buckled, this system blinks the seat belt warning light and sounds the multi buzzer as a reminder.

  5. Eco Driving Indicator lets the driver know whether the vehicle is driven in Eco-friendly using Eco Driving Indicator Light and Eco Driving Indicator Zone Display in multi-information display.



  6. An accessory meter assembly, which can display cruise information, is used.



    HINT:
    The diagrams shown above are for describing the new car features. They may differ from the actual design. If the LEDs malfunction, the entire combination meter assembly must be replaced. For details, refer to the corresponding Repair Manual for this model.
