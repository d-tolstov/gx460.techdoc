LIGHTING SYSTEM > DETAILS



FUNCTION

  1. The illuminated entry system has the following functions, with each function controlling different lights:

    Function
    		Outline
    Interior Light Control
    		Illuminates and extinguishes the map light, center console illumination light, No. 1 room light assembly, No. 2 room light assembly and engine switch illumination light.
    Interior Foot Light and Door Inside Handle Illumination Light Control
    		Illuminates and extinguishes the interior foot light and door inside handle illumination light.
    Side Step Light Control
    		Illuminates and extinguishes the side step light.
    Battery Saving
    1. Extinguishes each light*1 if no door is opened or closed for approx. 20 minutes after any door*2 is opened.
    2. When the light switch is in the on position, the battery saving function of the map light, No. 1 room light assembly and No. 2 room light assembly does not operate.
    Customization
    		The light illumination time (15 seconds) can be changed using the customization function. For details, refer to the corresponding Repair Manual for this model.

    HINT:
    *1: Map light, No. 1 room light assembly, No. 2 room light assembly, door inside handle illumination light, front interior foot light, rear interior foot light, and side step light
    *2: Front doors, rear doors, back door or glass hatch



  2. Interior Light Control Function



    1. The interior light control function operates as follows:




      No.
      		Condition
      (1)
      1. With engine switch off and all doors closed, any door is unlocked.
      2. Engine switch is off.
      3. With engine switch off and all doors closed, key enters any actuation area around the doors.
      (2)
      		Any door, back door or glass hatch is open.
      (3)
      		All doors, back door and glass hatch are closed.
      (4)
      1. More than 15 seconds have elapsed since the lights were turned on.
      2. All doors are closed and locked.
      3. Engine switch is on (IG) or on (ACC).
      4. Key is outside of all actuation areas around the doors.



  3. Interior Foot Light and Door Inside Handle Illumination Light Control Function



    1. The interior foot light and door inside handle illumination light control function operate as follows:




      No.
      		Condition
      (1)
      		With engine switch off and all doors closed, any door is unlocked.
      (2)
      1. Any door, back door or glass hatch is open.
      2. With shift lever in P, the engine switch is turned from off to on (IG).
      (3)
      1. With engine switch on (ACC) or off, all doors are closed.
      2. With all doors, back door and glass hatch closed, the engine switch is turned from on (IG) to off.
      (4)
      1. More than 15 seconds have elapsed since the lights were turned on.
      2. With engine switch off and all doors closed, all doors, back door and glass hatch are locked.
      (5)
      		With engine switch off and all doors closed, all doors are locked.
      (6)
      		With engine switch on (IG) and all doors, back door and glass hatch closed, the shift lever is moved to a position other than P.
      (7)
      1. With engine switch on (IG), any door, back door or glass hatch is open.
      2. With engine switch on (IG) and all doors, back door and glass hatch closed, the shift lever is moved to P.



  4. Side Step Light Control Function



    1. The side step light control function operates as follows:




      No.
      		Condition
      (1)
      1. With engine switch off and all doors closed, any door is unlocked.
      2. With the engine switch on (IG) and the shift lever in P, the driver or front passenger manual door lock switch is operated to unlock.
      3. With engine switch off and all doors closed, key enters any actuation area around the doors.
      (2)
      1. More than 15 seconds have elapsed in the side step light is turned on.
      2. All doors are closed and locked.
      3. With engine switch on (IG), the shift lever is moved to a position other than P.
      4. With engine switch off and all doors closed, key is outside of all actuation areas around the doors.
      (3)
      1. With engine switch off, any door other than the back door is open.
      2. With engine switch on (IG) and shift lever in P, any door other than the back door is open.
      (4)
      		With engine switch on (IG), the shift lever is moved to a position other than P.
      (5)
      		All doors are closed.
