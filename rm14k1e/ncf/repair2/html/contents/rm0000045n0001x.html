LIGHTING SYSTEM > GENERAL



OUTLINE

  1. Interior Light

    1. Each kind of light is used to enhance convenience and comfort.

  2. Illuminated Entry System

    1. An illuminated entry system is used, which turns on in response to the opening and closing of each door, to enhance usability at night and produce a pleasing cabin space.

    2. The illuminated entry system is controlled by the main body ECU.
