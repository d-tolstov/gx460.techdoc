FUNCTION OF MAIN COMPONENTS

Component
Function
Rack and Pinion Power Steering Gear Assembly
Increases operation force of the steering wheel and transmits operation force to the front wheel.
Vane Pump Assembly
Drives in accordance with the engine to pump power steering fluid to the steering gear assembly.
Vane Pump Oil Reservoir Assembly
Stores the power steering fluid.

CONSTRUCTION


  1. Rack and Pinion Power Steering Gear Assembly

    1. Operability has been improved through a lightweight, compact rack and pinion type steering gear assembly.



    2. A variable gear ratio type steering rack is used. This steering rack changes the gear ratio gradually by changing the from of the gear teeth. The gear ratio is set larger around the steering center and smaller around the end position. Thus, rack stroke around the end position is larger to reduce the steering operation while ensuring moderate response around the steering center.
