EXHAUST SYSTEM > GENERAL



OUTLINE

  1. The exhaust pipes are made of stainless steel for improved rust resistance.

  2. A thin-wall, ceramic type Three-Way Catalytic converter (TWC) is used.

  3. A muffler is used to ensure engine performance and reduce exhaust noise.

  4. A ball joint is used to join the front exhaust pipe assembly and center exhaust pipe assembly. As a result, in-vehicle sound has been muffled, vibration has been minimized, and weight reduction has been achieved.
