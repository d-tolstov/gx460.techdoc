AXLE SYSTEM > GENERAL



OUTLINE

  1. A unit-type double-row tapered roller bearing is used for the front axle.

  2. A unit-type double-row angular ball bearing is used for the rear axle.



  3. The speed sensor rotor is integrated in the bearing inner race of both the front and rear axles.
