WINDSHIELD / WINDOW GLASS SYSTEM > DETAILS



FUNCTION OF MAIN COMPONENTS

  1. Windshield Deicer System

    Component
    		Function
    Deicer Switch
    		Operates the windshield deicer system.
    Air Conditioning Amplifier Assembly
    		Operates and controls the windshield deicer system in accordance with the signals from the deicer switch.
    Windshield Deicer Wire
    		Applies electrical current to a windshield deicer wire in the windshield glass to generate heat and remove ice from the windshield glass.

  2. Window Defogger System

    Component
    		Function
    Defogger Switch
    		Inputs the defogger wire operation signal into the air conditioning amplifier assembly.
    Air Conditioning Amplifier Assembly
    		Operates and controls the window defogger system in accordance with the signals from the defogger switch.
    Defogger Wire
    		Applies electrical current to a defogger wire installed in the back door glass to remove fogging from the back door glass.


OPERATION

  1. Windshield Deicer System

    1. The windshield deicer system is activated when the engine switch is turned on (IG) and the deicer switch is pushed. The air conditioning amplifier assembly keeps the front wiper deicer on for approximately 15 minutes.

  2. Window Defogger System

    1. When the engine switch is turned on (IG) and the defogger and mirror heater switch is pushed, this system is activated to keep the defogger heater and mirror heaters on for approximately 15 minutes*.

      HINT:
      *: Continues for a maximum of 270 minutes in accordance with conditions.
