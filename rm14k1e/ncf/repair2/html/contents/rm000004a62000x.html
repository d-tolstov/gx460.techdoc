G-BOOK SYSTEM > GENERAL



OUTLINE

  1. The G-BOOK system is a telematics service that links the vehicle and G-BOOK network (provides information from the G-BOOK center or content providers).

  2. To use the G-BOOK system, it is necessary to connect the telematics transceiver (Data Communication Module) to the display and navigation module display.

  3. The user needs to follow the instructions on the display and navigation module display to start using the G-BOOK system service after applying for the service at a dealership.

  4. When the vehicle reaches an altitude greater than 5000 m, the device stops operating in order to protect the Hard Disc Drive (HDD). During this time, the G-BOOK system cannot be used.




MAIN FEATURES

  1. The G-BOOK main features are as follows:

    Contents
    		Outline
    Emergency Call Service
    		Calls the authorities for an emergency vehicle, calls emergency facilities or dealerships (or road service companies).
    Theft Tracking
    		Automatic Alarm Notification
    		Notifies users with telephone or Short Message Service (SMS) after the G-BOOK center confirms that the theft detection system has been activated.
    Vehicle Tracking Service
    		If the vehicle is stolen, this service searches the location information of the vehicle to track it if required by the user.
    Authorities Contact Service
    		If requested by the user, a G-BOOK operator asks the authorities to find the stolen vehicle and take the case under their control.
    Remote Maintenance Service
    		Inspection Notification
    		Sends an e-mail message to the vehicle to notify the user of necessary inspections based on vehicle use conditions.
    Inspection and Maintenance Reservation
    		Offers quick access for the user to easily make a reservation for periodic maintenance or service using the navigation system.
    Remote Diagnosis
    		Sends the G-BOOK center diagnosis data and warnings while the engine switch is on (ACC).
    Traffic Information
    		G Route Search
    		Predicts the occurrence of or changes in traffic congestion on the route to the destination and provides the most suitable route based on the latest traffic information, probe information* and past statistical data.
    Text Information
    		Uses text and voice to provide traffic congestion information and road information for the area around the present location and designated areas.
    Operator Service
    		Destination Setting
    		If requested by the user, a G-BOOK operator searches for information to set the destination.
    Point Of Interest (POI) Guidance
    		Provides POI information for facilities in the area around the present location.
    News and Weather Forecasts
    		Delivers news and weather forecast information as requested by the user.

    HINT:
    *: Probe information is gathered by using probe communication to aggregate driving data such as speed and position from vehicles equipped with the G-BOOK system.
