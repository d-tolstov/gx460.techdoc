OUTLINE

  1. Navigation System

    1. A display and navigation module display is provided as standard equipment on the center cluster panel. The display, which consists of a wide 8.0-inch Liquid Crystal Display (LCD) screen with a pressure sensitive touch panel, offers improved ease of use.

    2. 2 types of navigation receiver assemblies are available: a Hard Disk Drive (HDD) type, or a Digital Versatile Disc (DVD) type.

    3. The navigation system uses the Global Positioning System (GPS).

    4. The voice guidance function and the voice recognition function support various languages that can be selected by the user.
