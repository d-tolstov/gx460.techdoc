SFI SYSTEM > GENERAL



OUTLINE

  1. The engine control system performs highly accurate, integrated control of the following systems to achieve high performance, high power, high fuel efficiency and to reduce emissions.

    1. Sequential Multiport Fuel Injection (SFI)

    2. Electronic Spark Advance (ESA)

    3. Electronic Throttle Control System-intelligent (ETCS-i)

    4. Dual Variable Valve Timing-intelligent (Dual VVT-i)

    5. Acoustic Control Induction System (ACIS)

    6. Fuel Pump Control

    7. Air Conditioning Cut-off Control

    8. Starter Control (Cranking Hold Function)

    9. Air Fuel Ratio Sensor and Oxygen Sensor Heater Control

    10. Engine Immobiliser

    11. Diagnosis

    12. Fail-safe
