AIR SUSPENSION SYSTEM > GENERAL



OUTLINE

  1. A rear air suspension system is used.

  2. This system is a vehicle height control system that uses a rear pneumatic cylinder assembly in the rear suspension, instead of a coil spring.

  3. This system enables the vehicle to maintain a constant vehicle height regardless of the number of occupants or load, thus achieving excellent driving stability and ride comfort.


PRECAUTION

  1. Before raising the vehicle on a jack or hoist, make sure to press the height control OFF switch to prohibit height control.
