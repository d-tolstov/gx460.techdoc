HEATED STEERING WHEEL SYSTEM > GENERAL



OUTLINE

  1. A heated steering wheel system is available as optional equipment on some models.

  2. This system consists of a heater wire that is placed inside the steering wheel.

  3. This system is activated by operating the steering heater switch and is controlled by the steering wheel heater control assembly.

  4. The steering wheel heater control assembly uses the thermistor provided in the steering wheel to maintain the surface temperature of the steering wheel at approximately 37°C (99°F).

  5. The steering wheel heater control assembly contains a timer function that automatically turns off the heated steering wheel system after approximately 30 minutes even if the driver does not turn off the steering heater switch.

  6. When the steering wheel heater control assembly detects the following malfunctions, the indicator light of the steering heater switch blinks.

    1. An open or short in the thermistor continues for 2 seconds or more.

    2. The steering heater switch is kept pressed for 10 seconds or more.
