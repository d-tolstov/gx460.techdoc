GLASS HATCH OPENER SYSTEM > GENERAL



OUTLINE

  1. The glass hatch opener system enables the back door glass to be opened when the glass hatch opener switch is pressed.

  2. The glass hatch opener system enables the back door glass to be opened when the transmitter glass hatch opener button is pressed.

  3. The warning light illuminates when the back door glass is opened, and a warning is displayed on the Liquid Crystal Display (LCD) of the combination meter assembly.
