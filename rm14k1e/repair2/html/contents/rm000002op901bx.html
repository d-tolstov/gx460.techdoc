1. INSTALL STUD BOLT

NOTICE:
If a stud bolt is deformed or its threads are damaged, replace it.



  1. Using E7 and E8 "TORX" socket wrenches, install the stud bolts.

    Torque:
    9.0 N*m{ 92 kgf*cm , 80 in.*lbf }





    Text in Illustration

    *E
    		for Front Side of Bank 2
    		*F
    		for Front Side of Bank 1



2. INSTALL NO. 2 STRAIGHT SCREW PLUG



  1. Using a 14 mm hexagon wrench, install 2 new gaskets and the 2 No. 2 straight screw plugs.

    Torque:
    85 N*m{ 867 kgf*cm , 63 ft.*lbf }

    Text in Illustration 
    *A
    		for Bank 2
    *B
    		for Bank 1



3. INSTALL NO. 1 STRAIGHT SCREW PLUG



  1. Using a 10 mm hexagon wrench, install 6 new gaskets and the 6 No. 1 straight screw plugs.

    Torque:
    44 N*m{ 449 kgf*cm , 32 ft.*lbf }

    Text in Illustration 
    *A
    		for Bank 2
    *B
    		for Bank 1



4. INSTALL VALVE SPRING SEAT

  1. Install the valve spring seats to the cylinder head.



5. INSTALL VALVE STEM OIL SEAL



  1. Apply a light coat of engine oil to new valve stem oil seals.
    Text in Illustration 
    *A
    		for Intake Side
    *B
    		for Exhaust Side
    *1
    		Brown
    *2
    		Gray

    HINT:
    The intake valve oil seals are brown and the exhaust valve oil seals are gray.

    NOTICE:
    Pay attention when installing the intake and exhaust valve stem oil seals. Installing the intake valve stem oil seal to the exhaust side or installing the exhaust valve stem oil seal to the intake side can cause installation problems later.




  1. Using SST, push in the valve stem oil seals.

    SST
    09201-41020  
    Text in Illustration 
    *a
    		CORRECT
    *b
    		INCORRECT

    NOTICE:
    Failure to use SST will cause the seal to be damaged or improperly seated.



6. INSTALL INTAKE VALVE



  1. Apply a sufficient coat of engine oil to the tip area of the intake valve shown in the illustration.
    Text in Illustration 
    *a
    		40 mm (1.57 in.) or more

  1. Install the intake valve, compression spring and valve spring retainer to the cylinder head.

    NOTICE:
    Install the same parts in the same combination to their original locations.




  1. Using SST, compress the compression spring and install the 2 valve spring retainer locks.

    SST
    09202-70020   (09202-00010)




  1. Using a plastic-faced hammer, lightly tap the valve stem tip to ensure a proper fit.

    NOTICE:
    Be careful not to damage the retainer.
