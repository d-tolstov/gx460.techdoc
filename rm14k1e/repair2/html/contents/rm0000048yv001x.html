VOLTAGE INVERTER > ON-VEHICLE INSPECTION

for Preparation Click here



1. INSPECT VOLTAGE INVERTER ASSEMBLY

HINT:
Remove interior parts so that the voltage inverter can be seen.




  1. Disconnect the voltage inverter connector.

  1. Measure the voltage according to the value(s) in the table below.

    Standard Voltage:

    Tester Connection
    		Switch Condition
    		Specified Condition
    Q16-1 (GND) - Q16-5 (IG)
    		Engine switch on (IG)
    		11 to 14 V
    Q16-1 (GND) - Body ground
    		Always
    		Below 1 V

    If the result is not as specified, repair or replace the harness or connector.
    Text in Illustration 
    *a
    		Front view of wire harness connector
    (to Voltage Inverter Assembly)




  1. Reconnect the voltage inverter connector.

  1. Measure the voltage according to the value(s) in the table below.

    Standard Voltage:

    Tester Connection
    		Switch Condition
    		Specified Condition
    Q16-4 (AC1) - Q16-8 (AC2)
    		Engine switch on (IG), Main switch on
    		AC 220 V

    If the result is not as specified, replace the voltage inverter assembly.
    Text in Illustration 
    *a
    		Component with harness connected
    (Voltage Inverter Assembly)
