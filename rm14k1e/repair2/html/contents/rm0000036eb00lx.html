1. REMOVE REAR DOOR OUTSIDE MOULDING SUB-ASSEMBLY LH



HINT:
When removing the rear door outside moulding, heat the vehicle body and rear door outside moulding using a heat light.

Standard:
Item
Temperature
Vehicle Body
40 to 60°C (104 to 140°F)
Rear Door Outside Moulding
20 to 30°C (68 to 86°F)

NOTICE:
Do not heat the vehicle body or rear door outside moulding excessively.

  1. Put protective tape around the rear door outside moulding.

  1. Using a moulding remover, remove the double-sided tape to detach the outside moulding retainer and remove the rear door outside moulding.
