INSPECTION PROCEDURE

HINT:
Even if the portable player can play audio content, it may not be able to play via the in-vehicle device. This does not necessarily indicate a malfunction of the in-vehicle device.

1.CHECK OPERATION

  1. Check if the portable player operates normally.

    OK:
    The portable player operates normally.



NG
MALFUNCTION IN PORTABLE PLAYER
OK


2.CHECK AUDIO SETTINGS

  1. Check the radio receiver setting.

    1. Check that the volume is not set to "0".

    2. Check that "mute" is off.

      OK:
      The volume is not set to "0" and "mute" is off.



NG
TURN VOLUME UP AND SET MUTE OFF
OK


3.CHECK USING ANOTHER "BLUETOOTH" AUDIO COMPATIBLE VEHICLE OF SAME MODEL

  1. Check if the portable player plays normally on another "Bluetooth" audio compatible vehicle of the same model.

    OK:
    The portable player operates normally.



NG
MALFUNCTION IN PORTABLE PLAYER
OK
