BACK DOOR OUTSIDE GARNISH > DISASSEMBLY

for Preparation Click here



1. REMOVE BACK DOOR ELECTRICAL KEY SWITCH



  1. Detach the clamp.




  1. Remove the 2 screws.

  1. Detach the 2 guides and remove the back door electrical key switch.



2. REMOVE LICENSE PLATE LIGHT ASSEMBLY



  1. Disconnect the connector.

  1. Remove the 2 screws and lights.



3. REMOVE NO. 1 BACK DOOR GARNISH RETAINER



  1. Remove the 3 screws and No. 1 back door garnish retainer.



4. REMOVE BACK DOOR OUTSIDE GARNISH PROTECTOR



  1. For upper side:
    Remove the back door outside garnish protector.




  1. For left and right side:
    Remove the back door outside garnish protector.

    HINT:
    Use the same procedure for both back door outside garnish protectors.




  1. For lower side:
    Remove the back door outside garnish protector.

    HINT:
    Use the same procedure for both back door outside garnish protectors.



5. REMOVE NO. 2 BACK DOOR OUTSIDE GARNISH



  1. Remove the 8 screws.

  1. Detach the 2 claws and remove the No. 2 back door outside garnish.



6. REMOVE LOWER BACK DOOR OUTSIDE GARNISH



  1. Remove the 6 screws.

  1. Detach the 2 claws and remove the lower back door outside garnish.
