DESCRIPTION

WIRING DIAGRAM

INSPECTION PROCEDURE

PERFORM ACTIVE TEST USING INTELLIGENT TESTER (FUEL PUMP)

INSPECT CIRCUIT OPENING RELAY (C/OPN)

INSPECT CIRCUIT OPENING RELAY (C/OPN) (POWER SOURCE VOLTAGE)

CHECK HARNESS AND CONNECTOR (CIRCUIT OPENING RELAY (C/OPN) - ECM AND FUEL PUMP ECU)

CHECK HARNESS AND CONNECTOR (FUEL PUMP - FUEL PUMP ECU)

INSPECT FUEL PUMP

CHECK HARNESS AND CONNECTOR (FUEL PUMP ECU - ECM AND BODY GROUND)

REPLACE FUEL PUMP ECU

CONFIRM WHETHER MALFUNCTION HAS BEEN SUCCESSFULLY REPAIRED

CHECK HARNESS AND CONNECTOR (CIRCUIT OPENING RELAY (C/OPN) - FUEL PUMP RELAY (F/PMP) AND NO. 1 INTEGRATION RELAY)

INSPECT FUEL PUMP RELAY (F/PMP)

CHECK HARNESS AND CONNECTOR (FUEL PUMP RELAY (F/PMP) - NO. 1 INTEGRATION RELAY, BATTERY AND BODY GROUND)
