DESCRIPTION

This is the power source circuit of the multi-display controller sub-assembly.

INSPECTION PROCEDURE

1.CHECK HARNESS AND CONNECTOR (MULTI-DISPLAY CONTROLLER POWER SOURCE)

  1. Disconnect the H16 multi-display controller sub-assembly connector.

  1. Measure the resistance according to the value(s) in the table below.

    Standard Resistance:
    Tester Connection
    		Condition
    		Specified Condition
    H16-21 (GND) - Body ground
    		Always
    		Below 1 Ω
    H16-22 (GND5) - Body ground
    		Always
    		Below 1 Ω

  1. Measure the voltage according to the value(s) in the table below.

    Standard Voltage:
    Tester Connection
    		Condition
    		Specified Condition
    H16-14 (+B) - H16-21 (GND)
    		Always
    		11 to 14 V
    H16-28 (+B1) - H16-21 (GND)
    		Always
    		11 to 14 V
