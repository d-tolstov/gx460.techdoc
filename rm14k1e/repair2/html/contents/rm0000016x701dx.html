FRONT AXLE HUB > DISASSEMBLY

for Preparation Click here



1. REMOVE FRONT AXLE WITH ABS ROTOR BEARING ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Gently fix the front axle hub in a vise between aluminum plates.

  1. Using SST, remove the bearing.

    SST
    09710-30021   (09710-03051)
    09950-40011   (09951-04020, 09952-04010, 09953-04020, 09954-04010, 09955-04061, 09957-04010, 09958-04011)



2. REMOVE FRONT AXLE HUB OIL SEAL LH



  1. Using a screwdriver, remove the front axle hub oil seal.
