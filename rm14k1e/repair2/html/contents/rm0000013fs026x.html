1. INSPECT TRANSMISSION WIRE (ATF TEMPERATURE SENSOR)



  1. Measure the resistance according to the value(s) in the table below.

    HINT:
    If the resistance is out of the specified range at any of the ATF temperatures shown in the table below, the driveability of the vehicle may decrease.

    Standard Resistance:
    Tester Connection
    		ATF Temperature
    		Specified Condition
    2 (OT-) - 13 (OT+)
    		10°C (50°F)
    		5 to 8 kΩ
    2 (OT-) - 13 (OT+)
    		25°C (77°F)
    		2.5 to 4.5 kΩ
    2 (OT-) - 13 (OT+)
    		110°C (230°F)
    		0.22 to 0.28 kΩ
    3 (OT2-) - 14 (OT2+)
    		10°C (50°F)
    		5 to 8 kΩ
    3 (OT2-) - 14 (OT2+)
    		25°C (77°F)
    		2.5 to 4.5 kΩ
    3 (OT2-) - 14 (OT2+)
    		110°C (230°F)
    		0.22 to 0.28 kΩ

    Text in Illustration 
    *a
    		Component without harness connected
    (Transmission Wire)
    If the result is not as specified, replace the transmission wire.
