REAR NO. 1 SEAT ASSEMBLY (for 60/40 Split Slide Walk-in Seat Type RH Side) > DISASSEMBLY

for Preparation Click here


CAUTION:
Wear protective gloves. Sharp areas on the parts may injure your hands.



1. REMOVE REAR SEAT UPPER RECLINING COVER RH



  1. Using a screwdriver, detach the 2 claws and remove the cover.

    HINT:
    Tape the screwdriver tip before use.

    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape



2. REMOVE RECLINING ADJUSTER RELEASE HANDLE RH



  1. Remove the 3 screws and handle.



3. REMOVE REAR SEAT CUSHION MOULDING RH



  1. Using a clip remover, detach the clip.

  1. Using a moulding remover, detach the 3 claws.

  1. Move the moulding in the direction of the arrow to detach the guide and remove the moulding.



4. REMOVE REAR SEAT REAR CUSHION MOULDING LH



  1. Remove the screw.

  1. Using a moulding remover, detach the 3 claws.

  1. Detach the 2 guides and remove the moulding.



5. REMOVE REAR NO. 1 SEAT INNER BELT ASSEMBLY RH



  1. Remove the bolt and inner belt.



6. REMOVE REAR SEAT INNER BELT ASSEMBLY



  1. Remove the nut and inner belt.



7. REMOVE REAR SEATBACK LOCK STRIKER COVER RH



  1. Using a clip remover, detach the 3 clips and remove the cover.



8. REMOVE SEAT CUSHION COVER WITH PAD



  1. Detach the hooks.




  1. w/ Seat Heater System:

    1. Disconnect the 2 seat heater connectors.

    2. Detach the 2 clamps and disconnect the seat heater wire harness.

  1. Remove the seat cushion cover with pad from the seat cushion frame.



9. REMOVE REAR NO. 2 SEAT PROTECTOR



  1. Remove the 2 protectors from the cushion pad.



10. REMOVE SEPARATE TYPE REAR SEAT CUSHION COVER


  1. Remove the hog rings and seat cushion cover from the seat cushion pad.





11. REMOVE REAR SEAT CUSHION HEATER ASSEMBLY (w/ Seat Heater System)



  1. Cut off the tack pins and remove the seat cushion heater from the seat cushion cover.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Tack Pin



12. REMOVE REAR SEAT CUSHION EDGE PROTECTOR

  1. Remove the protector from the cushion spring.



13. REMOVE RECLINING ADJUSTER KNOB CAP RH



  1. Using a screwdriver, detach the claw and guide, and then remove the cap.

    HINT:
    Tape the screwdriver tip before use.

    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape



14. REMOVE REAR SEAT LOCK CONTROL LEVER SUB-ASSEMBLY RH



  1. Remove the 2 screws.




  1. Disconnect the cable in the order shown in the illustration and remove the lever.



15. REMOVE REAR SEAT COVER CAP



  1. Using a screwdriver, detach the claw and guide, and then remove the cover cap.

    HINT:
    Tape the screwdriver tip before use.



16. REMOVE REAR CENTER BACK LOCK CONTROL LEVER



  1. Remove the 2 screws.

  1. Move the lever in the direction of the arrow to detach the guide and remove the lever.

  1. Detach the cable clamp and disconnect the cable from the lever.



17. REMOVE REAR SEATBACK COVER



  1. Using a screwdriver, detach the 4 claws and remove the cover.

    HINT:
    Tape the screwdriver tip before use.



18. REMOVE REAR SEATBACK BOARD CARPET ASSEMBLY RH



  1. Using a clip remover, detach the 7 clips and remove the carpet.



19. REMOVE REAR NO. 1 SEAT HEADREST SUPPORT ASSEMBLY



  1. Detach the 4 claws and remove the 2 supports.



20. REMOVE SEATBACK COVER WITH PAD



  1. Remove the nut, detach the 2 guides and disconnect the seatback cover bracket from the seatback frame.




  1. Remove the hog rings and detach the hooks, and then remove the seatback cover with pad.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Hook
    *2
    		Hog Ring



21. REMOVE SEPARATE TYPE REAR SEATBACK COVER



  1. Disconnect the seatback cover bracket from the seatback pad.




  1. w/ Seat Heater System:
    Disconnect the seatback heater wire harness from the seatback pad.




  1. Remove the hog rings and cover from the seatback pad.



22. REMOVE REAR SEATBACK HEATER ASSEMBLY (w/ Seat Heater System)



  1. Cut off the tack pins which fasten the seatback heater to the seatback cover, and then remove the seatback heater from the seatback cover.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Tack Pin



23. REMOVE REAR SEATBACK EDGE PROTECTOR

  1. Remove the protector from the seatback frame.



24. REMOVE REAR SEAT INNER RECLINING COVER RH



  1. Remove the 2 screws.

  1. Detach the guide and remove the cover.



25. REMOVE RECLINING ADJUSTER INSIDE COVER RH



  1. Remove the 2 screws.

  1. Detach the 2 claws and remove the cover.



26. REMOVE RECLINING ADJUSTER INSIDE COVER RH



  1. Using a clip remover, detach the clip.

  1. Move the cover in the direction of the arrow to detach the 2 claws and remove the cover.



27. REMOVE RECLINING ADJUSTER INSIDE COVER RH



  1. Detach the claw and guide, and remove the cover.



28. REMOVE REAR SEAT RECLINING CONTROL CABLE


  1. Cut off the 3 cable ties.





  1. Detach the 2 cable clamps and disconnect the cable, and then remove the cable.





29. REMOVE REAR NO. 2 SEAT RECLINING CONTROL CABLE


  1. Detach the cable clamp and disconnect the cable, and then remove the cable.





30. REMOVE REAR SEAT CUSHION SET PLATE



  1. Remove the 2 nuts and set plate.



31. REMOVE REAR SEATBACK LOCK PLATE LH

  1. Detach the 2 cable clamps and disconnect the cable.



  1. Remove the nut and lock plate.





32. REMOVE SEAT HEATER CONTROL SUB-ASSEMBLY (w/ Seat Heater System)



  1. Disconnect the connector.

  1. Detach the 2 clamps and remove the seat heater control.



33. REMOVE REAR SEAT WIRE RH (w/ Seat Heater System)



  1. Detach the claw and disconnect the seat wire connector from the seat track rail.

  1. Detach the clamp and remove the seat wire from the seat cushion frame.



34. REMOVE REAR SEAT COVER



  1. Remove the screw and cover.



35. REMOVE REAR SEAT CUSHION SPRING



  1. Detach the 2 hooks and remove the spring.



36. REMOVE REAR SEAT CUSHION FRAME SUB-ASSEMBLY RH



  1. Cut off the cable tie.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Cable tie
    *2
    		Clamp

  1. Detach the claw and disconnect the side airbag connector from the seat track rail.

  1. Detach the 2 airbag wire harness clamps and disconnect the airbag wire harness from the seat cushion frame.




  1. Using a T45 "TORX" socket wrench, remove the 4 "TORX" bolts and seatback frame.




  1. Remove the 6 nuts and seat cushion frame together with the seat leg plate.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Seat Leg Plate



37. REMOVE REAR SEAT TRACK ADJUSTING HANDLE RH



  1. Remove the seat cushion side frame.




  1. Slide the slide rail to the rearmost position.

  1. Using a small screwdriver, push down the handle stopper to detach the claw and remove the adjusting handle.

    HINT:
    Insert the screwdriver in the hole shown into the illustration while looking into the hole.
