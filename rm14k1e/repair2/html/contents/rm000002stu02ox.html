POWER WINDOW MASTER SWITCH > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here




1. REMOVE POWER WINDOW REGULATOR MASTER SWITCH ASSEMBLY WITH FRONT DOOR ARMREST BASE PANEL



  1. Using a moulding remover B, detach the 2 clips, 4 claws and guide as shown in the illustration.

  1. Disconnect the connector and remove the power window regulator master switch assembly with front door armrest base panel.



2. REMOVE MULTIPLEX NETWORK MASTER SWITCH ASSEMBLY



  1. Remove the 3 screws.

  1. Detach the 4 claws and remove the multiplex network master switch assembly.
