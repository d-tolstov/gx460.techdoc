FRONT DIFFERENTIAL SIDE GEAR SHAFT OIL SEAL > REPLACEMENT

for Preparation Click here


HINT:
  1. Use the same procedure for the RH and LH sides.
  2. The procedure listed below is for the LH side.



1. REMOVE FRONT DRIVE SHAFT ASSEMBLY LH

  1. Remove the front drive shaft assembly LH (Click here).



2. REMOVE FRONT DRIVE SHAFT ASSEMBLY RH

HINT:
Use the same procedure described for the LH side.



3. REMOVE DIFFERENTIAL SIDE GEAR SHAFT OIL SEAL



  1. Using SST, tap out the 2 oil seals.

    SST
    09308-10010  



4. INSTALL DIFFERENTIAL SIDE GEAR SHAFT OIL SEAL

  1. Apply MP grease to the lip of 2 new oil seals.




  1. Using SST and a hammer, tap in the 2 oil seals.

    SST
    09550-00032  
    09950-70010   (09951-07100)

    Standard Oil Seal Depth:
    Item
    		Specified Condition
    LH Side
    		-0.45 to 0.45 mm (-0.0177 to 0.0177 in.)
    RH Side
    		4.8 to 5.8 mm (0.189 to 0.228 in.)

    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Differential
    *2
    		Seal
    *a
    		LH side
    *b
    		RH side

    NOTICE:
    Make sure the LH and RH oil seals are installed in the proper locations.



5. INSTALL FRONT DRIVE SHAFT ASSEMBLY LH

  1. Install the front drive shaft assembly LH (Click here).



6. INSTALL FRONT DRIVE SHAFT ASSEMBLY RH

HINT:
Use the same procedure described for the LH side.
