FRONT SHOCK ABSORBER > DISASSEMBLY

for Preparation Click here



1. REMOVE FRONT SUPPORT TO FRONT SHOCK ABSORBER NUT



  1. Using SST, compress the coil spring.

    SST
    09727-30021   (09727-00010, 09727-00031)
    09727-00060  

    CAUTION:
    Be careful as the coil spring may spring upward and cause an injury.

    NOTICE:
    1. Make sure that the suspension support is free from the coil spring.
    2. Do not compress the coil spring more than necessary.
    3. Do not use an impact wrench. It will damage SST.




  1. Remove the front support to front shock absorber nut.



2. REMOVE FRONT SUSPENSION SUPPORT SUB-ASSEMBLY LH

  1. Remove the suspension support, cushion and 2 retainers from the shock absorber rod.



3. REMOVE FRONT COIL SPRING LH

  1. Remove the coil spring from the shock absorber.



4. REMOVE FRONT SHOCK ABSORBER BUSH



  1. Using SST and a press, remove the absorber bush.

    SST
    09710-30012   (09710-04071, 09710-04081)
