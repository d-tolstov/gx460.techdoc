WASHER NOZZLE (for Front Side) > ADJUSTMENT

for Preparation Click here



1. REMOVE WASHER NOZZLE SUB-ASSEMBLY



  1. Using a screwdriver, detach the 2 claws and separate the washer nozzle sub-assembly as shown in the illustration.

    HINT:
    Tape the screwdriver tip before use.

    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape

    NOTICE:
    Be careful not to damage the windshield.




  1. Disconnect the washer nozzle sub-assembly from the washer hose.

    NOTICE:
    Washer nozzles cannot be reused.



2. ADJUST WASHER NOZZLE SUB-ASSEMBLY


  1. Select a washer nozzle so that the contact area is within the standard. Replace the washer nozzle with the selected one.




    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Available Washer Nozzles
    		*2
    		Washer Fluid Spray Angle
    *3
    		Part Number
    		-
    		-



3. INSTALL WASHER NOZZLE SUB-ASSEMBLY

  1. Connect a new washer nozzle sub-assembly to the washer hose.

  1. Attach the 2 claws to install the washer nozzle sub-assembly.
