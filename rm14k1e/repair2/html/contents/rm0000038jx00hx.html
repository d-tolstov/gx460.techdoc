FRONT BUMPER > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here



1. DISCONNECT CABLE FROM NEGATIVE BATTERY TERMINAL

NOTICE:
  1. w/ Navigation System (for HDD):
    After the engine switch is turned off, the display and navigation module display (HDD navigation system) records various types of memory and settings. As a result, after turning the engine switch off, be sure to wait for the time specified in the following table before disconnecting the cable from the negative (-) battery terminal.

    Waiting Time before Disconnecting Cable from Negative (-) Battery Terminal:

    Condition
    		Waiting Time
    Vehicle enrolled in safety connect system
    		6 minutes
    Vehicle not enrolled in safety connect system
    		1 minute

  2. When disconnecting the cable, some systems need to be initialized after the cable is reconnected (Click here).



2. REMOVE UPPER RADIATOR SUPPORT SEAL



  1. Remove the 13 clips and upper radiator support seal.



3. REMOVE RADIATOR GRILLE



  1. Put protective tape around the radiator grille.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape

  1. Remove the 2 screws and 2 bolts.

  1. Detach the 6 claws and remove the radiator grille.



4. REMOVE FRONT BUMPER COVER

  1. Put protective tape around the front bumper cover.

  1. Remove the 2 bolts labeled A and 2 bolts labeled B.




  1. Remove the 2 screws and 4 clips.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape
    *2
    		Bolt A
    *3
    		Bolt B




  1. Remove the 2 screws.

    HINT:
    Use the same procedure for the RH and LH sides.




  1. Detach the 7 claws.

    HINT:
    Use the same procedure for the RH and LH sides.

  1. w/ LEXUS Parking Assist-sensor System:
    Disconnect the connector.

  1. Disconnect the headlight cleaner hose and 2 fog light connectors.

  1. Remove the front bumper cover.



5. REMOVE FRONT BUMPER ENERGY ABSORBER



  1. Remove the front bumper energy absorber.



6. REMOVE LOWER FRONT BUMPER COVER



  1. Remove the clip, 5 bolts and lower front bumper cover.



7. REMOVE FRONT BUMPER REINFORCEMENT SUB-ASSEMBLY



  1. Remove the 6 nuts and front bumper reinforcement.



8. REMOVE NO. 2 FRONT BUMPER EXTENSION SUB-ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Remove the 4 bolts and No. 2 front bumper extension.



9. REMOVE NO. 2 FRONT BUMPER EXTENSION SUB-ASSEMBLY RH

HINT:
Use the same procedure described for the LH side.



10. REMOVE FRONT BUMPER BAR REINFORCEMENT LH



  1. Remove the 2 screws and front bumper bar reinforcement.



11. REMOVE FRONT BUMPER BAR REINFORCEMENT RH

HINT:
Use the same procedure described for the LH side.



12. REMOVE HEADLIGHT ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Remove the bolt, 2 screws and clip.

  1. Detach the clamp.

  1. Disconnect the connectors and remove the headlight.



13. REMOVE HEADLIGHT ASSEMBLY RH

HINT:
Use the same procedure described for the LH side.



14. REMOVE FRONT BUMPER SIDE SUPPORT LH



  1. Remove the screw.

  1. Using a clip remover, detach 2 claws and remove the front bumper side support.

    HINT:
    Tape the clip remover tip before use.

    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape



15. REMOVE FRONT BUMPER SIDE SUPPORT RH

HINT:
Use the same procedure described for the LH side.



16. REMOVE UPPER CENTER FRONT BUMPER RETAINER



  1. Remove the 3 bolts and upper center front bumper retainer.



17. REMOVE RADIATOR SIDE DEFLECTOR LH



  1. Detach the 2 claws and remove the radiator side deflector.



18. REMOVE RADIATOR SIDE DEFLECTOR RH

HINT:
Use the same procedure described for the LH side.



19. REMOVE FRONT BUMPER BRACKET SUB-ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Remove the 2 bolts and front bumper bracket.



20. REMOVE FRONT BUMPER BRACKET SUB-ASSEMBLY RH

HINT:
Use the same procedure described for the LH side.
