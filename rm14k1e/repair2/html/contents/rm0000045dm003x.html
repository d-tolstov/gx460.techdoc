POWER SEAT SWITCH (for Rear No. 2 Seat) > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here



1. REMOVE SWITCH BEZEL



  1. Put protective tape around the switch bezel.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape

  1. Using a moulding remover, detach the 2 claws.




  1. Disconnect the connector and remove the switch bezel.



2. REMOVE REAR POWER SEAT SWITCH



  1. Detach the 2 claws and remove the switch from the switch bezel.



3. REMOVE NO. 1 FOLD SEAT SWITCH ASSEMBLY



  1. Put protective tape around the No. 1 fold seat switch assembly.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape

  1. Using a moulding remover, detach the 2 claws.




  1. Disconnect the 2 connectors and remove the fold seat switch.



4. REMOVE REAR POWER SEAT SWITCH



  1. Detach the 2 claws and remove the switch from the fold seat switch.
