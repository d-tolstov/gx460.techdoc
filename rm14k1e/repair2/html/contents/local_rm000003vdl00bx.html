DTC    B27A2    Open in Front Passenger Side Electrical Antenna Circuit


DESCRIPTION

WIRING DIAGRAM

INSPECTION PROCEDURE

CHECK CONNECTOR CONNECTION

CHECK HARNESS AND CONNECTOR (CERTIFICATION ECU - FRONT DOOR OUTSIDE HANDLE)

CHECK CERTIFICATION ECU (OUTPUT TO FRONT PASSENGER SIDE ELECTRICAL KEY ANTENNA)

REPLACE FRONT DOOR OUTSIDE HANDLE ASSEMBLY RH

CLEAR DTC

CHECK FOR DTC
