DTC    P0230    Fuel Pump Primary Circuit


DESCRIPTION

MONITOR DESCRIPTION

WIRING DIAGRAM

INSPECTION PROCEDURE

CHECK FOR DTC (B1500)

PERFORM ACTIVE TEST USING INTELLIGENT TESTER (FUEL PUMP)

CHECK HARNESS AND CONNECTOR (FUEL PUMP ECU - ECM, BODY GROUND)

REPLACE FUEL PUMP ECU

CHECK WHETHER DTC OUTPUT RECURS

INSPECT CIRCUIT OPENING RELAY (C/OPN)

INSPECT CIRCUIT OPENING RELAY (C/OPN) (POWER SOURCE VOLTAGE)

CHECK HARNESS AND CONNECTOR (CIRCUIT OPENING RELAY (C/OPN) - ECM, FUEL PUMP ECU)

CHECK HARNESS AND CONNECTOR (FUEL PUMP - FUEL PUMP ECU)

INSPECT FUEL PUMP

CHECK HARNESS AND CONNECTOR (FUEL PUMP ECU - ECM, BODY GROUND)

REPLACE FUEL PUMP ECU

CHECK WHETHER DTC OUTPUT RECURS

CHECK HARNESS AND CONNECTOR (CIRCUIT OPENING RELAY (C/OPN) - FUEL PUMP RELAY (FUEL PMP), NO. 1 INTEGRATION RELAY)

INSPECT FUEL PUMP RELAY (FUEL PMP)

CHECK HARNESS AND CONNECTOR (FUEL PUMP RELAY (FUEL PMP) - NO. 1 INTEGRATION RELAY, BATTERY, BODY GROUND)
