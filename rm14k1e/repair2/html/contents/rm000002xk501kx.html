WASHER LEVEL WARNING SWITCH > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here



1. REMOVE WASHER INLET SUB-ASSEMBLY



  1. Detach the claw and remove the washer inlet sub-assembly.



2. REMOVE WINDSHIELD WASHER JAR ASSEMBLY

  1. Remove the windshield washer jar assembly (Click here).



3. REMOVE WASHER JAR PROTECTOR



  1. Detach the 3 claws and remove the washer jar protector.



4. REMOVE LEVEL WARNING SWITCH ASSEMBLY



  1. Disconnect the connector.

  1. Remove the level warning switch assembly from the packing of the washer jar.
