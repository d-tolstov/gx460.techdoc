DTC    B1418/18    Exhaust Gas Sensor Circuit

for Preparation Click here


DESCRIPTION

The smog ventilation sensor is installed on the front of the cooler condenser assembly to control automatic air inlet. This sensor detects CO and HC in the emission gas and transmits signals to the air conditioning amplifier assembly.

DTC Code
DTC Detection Condition
Trouble Area
B1418/18
Open or short in smog ventilation sensor circuit (HC, CO)
  1. Smog ventilation sensor
  2. Harness or connector
  3. Air conditioning amplifier assembly
