DIAGNOSIS SYSTEM

  1. Indicator light

    1. During vehicle stabilizer control operation, the KDSS indicator light comes on when there is a malfunction in the KDSS.

      NOTICE:
      1. When the malfunction has been corrected, the KDSS indicator light goes off.
      2. There are some malfunctions which do not cause the KDSS indicator light to come on (Click here for the items that cause the KDSS indicator light to come on).

  2. DTCs

    1. DTCs are stored in the stabilizer control ECU and output by using SST check wire or the intelligent tester (Click here).
