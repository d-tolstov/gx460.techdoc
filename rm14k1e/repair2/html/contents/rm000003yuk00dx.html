MANUAL MAINTENANCE CHECK

HINT:
  1. Using the manual maintenance check, it is possible to perform a maintenance check of the G-BOOK device (inspection or repair of the device, or maintenance check after a vehicle repair), and confirm if the customer is a registered member (when applying for or canceling the service, or changing the contract details). It is also possible to enable or disable the emergency call service.
  2. The screen illustrations provided are samples only and may be different from the actual screens.

  1. Check GPS Reception

    1. Check that the GPS mark is displayed on the present location screen.

      HINT:
      The purpose of the manual maintenance check is to confirm that the location information can be sent correctly. Therefore, the manual maintenance check should be performed in a wide-open area where GPS radio waves can be received. Do not perform the check in an area between buildings or in an indoor environment such as a workshop.



  2. Perform Manual Maintenance Check

    NOTICE:
    To prevent the accidental summoning of emergency vehicles, make sure to follow the following procedure from the beginning when performing a manual maintenance check.





    1. Open the emergency call switch panel cover.

    2. Turn the engine switch off.

    3. Turn the engine switch on (ACC) or on (IG).




    4. Press and hold about 10 seconds the emergency call switch immediately after the red and green indicators come on.

      HINT:
      1. Pressing and holding the emergency call switch at this time will cause the system to begin the manual maintenance check without summoning emergency vehicles.
      2. If only the green indicator is illuminated, do not press the emergency call switch, as this will trigger an emergency call and emergency vehicles may be summoned. Return to the first step and begin the procedure again.




    5. After the manual maintenance check starts, the green indicator will start blinking. Release the emergency call switch immediately after the green indicator starts blinking.




    6. The system starts accessing the G-BOOK center.




    7. After connecting to the G-BOOK center, data communication is first performed to confirm where the vehicle is, that a manual maintenance check is being performed, and that the customer is registered to the G-BOOK center.




    8. After data communication is completed, the system enters call mode.




    9. Try to talk to the G-BOOK center operator through the microphone to confirm that a call can be made normally and that the call location is correct.

      HINT:
      If the voice quality is poor, or the call location is incorrect, contact the G-BOOK support center.




    10. Finish the manual maintenance check after the call to the G-BOOK center operator ends.

    11. The present location screen is displayed after finishing the manual maintenance check.




    12. Check that the green indicator is illuminated.

      HINT:
      If the manual maintenance check cannot be finished normally, perform the inspections indicated by Problem Symptoms Table (the G-BOOK center cannot be accessed even after a manual maintenance check is performed) (Click here).


EMERGENCY CALL HISTORY

  1. Enter diagnostic mode (Click here).




  2. Select "Service Information" on the Service Menu screen.




  3. Select "Emergency Call History" on the Service Information screen.




  4. Emergency Call History

    1. Check the call results.
      Display
      		Content
      *1: Type of call
      		Displays the type of call.
      *2: Time and date of call
      		Displays the time and date when a call was made in the form of year/month/day hour:minute.
      *3: Call result
      		Displays the result of the communication with the G-BOOK center as follows:
      1. "OK": Communication with the center finished normally.
      2. "FAIL": Communication with the center did not finish normally.
      3. "STOP": Communication with the center was interrupted.
      4. "NO CONT": Membership registration has not been performed.

      OK:
      "OK" is displayed for the call result of the manual maintenance check.

      HINT:
      1. If "FAIL" or "STOP" is displayed, perform a manual maintenance check and confirm that a call can be made normally.
      2. If "NO CONT" is displayed, contact the customer to tell them that they need to perform membership registration.
      3. If the manual maintenance check cannot be finished normally, perform the inspections indicated by Problem Symptoms Table (the G-BOOK center cannot be accessed even after a manual maintenance check is performed) (Click here).


SECURITY HISTORY

  1. Enter diagnostic mode (Click here).




  2. Select "Service Information" on the Service Menu screen.




  3. Select "Security History" on the Service Information screen.




  4. Security History

    1. Check the results.
      Displayed Content of Security History Screen 
      Item
      		Displayed Content
      Date
      		Indicates the time and date when the vehicle automatic alarm was activated in the form of year/month/day hour:minute.
      Result
      		Displays the result of the call to the G-BOOK center when the vehicle automatic alarm was activated as follows:
      1. "OK (Signal Only)": A security horn sounding signal was received but the call to the center failed.
      2. "OK": A security horn sounding signal was received and the call to the center completed.

      OK:
      Either "OK" or "OK (Signal Only)" is displayed after the security horn sounds.

      HINT:
      1. If neither "OK" nor "OK (Signal Only)" is displayed after the security horn sounds, proceed to CAN communication system (Click here).
      2. History will be recorded only for vehicles that have a contract for the G-BOOK service.
