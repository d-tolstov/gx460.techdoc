RADIATOR > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here



1. REMOVE UPPER RADIATOR SUPPORT SEAL



  1. Remove the 13 clips and upper radiator support seal.



2. REMOVE V-BANK COVER SUB-ASSEMBLY



  1. Raise the front of the V-bank cover to detach the 2 pins. Then detach the 2 V-bank cover hooks from the No. 1 V-bank cover bracket and remove the V-bank cover.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Pin
    *2
    		Hook
    *3
    		No. 1 V-bank Cover Bracket



3. REMOVE LOWER FRONT BUMPER COVER

  1. Remove the clip, 5 bolts and lower front bumper cover.



4. REMOVE NO. 1 ENGINE UNDER COVER SUB-ASSEMBLY

  1. Remove the 4 bolts.




  1. Unhook the No. 1 engine under cover from the vehicle body as shown in the illustration.



5. DRAIN ENGINE COOLANT

CAUTION:
Do not remove the radiator cap while the engine and radiator are still hot. Pressurized, hot engine coolant and steam may be released and cause serious burns.

HINT:
Collect the coolant in a container and dispose of it according to the regulations in your area.

  1. Loosen the radiator drain cock plug.

  1. Remove the radiator cap. Then drain the coolant from the radiator.

  1. Loosen the 2 cylinder block drain cock plugs. Then drain the coolant from the engine.



  1. Tighten the 2 cylinder block drain cock plugs.




    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Radiator Reservoir
    		*2
    		Radiator Cap
    *3
    		Cylinder Block Drain Cock Plug
    		*4
    		Radiator Drain Cock Plug

    Torque:
    13 N*m{ 130 kgf*cm , 9 ft.*lbf }

  1. Tighten the radiator drain cock plug by hand.



6. REMOVE FRONT BUMPER COVER

  1. Remove the front bumper cover (Click here).



7. REMOVE RADIATOR SIDE DEFLECTOR LH



  1. Using a clip remover, detach the 3 claws and remove the clip. Then move the radiator side deflector so that the radiator can be removed in the step below.



8. REMOVE RADIATOR SIDE DEFLECTOR RH



  1. Using a clip remover, detach the 3 claws and remove the clip. Then move the radiator side deflector so that the radiator can be removed in the step below.



9. REMOVE RADIATOR RESERVOIR ASSEMBLY



  1. Disconnect the radiator reservoir hose from the radiator.

  1. Remove the 3 bolts and radiator reservoir.



10. REMOVE NO. 1 RADIATOR HOSE



  1. Remove the No. 1 radiator hose.



11. REMOVE FAN SHROUD



  1. Loosen the 4 nuts holding the fluid coupling.

  1. Remove the fan and generator V-ribbed belt (Click here).




  1. Remove the 2 bolts holding the fan shroud.

  1. Remove the 4 nuts of the fluid coupling, and then remove the fan shroud together with the fluid coupling.

    NOTICE:
    Be careful not to damage the radiator core.

  1. Remove the fan pulley from the water pump.



12. DISCONNECT NO. 2 RADIATOR HOSE



  1. Disconnect the No. 2 radiator hose.



13. DISCONNECT OIL COOLER ACCESSORY ASSEMBLY



  1. Disconnect the 2 hoses and oil cooler accessory from the radiator.



14. REMOVE RADIATOR ASSEMBLY



  1. Remove the 4 bolts and radiator.



15. REMOVE NO. 2 RADIATOR HOSE



  1. Remove the No. 2 radiator hose from the radiator.



16. REMOVE NO. 1 RADIATOR SUPPORT



  1. Remove the 2 No. 1 radiator supports from the radiator.



17. REMOVE NO. 2 RADIATOR SUPPORT



  1. Remove the 2 No. 2 radiator supports from the radiator.



18. REMOVE NO. 1 RADIATOR SUPPORT BUSH



  1. Remove the 4 No. 1 radiator support bushes from the radiator.



19. REMOVE NO. 1 RADIATOR TO SUPPORT SEAL



  1. Remove the No. 1 radiator to support seal from the radiator.



20. REMOVE NO. 2 RADIATOR TO SUPPORT SEAL



  1. Remove the No. 2 radiator to support seal from the radiator.
