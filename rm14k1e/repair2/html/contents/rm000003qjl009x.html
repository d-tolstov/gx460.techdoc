HEADLIGHT DIMMER SWITCH > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here



1. REMOVE SPIRAL WITH SENSOR CABLE SUB-ASSEMBLY

  1. Remove the spiral with sensor cable sub-assembly (Click here).



2. REMOVE WINDSHIELD WIPER SWITCH ASSEMBLY



  1. Disconnect the connector.

  1. Detach the claw and remove the windshield wiper switch assembly.



3. REMOVE HEADLIGHT DIMMER SWITCH ASSEMBLY



  1. Disconnect the connector.




  1. While loosening the band clamp as shown in the illustration, detach the claw to remove the headlight dimmer switch.

    NOTICE:
    If the claw is pressed forcefully, it will break.
