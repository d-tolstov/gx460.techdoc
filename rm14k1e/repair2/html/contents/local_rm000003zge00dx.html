INSPECTION PROCEDURE

CHECK COMMUNICATION SERVICE CONDITION

CHECK FOR DTC

DCM CHECK

CONTACT G-BOOK SUPPORT CENTER

CHECK DISPLAY AND NAVIGATION MODULE DISPLAY (USBV, USBG)

CHECK HARNESS AND CONNECTOR

CHECK RECEPTION LEVEL

REPLACE TELEMATICS TRANSCEIVER

INSPECT TELEPHONE ANTENNA ASSEMBLY

INSPECT NO. 3 ANTENNA CORD SUB-ASSEMBLY

INSPECT NO. 2 ANTENNA CORD SUB-ASSEMBLY

CHECK HARNESS AND CONNECTOR (INSTRUMENT PANEL WIRE NO. 2)

REPLACE TELEMATICS TRANSCEIVER

CHECK CONTRACT FLAG

CHECK PROBLEM SYMPTOM (ENGINE SWITCH OFF TO ON [ACC])
