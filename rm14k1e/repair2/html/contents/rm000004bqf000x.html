LEXUS PARKING ASSIST-SENSOR SYSTEM > Main Switch Circuit

for Preparation Click here


DESCRIPTION

When the driving support switch control ECU receives the clearance sonar on/off switch signal, the ECU sends it to the parking assist ECU*1 or clearance warning ECU*2.
  1. *1: w/ Parking Assist Monitor System and/or Side Monitor System
  2. *2: w/o Parking Assist Monitor System and/or Side Monitor System

INSPECTION PROCEDURE

1.PERFORM ACTIVE TEST USING INTELLIGENT TESTER

  1. Connect the intelligent tester to the DLC3.

  1. Turn the engine switch on (IG).

  1. Turn the intelligent tester on.

  1. Enter the following menus: Body / D-SEAT SW / Active Test.

  1. Check that the main switch operates by performing the Active Test.
