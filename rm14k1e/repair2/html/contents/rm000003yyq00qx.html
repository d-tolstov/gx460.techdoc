AUDIO AND VISUAL SYSTEM (w/ Multi-display) > Audio cannot be Recorded to HDD

for Preparation Click here


DESCRIPTION

HINT:
The HDD is compliant with the Serial Copy Management System (SCMS). Therefore, recording from a CD-R that is a copy of a commercially published music CD is not possible.


INSPECTION PROCEDURE

1.CHECK SOUND LIBRARY SETTING OF HDD ON SOUND LIBRARY INFORMATION SCREEN



  1. Enter the "Sound Library Information" (Check Sound Library Setting) (Click here).

  1. Select "ON" for the "Sound Library" setting.

  1. Check if recording is possible.

    OK:
    Malfunction disappears.

  1. Check free space in HDD from the sound library setting screen.
