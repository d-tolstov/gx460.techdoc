AUDIO AND VISUAL SYSTEM (w/o Multi-display) > Sound Signal Circuit between Radio Receiver and Multi-media Interface ECU

for Preparation Click here


DESCRIPTION

The multi-media interface ECU sends sound signals to the radio receiver assembly through this circuit.
If there is an open or short in the circuit, sound cannot be heard from the speakers even if there is no malfunction in the stereo component amplifier assembly, radio receiver assembly or speakers.
