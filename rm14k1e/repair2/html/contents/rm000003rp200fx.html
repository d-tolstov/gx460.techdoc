DTC    C1789/89    Vehicle Identification Malfunction

for Preparation Click here


DESCRIPTION

  1. This DTC is stored when the suspension control ECU is initialized and is cleared when the vehicle identification information received through CAN communication system is correctly stored in the EEPROM.
  2. DTC C1789/89 is stored when an incorrect ECM is installed as the vehicle specification and data do not match.

DTC Code
DTC Detection Condition
Trouble Area
C1789/89
Either condition is met:
  1. The vehicle identification information is not stored.
  2. The information stored in the suspension control ECU and the vehicle identification information received through CAN communication system do not match.
  1. Suspension control ECU
  2. ECM
