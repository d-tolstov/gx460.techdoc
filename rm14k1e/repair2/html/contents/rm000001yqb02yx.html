DTC    74-40    Short in Speaker Circuit

for Preparation Click here


DESCRIPTION


DTC Code
DTC Detection Condition
Trouble Area
74-40
A short is detected in the speaker output circuit.
  1. Harness or connector
  2. Speaker
  3. Stereo component amplifier assembly

WIRING DIAGRAM




INSPECTION PROCEDURE

HINT:
After the inspection is completed, clear the DTCs.

1.CHECK FOR DTC

  1. Disconnect the H7 and H11 stereo component amplifier assembly connectors.

  1. Clear the DTC.

  1. Check if DTC 74-40 is output.

    OK:
    No DTC is output.



NG
REPLACE STEREO COMPONENT AMPLIFIER ASSEMBLY (Click here)
