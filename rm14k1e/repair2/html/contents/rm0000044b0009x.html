INSPECT SET SWITCH



  1. Turn the cruise control switch on.

  2. Check that the cruise control indicator light in the combination meter assembly illuminates.

  3. Drive the vehicle at a desired speed (between 40 km/h and 200 km/h [25 mph and 125 mph]).




  4. Push the cruise control switch to -SET.

  5. Check that the cruise control SET indicator light in the combination meter assembly illuminates.

  6. After releasing the switch, check that the vehicle cruises at the desired speed.


INSPECT "+" SWITCH



  1. Turn the cruise control switch on.

  2. Drive the vehicle at a desired speed (between 40 km/h and 200 km/h [25 mph and 125 mph]).




  3. Push the cruise control switch to -SET.




  4. Check that the vehicle speed increases while the cruise control switch is pushed to +RES, and that the vehicle cruises at the set speed when the cruise control switch is released.

  5. Momentarily push the cruise control switch upward to +RES and immediately release it. Check that the vehicle speed increases by about 1.6 km/h (1 mph) (tap-up function).


INSPECT "-" SWITCH



  1. Turn the cruise control switch on.

  2. Drive the vehicle at a desired speed (between 40 km/h and 200 km/h [25 mph and 125 mph]).




  3. Push the cruise control switch to -SET.

  4. Check that the vehicle speed decreases while the cruise control switch is pushed down to -SET, and that the vehicle cruises at the set speed when the cruise control switch is released.

  5. Momentarily push the cruise control switch downward to -SET and immediately release it. Check that the vehicle speed decreases by about 1.6 km/h (1 mph) (tap-down function).


INSPECT CANCEL FUNCTION (CANCEL SWITCH)



  1. Turn the cruise control switch on.

  2. Drive the vehicle at a desired speed (between 40 km/h and 200 km/h [25 mph and 125 mph]).




  3. Push the cruise control switch to -SET.

  4. When performing one of the following, check that the cruise control system is canceled and that the normal driving mode is reset.

    1. Depressing the brake pedal
    2. Pushing the cruise control switch off
    3. Pulling the cruise control switch to CANCEL
    4. Driving the vehicle while the vehicle stability control system is operating
    5. Moving the shift lever from D to N, or selecting 3rd, 2nd or 1st range with the shift lever in S


INSPECT RES (RESUME) SWITCH



  1. Turn the cruise control switch on.

  2. Drive the vehicle at a desired speed (between 40 km/h and 200 km/h [25 mph and 125 mph]).




  3. Push the cruise control switch to -SET.

  4. Cancel the cruise control system by performing one of the following.

    1. Depressing the brake pedal


    2. Pulling the cruise control switch to CANCEL
    3. Driving the vehicle while the vehicle stability control system is operating
    4. Moving the shift lever from D to N, or selecting 3rd, 2nd or 1st range with the shift lever in S




  5. After the cruise control switch is pushed up to +RES at a driving speed of more than 40 km/h (25 mph), check that the vehicle restores the speed that was set before the cancellation.
