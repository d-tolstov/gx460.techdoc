DTC    U1002    Lost Communication with Gateway Module (Sensor Bus)


DESCRIPTION

WIRING DIAGRAM

INSPECTION PROCEDURE

DISCONNECT CABLE FROM NEGATIVE BATTERY TERMINAL

CHECK CAN BUS WIRE (MAIN WIRE FOR DISCONNECTION, BUS LINE FOR SHORT CIRCUIT)

CHECK FOR OPEN IN CAN BUS MAIN WIRE (NO. 3 JUNCTION CONNECTOR - DRIVING SUPPORT ECU)

CHECK FOR OPEN IN CAN BUS MAIN WIRE (NO. 3 JUNCTION CONNECTOR - MILLIMETER WAVE RADAR SENSOR)

CONNECT CONNECTOR

CHECK FOR OPEN IN CAN BUS MAIN WIRE (DRIVING SUPPORT ECU - NO. 3 JUNCTION CONNECTOR)

CONNECT CONNECTOR

CHECK FOR OPEN IN CAN BUS MAIN WIRE (MILLIMETER WAVE RADAR SENSOR - NO. 3 JUNCTION CONNECTOR)

CHECK FOR SHORT IN CAN BUS WIRES (NO. 3 JUNCTION CONNECTOR - MILLIMETER WAVE RADAR SENSOR)

CHECK FOR SHORT IN CAN BUS WIRES (NO. 3 JUNCTION CONNECTOR - DRIVING SUPPORT ECU)

CONNECT CONNECTOR

CHECK FOR SHORT IN CAN BUS WIRES (MILLIMETER WAVE RADAR SENSOR)

CONNECT CONNECTOR

CHECK FOR SHORT IN CAN BUS WIRES (DRIVING SUPPORT ECU)

CHECK HARNESS AND CONNECTOR (DRIVING SUPPORT ECU - BATTERY AND GROUND)
