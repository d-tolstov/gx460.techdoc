AIR PUMP > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here



1. REMOVE UPPER RADIATOR SUPPORT SEAL



  1. Remove the 13 clips and upper radiator support seal.



2. DISCONNECT NO. 4 AIR INJECTION SYSTEM HOSE



  1. Disconnect the No. 4 air injection system hose from the air pump.



3. REMOVE AIR PUMP ASSEMBLY



  1. Disconnect the air pump connector and detach the wire harness clamp.




  1. Remove the 3 bolts and air pump with bracket.




  1. Detach the 2 air pump connector clamps.




  1. Detach the 3 fittings of the air pump insulator and remove the air pump from the bracket.
