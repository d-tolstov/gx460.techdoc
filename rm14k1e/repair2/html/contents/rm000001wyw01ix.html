1. INSPECT SKID CONTROL BUZZER ASSEMBLY



  1. Apply battery voltage to the skid control buzzer and check that the buzzer sounds.

    OK:
    Measurement Condition
    		Specified Condition
    Battery positive (+) voltage → Terminal 2
    		Skid control buzzer sounds
    Battery negative (-) voltage → Terminal 1

    If the result is not as specified, replace the skid control buzzer assembly.
