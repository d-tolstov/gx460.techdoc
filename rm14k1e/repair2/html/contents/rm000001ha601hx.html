    HINT:
    As the temperature increases, the resistance decreases (refer to the graph).

    NOTICE:
    1. Even slightly touching the sensor may change the resistance value. Be sure to hold the connector of the sensor.
    1. When measuring, the sensor temperature must be almost the same as the ambient temperature.

    If the result is not as specified, replace the No. 1 cooler thermistor.
