    If the result is not as specified, replace the fuel injector assembly.



  1. Inspect the injection volume.

    CAUTION:
    This test involves high-pressure fuel and electricity. Take all precautions regarding safe handling of both the fuel and the electricity. Perform this test in a safe area and avoid any sparks or flames. Do not smoke.





    1. Connect SST to the fuel filter.

    2. Install the O-ring to the fuel injector.

    3. Install SST and the tube to the fuel injector.

      SST
      09268-31012   (09268-41500, 90467-13001, 95336-08070, 09268-41300, 09268-41600)

    4. Put the injector into a graduated cylinder.

      CAUTION:
      Install a strong rubber tube to the injector to prevent gasoline from splashing.

    5. Operate the fuel pump (Click here).




    6. Connect SST (wire) to the injector and battery for 15 seconds, and then measure the injection volume with the graduated cylinder. Test each injector 2 or 3 times.

      SST
      09842-30090  

      Standard injection volume:
      82 to 99 cm3 (5.0 to 6.0 cu in.) per 15 seconds

      Difference between each injector:
      17 cm3 (1.0 cu in.) or less

      Text in Illustration 
      *1

      *a
      		Connect
      If the injection volume is not as specified, replace the injector assembly.

  1. Check for leakage.

    1. In the condition above, disconnect the tester probes of SST (wire) from the battery and check for fuel leakage from the injector.

      Standard fuel leakage:
      1 drop or less in 12 minutes
