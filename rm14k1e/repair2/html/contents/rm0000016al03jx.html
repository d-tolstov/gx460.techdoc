1. REMOVE COOLER BRACKET



  1. Detach the clamp.

  1. Remove the screw and cooler bracket.



2. REMOVE MAGNET CLUTCH ASSEMBLY



  1. Clamp the cooler compressor in a vise.

  1. Using SST, hold the magnet clutch hub.

    SST
    09985-00270  

  1. Remove the bolt, magnet clutch hub and magnet clutch washer(s).

    HINT:
    There is no set number of magnet clutch washers since they are used for adjusting.




  1. Using a snap ring expander, remove the snap ring and magnet clutch rotor.

    NOTICE:
    Do not damage the seal cover of the bearing when removing the snap ring.

  1. Disconnect the connector.




  1. Using a snap ring expander, remove the snap ring and magnet clutch stator.
