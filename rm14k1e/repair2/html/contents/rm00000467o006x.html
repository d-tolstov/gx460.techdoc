LUMBAR SWITCH > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here


CAUTION:
Wear protective gloves. Sharp areas on the parts may injure your hands.

HINT:
  1. Use the same procedure for the RH and LH sides.
  2. The procedure listed below is for the LH side.



1. REMOVE FRONT SEAT ASSEMBLY

  1. Remove the front seat assembly (Click here).



2. REMOVE SLIDE AND VERTICAL POWER SEAT SWITCH KNOB



  1. Using a moulding remover, detach the 2 claws and remove the knob.



3. REMOVE RECLINING POWER SEAT SWITCH KNOB



  1. Using a moulding remover, detach the 2 claws and remove the knob.



4. REMOVE FRONT SEAT CUSHION SHIELD LH

  1. Remove the screw of the front seat No. 1 inner cushion shield and detach the guide.

  1. Detach the clip of the front seat No. 1 inner cushion shield.

  1. Using a moulding remover, detach the 3 claws and guide.



  1. Move the cushion shield in the direction of the arrow to detach the clip and remove the front seat No. 1 inner cushion shield together with the cushion shield as a unit.






  1. Disconnect the lifter motor connector.

  1. Detach the 3 claws and open the wire harness cover.

  1. Detach the 3 wire harness clamps and disconnect the seat wire connector.



5. REMOVE FRONT POWER SEAT SWITCH LH



  1. for Driver Side:

    1. Remove the 3 screws.

    2. Disconnect the 2 connectors and remove the switch.




  1. for Front Passenger Side:

    1. Remove the 3 screws.

    2. Disconnect the connector and remove the switch.



6. REMOVE LUMBAR SWITCH ASSEMBLY



  1. Using a screwdriver, detach the 4 claws.

  1. Disconnect the lumbar switch connector and remove the lumbar switch.
