AUDIO AND VISUAL SYSTEM (w/ Multi-display) > Mute Signal Circuit between Stereo Component Amplifier and Telematics Transceiver

for Preparation Click here


DESCRIPTION

The telematics transceiver sends a mute signal to the stereo component amplifier assembly.
The stereo component amplifier assembly controls the volume according to the mute signal from the telematics transceiver.

2.CHECK HARNESS AND CONNECTOR (STEREO COMPONENT AMPLIFIER - TELEMATICS TRANSCEIVER)

  1. Disconnect the R34 stereo component amplifier assembly connector.

  1. Disconnect the G56 telematics transceiver connector.

  1. Measure the resistance according to the value(s) in the table below.

    Standard Resistance:
    Tester Connection
    		Condition
    		Specified Condition
    R34-7 (TMUT) - G56-17 (MUTE)
    		Always
    		Below 1 Ω
    R34-7 (TMUT) - Body ground
    		Always
    		10 kΩ or higher
