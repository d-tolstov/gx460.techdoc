DTC    B2090    Driving Support System Malfunction

for Preparation Click here


DESCRIPTION

The seat belt control ECU receives crash information from the driving support ECU and skid control ECU via CAN communication and tightens the seat belt by operating the motor inside the front seat outer belt.
When the driving support ECU detects a malfunction in the dynamic radar cruise control system or pre-crash safety system (Pre-Crash 2), the malfunction information is sent to the seat belt control ECU, which stores this DTC.
When DTC B2090 is stored, other DTCs stored by the driving support ECU (dynamic radar cruise control system and Pre-Crash 2) are also stored.

DTC Code
DTC Detection Condition
Trouble Area
B2090
During the initial check, while the engine switch on (IG) or while the pre-crash safety system is operating, the driving support ECU detects a malfunction in the dynamic radar cruise control system or pre-crash safety system (Pre-Crash 2) for 1 second or more.
  1. Dynamic radar cruise control system
  2. Pre-crash safety system (Pre-Crash 2)
  3. Seat belt control ECU
