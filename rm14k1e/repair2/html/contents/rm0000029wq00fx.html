1. REMOVE REAR PROPELLER SHAFT UNIVERSAL JOINT SPIDER BEARING

HINT:
Use the same procedure for all rear propeller shaft universal joint spider bearing.




  1. Place matchmarks on the flange yoke and sleeve yoke.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Matchmark




  1. Using a brass bar and a hammer, slightly tap in the spider bearing outer races.

  1. Using 2 screwdrivers, remove the 4 snap rings from the grooves.




  1. Using SST, remove the spider bearing from the sleeve yoke.

    SST
    09332-25010  

    HINT:
    Before installing SST, sufficiently raise the part labeled A. If the part labeled A is too low, it may be difficult to install SST.




  1. Clamp the spider bearing outer race in a vise and tap off the propeller shaft with a hammer.
    Text in Illustration 
    *a
    		Hammering Point

    HINT:
    Remove the spider bearing on opposite side in the same procedure.

    NOTICE:
    Do not tap the shaft.




  1. Install the 2 removed spider bearing outer races to the universal joint spider.

  1. Using SST, remove the bearing from the yoke.

    SST
    09332-25010  

    HINT:
    Sufficiently raise the part indicated by (A) so that it does not come into contact with the bearing.




  1. Clamp the outer bearing race in a vise between aluminum plates and tap off the yoke with a hammer.
    Text in Illustration 
    *a
    		Hammering Point

  1. Remove the universal joint spider.
