1. REMOVE NO. 4 INSTRUMENT PANEL REGISTER ASSEMBLY



  1. Detach the 8 claws and remove the No. 4 instrument panel register assembly.



2. REMOVE ACCESSORY METER ASSEMBLY WITH BRACKET (w/o Navigation System)



  1. Remove the 7 screws.

  1. Detach the 2 clamps and remove the accessory meter assembly.



3. REMOVE HEATER CONTROL RETAINER (w/o Navigation System)



  1. Detach the 4 claws and remove the 9 screws and heater control retainer.



4. REMOVE HEATER CONTROL SWITCH BOARD (w/o Navigation System)



  1. Remove the 4 screws and heater control switch board.

  1. Disconnect the flexible ribbon cable.
