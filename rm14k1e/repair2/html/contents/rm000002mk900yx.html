1. INSPECT REAR SPEAKER ASSEMBLY


  1. for 14 Speakers:




    1. Measure the resistance according to the value(s) in the table below.

      Standard Resistance:
      Tester Connection
      		Condition
      		Specified Condition
      1 - 2
      		Always
      		4.8 to 7.2 Ω

      If the result is not as specified, replace the rear speaker.
      Text in Illustration 
      *a
      		Component without harness connected
      (Rear Speaker Assembly)



  1. for 9 Speakers:




    1. Measure the resistance according to the value(s) in the table below.

      Standard Resistance:
      Tester Connection
      		Condition
      		Specified Condition
      1 - 2
      		Always
      		4 Ω

      If the result is not as specified, replace the rear speaker.
      Text in Illustration 
      *a
      		Component without harness connected
      (Rear Speaker Assembly)



2. INSPECT REAR NO. 2 SPEAKER ASSEMBLY (for 9 Speakers)



  1. Measure the resistance according to the value(s) in the table below.

    Standard Resistance:
    Tester Connection
    		Condition
    		Specified Condition
    1 (+TW) - 2 (-TW)
    		Always
    		Higher than 6.15 Ω

    If the result is not as specified, replace the rear No. 2 speaker assembly.
    Text in Illustration 
    *a
    		Component without harness connected
    (Rear No. 2 Speaker Assembly)
