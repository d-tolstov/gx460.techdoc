1. INSTALL STEP LIGHT BRACKET



HINT:
Use the same procedure for both step light bracket retainers.

  1. Install a nose piece to an air riveter or hand riveter.

  1. Insert the mandrel part of a new rivet into the nose piece.

  1. Using the riveter, install the step light bracket with the 3 rivets as shown in the illustration.

    HINT:
    If the rivet cannot be cut, pull it once and cut it.

    NOTICE:


    1. Do not pry the rivet with the riveter as this will cause damage to the riveter and mandrel.

8. INSTALL SIDE DOOR STEP PLATE REAR COVER LH

HINT:
When installing the side door step plate rear cover, heat the step plate cover and side door step plate rear cover using a heat light.

Standard:
Item
Temperature
Step Plate Cover
20 to 30°C (68 to 86°F)
Side Door Step Plate Rear Cover
20 to 30°C (68 to 86°F)

NOTICE:
Do not heat the step plate cover or side door step plate rear cover excessively.

  1. Clean the step plate cover surface.

    1. Using a heat light, heat the step plate cover surface.

    2. Remove the double-sided tape from the step plate cover surface.

    3. Wipe off any tape adhesive residue with cleaner.

  1. When using a new side door step plate rear cover:

    1. Using a heat light, heat the side door step plate rear cover surface.




  1. When reusing the side door step plate rear cover:

    1. Using a heat light, heat the side door step plate rear cover surface.

    2. Remove the double-sided tape from the side door step plate rear cover.

    3. Wipe off any tape adhesive residue with cleaner.

    4. Apply double-sided tape to the side door step plate rear cover as shown in the illustration.

    HINT:
