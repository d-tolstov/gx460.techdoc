SHIFT LEVER > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here



1. REMOVE REAR CONSOLE BOX ASSEMBLY

  1. Remove the rear console box (Click here).



2. REMOVE NO. 1 CONSOLE BOX DUCT

  1. Remove the clip and console box duct.



3. DISCONNECT TRANSMISSION CONTROL CABLE ASSEMBLY

  1. Move the shift lever to N.




  1. Disconnect the transmission control cable end from the shift lever.




  1. Rotate the socket counterclockwise approximately 180° and, while holding the socket in that position, disconnect the transmission control cable from the shift lever retainer.

    NOTICE:
    Do not over-rotate the socket as it will come off the internal spring and the transmission control cable will not be reusable.



4. REMOVE TRANSMISSION FLOOR SHIFT ASSEMBLY

  1. Disconnect the 2 connectors and detach the wire harness clamp.



  1. Remove the 4 bolts and transmission floor shift.





5. REMOVE NO. 1 CONSOLE BOX MOUNTING BRACKET

  1. Remove the 2 screws and console box mounting bracket.
