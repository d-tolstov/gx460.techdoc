DTC    C1AED    Rear Sensor Communication Malfunction


DESCRIPTION

WIRING DIAGRAM

INSPECTION PROCEDURE

CHECK FOR DTC (C1AED)

CHECK HARNESS AND CONNECTOR (CLEARANCE WARNING ECU - REAR CORNER SENSOR LH)

CHECK HARNESS AND CONNECTOR (REAR CORNER SENSOR LH - REAR CENTER SENSOR LH)

CHECK HARNESS AND CONNECTOR (REAR CENTER SENSOR LH - REAR CENTER SENSOR RH)

CHECK HARNESS AND CONNECTOR (REAR CENTER SENSOR RH - REAR CORNER SENSOR RH)

INSPECT NO. 1 ULTRASONIC SENSOR (REAR CORNER SENSOR LH)

INSPECT NO. 1 ULTRASONIC SENSOR (REAR CENTER SENSOR LH)

INSPECT NO. 1 ULTRASONIC SENSOR (REAR CENTER SENSOR RH)

REPLACE NO. 1 ULTRASONIC SENSOR (REAR CORNER SENSOR LH)

CHECK FOR DTC (C1AED)

REPLACE NO. 1 ULTRASONIC SENSOR (REAR CENTER SENSOR LH)

CHECK FOR DTC (C1AED)

REPLACE NO. 1 ULTRASONIC SENSOR (REAR CENTER SENSOR RH)

CHECK FOR DTC (C1AED)

REPLACE NO. 1 ULTRASONIC SENSOR (REAR CORNER SENSOR RH)

CHECK FOR DTC (C1AED)

CHECK HARNESS AND CONNECTOR (PARKING ASSIST ECU - REAR CORNER SENSOR LH)

CHECK HARNESS AND CONNECTOR (REAR CORNER SENSOR LH - REAR CENTER SENSOR LH)

CHECK HARNESS AND CONNECTOR (REAR CENTER SENSOR LH - REAR CENTER SENSOR RH)

CHECK HARNESS AND CONNECTOR (REAR CENTER SENSOR RH - REAR CORNER SENSOR RH)

INSPECT NO. 1 ULTRASONIC SENSOR (REAR CORNER SENSOR LH)

INSPECT NO. 1 ULTRASONIC SENSOR (REAR CENTER SENSOR LH)

INSPECT NO. 1 ULTRASONIC SENSOR (REAR CENTER SENSOR RH)

REPLACE NO. 1 ULTRASONIC SENSOR (REAR CORNER SENSOR LH)

CHECK FOR DTC (C1AED)

REPLACE NO. 1 ULTRASONIC SENSOR (REAR CENTER SENSOR LH)

CHECK FOR DTC (C1AED)

REPLACE NO. 1 ULTRASONIC SENSOR (REAR CENTER SENSOR RH)

CHECK FOR DTC (C1AED)

REPLACE NO. 1 ULTRASONIC SENSOR (REAR CORNER SENSOR RH)

CHECK FOR DTC (C1AED)
