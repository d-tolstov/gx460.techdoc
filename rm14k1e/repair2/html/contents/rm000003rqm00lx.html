1. INSPECT FRONT SEAT INNER BELT ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Measure the resistance according to the value(s) in the table below.

    Standard Resistance:
    Tester Connection
    		Condition
    		Specified Condition
    1 - 2
    		Seat belt unfastened
    		10 kΩ or higher
    1 - 2
    		Seat belt fastened
    		Below 1 Ω

    If the result is not as specified, replace the front seat inner belt assembly LH.
    Text in Illustration 
    *a
    		Component without harness connected
    (Front Seat Inner Belt Assembly LH)



2. INSPECT FRONT SEAT INNER BELT ASSEMBLY RH



  1. Measure the resistance according to the value(s) in the table below.

    Standard Resistance:
    Tester Connection
    		Condition
    		Specified Condition
    A-1 - A-2
    		Seat belt unfastened
    		10 kΩ or higher
    Seat belt fastened
    		Below 1 Ω
    A-2 - B-1
    		Seat belt unfastened
    		Below 1 Ω
    Seat belt fastened
    		10 kΩ or higher
    A-3 - B-3
    		Always
    		Below 1 Ω

    If the result is not as specified, replace the front seat inner belt assembly RH.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Connector A
    *2
    		Connector B
    *a
    		Component without harness connected
    (Front Seat Inner Belt Assembly RH)
