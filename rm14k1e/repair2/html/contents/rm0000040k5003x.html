EGR COOLER > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here



1. REMOVE INTAKE MANIFOLD

  1. Remove the intake manifold (Click here).



2. REMOVE NO. 3 WATER BY-PASS PIPE



  1. Disconnect the No. 4 water by-pass hose.

  1. Disconnect the hose and remove the 2 bolts and No. 3 water by-pass pipe.



3. DISCONNECT NO. 8 WATER BY-PASS HOSE





4. DISCONNECT NO. 11 WATER BY-PASS HOSE





5. REMOVE NO. 1 EGR PIPE SUB-ASSEMBLY



  1. Remove the 4 nuts and EGR pipe.

  1. Remove the 2 gaskets.



6. REMOVE EGR COOLER ASSEMBLY



  1. Remove the 2 bolts and EGR cooler.



7. REMOVE NO. 2 EGR PIPE SUB-ASSEMBLY



  1. Remove the 2 bolts, No. 2 EGR pipe and gasket.

    NOTICE:
    Be careful not to damage the surfaces that contact the gasket.
