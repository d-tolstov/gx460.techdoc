DTC    B2784    Antenna Coil Open / Short

for Preparation Click here


DESCRIPTION

When an open or short circuit is detected in the transponder key amplifier coil built into the engine switch, the certification ECU stores this DTC. This DTC is also stored as a past DTC.

DTC Code
DTC Detection Condition
Trouble Area
DTC Output Confirmation Operation
B2784
The transponder key amplifier coil built into the engine switch is open or shorted (determined by communication with certification ECU) (1 trip detection logic*).
  1. Terminal VC5, TXCT or CODE is open (includes disconnection of the engine switch).
  2. The transponder key amplifier coil built into the engine switch is open.
  1. Engine switch
  2. Harness or connector
  3. Certification ECU
With the shift lever in P, the key is held against the engine switch and an engine start operation is performed by pressing and holding the engine switch when the key battery is depleted.
  1. *: Only output while a malfunction is present.

  2. The certification ECU outputs a fixed 5 V power source voltage through a resistance from terminal CODE. The engine switch grounds or does not ground this power source voltage accordingly.
  3. The certification ECU outputs a fixed 5 V power source voltage through a resistance and transistor. The engine switch grounds or does not ground this signal accordingly.
  4. After performing repair, perform the operation that fulfills the DTC output confirmation operation, and then confirm that no DTCs are output again.
