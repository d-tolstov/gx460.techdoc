CHECK COMBINATION METER


  1. Disconnect the G3 meter connector.

  2. Measure the resistance and voltage according to the value(s) in the table below.

    Terminal No. (Symbol)
    		Wiring Color
    		Terminal Description
    		Condition
    		Specified Condition
    G3-1 (EP) - Body ground
    		W-B - Body ground
    		Ground
    		Always
    		Below 1 立
    G3-25 (B) - Body ground
    		L - Body ground
    		Combination meter B line
    		Always
    		11 to 14 V
    G3-27 (IG+) - Body ground
    		R - Body ground
    		Combination meter IG+ line
    		Engine switch on (IG)
    		11 to 14 V
    G3-39 (CANL) - Body ground
    		W - Body ground
    		CAN communication line
    		Engine switch off
    		200 立 or higher
    G3-40 (CANH) - Body ground
    		LG - Body ground
    		CAN communication line
    		Engine switch off
    		200 立 or higher
    If the result is not as specified, there may be a malfunction on the wire harness side.

  3. Reconnect the G3 meter connector.

  4. Measure the voltage according to the value(s) in the table below.

    Terminal No. (Symbol)
    		Wiring Color
    		Terminal Description
    		Condition
    		Specified Condition
    G3-12 (P/SB) - Body ground
    		L - Body ground
    		Front passenger seat belt signal
    1. Engine switch on (IG)
    2. Front passenger seat occupied
    3. Seat belt fastened
    		11 to 14 V
    1. Engine switch on (IG)
    2. Front passenger seat occupied
    3. Seat belt unfastened
    		Alternating between 11 to 14 V and below 1 V
    G3-18 (WRNP) - Body ground
    		L - Body ground
    		Front passenger seat belt warning signal
    1. Engine switch on (IG)
    2. Front passenger seat occupied, seat belt fastened (Front passenger side seat belt warning light off)
    		11 to 14 V
    1. Engine switch on (IG)
    2. Front passenger seat occupied, seat belt unfastened (Front passenger side seat belt warning light blinks)
    		Alternating between 11 to 14 V and below 1 V
    If the result is not as specified, the meter may have a malfunction.
