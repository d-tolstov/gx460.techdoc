DTC    B15CB    Telematics Transceiver Antenna Disconnected

for Preparation Click here


DESCRIPTION

If an open circuit in the telephone antenna assembly is detected continuously for 1 minute during the terminal check performed by the telematics transceiver every minute after the engine switch is turned on (ACC), this DTC will be stored.

DTC Code
DTC Detection Condition
Trouble Area
B15CB
The DCM antenna is not connected or has an open circuit.
  1. Telephone antenna assembly
  2. No. 3 antenna cord sub-assembly
  3. No. 2 antenna cord sub-assembly
  4. Harness or connector
  5. Telematics transceiver
  6. Display and navigation module display
