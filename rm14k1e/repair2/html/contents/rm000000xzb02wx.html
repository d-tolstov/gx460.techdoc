DTC    B2620    ECU Power Source Circuit Malfunction

for Preparation Click here


DESCRIPTION

The ECU power source circuit supplies positive voltage to the multiplex tilt and telescopic ECU.

DTC Code
Detection Condition
Trouble Area
B2620
The ECU power source voltage drops to 8 V or less for 10 seconds or more.
  1. ECU-B fuse
  2. Harness or connector
  3. Multiplex tilt and telescopic ECU
