OIL PRESSURE SWITCH > INSTALLATION

for Preparation Click here



1. INSTALL ENGINE OIL PRESSURE SWITCH ASSEMBLY

  1. Apply adhesive to 2 or 3 threads of the oil pressure switch.

    Adhesive:
    Toyota Genuine Adhesive 1344, Three Bond 1344 or equivalent

    NOTICE:
    Do not apply adhesive to the oil inlet port of the oil pressure switch.

  1. Install the oil pressure switch.

    Torque:
    15 N*m{ 153 kgf*cm , 11 ft.*lbf }

    NOTICE:
    Do not start the engine within 1 hour after installation.

  1. Connect the oil pressure switch connector.



2. CHECK ENGINE OIL LEVEL

  1. Warm up the engine. Then stop the engine and wait for 5 minutes.

  1. Check that the engine oil level is between the low level and full level marks on the dipstick.
    If low, check for leakage and add oil up to the full level mark.

    NOTICE:
    Do not fill engine oil above the full level mark.



3. INSPECT FOR OIL LEAK

  1. Start the engine. Make sure that there are no oil leaks from the area that was worked on.



4. INSTALL NO. 1 ENGINE UNDER COVER SUB-ASSEMBLY



  1. Hook the No. 1 engine under cover to the vehicle body as shown in the illustration.

  1. Install the 4 bolts.

    Torque:
    29 N*m{ 296 kgf*cm , 21 ft.*lbf }



5. INSTALL LOWER FRONT BUMPER COVER

  1. Install the lower front bumper cover with the 5 bolts and clip.

    Torque:
    8.0 N*m{ 82 kgf*cm , 71 in.*lbf }
