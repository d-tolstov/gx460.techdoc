1. DISPOSE OF STABILIZER CONTROL WITH ACCUMULATOR HOUSING ASSEMBLY



  1. Using a drill, make a hole of 90 mm (3.54 in.) in the areas of the accumulator housing indicated in the illustration to discharge the gas inside.

    CAUTION:
    1. Be careful and always use the proper safety equipment when drilling because shards of metal may fly about.
    2. The gas is colorless, odorless and non-poisonous.
