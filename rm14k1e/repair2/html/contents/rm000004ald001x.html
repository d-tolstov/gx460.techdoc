REAR SEAT CUSHION HEATER (for 60/40 Split Slide Walk-in Seat Type RH Side) > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here


CAUTION:
Wear protective gloves. Sharp areas on the parts may injure your hands.



1. REMOVE REAR NO. 1 SEAT ASSEMBLY RH

  1. Remove the rear No. 1 seat assembly (Click here).



2. REMOVE REAR SEAT UPPER RECLINING COVER RH



  1. Using a screwdriver, detach the 2 claws and remove the cover.

    HINT:
    Tape the screwdriver tip before use.

    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape



3. REMOVE RECLINING ADJUSTER RELEASE HANDLE RH



  1. Remove the 3 screws and handle.



4. REMOVE REAR SEAT CUSHION MOULDING RH



  1. Using a clip remover, detach the clip.

  1. Using a moulding remover, detach the 3 claws.

  1. Move the moulding in the direction of the arrow to detach the guide and remove the moulding.



5. REMOVE REAR SEAT REAR CUSHION MOULDING LH



  1. Remove the screw.

  1. Using a moulding remover, detach the 3 claws.

  1. Detach the 2 guides and remove the moulding.



6. REMOVE SEAT CUSHION COVER WITH PAD



  1. Detach the hooks.




  1. w/ Seat Heater System:

    1. Disconnect the 2 seat heater connectors.

    2. Detach the 2 clamps and disconnect the seat heater wire harness.

  1. Remove the seat cushion cover with pad from the seat cushion frame.



7. REMOVE REAR NO. 2 SEAT PROTECTOR



  1. Remove the 2 protectors from the cushion pad.



8. REMOVE SEPARATE TYPE REAR SEAT CUSHION COVER


  1. Remove the hog rings and seat cushion cover from the seat cushion pad.





9. REMOVE REAR SEAT CUSHION HEATER ASSEMBLY



  1. Cut off the tack pins and remove the seat cushion heater from the seat cushion cover.
