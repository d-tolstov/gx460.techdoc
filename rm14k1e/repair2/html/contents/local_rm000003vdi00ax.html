DTC    B27A5    Open in Front Floor Electrical Key Oscillator Circuit


DESCRIPTION

WIRING DIAGRAM

INSPECTION PROCEDURE

CHECK CONNECTOR CONNECTION

CHECK HARNESS AND CONNECTOR (CERTIFICATION ECU - INDOOR NO. 1 ELECTRICAL KEY ANTENNA)

CHECK CERTIFICATION ECU (OUTPUT TO INDOOR NO. 1 ELECTRICAL KEY ANTENNA [FRONT FLOOR])

REPLACE INDOOR NO. 1 ELECTRICAL KEY ANTENNA ASSEMBLY (FRONT FLOOR)

CLEAR DTC

CHECK FOR DTC
