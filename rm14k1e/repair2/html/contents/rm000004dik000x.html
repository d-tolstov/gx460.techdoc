1. CUSTOMIZE LEXUS PARKING ASSIST-SENSOR SYSTEM

HINT:
The following items can be customized.

NOTICE:
  1. When the customer requests a change in a function, first make sure that the function can be customized.
  2. Record the current settings before customizing.

  1. Customizing with the Intelligent Tester

    1. Connect the intelligent tester to the DLC3.

    2. Turn the engine switch on (IG).

    3. Turn the intelligent tester on.

    4. Enter the following menus: Utility / Customize / Warning, Sensor or Display.

    5. Select the setting by referring to the table below.

      Display
      		Default
      		Content
      		Setting
      		Relevant ECU
      Rr Sensor Onset Range
      		Wide
      		Set the buzzer activation range for the rear center sensors.
      		Narrow or Wide
      1. Narrow: 650 mm (25.59 in.)
      2. Wide: 1500 mm (59.06 in.)
      1. Clearance warning ECU*1
      2. Parking assist ECU*2
      Fr Sensor Onset Range
      		Wide
      		Set the buzzer activation range for the front center sensors.
      		Narrow or Wide
      1. Narrow: 500 mm (19.69 in.)
      2. Wide: 1000 mm (39.37 in.)
      1. Clearance warning ECU*1
      2. Parking assist ECU*2
      Keep Sense Buzzer
      		Avail
      		Make the buzzer sound when the distance between the vehicle and obstacle does not change for more than 3 seconds.
      		Not Avail or Avail
      1. Not Avail: Not Available
      2. Avail: Available
      1. Clearance warning ECU*1
      2. Parking assist ECU*2
      Fr & Rr Buzzer Volume
      		M2
      		Change the buzzer volume setting.
      		L, M1, M2, M3 or H
      1. L: Low volume
      2. M1: Medium low volume
      3. M2: Medium volume
      4. M3: Medium high volume
      5. H: High volume
      1. Clearance warning ECU*1
      2. Parking assist ECU*2
      Sensor Condition N
      		Avail
      		Make the front sensors available when the shift lever is in N.
      		Not Avail or Avail
      1. Not Avail: Not Available
      2. Avail: Available
      1. Clearance warning ECU*1
      2. Parking assist ECU*2
      Reverse Range Front Sensor
      		Fcrn
      		Change the operation setting for the front sensors when the shift lever is in R.
      		F, Fcrn or F Stop
      1. F: Front sensors are available
      2. Fcrn: Front corner sensors are available
      3. F Stop: Front sensors are not available
      1. Clearance warning ECU*1
      2. Parking assist ECU*2
      Non P/R Range Rear Sensor
      		R Stop
      		Change the operation setting for the rear sensors when the shift lever is in any position except P or R.
      		R, Rcrn or R Stop
      1. R: All rear sensors are available
      2. Rcrn: Rear corner sensors are available
      3. R Stop: All rear sensors are not available
      1. Clearance warning ECU*1
      2. Parking assist ECU*2
      Display Mode
      		All
      		Change the display mode setting (when the LEXUS parking assist-sensor system is normal).
      		All or Undisp
      1. Clearance warning ECU*1
      2. Parking assist ECU*2

      1. *1: w/o Parking Assist Monitor System and/or Side Monitor System
      2. *2: w/ Parking Assist Monitor System and/or Side Monitor System
