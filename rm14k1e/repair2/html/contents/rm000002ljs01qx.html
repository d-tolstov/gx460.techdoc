FRONT SEAT OUTER BELT ASSEMBLY > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here


HINT:
  1. Use the same procedure for the RH and LH sides.
  2. The procedure listed below is for the LH side.



1. DISCONNECT CABLE FROM NEGATIVE BATTERY TERMINAL

NOTICE:
  1. w/ Navigation System (for HDD):
    After the engine switch is turned off, the display and navigation module display (HDD navigation system) records various types of memory and settings. As a result, after turning the engine switch off, be sure to wait for the time specified in the following table before disconnecting the cable from the negative (-) battery terminal.

    Waiting Time before Disconnecting Cable from Negative (-) Battery Terminal:

    Condition
    		Waiting Time
    Vehicle enrolled in G-BOOK system
    		6 minutes
    Vehicle not enrolled in G-BOOK system
    		1 minute

  2. When disconnecting the cable, some systems need to be initialized after the cable is reconnected (Click here).



2. REMOVE DOOR SCUFF PLATE ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Put protective tape around the door scuff plate.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape

  1. Using a screwdriver, detach the 4 clips, 10 claws and 2 guides.

    HINT:
    Tape the screwdriver tip before use.

  1. Disconnect the connector and remove the door scuff plate.



3. REMOVE FRONT DOOR OPENING TRIM WEATHERSTRIP LH

  1. Partially remove the front door opening trim weatherstrip so that the lower center pillar garnish and center pillar garnish can be removed (Click here).



4. REMOVE REAR NO. 1 FLOOR STEP COVER



HINT:
Use the same procedure for all rear No. 1 floor step covers.

  1. Using a screwdriver, detach the 2 claws and remove the rear No. 1 floor step cover.

    HINT:
    Tape the screwdriver tip before use.

    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape



5. REMOVE QUARTER SCUFF PLATE LH



  1. Remove the 2 bolts and quarter scuff plate.



6. REMOVE REAR DOOR SCUFF PLATE LH



  1. Put protective tape around the rear door scuff plate.

  1. Using a screwdriver, detach the 3 clips, 6 claws and 2 guides and remove the rear door scuff plate.

    HINT:
    Tape the screwdriver tip before use.

    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape



7. REMOVE REAR DOOR OPENING TRIM WEATHERSTRIP LH

  1. Partially remove the rear door opening trim weatherstrip so that the lower center pillar garnish and center pillar garnish can be removed (Click here).



8. REMOVE OUTER LAP BELT ANCHOR COVER



HINT:
Use the same procedure for the other outer lap belt anchor cover.

  1. Detach the 3 claws and remove the outer lap belt anchor cover.



9. REMOVE LOWER CENTER PILLAR GARNISH LH



  1. Remove the bolt and disconnect the front seat outer belt floor anchor.




  1. Detach the 4 claws and 2 clips and remove the lower center pillar garnish.



10. REMOVE NO. 2 ASSIST GRIP PLUG LH



HINT:
Use the same procedure for the other front No. 2 assist grip plug.

  1. Using a screwdriver, detach the 2 claws and remove the front No. 2 assist grip plug.

    HINT:
    Tape the screwdriver tip before use.

    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape



11. REMOVE NO. 2 ASSIST GRIP LH



HINT:
Use the same procedure for the other No. 2 assist grip.

  1. Remove the 2 bolts.

  1. Detach the 2 claws and remove the No. 2 assist grip.



12. REMOVE CENTER PILLAR GARNISH LH



  1. Move the front shoulder belt anchor adjuster to the lowest position.

  1. Remove the bolt.

  1. Detach the 2 clips and 2 guides.

  1. Pass the front seat outer belt floor anchor through the center pillar garnish and remove the center pillar garnish.



13. REMOVE FRONT SEAT OUTER BELT ASSEMBLY



  1. Remove the nut and shoulder anchor.




  1. Disconnect the pretensioner connector labeled A as shown in the illustration.

  1. Disconnect the tension reducer connector labeled B.

  1. w/ Pre-crash Safety System:
    Disconnect the pre-crash safety system connector labeled C.




  1. Remove the bolt and seat belt.



14. REMOVE FRONT SHOULDER BELT ANCHOR ADJUSTER ASSEMBLY



  1. Remove the 2 bolts and anchor adjuster.
