HEATED OXYGEN SENSOR > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here


CAUTION:
  1. The exhaust pipe is extremely hot immediately after the engine has stopped.
  2. Confirm that the exhaust pipe has cooled down before removing it.



1. REMOVE HEATED OXYGEN SENSOR (for Bank 1 Sensor 2)



  1. Disconnect the heated oxygen sensor connector.

  1. Using SST, remove the heated oxygen sensor from the front No. 2 exhaust pipe.

    SST
    09224-00010  



2. REMOVE HEATED OXYGEN SENSOR (for Bank 2 Sensor 2)



  1. Detach the 2 clamps and disconnect the heated oxygen sensor connector.




  1. Using SST, remove the heated oxygen sensor from the front exhaust pipe.

    SST
    09224-00010
