INSPECTION PROCEDURE

1.CHECK IF A PROPER CD IS INSERTED

  1. Make sure that the CD is an audio CD or a CD with an MP3 or WMA file, and that it is not deformed, flawed, stained, burred, or otherwise defective.

    OK:
    Normal CD.

    HINT:
    1. Translucent or uniquely-shaped CDs cannot be played.
    2. CDs with adhesive paper labels should not be played.
    3. Commercial audio CDs can be played.
    4. CD-DA files on CD-ROMs, CD-Rs, and CD-RWs can be played.
    5. MP3 and WMA files on CD-ROMs, CD-Rs, and CD-RWs can be played.
    6. For details on playable CDs, refer to the Owner's Manual.

2.CHECK THAT CD IS INSERTED PROPERLY

  1. Check whether or not the CD is inserted upside down.

    OK:
    The CD is properly inserted.

3.CHECK CD



  1. Clean the CD by wiping it with a soft cloth from the inside to the outside in a radial direction.

    OK:
    Malfunction disappears.

    NOTICE:
    Do not use a conventional record cleaner or antistatic preservative.
