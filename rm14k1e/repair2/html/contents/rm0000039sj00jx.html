STEERING COLUMN ASSEMBLY > DISASSEMBLY

for Preparation Click here



1. REMOVE STEERING LOCK ACTUATOR ASSEMBLY

  1. Using a center punch, mark the center of the 2 tapered-head bolts.

  1. Using a 3 to 4 mm (0.118 to 0.157 in.) drill, drill a hole in the 2 bolts.




  1. Using a screw extractor, remove the 2 bolts and steering lock actuator assembly from the steering column assembly.



2. REMOVE MULTIPLEX TILT AND TELESCOPIC ECU



  1. Disconnect the connector.

  1. Remove the bolt and ECU.
