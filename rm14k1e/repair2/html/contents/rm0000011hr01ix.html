GENERAL DESCRIPTION

  1. By operating the seat heater switch, which is located in the rear console end panel, the seat temperature can be controlled within the range of 30 to 40°C (86 to 104°F).

  2. The seat heater switch indicator light indicates seat heater on/off status.

  3. The seat heaters of both seats are controlled by the seat heater controls, which are located under the respective seat.
