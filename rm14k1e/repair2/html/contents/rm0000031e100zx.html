POWER STEERING OIL PRESSURE SWITCH > ON-VEHICLE INSPECTION

for Preparation Click here



1. INSPECT POWER STEERING OIL PRESSURE SWITCH

  1. Connect the intelligent tester to the DLC3.

  1. Turn the engine switch on (IG).

  1. Turn the intelligent tester on.

  1. Enter the following menus: Powertrain / Engine and ECT / Data List / All Data / Power Steering Pressure.

  1. Start the engine.

  1. Operate the steering wheel while idling and read the value on the intelligent tester.

    Standard:

    Measurement Condition
    		Specified Condition
    Power steering not operating
    		0 to 2000 kPa (0 to 20 kgf/cm2, 0 to 290 psi)
    Power steering operating
    		Value changes from value displayed when power steering not operating

    If the result is not as specified, check for DTCs (Click here). If the power steering system inspection results are normal and a DTC is not output, replace the power steering oil pressure switch.
