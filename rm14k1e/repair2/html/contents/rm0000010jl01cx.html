FRONT AIRBAG SENSOR > ON-VEHICLE INSPECTION

for Preparation Click here



1. CHECK FRONT AIRBAG SENSOR LH (VEHICLE NOT INVOLVED IN COLLISION AND AIRBAG NOT DEPLOYED)

  1. Perform a diagnostic system check (Click here).



2. CHECK FRONT AIRBAG SENSOR LH (VEHICLE INVOLVED IN COLLISION AND AIRBAG NOT DEPLOYED)

  1. Perform a diagnostic system check (Click here).

  1. If the front fender of the vehicle or its periphery are damaged, perform a visual check:

    1. Check for cracks, dents or chips in the case.
    2. Check for cracks, dents, chips and scratches in the connector.
    3. Check for peeling of the label or damage of the serial number.
    1. If any of the defects mentioned above are present, replace the front airbag sensor with a new one.



3. CHECK FRONT AIRBAG SENSOR LH (VEHICLE INVOLVED IN COLLISION AND AIRBAG DEPLOYED)

  1. Replace the front airbag sensor.

    CAUTION:
    For removal and installation of the front airbag sensor, be sure to follow the correct procedures.

    HINT:
    If the airbags have deployed, replace the front airbag sensor on the side in which the collision occurred. Replace both if necessary.
