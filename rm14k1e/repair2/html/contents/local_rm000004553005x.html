DTC    B2661    Lost Communication with Combination Meter


DESCRIPTION

WIRING DIAGRAM

INSPECTION PROCEDURE

CLEAR DTC

CHECK FOR DTC

CHECK HARNESS AND CONNECTOR (DRIVING SUPPORT SWITCH CONTROL ECU - COMBINATION METER)

CHECK HARNESS AND CONNECTOR (COMBINATION METER - BATTERY AND BODY GROUND)

REPLACE COMBINATION METER ASSEMBLY

CLEAR DTC

CHECK FOR DTC
