DTC    C1625    Open or Short in Steering Angle Sensor +B

for Preparation Click here


DESCRIPTION

This DTC is stored if the parking assist ECU receives a signal via CAN communication from the spiral with sensor cable sub-assembly that indicates a power supply system problem.

DTC Code
DTC Detection Condition
Trouble Area
C1625
Open or short in steering angle sensor +B
  1. Harness or connector
  2. Spiral with sensor cable sub-assembly (Steering angle sensor)

INSPECTION PROCEDURE

NOTICE:
  1. When "System initializing" is displayed on the display and navigation module display after the battery terminal disconnected, correct the steering angle neutral point (Click here).
  2. Depending on the parts that are replaced or operations that are performed during vehicle inspection or maintenance, calibration of other systems as well as the parking assist monitor system may be needed (Click here).
  3. Inspect the fuse for circuits related to this system before performing the following inspection procedure.
