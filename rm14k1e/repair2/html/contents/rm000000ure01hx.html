GENERAL

  1. The heater and blower both have 3 levels controlled by operating the dial-type refreshing seat switch, which is located in the upper console panel.

  2. The on/off state of the heater or blower can be determined by checking if the refreshing seat switch indicator light is on or off.

  3. The blowers of both seats are controlled by the air conditioning amplifier assembly.

  4. The seat heaters of both seats are controlled by the seat heater controls, which are located under their respective seats.
