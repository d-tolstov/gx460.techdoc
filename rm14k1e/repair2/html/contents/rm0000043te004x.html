DTC    B2410    Headlight Swivel Motor LH Communication Malfunction

DTC    B2411    Headlight Swivel Motor RH Communication Malfunction

for Preparation Click here


DESCRIPTION

These DTCs are stored when the headlight swivel ECU assembly (AFS ECU) detects errors in its own communications with the headlight assemblies (headlight swivel motors).

DTC Code
DTC Detection Condition
Trouble Area
B2410
The headlight swivel ECU assembly (AFS ECU) detects errors in its own communication with the headlight assembly LH (headlight swivel motor LH).
  1. Headlight assembly LH (headlight swivel motor LH)
  2. Headlight swivel ECU assembly (AFS ECU)
  3. Harness or connector
B2411
The headlight swivel ECU assembly (AFS ECU) detects errors in its own communication with the headlight assembly RH (headlight swivel motor RH).
  1. Headlight assembly RH (headlight swivel motor RH)
  2. Headlight swivel ECU assembly (AFS ECU)
  3. Harness or connector

INSPECTION PROCEDURE

Refer to Lighting System "DTC B2410" (Click here).
