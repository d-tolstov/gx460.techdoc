FRONT CRANKSHAFT OIL SEAL > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here



1. REMOVE RADIATOR ASSEMBLY

  1. Remove the radiator assembly (Click here).



2. REMOVE CRANKSHAFT PULLEY



  1. Using SST, remove the crankshaft pulley set bolt.

    SST
    09213-70011  
    09330-00021  
    Text in Illustration 
    *a
    		Hold

    		Turn

  1. Temporarily install the pulley set bolt to the crankshaft until 2 or 3 threads are engaged.




  1. Using the pulley set bolt and SST, remove the crankshaft pulley.

    SST
    09950-50013   (09951-05010, 09952-05010, 09953-05010, 09954-05011)
    Text in Illustration 
    *a
    		Hold

    		Turn



3. REMOVE CRANKSHAFT TIMING GEAR KEY



  1. Remove the crankshaft timing gear key from the crankshaft.



4. REMOVE FRONT CRANKSHAFT OIL SEAL



  1. Using a screwdriver, pry out the front crankshaft oil seal.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape

    HINT:
    Tape the screwdriver tip before use.

    NOTICE:
    Do not damage the surface of the front crankshaft oil seal press fit hole or crankshaft.
