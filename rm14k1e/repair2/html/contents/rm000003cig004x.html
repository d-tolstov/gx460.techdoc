ROOF RACK > REASSEMBLY

for Preparation Click here


HINT:
  1. Use the same procedure for the RH and LH sides.
  2. The procedure listed below is for the LH side.



1. INSTALL FRONT ROOF RACK LEG CUSHION LH

HINT:
When installing the front roof rack leg cushion, heat the roof rack assembly surface using a heat light.

Standard:
Item
Temperature
Roof Rack Assembly
20 to 30°C (68 to 86°F)

NOTICE:
Do not heat the roof rack assembly excessively.

  1. Clean the roof rack assembly surface.

    1. Using a heat light, heat the roof rack assembly surface.

    2. Remove the double-sided tape from the roof rack assembly surface.

    3. Wipe off any tape adhesive residue with cleaner.




  1. Install a new front roof rack leg cushion.

    1. Remove the peeling paper from the face of a new front roof rack leg cushion.

      HINT:
      After removing the peeling paper, keep the exposed adhesive free from foreign matter.

    2. Install the front roof rack leg cushion as shown in the illustration.

      HINT:
      Press the front roof rack leg cushion firmly to install it.
