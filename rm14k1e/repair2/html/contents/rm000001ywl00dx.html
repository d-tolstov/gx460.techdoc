REAR POWER SEAT CONTROL SYSTEM > Park / Neutral Position Switch Circuit

for Preparation Click here


DESCRIPTION

The fold seat control ECU receives signals from the park/neutral position switch and controls the seat folding and return operations. If the shift lever is in any position other than P when the engine switch is on (IG), the seat cannot be operated.
