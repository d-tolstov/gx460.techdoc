LIGHTING SYSTEM > DTC CHECK / CLEAR

for Preparation Click here



CHECK DTC

  1. Connect the intelligent tester to the DLC3.

  2. Turn the engine switch on (IG).

  3. Turn the intelligent tester on.

  4. Enter the following menus: Body Electrical / Trouble Codes.

  5. Check for DTCs.


CLEAR DTC

  1. Connect the intelligent tester to the DLC3.

  2. Turn the engine switch on (IG).

  3. Turn the intelligent tester on.

  4. Enter the following menus: Body Electrical / Trouble Code.

  5. Clear the DTCs.
