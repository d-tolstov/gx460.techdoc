INSPECTION PROCEDURE

HINT:
When replacing the telematics transceiver, perform the vehicle contract setting (Click here).

1.CHECK HARNESS AND CONNECTOR (TELEMATICS TRANSCEIVER - MAP LIGHT)

  1. Disconnect the G56 telematics transceiver connector.

  1. Disconnect the W7 map light assembly connector.

  1. Measure the resistance according to the value(s) in the table below.

    Standard Resistance:
    Tester Connection
    		Condition
    		Specified Condition
    G56-11 (IND1) - W7-19 (IND1)
    		Always
    		Below 1 立
    G56-12 (IND2) - W7-18 (IND2)
    		Always
    		Below 1 立
    G56-11 (IND1) - Body ground
    		Always
    		10 k立 or higher
    G56-12 (IND2) - Body ground
    		Always
    		10 k立 or higher



NG
REPAIR OR REPLACE HARNESS OR CONNECTOR
OK


2.INSPECT MAP LIGHT ASSEMBLY

  1. Remove the map light assembly (Click here).

  1. Connect 2 dry-cell batteries in series.




  1. Connect the positive (+) lead from the batteries to terminal W7-19 (IND1) or W7-18 (IND2), and the negative (-) lead from the batteries to terminal W7-5 (GND3) of the map light assembly connector.

  1. Check if the illumination for the emergency call switch comes on.

    OK:
    Measurement Condition
    		Specified Condition
    Battery positive (+) → W7-19 (IND1)
    Battery negative (-) → W7-5 (GND3)
    		Red indicator comes on
    Battery positive (+) → W7-18 (IND2)
    Battery negative (-) → W7-5 (GND3)
    		Green indicator comes on



NG
REPLACE MAP LIGHT ASSEMBLY (Click here)
OK
