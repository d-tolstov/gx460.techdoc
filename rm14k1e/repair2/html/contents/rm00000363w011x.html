1. REMOVE TRANSMITTER BATTERY

NOTICE:
Take extra care when handling these precision electronic components.




  1. Push and hold the release knob in the direction indicated by the arrow in the illustration. Then take the mechanical key out of the case.




  1. Insert a precision screwdriver into the gap and turn the screwdriver to detach the transmitter housing cover.

    HINT:
    Tape the screwdriver tip before use.




  1. Insert a precision screwdriver into the gap and remove the battery (lithium battery: CR1632).

    NOTICE:
    1. Do not push the terminals with your finger.
    2. Do not forcibly pry up the battery. The terminals may be damaged.
    3. Do not touch the battery with wet hands. Water may cause rust.
    4. Do not touch or move any components inside the transmitter. It may interfere with proper operation.



2. REMOVE TRANSMITTER BATTERY (w/ Battery Cover)

NOTICE:
Take extra care when handling these precision electronic components.




  1. Push and hold the release knob in the direction indicated by the arrow in the illustration.

  1. Remove the mechanical key from the case.




  1. Detach the claw and remove the slide cover.




  1. Remove the 4 screws and battery cover.

  1. Remove the O-ring.




  1. Insert a precision screwdriver into the gap and remove the battery (lithium battery: CR1632).

    NOTICE:
    1. Do not touch the terminals.
    2. Do not forcibly pry up the battery. The terminals may be damaged.
    3. Do not touch the battery with wet hands. Water may cause rust.
    4. Do not touch or move any components inside the transmitter. It may interfere with proper operation.



3. INSTALL TRANSMITTER BATTERY (w/ Battery Cover)



  1. Install a battery (lithium battery: CR1632) with the positive (+) side facing upward as shown in the illustration.

    NOTICE:
    1. When inserting the transmitter battery, do not bend the terminal.
    2. Keep the transmitter cover interior free of dust and oil.

  1. Install a new O-ring.




  1. Install the battery cover with the 4 screws.




  1. Attach the claw to install the slide cover.

  1. Insert the mechanical key into the transmitter.

  1. Press one of the transmitter's switches and check that the LED illuminates.



4. INSTALL TRANSMITTER BATTERY



  1. Install the battery (lithium battery: CR1632) with the positive (+) side facing upward as shown in the illustration.

    NOTICE:
    1. Do not bend the transmitter battery electrode during insertion.
    2. Keep the transmitter cover interior free of dust and oil.

  1. Install the transmitter housing cover by pressing down on it.

  1. Insert the mechanical key into the transmitter.

  1. Press one of the transmitter's switches and check that the LED illuminates.
