REAR POWER SEAT CONTROL SYSTEM > Rear Power Seat Switch Circuit

for Preparation Click here


DESCRIPTION

When the rear power seat switch is operated, a recline signal is sent to the fold seat control ECU. The ECU activates the power seat motor based on the signal from the rear power seat switch.
