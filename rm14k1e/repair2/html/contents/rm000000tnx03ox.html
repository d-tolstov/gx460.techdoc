POWER DOOR LOCK CONTROL SYSTEM > DTC CHECK / CLEAR

for Preparation Click here



CHECK DTC

  1. Connect the intelligent tester to the DLC3.

  2. Turn the engine switch on (IG).

  3. Turn the intelligent tester on.

  4. Enter the following menus: Body / (desired system) / DTC.

  5. Read the DTCs.


CLEAR DTC

  1. Connect the intelligent tester to the DLC3.

  2. Turn the engine switch on (IG).

  3. Turn the intelligent tester on.

  4. Enter the following menus: Body / (desired system) / DTC.

  5. Clear the DTCs.
