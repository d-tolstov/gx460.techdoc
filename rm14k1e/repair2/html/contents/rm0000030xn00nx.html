ENGINE UNIT > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here



1. REMOVE ENGINE WIRE


2. REMOVE FUEL HOSE



  1. Detach the clamp and remove the fuel hose (Click here).



3. REMOVE NO. 2 FUEL TUBE SUB-ASSEMBLY



  1. Remove the bolt.

  1. Detach the clamp and remove the No. 2 fuel tube (Click here).



4. REMOVE AIR SWITCHING VALVE ASSEMBLY (for Bank 1)



  1. Remove the 2 bolts and 2 wire harness clamp brackets.




  1. Remove the 2 bolts.




  1. Remove the 3 bolts, air switching valve and gasket.

    NOTICE:
    Be careful not to damage the installation surface of the gasket.



5. REMOVE AIR SWITCHING VALVE ASSEMBLY (for Bank 2)



  1. Remove the 2 bolts and 2 wire harness clamp brackets.




  1. Remove the 2 bolts.




  1. Remove the 3 bolts, air switching valve and gasket.

    NOTICE:
    Be careful not to damage the installation surface of the gasket.



6. REMOVE NO. 3 WATER BY-PASS PIPE



  1. Disconnect the hose.

  1. Remove the 2 bolts and water by-pass pipe.



7. REMOVE NO. 3 WATER BY-PASS HOSE





8. REMOVE NO. 4 AIR TUBE



  1. Remove the bolt and No. 4 air tube.



9. REMOVE NO. 3 AIR TUBE



  1. Remove the bolt and No. 3 air tube.



10. REMOVE NO. 2 WATER BY-PASS PIPE



  1. Disconnect the hose and remove the 2 bolts and No. 2 water by-pass pipe.



11. REMOVE NO. 8 WATER BY-PASS HOSE





12. DISCONNECT NO. 11 WATER BY-PASS HOSE





13. REMOVE EGR COOLER ASSEMBLY



  1. Remove the 2 bolts and EGR cooler.



14. REMOVE FRONT NO. 1 ENGINE MOUNTING BRACKET RH



  1. Remove the 4 bolts and front No. 1 engine mounting bracket RH.



15. REMOVE FRONT NO. 1 ENGINE MOUNTING BRACKET LH



  1. Remove the 4 bolts and front No. 1 engine mounting bracket LH.



16. REMOVE FUEL DELIVERY PIPE SUB-ASSEMBLY

  1. Disconnect the No. 6 wire harness connector.




  1. Remove the 2 bolts and fuel delivery pipe.

    NOTICE:
    When removing the delivery pipe, hold the pipe by both ends and pull it straight upward.

  1. Remove the 2 delivery pipe spacers and 4 insulators from the intake manifold.



17. REMOVE NO. 2 FUEL DELIVERY PIPE SUB-ASSEMBLY

  1. Disconnect the No. 7 wire harness connector.




  1. Remove the 2 bolts and No. 2 fuel delivery pipe.

    NOTICE:
    When removing the delivery pipe, hold the pipe by both ends and pull it straight upward.

  1. Remove the 2 delivery pipe spacers and 4 insulators from the intake manifold.



18. REMOVE NO. 4 ENGINE COVER





19. REMOVE NO. 3 ENGINE COVER





20. REMOVE NO. 1 WATER BY-PASS HOSE



  1. Remove the No. 1 water by-pass hose.



21. REMOVE WATER PUMP PULLEY



  1. Using SST, hold the water pump pulley.

    SST
    09960-10010   (09962-01000, 09963-01000)

  1. Remove the 4 bolts and water pump pulley.



22. REMOVE NO. 1 IDLER PULLEY SUB-ASSEMBLY



  1. Remove the bolt and idler pulley.



23. REMOVE FAN BRACKET ASSEMBLY



  1. Remove the 5 bolts and fan bracket.



24. REMOVE SEPARATOR CASE



  1. Remove the 4 bolts and separator case.



25. REMOVE NO. 2 ENGINE COVER





26. REMOVE NO. 1 ENGINE COVER





27. REMOVE ENGINE WIRE



  1. Disconnect the 4 knock sensor connectors.

  1. Detach the 3 wire harness clamps.

  1. Remove the bolt and engine wire.



28. REMOVE NO. 11 WATER BY-PASS HOSE





29. REMOVE IGNITION COIL ASSEMBLY

  1. Remove the 8 bolts and 8 ignition coils.



30. REMOVE NOISE FILTER



  1. for Bank 1:
    Remove the bolt and noise filter from the cylinder head cover LH.




  1. for Bank 2:
    Remove the bolt and noise filter from the cylinder head cover RH.
