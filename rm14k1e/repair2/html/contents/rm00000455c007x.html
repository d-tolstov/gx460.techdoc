FRONT UPPER SUSPENSION ARM > DISASSEMBLY

for Preparation Click here



1. REMOVE FRONT SUSPENSION UPPER ARM BUSH LH

HINT:
Use the same procedure for the front and rear sides.




  1. Using a hammer and chisel, strike and bend the entire flange of the upper arm bush as shown in the illustration.




  1. Using SST and a press, press out the bush.

    SST
    09613-26010  
    09710-22021   (09710-01031)
    09950-00020  



2. REMOVE FRONT UPPER BALL JOINT DUST COVER LH



  1. Using a snap ring expander, remove the dust cover set ring and dust cover from the upper arm.

    NOTICE:
    1. Do not damage the ball joint dust cover.
    2. If the ball joint dust cover is damaged, replace it.
