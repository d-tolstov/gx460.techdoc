DTC    P2440    Secondary Air Injection System Switching Valve Stuck Open Bank1


DESCRIPTION

MONITOR DESCRIPTION

MONITOR STRATEGY

TYPICAL MALFUNCTION THRESHOLDS

CONFIRMATION DRIVING PATTERN

WIRING DIAGRAM

INSPECTION PROCEDURE

CHECK FOR ANY OTHER DTCS OUTPUT (IN ADDITION TO SECONDARY AIR INJECTION SYSTEM DTCS)

PERFORM ACTIVE TEST USING INTELLIGENT TESTER (SECONDARY AIR INJECTION SYSTEM OPERATION)

INSPECT AIR SWITCHING VALVE ASSEMBLY (PRESSURE SENSOR)

INSPECT AIR TUBE (BLOCKAGE AND LEAK)

INSPECT AIR SWITCHING VALVE ASSEMBLY

INSPECT AIR INJECTION VSV RELAY (AI-VSV)

CHECK AIR INJECTION VSV RELAY (AI-VSV) (POWER SOURCE)

CHECK HARNESS AND CONNECTOR (AIR INJECTION VSV RELAY (AI-VSV) - ECM)

INSPECT AIR INJECTION CONTROL DRIVER (POWER SOURCE OF AIR INJECTION CONTROL DRIVER)

CHECK HARNESS AND CONNECTOR (ECM - AIR INJECTION CONTROL DRIVER)

PERFORM ACTIVE TEST USING INTELLIGENT TESTER

REPLACE AIR INJECTION CONTROL DRIVER

CHECK WHETHER DTC OUTPUT RECURS (DTC P2440 AND/OR P2442)
