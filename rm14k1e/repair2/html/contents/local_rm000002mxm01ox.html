DTC    U1002    Lost Communication with Gateway Module (Power Management2)


DESCRIPTION

WIRING DIAGRAM

INSPECTION PROCEDURE

DISCONNECT CABLE FROM NEGATIVE BATTERY TERMINAL

CHECK CAN BUS WIRE (MAIN WIRE FOR DISCONNECTION, BUS LINE FOR SHORT CIRCUIT)

CHECK FOR OPEN IN CAN BUS MAIN WIRE (NO. 1 JUNCTION CONNECTOR - ECM)

CHECK FOR OPEN IN CAN BUS MAIN WIRE (NO. 1 JUNCTION CONNECTOR - POWER MANAGEMENT CONTROL ECU)

CONNECT CONNECTOR

CHECK FOR OPEN IN CAN BUS MAIN WIRE (ECM - NO. 1 JUNCTION CONNECTOR)

CONNECT CONNECTOR

CHECK FOR OPEN IN CAN BUS MAIN WIRE (POWER MANAGEMENT CONTROL ECU - NO. 1 JUNCTION CONNECTOR)

CHECK FOR SHORT IN CAN BUS WIRES (NO. 1 JUNCTION CONNECTOR - AIR CONDITIONING AMPLIFIER)

CHECK FOR SHORT IN CAN BUS WIRES (NO. 1 JUNCTION CONNECTOR - ECM)

CHECK FOR SHORT IN CAN BUS WIRES (NO. 1 JUNCTION CONNECTOR - POWER MANAGEMENT CONTROL ECU)

CONNECT CONNECTOR

CHECK FOR SHORT IN CAN BUS WIRES (AIR CONDITIONING AMPLIFIER)

CONNECT CONNECTOR

CHECK FOR SHORT IN CAN BUS WIRES (ECM)

CONNECT CONNECTOR

CHECK FOR SHORT IN CAN BUS WIRES (POWER MANAGEMENT CONTROL ECU)

CHECK HARNESS AND CONNECTOR (POWER MANAGEMENT CONTROL ECU - BATTERY AND GROUND)
