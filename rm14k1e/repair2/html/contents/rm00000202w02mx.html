1. INSPECT AIR CONDITIONING PRESSURE SENSOR

  1. Install a manifold gauge set.

  1. Disconnect the connector from the air conditioning pressure sensor.




  1. Connect the positive (+) lead of a DC 5 V power source to terminal 3 and the negative (-) lead to terminal 1.

  1. Connect the positive (+) lead of the voltmeter to terminal 2 and the negative (-) lead to terminal 1.

  1. Measure the voltage according to the value(s) in the table below.

    Standard Voltage:
    Tester Connection
    		Condition
    		Specified Condition
    2 (PR) - 1 (-)
    		Refrigerant pressure:
    0.39 to 3.187 MPa (57 to 463 psi)
    		0.92 to 4.9 V

    Text in Illustration 
    *a
    		Component without harness connected
    (Air Conditioning Pressure Sensor)
    If the result is not as specified, replace the air conditioning pressure sensor.
