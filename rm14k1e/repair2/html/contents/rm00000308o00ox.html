TRANSFER OIL > ON-VEHICLE INSPECTION

for Preparation Click here



1. CHECK TRANSFER OIL



  1. Remove the filler plug and gasket.




  1. Check that the oil level is between 0 to 5.0 mm (0 to 0.196 in.) from the bottom lip of the filler plug hole.
    If the result is not as specified, add transfer oil.
    Text in Illustration 
    *a
    		0 to 5 mm

    NOTICE:
    Too much or too little oil will lead to transfer problems.

  1. When the oil level is too low, check for oil leaks.

  1. Install a new gasket to the filler plug and then tighten the plug.

    Torque:
    37 N*m{ 377 kgf*cm , 27 ft.*lbf }



2. ADD TRANSFER OIL

  1. Remove the filler plug and gasket.

  1. Add oil so that the oil level is between 0 to 5.0 mm (0 to 0.196 in.) from the bottom lip of the filler plug hole.

    NOTICE:
    1. Add oil slowly.
    2. Add oil a little at a time, waiting several minutes between each addition of oil.

  1. Wait approximately 5 minutes and check that the oil level has not changed.

  1. Install a new gasket to the filler plug and then tighten the plug.

    Torque:
    37 N*m{ 377 kgf*cm , 27 ft.*lbf }
