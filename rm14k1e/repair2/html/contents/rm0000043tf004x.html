DTC    B2424    Headlight Leveling Motor LH Communication Malfunction

DTC    B2425    Headlight Leveling Motor RH Communication Malfunction

for Preparation Click here


DESCRIPTION

The headlight leveling motor operates by receiving the signal from the headlight swivel ECU (AFS ECU). The headlight swivel ECU (AFS ECU) detects the headlight leveling motor status using the signal from the headlight leveling motor.

DTC Code
DTC Detection Condition
Trouble Area
B2424
  1. Malfunction in the headlight leveling motor LH.
  2. Open or short in the headlight leveling motor LH communication circuit.
  1. Headlight assembly LH (headlight leveling motor LH)
  2. Headlight swivel ECU assembly (AFS ECU)
  3. Harness or connector
B2425
  1. Malfunction in the headlight leveling motor RH.
  2. Open or short in the headlight leveling motor RH communication circuit.
  1. Headlight assembly RH (headlight leveling motor RH)
  2. Headlight swivel ECU assembly (AFS ECU)
  3. Harness or connector
