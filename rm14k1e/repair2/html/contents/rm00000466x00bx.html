1. DISCONNECT CABLE FROM NEGATIVE BATTERY TERMINAL

CAUTION:
Wait at least 90 seconds after disconnecting the cable from the negative (-) battery terminal to disable the SRS system.

NOTICE:
  1. w/ Navigation System (for HDD):
    After the engine switch is turned off, the display and navigation module display (HDD navigation system) records various types of memory and settings. As a result, after turning the engine switch off, be sure to wait for the time specified in the following table before disconnecting the cable from the negative (-) battery terminal.

    Waiting Time before Disconnecting Cable from Negative (-) Battery Terminal :

    Condition
    		Waiting Time
    Vehicle enrolled in safety connect system
    		6 minutes
    Vehicle not enrolled in safety connect system
    		1 minute

  2. When disconnecting the cable, some systems need to be initialized after the cable is reconnected (Click here).



2. REMOVE DOOR SCUFF PLATE ASSEMBLY RH



  1. Put protective tape around the door scuff plate.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape

  1. w/o Illumination:

    1. Using a screwdriver, detach the 4 clips, 10 claws and 2 guides and remove the door scuff plate.

      HINT:
      Tape the screwdriver tip before use.

  1. w/ Illumination:

    1. Using a screwdriver, detach the 4 clips, 10 claws and 2 guides.

      HINT:
      Tape the screwdriver tip before use.

    2. Disconnect the connector and remove the door scuff plate.



3. REMOVE COWL SIDE TRIM BOARD RH



  1. Remove the clip.

  1. Detach the clip and claw and remove the cowl side trim board.



4. REMOVE NO. 2 INSTRUMENT PANEL UNDER COVER SUB-ASSEMBLY



  1. Remove the screw.

  1. Detach the 3 clips and 2 guides.

  1. Disconnect the foot light, detach the clamp and remove the No. 2 instrument panel under cover.



5. REMOVE FRONT NO. 2 CONSOLE BOX INSERT



  1. Detach the 2 clips and guide and remove the front No. 2 console box insert.



6. REMOVE INSTRUMENT PANEL FINISH PANEL END RH



  1. Detach the 11 clips and remove the instrument panel finish panel end.



7. REMOVE INSTRUMENT SIDE PANEL RH



  1. Put protective tape around the instrument side panel.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape

  1. Using a moulding remover, detach the 3 clips and 2 guides and remove the instrument side panel.



8. REMOVE CENTER INSTRUMENT CLUSTER FINISH PANEL GARNISH



  1. Detach the 13 clips and remove the center instrument cluster finish panel garnish together with the instrument cluster finish panel garnish.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Center Instrument Cluster Finish Panel Garnish
    *2
    		Instrument Cluster Finish Panel Garnish



9. REMOVE NO. 2 INSTRUMENT PANEL REGISTER ASSEMBLY



  1. Detach the 8 clips and remove the No. 2 instrument panel register.



10. REMOVE LOWER INSTRUMENT COVER LH



  1. Put protective tape around the lower instrument cover.

  1. Using a screwdriver, detach the 4 claws and remove the lower instrument cover.

    HINT:
    Tape the screwdriver tip before use.

    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape



11. REMOVE LOWER NO. 2 INSTRUMENT PANEL AIRBAG ASSEMBLY



  1. Remove the 3 bolts.

  1. Detach the 6 claws and remove the instrument panel airbag.

  1. Disconnect the connector.

    NOTICE:
    When handling the airbag connector, take care not to damage the airbag wire harness.



12. REMOVE INSTRUMENT PANEL BOX DOOR COVER



HINT:
Use the same procedure for all instrument panel box door covers.

  1. Using a screwdriver, detach the 2 claws and 2 guides and remove the instrument panel box door cover.

    HINT:
    Tape the screwdriver tip before use.

    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape



13. REMOVE INSTRUMENT PANEL BOX ASSEMBLY



  1. Remove the 3 screws <B> and 2 bolts <C>.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Screw <B>
    *2
    		Bolt <C>

  1. Detach the 4 clips.

  1. Disconnect each connector, detach the clamp and remove the instrument panel box.



14. REMOVE CLEARANCE WARNING ECU ASSEMBLY



  1. Disconnect the connector.

  1. Remove the bolt and clearance warning ECU.
