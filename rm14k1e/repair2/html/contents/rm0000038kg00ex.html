1. REMOVE REAR NO. 1 SPOILER COVER



  1. Detach the fastening tape and 5 clips and remove the rear No. 1 spoiler cover.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Fastening Tape



2. REMOVE REAR SPOILER COVER RH



  1. Put protective tape around the rear spoiler cover.

  1. Using a screwdriver, detach the 2 clips and remove the rear spoiler cover as shown in the illustration.

    HINT:
    Tape the screwdriver tip before use.

    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape



3. REMOVE REAR SPOILER SUB-ASSEMBLY



  1. Disconnect the connector.




  1. Remove the 6 bolts.




  1. Detach the 3 clips and rear spoiler.
