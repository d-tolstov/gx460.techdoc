1.NOTICE FOR ARMED STATE
If the vehicle is left in the armed state for an extended period of time or in another condition that causes the battery to be fully depleted, the alarm may sound simultaneously with the recharging of the battery or the replacement of the battery with a new one.* If this occurs, immediately unlock the doors through a wireless unlock operation or entry unlock operation to stop the alarm.

HINT:
*: This may occur in the following situation: during active arming mode while in the armed state, the cable is disconnected from the negative (-) battery terminal and then reconnected (Click here).
