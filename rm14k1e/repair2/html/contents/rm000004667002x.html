  1. Check the rear height control sensor.

    1. Connect 3 dry cell batteries of 1.5 V in series.

    2. Connect the positive (+) end of the batteries to terminal 1 (SHB) of the height control sensor and the negative (-) end of the batteries to terminal 3 (SHG). While slowly moving the sensor link up and down, measure the voltage according to the value(s) in the table below.

      Standard Voltage:
      for RH 
      Tester Connection
      		Sensor Condition
      		Specified Condition
      3 (SHG) - 2 (SHRR)
      		High (45°)
      		4.4 to 4.6 V
      Normal (0°)
      		2.3 to 2.6 V
      Low (-45°)
      		0.4 to 0.6 V
      for LH 
      Tester Connection
      		Sensor Condition
      		Specified Condition
      3 (SHG) - 2 (SHRL)
      		High (45°)
      		4.4 to 4.6 V
      Normal (0°)
      		2.3 to 2.6 V
      Low (-45°)
      		0.4 to 0.6 V

      NOTICE:
      Do not apply higher than 6 V.

      If the result is not as specified, replace the height control sensor.
