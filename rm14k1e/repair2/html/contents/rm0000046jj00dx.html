10. INSTALL ROOF HEADLINING ASSEMBLY

  1. Place the roof headlining into the vehicle through the back door.

    NOTICE:
    Be careful not to damage the roof headlining when placing it in the cabin.

  1. Connect the antenna cord connector.




  1. Attach the 2 clips, 2 guides and 8 fasteners to install the roof headlining.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Guide
    *2
    		Fastener

  1. Connect the connector and attach the 3 clamps to the rear pillar RH.

  1. Connect the 2 connectors and attach the clamp to the rear pillar LH.

  1. Connect the 2 connectors and attach the 3 clamps to the front pillar RH.

  1. Install the bolt.

  1. Connect the 2 connectors and attach the 3 clamps to the front pillar LH.

  1. Connect the drive gear connector.

  1. Connect the rain sensor connector.

  1. Connect the inner mirror connector.
