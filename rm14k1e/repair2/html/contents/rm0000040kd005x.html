AIR SWITCHING VALVE (for Bank 2) > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here



1. REMOVE ENGINE ROOM SIDE COVER



  1. Remove the 4 clips and engine room side cover.



2. REMOVE V-BANK COVER SUB-ASSEMBLY



  1. Raise the front of the V-bank cover to detach the 2 pins. Then detach the 2 V-bank cover hooks from the No. 1 V-bank cover bracket and remove the V-bank cover.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Pin
    *2
    		Hook
    *3
    		No. 1 V-bank Cover Bracket



3. REMOVE AIR CLEANER CAP AND HOSE



  1. Disconnect the mass air flow meter connector, No. 1 air hose and No. 2 ventilation hose and detach the wire harness clamp.

  1. Loosen the hose clamp.

  1. Detach the 4 clamps and remove the air cleaner cap and hose.



4. DISCONNECT NO. 3 VENTILATION HOSE





5. DISCONNECT NO. 1 AIR INJECTION SYSTEM HOSE





6. REMOVE AIR SWITCHING VALVE ASSEMBLY



  1. Detach the wire harness clamp and disconnect the air switching valve connector.

  1. Remove the bolt and disconnect the wire harness clamp bracket.




  1. Remove the 2 bolts.




  1. Remove the 3 bolts, air switching valve and gasket.

    NOTICE:
    Be careful not to damage the surfaces that contact the gasket.
