GENERAL

  1. This system has a side television camera assembly built into the outer rear view mirror assembly (passenger side) to display the passenger side view of the vehicle on the display and navigation module display.

  2. This system consists of the following components:

    1. Front passenger side television camera assembly

    2. Driver side television camera assembly*

    3. Parking assist ECU

    4. Display and navigation module display

    5. Spiral with sensor cable sub-assembly

    6. Steering pad switch assembly (wide view front and side monitor switch, multi-function switch*)

    7. Skid control ECU

    8. Park/neutral position switch assembly

  3. This system is equipped with a self-diagnosis system, which is operated on a designated window that appears on the display panel, just as in the navigation system.


FUNCTION OF COMPONENTS

  1. The parking assist ECU controls the system by using information from the following components.

    Item
    		Function
    1. Front Passenger Side Television Camera Assembly
    2. Driver Side Television Camera Assembly*
    1. Built into the outer rear view mirror assembly to the transmit the side view of the vehicle to the parking assist ECU.
    2. Has a color video camera that uses a Charge Coupled Device (CCD) and a wide-angle lens.
    Parking Assist ECU
    1. Transmits video signals, which contain a composite consisting of the passenger side view of the vehicle taken with the television camera, to the display and navigation module display.
    2. Performs overall control of the system by receiving signals from the sensors and display and navigation module display.
    Display and Navigation Module Display
    		Receives the video signals containing a composite of the passenger side view of the vehicle from the parking assist ECU, and displays them on the display panel.
    Park/Neutral Position Switch Assembly
    		Sends the shift position signal to the parking assist ECU through CAN communication.
    Spiral with Sensor Cable Sub-assembly
    		Transmits a steering angle sensor signal to the parking assist ECU.
    Steering Pad Switch Assembly
    1. Transmits the wide view front and side monitor switch signal to the parking assist ECU.
    2. Transmits the multi-function switch signal to the parking assist ECU through the driving support switch control ECU*.
    Driving Support Switch Control ECU*
    		Receives a steering pad switch signal and transmits it to the parking assist ECU through CAN communication
    Skid Control ECU
    		Transmits a vehicle speed signal to the parking assist ECU.


OPERATION EXPLANATION

  1. The wide view front and side monitor switch on signal is sent from the steering pad switch to the parking assist ECU.
    After receiving the wide view front and side monitor switch on signal, the parking assist ECU switches the display signal for the display and navigation module display from the navigation system to the side monitor system.

COMMUNICATION SYSTEM OUTLINE

  1. The components of the side monitor system communicate with each other through the AVC-LAN. If a short circuit or open circuit occurs in the AVC-LAN, communication is interrupted and the side monitor system will stop functioning.
