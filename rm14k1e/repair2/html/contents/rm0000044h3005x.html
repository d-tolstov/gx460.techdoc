  1. If the conditions listed under the Auto Cancel Condition column in the following table are detected while the dynamic radar cruise control system is in operation, the system clears the stored vehicle speed in the ECM and cancels the dynamic radar cruise control operation.

    Vehicle Condition
    		Auto Cancel Condition
    		Fail-safe Deactivation Condition
    1. Cruise control indicator light blinks
    2. Master warning light turns on
    3. "CHECK CRUISE CONTROL SYSTEM" is displayed
    4. "Pong" warning sound is heard
    1. Open or short in stop light switch circuit
    2. Problem with vehicle speed signal
    3. Driving support ECU and millimeter wave radar sensor assembly country specification information malfunction
    		Cruise control switch turned on again
