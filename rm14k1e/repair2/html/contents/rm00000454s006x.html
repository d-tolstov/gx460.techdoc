1. INSTALL TRANSMISSION CONTROL CABLE ASSEMBLY

  1. Insert the transmission control cable from the outside of the vehicle and attach the 3 claws of the cable retainer.

  1. Install the 2 nuts.

    Torque:
    5.5 N*m{ 56 kgf*cm , 49 in.*lbf }

  1. Connect the transmission control cable to the transmission control cable bracket with a new clip and connect the cable end to the control shaft lever with the nut.

    Torque:
    14 N*m{ 143 kgf*cm , 10 ft.*lbf }




  1. Connect the transmission control cable support and ground cable with the 2 nuts.

    Torque:
    5.5 N*m{ 56 kgf*cm , 49 in.*lbf }

    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Ground Cable



  1. Turn the socket of the transmission control cable 180° counterclockwise. While holding the socket in place, push in the lock piece until the lock piece clicks twice.

    NOTICE:
    Do not over-rotate the socket as it will come off the internal spring and the transmission control cable will not be reusable.

    		-

    HINT:
    If the lock piece cannot be pushed in, slightly turn the socket clockwise and then push in the lock piece again.



  1. Install the outer part of the transmission control cable to the shift lever retainer. Check that the socket and lock piece are in their original positions.




    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Shift Lever Retainer
    		-
    		-




  1. Slide the slider in the direction shown in the illustration and pull out the lock piece.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Slider
    *2
    		Lock Piece




  1. Connect the end of the cable to the shift lever.

    NOTICE:
    1. Make sure that the lock piece is pulled out.
    2. Push the end of the cable all the way to the base of the pin.




  1. Push the lock piece into the adjuster case and lock it.

    NOTICE:
    Make sure that the lock piece is completely locked by the slider.
