1. INSPECT SEAT HEATER CONTROL SUB-ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Disconnect the u1 heater control connector.

  1. Measure the resistance according to the value(s) in the table below.

    Standard Resistance:
    Tester Connection
    		Condition
    		Specified Condition
    u1-6 (E) - Body ground
    		Always
    		Below 1 Ω

  1. Measure the voltage according to the value(s) in the table below.

    Standard Voltage:
    Tester Connection
    		Switch Condition
    		Specified Condition
    u1-1 (SWL) - u1-6 (E)
    		Engine switch on (IG)
    Seat heater switch LH off
    		Below 1 V
    Engine switch on (IG)
    Seat heater switch LH on
    		11 to 14 V
    u1-4 (B) - u1-6 (E)
    		Engine switch off
    		Below 1 V
    Engine switch on (IG)
    		11 to 14 V

    Text in Illustration 
    *a
    		Front view of wire harness connector
    (to Seat Heater Control Sub-assembly LH)
    If the result is not as specified, there may be a malfunction on the wire harness side.

  1. Connect the u1 heater control connector.




  1. Measure the voltage according to the value(s) in the table below.

    Standard Voltage:
    Tester Connection
    		Switch Condition
    		Specified Condition
    u1-5 (THL) - Body ground
    		Engine switch on (IG)
    Seat heater switch LH on, LO → HI
    		Gradual increase between value below 1 V and value from 11 to 14 V

    Text in Illustration 
    *a
    		Component with harness connected
    (Seat Heater Control Sub-assembly LH)
    If the result is not as specified, the seat heater control may have a malfunction.
