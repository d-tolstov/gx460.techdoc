QUARTER PANEL WHEEL OPENING MOULDING > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here


HINT:
  1. Use the same procedure for the RH and LH sides.
  2. The procedure listed below is for the LH side.



1. REMOVE SIDE STEP ASSEMBLY LH

  1. Remove the side step assembly (Click here).



2. REMOVE QUARTER PANEL WHEEL OPENING MOULDING LH



HINT:
When removing the quarter panel wheel opening moulding, heat the vehicle body and quarter panel wheel opening moulding using a heat light.

Standard:
Item
Temperature
Vehicle Body
40 to 60°C (104 to 140°F)
Quarter Panel Wheel Opening Moulding
20 to 30°C (68 to 86°F)

NOTICE:
Do not heat the vehicle body or quarter panel wheel opening moulding excessively.

  1. Put protective tape around the quarter panel wheel opening moulding.

  1. Remove the screw and grommet.

  1. Remove the double-sided tape and quarter panel wheel opening moulding.
