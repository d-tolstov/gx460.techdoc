DTC    U0208    Lost Communication with "Seat Control Module A"


DESCRIPTION

WIRING DIAGRAM

INSPECTION PROCEDURE

DISCONNECT CABLE FROM NEGATIVE BATTERY TERMINAL

CHECK FOR OPEN IN CAN BUS WIRE (FRONT POWER SEAT SWITCH LH BRANCH WIRE)

CHECK HARNESS AND CONNECTOR (FRONT POWER SEAT SWITCH LH - BATTERY AND BODY GROUND)
