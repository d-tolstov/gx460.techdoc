1. REMOVE MAP LIGHT ASSEMBLY



  1. Detach the 4 clips.

  1. Disconnect the connector and remove the map light.



2. REMOVE TELEPHONE MICROPHONE ASSEMBLY

  1. Remove the tape.




  1. Using a thin-bladed screwdriver, disconnect the connector.

    HINT:
    Tape the screwdriver tip before use.

  1. Remove the 7 screws and cover.




  1. Remove the circuit board.




  1. Remove the bezel.




  1. Using a thin-bladed screwdriver, detach the 2 claws and remove the microphone.

    HINT:
    Tape the screwdriver tip before use.
