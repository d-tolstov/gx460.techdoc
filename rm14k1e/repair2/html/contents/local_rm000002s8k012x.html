DTC    B2322    Front Passenger Side Door ECU Communication Stop


DESCRIPTION

WIRING DIAGRAM

INSPECTION PROCEDURE

CLEAR DTC

CHECK FOR DTC

CHECK HARNESS AND CONNECTOR (MAIN BODY ECU - FRONT POWER WINDOW REGULATOR MOTOR RH)

CHECK HARNESS AND CONNECTOR (FRONT POWER WINDOW REGULATOR MOTOR RH - BATTERY AND BODY GROUND)

REPLACE FRONT POWER WINDOW REGULATOR MOTOR ASSEMBLY RH

CLEAR DTC

CHECK FOR DTC
