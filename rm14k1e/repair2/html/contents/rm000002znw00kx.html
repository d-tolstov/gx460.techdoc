BACK WINDOW STAY > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here


HINT:
  1. Use the same procedure for the RH and LH sides.
  2. The procedure listed below is for the LH side.



1. REMOVE BACK WINDOW STAY ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Using a screwdriver, remove the 2 stop rings as shown in the illustration, and then remove the back window stay.

    CAUTION:
    Remove the back window stay while supporting the back door with one hand.

    HINT:
    Tape the screwdriver tip before use.

    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape



2. REMOVE BACK DOOR STAY BOLT



  1. Remove the back door stay bolt.
