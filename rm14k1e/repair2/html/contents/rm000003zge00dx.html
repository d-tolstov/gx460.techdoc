5.CONTACT G-BOOK SUPPORT CENTER



  1. Check the contract flag.

    1. Enter diagnostic mode (Click here).

    2. Select "Service Information" on the Service Menu screen.




    3. Select "G-BOOK Information for Contract" on the Service Information screen.




    4. Check the flag information item.



  1. Check the device information.




    1. Select "DCM Information" on the Service Information screen.




    2. Check the following items on the DCM Information screen.
      Item
      		Content
      Tel No
      		Check if the DCM phone number is displayed. If the number is displayed, record it.
      DCM Serial
      		Record the DCM serial number.

  1. Contact the G-BOOK support center.

    1. Contact the G-BOOK support center and inform them about the following:

      1. Phenomenon: Communication has been initialized but communication is not possible.
      2. Flag information
      3. Telephone number (Tel No): Not displayed / Displayed number
      4. Serial number (DCM Serial): Displayed number
      5. VIN

      HINT:
      The G-BOOK support center will perform an investigation and provide the results.

    2. Check for problem symptoms after receiving a call from the G-BOOK support center.

8.CHECK RECEPTION LEVEL



  1. Check the reception level.

    1. Check the reception level of the DCM.

      HINT:
      1. Check if the reception level is significantly lower by comparing it to a China Telecom cellular phone.
      2. If there is no China Telecom cellular phone available, move the vehicle to a location where the reception level indicator shows 3 bars or more.
      3. If a problem occurred because the G-BOOK contract has not been completed, proceed to the contract procedure and check the DCM reception level on the screen during the contract procedure.

      Result 
      Result
      		Proceed to
      1. Reception level not lower than China Telecom cellular phone
      2. 3 or more bars displayed for reception level
      		A
      1. Reception level lower than China Telecom cellular phone
      2. Cannot reach 3 or more bars for reception level even after moving vehicle
      		B



B
Go to step 10

15.CHECK CONTRACT FLAG



  1. Check the contract flag.

    1. Enter diagnostic mode (Click here).

    2. Select "Service Information" on the Service Menu screen.




    3. Select "G-BOOK Information for Contract" on the Service Information screen.




    4. Check if the flag information shown in the illustration indicates "1".

    5. Turn the engine switch off and then on (ACC).

    6. Check again if the flag information shown in the illustration indicates "1".

  1. Recheck the problem symptom.

    1. Select G-BOOK.com and perform the contract procedure again.

    2. Check for the problem symptom.
      Result
