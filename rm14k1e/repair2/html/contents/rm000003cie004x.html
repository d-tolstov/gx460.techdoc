HINT:

  2. The procedure listed below is for the LH side.



1. REMOVE NO. 1 ROOF SIDE GARNISH LID LH



HINT:
Use the same procedure for all No. 1 roof side garnish lids.

  1. Put protective tape around the No. 1 roof side garnish lid.

  1. Using a screwdriver, detach the 2 claws and 2 guides and remove the No. 1 roof side garnish lid.

    HINT:
    Tape the screwdriver tip before use.
