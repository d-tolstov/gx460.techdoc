MANIFOLD ABSOLUTE PRESSURE SENSOR > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here



1. REMOVE V-BANK COVER SUB-ASSEMBLY



  1. Raise the front of the V-bank cover to detach the 2 pins. Then detach the 2 V-bank cover hooks from the No. 1 V-bank cover bracket and remove the V-bank cover.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Pin
    *2
    		Hook
    *3
    		No. 1 V-bank Cover Bracket



2. REMOVE MANIFOLD ABSOLUTE PRESSURE SENSOR



  1. Disconnect the manifold absolute pressure sensor connector.

  1. Remove the bolt and manifold absolute pressure sensor.

  1. Remove the O-ring from the manifold absolute pressure sensor.
