VEHICLE BATTERY SAVING FUNCTION

  1. When the entry and start system (for Entry Function) is operating, the vehicle performs key detection operations every 0.25 seconds. Therefore, if the vehicle left as is for a long period of time, the battery may become fully depleted. The following controls help reduce the rate at which the battery is depleted.

    1. If the engine is not started for 6 days or more, periodic signal transmission from the electrical key antennas stops. However, the doors can still be unlocked by touching the unlock sensor of a front door outside handle assembly.

    2. If the engine is not started for 15 days or more, the lock and unlock sensors of the front passenger side door outside handle assembly are disabled. Therefore, only the unlock sensor of the front door outside handle assembly of the driver side door can be used to unlock the doors.

    3. If 10 minutes or more elapse while the key is in the detection area (including the vehicle interior), entry and start system control stops operating.

  2. To restore operation of the entry and start system, perform any of the following operations.

    1. While carrying the key, touch the lock and unlock sensor of the front door outside handle assembly of the driver side door and perform a door lock or unlock operation.

    2. Lock or unlock the doors with the wireless operation.

    3. Insert the mechanical key into the door key cylinder of the driver side door and lock or unlock the doors.


WIRELESS BUZZER VOLUME ADJUSTMENT FUNCTION (w/ Wireless Buzzer Answer-back)

  1. The volume of the wireless buzzer can be changed through the customize function.

    HINT:
    The volume of the following wireless buzzer operations cannot be adjusted.
    1. Warning function buzzer operations (w/ Warning Function Buzzer Operations)
    2. Customize function buzzer operations
    3. Buzzer operations for key diagnostic mode using the intelligent tester
    4. Key registration buzzer operations


WARNING FUNCTION

  1. Shift lever not in P (power source cut reminder) vehicle exit warning

    1. Situation A: The driver side door is opened and vehicle exit is attempted while the shift lever is not in P.

      Item
      		Content
      Reason for warning
      1. Prevention of sudden forward movement of vehicle
      2. Vehicle theft prevention
      3. Vehicle roll-away (vehicle starts moving even when nobody is aboard due to slope, etc.) prevention
      Warning start condition
      (All conditions are met)
      1. Shift lever is not in P
      2. Engine switch is on (ACC) or on (IG)
      3. Driver side door is opened
      4. Vehicle speed is 0 km/h (0 mph)
      Warning description
      1. Buzzer in combination meter assembly sounds continuously (beep)
      2. "Shift to P position" is displayed on multi-information display in combination meter assembly
      Warning stop condition
      (Any condition is met)
      1. Shift lever moved to P
      2. Engine switch is turned off
      3. Driver side door is closed
      4. Vehicle is driven

    2. Situation B: Under the conditions specified in situation A, the key is brought out of the vehicle, the driver side door is closed and movement away from the vehicle is attempted.

      Item
      		Content
      Reason for warning
      1. Prevention of sudden forward movement of vehicle
      2. Vehicle theft prevention
      3. Vehicle roll-away prevention
      Warning start condition
      (All conditions are met)
      1. Shift lever is not in P
      2. Engine switch is on (ACC) or on (IG)
      3. Driver side door is opened and then closed
      4. Vehicle speed is 0 km/h (0 mph)
      5. Key is not inside vehicle (matching code is not detected in vehicle interior)
      Warning description
      1. Buzzer in combination meter assembly sounds continuously (beep)
      2. "Shift to P position" is displayed on multi-information display in combination meter assembly
      3. Wireless buzzer sounds continuously (beep)*
      Warning stop condition
      (Any condition is met)
      1. Shift lever is moved to P or vehicle is driven: Buzzer stops and only "Key not detected" is displayed on multi-information display
      2. Engine switch is turned off: All warnings stop
      3. Key is detected inside vehicle (matching code is detected in vehicle interior): Buzzer stops and only "Shift to P position" is displayed on multi-information display

      1. *: w/ Warning Function Buzzer Operations
