1. REMOVE FUEL SUCTION WITH PUMP AND GAUGE TUBE ASSEMBLY

  1. Remove the fuel suction with pump and gauge tube assembly (Click here).



2. REMOVE FUEL SENDER GAUGE ASSEMBLY



  1. Disconnect the fuel sender gauge connector.




  1. Press down on the fuel sender gauge claw labeled A. Then slide the fuel sender gauge upward to remove it.
