DTC    C1A02    Vehicle Information Not Obtained

for Preparation Click here


DESCRIPTION

When the driving support ECU is replaced with a new one, it acquires the country specification information from the main body ECU (multiplex network body ECU). If the driving support ECU cannot store the country specification information, DTC C1A02 is stored.

DTC Code
DTC Detection Condition
Trouble Area
C1A02
While the engine switch on (IG) and the pre-crash safety system is operating, the driving support ECU cannot store the country specification information.
  1. Main body ECU (Multiplex network body ECU)
  2. Driving support ECU
