DTC    C1731/31    FR Damping Force Control Actuator Circuit

DTC    C1732/32    FL Damping Force Control Actuator Circuit

DTC    C1733/33    RR Damping Force Control Actuator Circuit

DTC    C1734/34    RL Damping Force Control Actuator Circuit

for Preparation Click here


DESCRIPTION

The absorber control actuator switches the damping force depending on the suspension control ECU signals.

DTC Code
DTC Detection Condition
Trouble Area
C1731/31
Either condition is met:
  1. An actuator open-circuit signal is detected for at least 1 second when the actuator operates.
  2. An actuator short-circuit signal is consecutively detected 8 times when the actuator operates.
  1. Harness or connector
  2. Front absorber control actuator RH
  3. Suspension control ECU
C1732/32
Either condition is met:
  1. An actuator open-circuit signal is detected for at least 1 second when the actuator operates.
  2. An actuator short-circuit signal is consecutively detected 8 times when the actuator operates.
  1. Harness or connector
  2. Front absorber control actuator LH
  3. Suspension control ECU
C1733/33
Either condition is met:
  1. An actuator open-circuit signal is detected for at least 1 second when the actuator operates.
  2. An actuator short-circuit signal is consecutively detected 8 times when the actuator operates.
  1. Harness or connector
  2. Rear absorber control actuator RH (Rear shock absorber assembly)
  3. Suspension control ECU
C1734/34
Either condition is met:
  1. An actuator open-circuit signal is detected for at least 1 second when the actuator operates.
  2. An actuator short-circuit signal is consecutively detected 8 times when the actuator operates.
  1. Harness or connector
  2. Rear absorber control actuator LH (Rear shock absorber assembly)
  3. Suspension control ECU
