KNEE AIRBAG ASSEMBLY (for Driver Side) > ON-VEHICLE INSPECTION

for Preparation Click here



1. CHECK LOWER NO. 1 INSTRUMENT PANEL AIRBAG ASSEMBLY (VEHICLE NOT INVOLVED IN COLLISION)

  1. Perform a diagnostic system check (Click here).

  1. With the lower No. 1 instrument panel airbag installed on the vehicle, perform a visual check. If there are any defects as mentioned below, replace the lower No. 1 instrument panel airbag with a new one:
    Cuts, minute cracks or marked discoloration on the lower No. 1 instrument panel airbag.



2. CHECK LOWER NO. 1 INSTRUMENT PANEL AIRBAG ASSEMBLY (VEHICLE INVOLVED IN COLLISION AND AIRBAG HAS NOT DEPLOYED)

  1. Perform a diagnostic system check (Click here).

  1. With the lower No. 1 instrument panel airbag removed from the vehicle, perform a visual check. If there are any defects as mentioned below, replace the lower No. 1 instrument panel airbag with a new one:

    1. Cuts, minute cracks or marked discoloration on the lower No. 1 instrument panel airbag.
    2. Cracks or other damage to the connector.
    3. Deformation or cracks in the instrument panel reinforcement.

    CAUTION:
    For removal and installation of the lower No. 1 instrument panel airbag, be sure to follow the correct procedure.
