ULTRASONIC SENSOR (for Rear) > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here



1. REMOVE REAR QUARTER PANEL MUDGUARD LH



  1. Remove the 4 screws and rear quarter panel mudguard.



2. REMOVE REAR QUARTER PANEL MUDGUARD RH

HINT:
Use the same procedure described for the LH side.



3. REMOVE REAR BUMPER PROTECTOR LH



  1. Remove the clip, 2 screws and rear bumper protector.



4. REMOVE REAR BUMPER PROTECTOR RH

HINT:
Use the same procedure described for the LH side.



5. REMOVE JACK BOX HOLE COVER



  1. Using a screwdriver, detach the 2 claws and remove the jack box hole cover.

    HINT:
    Tape the screwdriver tip before use.

    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape



6. REMOVE REAR BUMPER BAR PLATE

  1. Put protective tape around the rear bumper bar plate.




  1. Detach the 14 claws and remove the rear bumper bar plate.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape



7. REMOVE REAR BUMPER COVER



  1. Put protective tape around the rear bumper cover.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape

  1. Remove the 2 clips, 2 screws and 7 bolts.




  1. Detach the 6 claws.

    HINT:
    Use the same procedure for the RH side and LH side.

  1. Disconnect the 3 connectors.

  1. Remove the rear bumper cover.



8. REMOVE ULTRASONIC SENSOR CLIP



HINT:
Use the same procedure to remove the ultrasonic sensor clip on the other side.

  1. Detach the 4 claws and remove the ultrasonic sensor clip.



9. REMOVE NO. 1 ULTRASONIC SENSOR



HINT:
Use the same procedure to remove the No. 1 ultrasonic sensor on the other side.

  1. Disconnect the connector.

  1. Detach the 2 claws and remove the No. 1 ultrasonic sensor.



10. REMOVE NO. 1 ULTRASONIC SENSOR RETAINER



HINT:
Use the same procedure to remove the No. 1 ultrasonic sensor retainer on the other side.

  1. Detach the 2 claws and remove the No. 1 ultrasonic sensor retainer.



11. REMOVE NO. 2 ULTRASONIC SENSOR RETAINER



HINT:
Use the same procedure to remove the No. 2 ultrasonic sensor retainer on the other side.

  1. Detach the 2 claws and remove the No. 2 ultrasonic sensor retainer.
