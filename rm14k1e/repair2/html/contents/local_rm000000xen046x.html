DTC    B2274    ACC Monitor Malfunction


DESCRIPTION

WIRING DIAGRAM

INSPECTION PROCEDURE

CHECK HARNESS AND CONNECTOR (BATTERY - POWER MANAGEMENT CONTROL ECU)

CHECK HARNESS AND CONNECTOR (POWER MANAGEMENT CONTROL ECU - BODY GROUND)

INSPECT DRIVER SIDE JUNCTION BLOCK ASSEMBLY (ACC RELAY)

CHECK HARNESS AND CONNECTOR (POWER MANAGEMENT CONTROL ECU - DRIVER SIDE JUNCTION BLOCK ASSEMBLY)

CHECK POWER MANAGEMENT CONTROL ECU
