REAR AXLE SHAFT > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here


HINT:
  1. Use the same procedure for the RH and LH sides.
  2. The procedure listed below is for the LH side.



1. REMOVE REAR WHEEL


2. DRAIN BRAKE FLUID


3. REMOVE REAR FLEXIBLE HOSE LH



  1. Remove the union bolt, gasket and rear flexible hose from the rear disc brake cylinder.




  1. Disconnect the brake tube from the flexible hose with a union nut wrench while holding the flexible hose with a wrench.

    NOTICE:
    1. Do not bend or damage the brake tube.
    2. Do not allow any foreign matter such as dirt and dust to enter the brake tube from the connecting point.

  1. Remove the clip.



4. REMOVE REAR SPEED SENSOR LH



  1. Disconnect the speed sensor connector.

  1. Remove the nut and speed sensor.

    NOTICE:
    Pull out the sensor while trying as much as possible not to rotate it.



5. REMOVE PARKING BRAKE ASSEMBLY

  1. Remove the parking brake assembly (Click here).



6. REMOVE REAR AXLE SHAFT WITH PARKING BRAKE PLATE LH



  1. Remove the 4 nuts and rear axle shaft with parking brake plate.

  1. Remove the O-ring.



7. REMOVE REAR AXLE SHAFT OIL SEAL LH



  1. Using SST, remove the rear axle shaft oil seal.
