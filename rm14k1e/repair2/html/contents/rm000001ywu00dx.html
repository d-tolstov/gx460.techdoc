REAR POWER SEAT CONTROL SYSTEM > Fold Seat Switch Circuit

for Preparation Click here


DESCRIPTION

When the fold seat switch is operated, a switch operation signal is sent to the fold seat control ECU. The ECU receives switch operation signals from each switch and activates the reclining motor and cushion motor.
