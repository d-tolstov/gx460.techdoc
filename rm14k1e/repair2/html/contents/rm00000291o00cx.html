1. INSTALL FRONT PROPELLER SHAFT ASSEMBLY

  1. Align the matchmarks on the yoke and differential flange.

  1. Install the propeller shaft assembly with the 4 bolts, 4 washers and 4 nuts.

    Torque:
    88 N*m{ 899 kgf*cm , 65 ft.*lbf }

  1. Align the matchmarks on the yoke and transfer flange.

  1. Install the propeller shaft assembly with the 4 washers and 4 nuts.

    Torque:
    88 N*m{ 899 kgf*cm , 65 ft.*lbf }



2. INSTALL PROPELLER SHAFT HEAT INSULATOR WITH BRACKET

  1. Install the propeller shaft heat insulator with bracket with the 2 bolts.

    Torque:
    16 N*m{ 160 kgf*cm , 12 ft.*lbf }
