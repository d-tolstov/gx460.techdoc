FRONT DOOR > DISASSEMBLY

for Preparation Click here


HINT:
  1. Use the same procedure for the RH and LH sides.
  2. The procedure listed below is for the LH side.



1. DISCONNECT CABLE FROM NEGATIVE BATTERY TERMINAL

CAUTION:
Wait at least 90 seconds after disconnecting the cable from the negative (-) battery terminal to disable the SRS system.

NOTICE:
  1. w/ Navigation System (for HDD):
    After the engine switch is turned off, the display and navigation module display (HDD navigation system) records various types of memory and settings. As a result, after turning the engine switch off, be sure to wait for the time specified in the following table before disconnecting the cable from the negative (-) battery terminal.

    Waiting Time before Disconnecting Cable from Negative (-) Battery Terminal :

    Condition
    		Waiting Time
    Vehicle enrolled in G-BOOK system
    		6 minutes
    Vehicle not enrolled in G-BOOK system
    		1 minute

  2. When disconnecting the cable, some systems need to be initialized after the cable is reconnected (Click here).



2. REMOVE FRONT DOOR LOWER FRAME BRACKET GARNISH LH



  1. Detach the 2 clips and remove the front door lower frame bracket garnish LH.



3. REMOVE FRONT DOOR INSIDE HANDLE BEZEL PLUG LH



  1. Using a moulding remover, detach the 3 claws and remove the inside handle bezel as shown in the illustration.



4. REMOVE FRONT ARMREST ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Using moulding remover B, detach the 7 claws and 7 guides, and remove the front armrest assembly as shown in the illustration.



5. REMOVE POWER WINDOW REGULATOR MASTER SWITCH ASSEMBLY WITH FRONT DOOR ARMREST BASE PANEL



  1. Using a moulding remover B, detach the 2 clips, 4 claws and guide as shown in the illustration.

  1. Disconnect the connector and remove the power window regulator master switch assembly with front door armrest base panel.



6. REMOVE MULTIPLEX NETWORK MASTER SWITCH ASSEMBLY (for Driver Side)



  1. Remove the 3 screws.

  1. Detach the 4 claws and remove the multiplex network master switch assembly.



7. REMOVE POWER WINDOW REGULATOR SWITCH ASSEMBLY (for Front Passenger Side)



  1. Detach the 2 claws and remove the power window regulator switch assembly.



8. REMOVE FRONT DOOR TRIM BOARD SUB-ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Remove the 2 screws.

  1. Remove the 12 clips.




  1. Pull out the front door trim board sub-assembly in the direction indicated by the arrow in the illustration.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Reference Boss

  1. Raise the front door trim board sub-assembly to detach the 4 claws and remove the front door trim board sub-assembly together with the front door inner glass weatherstrip LH.

  1. Disconnect the 2 connectors.




  1. Disconnect the front door lock remote control cable assembly and front door inside locking cable assembly.



9. REMOVE SEAT MEMORY SWITCH



  1. Disconnect the connector and remove the 2 screws.




  1. Detach the 2 claws and remove the switch.



10. REMOVE FRONT DOOR INNER GLASS WEATHERSTRIP LH



  1. Using a screwdriver, detach the 4 claws and remove the front door inner glass weatherstrip from the front door trim board sub-assembly as shown in the illustration.



11. REMOVE FRONT DOOR INSIDE HANDLE SUB-ASSEMBLY LH

NOTICE:
When removing the inside handle sub-assembly, do not damage the door trim.




  1. Remove the 14 screws.




  1. Move the assist grip together with the inside handle sub-assembly to detach the 9 guides.




  1. Slide the assist grip together with the inside handle sub-assembly to make a space between the ornament panel and assist grip.




  1. Rotate the assist grip together with the inside handle sub-assembly to detach the 2 guides on the rear of the trim, and then pull out the assist grip together with the inside handle sub-assembly.




  1. Detach the 5 claws and remove the inside handle sub-assembly.



12. REMOVE COURTESY LIGHT ASSEMBLY



  1. Detach the 2 claws and remove the light.

  1. Disconnect the connector.



13. REMOVE FRONT DOOR INSIDE HANDLE ILLUMINATION LIGHT ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Disconnect the connector.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape

  1. Using a screwdriver, detach the claw and remove the light.

    HINT:
    Tape the screwdriver tip before use.



14. REMOVE OUTER MIRROR CONTROL ECU ASSEMBLY



  1. Using a clip remover, detach the clamp.

  1. Disconnect the 2 connectors.

  1. Remove the 2 screws and outer mirror control ECU assembly.



15. REMOVE FRONT NO. 1 SPEAKER ASSEMBLY



  1. Disconnect the speaker connector.

  1. Remove the 4 screws.

  1. Detach the 2 claws and remove the speaker.

    NOTICE:
    Do not touch the cone of the speaker.



16. REMOVE SIDE AIRBAG SENSOR ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Disconnect the connector.

  1. Remove the bolt and side airbag sensor.



17. REMOVE FRONT DOOR SERVICE HOLE COVER LH



  1. Remove the bolt.

  1. Detach the 2 clamps, move the wire harness out of the way and remove the service hole cover.

    HINT:
    Remove any remaining butyl tape from the door.



18. REMOVE OUTER REAR VIEW MIRROR ASSEMBLY LH




  1. w/ Side Monitor System:
    Disconnect the 2 connectors and detach the clamp.

  1. w/o Side Monitor System:
    Disconnect the connector and detach the clamp.

  1. Remove the 3 nuts.

  1. Detach the claw and remove the outer rear view mirror.



19. REMOVE FRONT DOOR GLASS SUB-ASSEMBLY LH

  1. Connect the cable to the negative (-) battery terminal.

  1. Connect the power window regulator master switch assembly and move the front door glass sub-assembly so that the door glass bolts can be seen.

  1. Disconnect the cable from the negative (-) battery terminal and power window regulator master switch assembly.

    NOTICE:
    1. w/ Navigation System (for HDD):
      After the engine switch is turned off, the display and navigation module display (HDD navigation system) records various types of memory and settings. As a result, after turning the engine switch off, be sure to wait for the time specified in the following table before disconnecting the cable from the negative (-) battery terminal.

      Waiting Time before Disconnecting Cable from Negative (-) Battery Terminal :

      Condition
      		Waiting Time
      Vehicle enrolled in G-BOOK system
      		6 minutes
      Vehicle not enrolled in G-BOOK system
      		1 minute

    2. When disconnecting the cable, some systems need to be initialized after the cable is reconnected (Click here).




  1. Remove the 2 bolts.

    NOTICE:
    After the bolts are removed, do not allow the door glass to fall.




  1. Remove the front door glass sub-assembly as indicated by the arrows in the order shown in the illustration.

    NOTICE:
    Do not damage the door glass.



20. REMOVE FRONT DOOR WINDOW REGULATOR SUB-ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Disconnect the connector.

  1. Loosen the temporary bolt.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Temporary Bolt

    NOTICE:
    Do not remove the temporary bolt. If the temporary bolt is removed, the front door window regulator may fall and cause damage.

  1. Remove the 5 bolts.

  1. Remove the front door window regulator sub-assembly.

  1. Remove the temporary bolt from the front door window regulator sub-assembly.



21. REMOVE POWER WINDOW REGULATOR MOTOR ASSEMBLY LH


  1. Using a T25 "TORX" socket wrench, remove the 3 screws and power window regulator motor.




    Text in Illustration

    CAUTION:
    Do not place your finger, etc. within the range of movement of the window regulator. When removing the power window regulator motor, the force of the spring causes the window regulator to move in the direction of the arrow shown in the illustration (the direction in which the window rises), which may cause injury.

    NOTICE:
    Be careful when removing the screws as the motor may fall and become damaged.



22. REMOVE FRONT DOOR GLASS RUN LH



  1. Remove the front door glass run.

  1. Detach the clip and remove the door frame garnish.



24. REMOVE FRONT DOOR REAR LOWER FRAME SUB-ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Remove the bolt and front door rear lower frame sub-assembly as shown in the illustration.



25. REMOVE FRONT DOOR OUTSIDE HANDLE COVER WITH LOCK CYLINDER ASSEMBLY (for Driver Side)



  1. Remove the hole plug.




  1. Using a T30 "TORX" socket wrench, loosen the screw and remove the front door outside handle cover with lock cylinder assembly.

    HINT:
    The screw cannot be removed because it is integrated into the front door outside handle frame sub-assembly.



26. REMOVE FRONT DOOR OUTSIDE HANDLE COVER LH (for Driver Side)



  1. Using a screwdriver, detach the claw and remove the front door outside handle cover.



27. REMOVE FRONT DOOR OUTSIDE HANDLE COVER RH (for Front Passenger Side)



  1. Remove the hole plug.




  1. Using a T30 "TORX" socket wrench, loosen the screw and remove the front door outside handle cover.

    HINT:
    The screw cannot be removed because it is integrated into the front door outside handle frame sub-assembly.

31. REMOVE FRONT DOOR LOCK ASSEMBLY LH

  1. Disconnect the connector.




  1. Using a T30 "TORX" wrench, remove the 3 screws.
    Text in Illustration

  1. Remove the front door lock open rod from the front door lock assembly.

  1. Remove the door lock wiring harness seal from the front door lock assembly.



32. REMOVE FRONT DOOR LOCK REMOTE CONTROL CABLE ASSEMBLY LH

33. REMOVE FRONT DOOR INSIDE LOCKING CABLE ASSEMBLY LH

    HINT:

  1. Remove the front door inside locking cable assembly.

  1. Slide the front door outside handle frame sub-assembly to detach the door handle nut and claw of the front door outside handle frame sub-assembly, and then remove the front door outside handle frame sub-assembly.

41. REMOVE FRONT DOOR REAR WINDOW FRAME MOULDING LH

HINT:
When removing the window frame moulding, heat the vehicle body and window frame moulding using a heat light.

Standard:
Item
Temperature
Vehicle Body
40 to 60°C (104 to 140°F)
Window Frame Moulding
20 to 30°C (68 to 86°F)

NOTICE:
Do not heat the vehicle body or window frame moulding excessively.




  1. Remove the door window frame moulding clip.




  1. Put protective tape around the front door rear window frame moulding.

  1. Using a moulding remover, detach the clip and remove the double-sided tape to remove the front door rear window frame moulding.
