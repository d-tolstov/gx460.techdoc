5. INSTALL BACK DOOR LOWER DAMPER STAY BRACKET LH

  1. Bumper side:

    1. Install a new back door check to the back door lower damper stay bracket.

    1. Install the back door check to the back door lower damper stay bracket.

  1. Check that the back door check is securely attached to the ball joint and cannot be pulled off.



6. INSTALL BACK DOOR CHECK


  1. Bumper Side:




    1. Install the back door check with back door lower damper stay bracket with the 2 bolts labeled A.

      Torque:
      30 N*m{ 306 kgf*cm , 22 ft.*lbf }

    2. Install the bolt labeled B.

      Torque:
      8.0 N*m{ 82 kgf*cm , 71 in.*lbf }

    3. Attach the wire harness.



  1. Back Door Side:




    1. Attach the clip to install the back door check with back door lower damper stay bracket.

    2. Install the 2 bolts.

      Torque:
      30 N*m{ 306 kgf*cm , 22 ft.*lbf }

10. INSTALL BACK DOOR STAY BOLT


  1. When replacing the back door stay bolt with a new one:

    1. Clean the threaded portion on the vehicle body with a non-residue solvent.




    2. Install the 2 back door stay bolts.

      Torque:
      22 N*m{ 224 kgf*cm , 16 ft.*lbf }

      Text in Illustration 
      *A
      		for RH Side
      *B
      		for LH Side

  1. When reusing the back door stay bolt:

    1. Clean the threaded portion on the vehicle body with a non-residue solvent.

    2. Apply adhesive to the threads of the back door stay bolt.

      Adhesive:
      Toyota Genuine Adhesive 1324, Three Bond 1324 or equivalent

    3. Install the 2 back door stay bolts.

      Torque:
      22 N*m{ 224 kgf*cm , 16 ft.*lbf }

26. INSTALL LOWER BACK DOOR GARNISH SUB-ASSEMBLY OUTSIDE LH

  1. Clean the vehicle body surface.

    1. Using a heat light, heat the vehicle body surface.

    2. Wipe off any tape with cleaner.

  1. Install a new outside garnish.

    1. Using a heat light, heat the vehicle body and garnish.

    2. Remove the peeling paper from the face of the outside garnish.

  1. Attach the 5 claws to install the garnish.

  1. Install the screw.

  1. Attach the clamp and connect the connector.



27. INSTALL LOWER BACK DOOR GARNISH SUB-ASSEMBLY OUTSIDE RH

  1. Clean the vehicle body surface.

    1. Using a heat light, heat the vehicle body surface.

      Standard:
      Item
      		Temperature
      Vehicle body
      		40 to 60°C (104 to 140°F)

      NOTICE:
      Do not heat the vehicle body excessively.

    2. Wipe off any tape adhesive residue with cleaner.

  1. Install a new rear lower back door garnish sub-assembly outside RH.

    1. Using a heat light, heat the vehicle body and a new rear lower back door garnish sub-assembly outside RH.

    2. Remove the release paper from the face of the rear lower back door garnish sub-assembly outside RH.

      HINT:
      After removing the release paper, keep the exposed adhesive free from foreign matter.

  1. Attach the 4 claws to install the rear lower back door garnish sub-assembly outside RH, and then install the screw.

33. INSTALL BACK DOOR LOCK ASSEMBLY

NOTICE:
  1. When reusing the removed back door lock assembly, replace the door lock wiring harness seal on the connector with a new one.
  2. Do not allow grease or dust to adhere to the surface of the connector which contacts the door lock wiring harness seal.
  3. Reusing the door lock wiring harness seal or using a damaged door lock wiring harness seal may allow water into the connection. This may result in a malfunction of the front door lock assembly.




  1. Connect the cable.

  1. Install a new door lock wiring harness seal to the front door lock assembly.

  1. Using a T30 "TORX" wrench, install the back door lock assembly with the 3 screws.

    Torque:
    5.0 N*m{ 51 kgf*cm , 44 in.*lbf }

  1. Connect the connector.
