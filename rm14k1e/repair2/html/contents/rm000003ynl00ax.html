HINT:

  2. The procedure listed below is for the LH side.



1. REMOVE ROOF HEADLINING ASSEMBLY

  1. Remove the roof headlining assembly (Click here).



2. REMOVE VANITY LIGHT ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Detach the 2 claws (A) and separate the bulb holder from the vanity light as shown in the illustration.

  1. Detach the claw (B) and remove the vanity light.
