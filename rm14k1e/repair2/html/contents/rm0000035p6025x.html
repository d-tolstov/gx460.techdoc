DTC    C1420    Acceleration Sensor Malfunction

for Preparation Click here


DESCRIPTION

Refer to DTCs C1419 and C1435 (Click here).

DTC Code
DTC Detection Condition
Trouble Area
C1420
After the difference between GL1 and GL2 becomes 0.6 G or more with the vehicle stationary, the difference remains 0.4 G or more for 60 seconds or more.
  1. Sensor installation
  2. Yaw rate and acceleration sensor

WIRING DIAGRAM

Refer to DTCs C1232, C1243 and C1245 (Click here).
