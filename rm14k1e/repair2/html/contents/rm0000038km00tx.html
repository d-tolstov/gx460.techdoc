FRONT DOOR WINDOW FRAME MOULDING > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here


HINT:
  1. Use the same procedure for the RH and LH sides.
  2. The procedure listed below is for the LH side.



1. DISCONNECT CABLE FROM NEGATIVE BATTERY TERMINAL

NOTICE:
  1. w/ Navigation System (for HDD):
    After the engine switch is turned off, the display and navigation module display (HDD navigation system) records various types of memory and settings. As a result, after turning the engine switch off, be sure to wait for the time specified in the following table before disconnecting the cable from the negative (-) battery terminal.

    Waiting Time before Disconnecting Cable from Negative (-) Battery Terminal:

    Condition
    		Waiting Time
    Vehicle enrolled in safety connect system
    		6 minutes
    Vehicle not enrolled in safety connect system
    		1 minute

  2. When disconnecting the cable, some systems need to be initialized after the cable is reconnected (Click here).



2. REMOVE FRONT DOOR BELT MOULDING LH

  1. Remove the front door belt moulding (Click here).



3. REMOVE FRONT DOOR WEATHERSTRIP LH



  1. Detach the 3 clips and remove the upper part of the front door weatherstrip so that the moulding can be removed.



4. REMOVE FRONT DOOR REAR WINDOW FRAME MOULDING LH

HINT:
When removing the window frame moulding, heat the vehicle body and window frame moulding using a heat light.

Standard:
Item
Temperature
Vehicle Body
40 to 60°C (104 to 140°F)
Window Frame Moulding
20 to 30°C (68 to 86°F)

NOTICE:
Do not heat the vehicle body or window frame moulding excessively.




  1. Remove the door window frame moulding clip.




  1. Put protective tape around the front door rear window frame moulding.

  1. Using a moulding remover, detach the clip and remove the double-sided tape to remove the front door rear window frame moulding.
