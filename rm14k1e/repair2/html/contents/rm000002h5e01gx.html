VANE PUMP > DISASSEMBLY

for Preparation Click here



1. SECURE VANE PUMP ASSEMBLY



  1. Using SST, secure the vane pump in a vise.

    SST
    09630-00014   (09631-00132)



2. REMOVE SUCTION PORT UNION



  1. Remove the bolt and suction port union from the vane pump.

  1. Using a screwdriver, remove the O-ring from the suction port union.



3. REMOVE PUMP REAR BRACKET



  1. Remove the bolt and bracket.



4. REMOVE PRESSURE FEED TUBE

  1. Remove the union bolt and pressure tube.



5. REMOVE PRESSURE PORT UNION

  1. Remove the pressure port union and O-ring.



6. REMOVE VANE PUMP REAR HOUSING



  1. Remove the 4 bolts and vane pump rear housing from the vane pump front housing.




  1. Using a screwdriver, remove the O-ring from the vane pump rear housing.



7. REMOVE VANE PUMP SHAFT WITH PULLEY



  1. Using 2 screwdrivers, remove the snap ring from the vane pump shaft.

  1. Remove the vane pump shaft with pulley.

    NOTICE:
    Be careful not to drop or damage the vane pump shaft with vane pump pulley.

    If it is damaged, replace the vane pump assembly.



8. REMOVE VANE PUMP ROTOR



  1. Remove the 10 vane pump plates.

    NOTICE:
    Take care not to drop the vane pump plates.

  1. Remove the vane pump rotor from the vane pump front housing.



9. REMOVE VANE PUMP CAM RING



  1. Remove the cam ring from the vane pump front housing.



10. REMOVE VANE PUMP FRONT SIDE PLATE



  1. Remove the front side plate from the vane pump front housing.




  1. Using a screwdriver, remove the O-ring from the front side plate.




  1. Remove the O-ring from the vane pump front housing.



11. REMOVE VANE PUMP HOUSING OIL SEAL



  1. Using a screwdriver and piece of cloth, pry out the oil seal.
