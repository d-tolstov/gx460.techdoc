AUTOMATIC LIGHT CONTROL SYSTEM OPERATION CHECK

  1. Turn the engine switch on (IG).

  2. Turn the headlight dimmer switch to the AUTO position.

  3. Cover the automatic light control sensor.

  4. Check that the taillights and low beam headlights come on.

  5. Uncover the automatic light control sensor.

  6. Check that the low beam headlights and taillights turn off.


LIGHT AUTO TURN-OFF SYSTEM OPERATION CHECK

  1. When the headlight dimmer switch is in the tail or head position:

    1. Turn the engine switch on (IG).

    2. Turn the low beam headlights, high beam headlights, front fog lights and rear fog lights on.

    3. Turn the engine switch off.

    4. Check that the low beam headlights, high beam headlights and front fog lights go off immediately.

      HINT:
      The taillights remain on.

  2. When the headlight dimmer switch is in the AUTO position:

    1. Close all doors and the glass hatch.

    2. Turn the engine switch on (IG).

    3. Turn the low beam headlights, high beam headlights, front fog lights and rear fog lights on.

    4. Turn the engine switch off.

    5. Open and close a door or the glass hatch.

    6. When the delay time set through the customize function elapses, check that all the lights turn off.


HID HEADLIGHT SYSTEM OPERATION CHECK

  1. Perform the following diagnosis procedure when both of the following conditions are met: 1) the light control ECU is not visibly damaged, and 2) the light control ECU has been struck but is operating normally. If all of the following diagnosis results are normal, the light control ECU can be reused.

    1. Perform low beam headlight cold starts (10 minutes or more off, then turned on) and hot starts (15 minutes or more on, 1 minute off, then turned on again) several times. Check that the low beam headlights illuminate normally.

    2. For 5 minutes immediately after performing a headlight cold start as described above, visually check the illumination and check that the lights do not flicker or show signs of instability.

    3. Install bulbs that have approximately the same usage time, and illuminate the low beam headlights for approximately 30 seconds. Check that the brightness of the left and right bulbs is approximately the same, and that the illumination is stable.


AFS OPERATION CHECK

  1. Check that the initialization (determination of the initial position) of the leveling motor and swivel motor is performed after the engine is started.

  2. Check that the AFS OFF indicator light in the combination meter assembly comes on for approximately 3 seconds and then goes off when the engine switch is turned on (IG).
