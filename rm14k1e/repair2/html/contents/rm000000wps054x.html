CHECK AIR CONDITIONING AMPLIFIER ASSEMBLY


  1. Disconnect the G13 air conditioning amplifier connector.

  2. Measure the voltage and resistance according to the value(s) in the table below.

    Terminal No. (Symbol)
    		Wiring Color
    		Terminal Description
    		Condition
    		Specified Condition
    G13-21 (B) - G13-14 (GND)
    		V - W-B
    		Battery power source
    		Always
    		11 to 14 V
    G13-1 (IG+) - G13-14 (GND)
    		L - W-B
    		Ignition power supply
    		Engine switch on (IG)
    		11 to 14 V
    G13-1 (IG+) - G13-14 (GND)
    		L - W-B
    		Ignition power supply
    		Engine switch off
    		Below 1 V
    G13-14 (GND) - Body ground
    		W-B - Body ground
    		Ground
    		Always
    		Below 1 Ω

    1. If the result is not as specified, there may be a malfunction on the wire harness side.

  3. Reconnect the G13 air conditioning amplifier connector.

  4. Measure the voltage according to the value(s) in the table below.

    Terminal No. (Symbol)
    		Wiring Color
    		Terminal Description
    		Condition
    		Specified Condition
    G13-38 (RDEF) - G13-14 (GND)
    		P - W-B
    		Rear defogger signal
    		Engine switch on (IG), rear window defogger switch off
    		11 to 14 V
    G13-38 (RDEF) - G13-14 (GND)
    		P - W-B
    		Rear defogger signal
    		Engine switch on (IG), rear window defogger switch on
    		Below 1 V

    1. If the result is not as specified, there may be a malfunction in the air conditioning amplifier.


CHECK DISPLAY AND NAVIGATION MODULE DISPLAY (w/ Navigation System)


  1. Disconnect the H4 display connector.

  2. Measure the resistance and voltage according to the value(s) in the table below.

    Terminal No. (Symbol)
    		Wiring Color
    		Terminal Description
    		Condition
    		Specified Condition
    H4-1 (+B1) - H4-10 (GND1)
    		SB - BR
    		Battery power source
    		Always
    		11 to 14 V
    H4-2 (ACC) - H4-10 (GND1)
    		GR - BR
    		ACC power supply
    		Engine switch on (ACC)
    		11 to 14 V
    H4-3 (IG) - H4-10 (GND1)
    		L - BR
    		IG power supply
    		Engine switch on (IG)
    		11 to 14 V
    H4-10 (GND1) - Body ground
    		BR - Body ground
    		Ground
    		Always
    		Below 1 Ω
    If the result is not as specified, there may be a malfunction on the wire harness side.
