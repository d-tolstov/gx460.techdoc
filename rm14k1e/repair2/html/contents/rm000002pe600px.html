DTC    C1796/96    (Up & Down) G Sensor FR

DTC    C1797/97    (Up & Down) G Sensor FL

DTC    C1798/98    (Up & Down) G Sensor Rear

for Preparation Click here


DESCRIPTION

The acceleration sensor (up and down G sensor) detects the upward and downward acceleration of the vehicle, and outputs it as a voltage to the suspension control ECU. Up and down G sensors are installed in 3 locations: 1) the suspension control ECU, 2) the RH side instrument panel, and 3) behind the deck trim side panel assembly LH. Each up and down G sensor independently detects the upward and downward acceleration. During a test mode inspection, the suspension control ECU reads the fluctuations in each sensor signal. If a sensor signal does not fluctuate, test mode DTCs C1796/96, C1797/97 and C1798/98 are stored.

DTC Code
DTC Detection Condition
Trouble Area
C1715/15
Either condition is met:
  1. With the engine switch on (IG), a voltage of 5.5 V or higher or 4.3 V or less at the power source of the front acceleration sensor RH is detected for 0.5 seconds.
  2. With the engine switch on (IG), a voltage of 4.7 V or higher or 0.3 V or less at the front acceleration sensor RH is detected for 1 second.
  1. Harness or connector
  2. Front acceleration sensor RH
  3. Suspension control ECU
C1716/16
Either condition is met:
  1. With the engine switch on (IG), a voltage of 5.5 V or higher or 4.3 V or less at the power source of the front acceleration sensor LH is detected for 0.5 seconds.
  2. With the engine switch on (IG), a voltage of 4.7 V or higher or 0.3 V or less at the front acceleration sensor LH is detected for 1 second.
Suspension control ECU (front acceleration sensor LH)
C1717/17
Either condition is met:
  1. With the engine switch on (IG), a voltage of 5.5 V or higher or 4.3 V or less at the power source of the rear acceleration sensor is detected for 0.5 seconds.
  2. With the engine switch on (IG), a voltage of 4.7 V or higher or 0.3 V or less at the rear acceleration sensor is detected for 1 second.
  1. Harness or connector
  2. Rear acceleration sensor
  3. Suspension control ECU
C1796/96
On a level surface, the G sensor input is +/- 1.96 m/s2 or more for 1 second or more.
  1. Harness or connector
  2. Front acceleration sensor RH
  3. Suspension control ECU
C1797/97
On a level surface, the G sensor input is +/- 1.96 m/s2 or more for 1 second or more.
Suspension control ECU (front acceleration sensor LH)
C1798/98
On a level surface, the G sensor input is +/- 1.96 m/s2 or more for 1 second or more.
  1. Harness or connector
  2. Rear acceleration sensor
  3. Suspension control ECU

HINT:
If DTCs C1716/16 and C1797/97 are output, replace the suspension control ECU (houses the front acceleration sensor LH).

  1. Check the front acceleration sensor RH (when DTC C1715/15 or C1796/96 is output).
