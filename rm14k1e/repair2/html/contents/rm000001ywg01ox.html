1.POWER FOLDING SEAT FUNCTION HANDLING PRECAUTIONS

  1. Before operating the power folding seat, make sure that there is nothing on the third-row seats or in their path of movement.

    CAUTION:
    If someone or something is caught between the seat and other parts, injury or damage may result.

    HINT:
    If the system detects that an object is jammed between the seat cushion and the floor or the seatback, movement is terminated and the operation warning buzzer sounds.
