REAR SPOILER > REASSEMBLY

for Preparation Click here


HINT:
When installing the rear spoiler protector, heat the rear spoiler surface using a heat light.

Standard:
Item
Temperature
Rear Spoiler
20 to 30°C (68 to 86°F)



1. INSTALL REAR NO. 1 SPOILER PROTECTOR



  1. When using a new rear spoiler:

    1. Clean the rear spoiler surface.

    2. Using a heat light, heat the rear spoiler surface.

  1. When reusing the rear spoiler:

    1. Using a heat light, heat the rear spoiler surface.

    2. Remove the double-sided tape from the rear spoiler.

    3. Wipe off any tape adhesive residue with cleaner.

  1. Install a new rear No. 1 spoiler protector.

    1. Remove the peeling paper from the face of a new rear No. 1 spoiler protector.

      HINT:
      After removing the peeling paper, keep the exposed adhesive free from foreign matter.

    2. Install the rear No. 1 spoiler protector.



2. INSTALL REAR SPOILER SIDE PROTECTOR LH



  1. When using a new rear spoiler:

    1. Clean the rear spoiler surface.

    2. Using a heat light, heat the rear spoiler surface.

  1. When reusing the rear spoiler:

    1. Using a heat light, heat the rear spoiler surface.

    2. Remove the double-sided tape from the rear spoiler.

    3. Wipe off any tape adhesive residue with cleaner.

  1. Install a new rear spoiler side protector.

    1. Remove the peeling paper from the face of a new rear spoiler side protector.

      HINT:

    2. Install the rear spoiler side protector.



3. INSTALL REAR SPOILER SIDE PROTECTOR RH

HINT:
Use the same procedure described for the LH side.
