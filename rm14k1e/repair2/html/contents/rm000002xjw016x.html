REAR WIPER MOTOR > INSPECTION

for Preparation Click here



1. INSPECT REAR WIPER MOTOR ASSEMBLY



  1. Check the wiper continuous operation.

    1. Check that the rear wiper motor assembly operates when the battery voltage is applied between the terminals.

      Specified Condition:
      Tester Connection
      		Specified Condition
      Battery positive (+) → Terminal 5 (B)
      Battery negative (-) → Terminal 2 (LS)
      Battery negative (-) → Terminal 3 (E)
      		Motor operates at low speed (LO)

      If the result is not as specified, replace the motor assembly.
      Text in Illustration 
      *a
      		Component without harness connected
      (Rear Wiper Motor Assembly)




  1. Check the wiper intermittent operation.

    1. Check that the rear wiper motor assembly operates when the battery voltage is applied between the terminals.

      Specified Condition:
      Tester Connection
      		Specified Condition
      Battery positive (+) → Terminal 5 (B)
      Battery negative (-) → Terminal 4 (C1)
      Battery negative (-) → Terminal 3 (E)
      		Rear wiper motor operates intermittently

      If the result is not as specified, replace the motor assembly.
      Text in Illustration 
      *a
      		Component without harness connected
      (Rear Wiper Motor Assembly)
