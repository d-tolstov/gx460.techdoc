ULTRASONIC SENSOR (for Front) > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here



1. REMOVE UPPER RADIATOR SUPPORT SEAL



  1. Remove the 13 clips and upper radiator support seal.



2. REMOVE RADIATOR GRILLE



  1. Put protective tape around the radiator grille.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape

  1. Remove the 2 screws and 2 bolts.

  1. Detach the 6 claws and remove the radiator grille.



3. REMOVE FRONT BUMPER COVER

  1. Put protective tape around the front bumper cover.

  1. Remove the 2 bolts labeled A and 2 bolts labeled B.




  1. Remove the 2 screws and 4 clips.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape
    *2
    		Bolt A
    *3
    		Bolt B




  1. Remove the 2 screws.

    HINT:
    Use the same procedure for the RH and LH sides.




  1. Detach the 7 claws.

    HINT:
    Use the same procedure for the RH and LH sides.

  1. w/ LEXUS Parking Assist-sensor System:
    Disconnect the connector.

  1. Disconnect the headlight cleaner hose and 2 fog light connectors.

  1. Remove the front bumper cover.



4. REMOVE NO. 1 ULTRASONIC SENSOR



HINT:
Use the same procedure to remove the No. 1 ultrasonic sensor on the other side.

  1. Disconnect the connector.

  1. Detach the 2 claws and remove the No. 1 ultrasonic sensor.



5. REMOVE NO. 1 ULTRASONIC SENSOR RETAINER



HINT:
Use the same procedure to remove the No. 1 ultrasonic sensor retainer on the other side.

  1. Detach the 2 claws and remove the No. 1 ultrasonic sensor retainer.
