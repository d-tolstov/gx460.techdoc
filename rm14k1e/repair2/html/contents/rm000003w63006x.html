HINT:
  1. Use the same procedure for the RH and LH sides.
  2. The procedure listed below is for the LH side.



1. INSTALL QUARTER PANEL WHEEL OPENING MOULDING LH

HINT:
When installing the quarter panel wheel opening moulding, heat the vehicle body and quarter panel wheel opening moulding using a heat light.

Standard:
Item
Temperature
Vehicle Body
40 to 60°C (104 to 140°F)
Quarter Panel Wheel Opening Moulding
20 to 30°C (68 to 86°F)

NOTICE:
Do not heat the vehicle body or quarter panel wheel opening moulding excessively.

  1. Clean the vehicle body surface.

    1. Using a heat light, heat the vehicle body surface.

    2. Remove the double-sided tape from the vehicle body surface.

    3. Wipe off any tape adhesive residue with cleaner.

  1. Install a new quarter panel wheel opening moulding.

    1. Using a heat light, heat a new quarter panel wheel opening moulding and the vehicle body surface.

    2. Remove the peeling paper from the face of the quarter panel wheel opening moulding.

      HINT:
      After removing the peeling paper, keep the exposed adhesive free from foreign matter.

    3. Install the quarter panel wheel opening moulding.

      HINT:
      Press the quarter panel wheel opening moulding firmly to install it.

    4. Install the screw and grommet.
