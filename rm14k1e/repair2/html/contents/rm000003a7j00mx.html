RELAY (for Window Defogger) > ON-VEHICLE INSPECTION

for Preparation Click here



1. REMOVE DEFOGGER RELAY (DEFOG)

  1. Remove the defogger relay from the main body ECU (instrument panel junction block).



2. INSPECT DEFOGGER RELAY



  1. Measure the resistance according to the value(s) in the table below.

    Standard Resistance:
    Tester Connection
    		Condition
    		Specified Condition
    3 - 5
    		Battery voltage not applied to terminals 1 and 2
    		10 kΩ or higher
    Battery voltage applied to terminals 1 and 2
    		Below 1 Ω

    If the result is not as specified, replace the relay.



3. INSTALL DEFOGGER RELAY

  1. Install the defogger relay to the main body ECU (instrument panel junction block).
