INSPECTION PROCEDURE

1.CHECK CELLULAR PHONE

  1. Check if the other caller can hear your voice properly.

    OK:
    Your voice can be heard correctly.

2.CHECK SETTINGS



  1. Check if the mute switch is set to on.

    OK:
    Mute switch is not set to on.



NG
TURN MUTE SWITCH OFF
OK


3.CHECK MICROPHONE (NAVIGATION CHECK MODE)



  1. Enter "Microphone & Voice Recognition Check" (Check Mic & Voice Recognition) (Click here).

  1. When voice is input into the microphone, check that the microphone input level meter changes according to the input voice.

  1. Push the recording switch and perform voice recording.

    HINT:
    1. Select the recording switch with the blower motor of the air conditioning system stopped. If an outlet of the air conditioning system is facing the microphone, noise may be recorded.
    2. Voice can be recorded for up to 5 seconds.

  1. Check that the recording indicator remains on while recording and that the recorded voice is played normally without noise or distortion.

    OK:
    All check results are normal.
