AIR SUSPENSION SYSTEM > REGISTRATION

for Preparation Click here


NOTICE:
  1. CAN communication system must be functioning normally.
  2. Make sure that the ECM is the correct type, normally functioning and correctly installed.

HINT:
  1. If the suspension control ECU is replaced with a new one, perform registration of vehicle identification information.
  2. Vehicle identification information is obtained automatically through CAN communication when in test mode.



1. REGISTRATION OF VEHICLE IDENTIFICATION INFORMATION (WHEN USING INTELLIGENT TESTER)

  1. Turn the engine switch off.

  1. Connect the intelligent tester to the DLC3.

  1. Turn the engine switch on (IG).

  1. Turn the intelligent tester on.

  1. Enter the following menus: Chassis / Air suspension / Utility / Signal Check.

  1. Check the display of the multi-information display and confirm that the system is in test mode.

    HINT:
    1. When the vehicle identification information is stored, the multi-information display indicates that the system is in test mode.
    2. When vehicle identification information is not stored, the height control OFF indicator blinks at 1 second intervals (1 second on and 1 second off).



2. REGISTRATION OF VEHICLE IDENTIFICATION INFORMATION (WHEN USING SST CHECK WIRE)

  1. Turn the engine switch off.




  1. Using SST, connect terminals 12 (TS) and 4 (CG) of the DLC3.

    SST
    09843-18040  
    Text in Illustration 
    *a
    		Front view of DLC3

    NOTICE:
    Do not connect the incorrect terminals as this will cause damage.

  1. Turn the engine switch on (IG).

  1. Check the display of the multi-information display and confirm that the system is in test mode.

    HINT:
    1. When the vehicle identification information is stored, the multi-information display indicates that the system is in test mode.
    2. When vehicle identification information is not stored, the height control OFF indicator blinks at 1 second intervals (1 second on and 1 second off).
