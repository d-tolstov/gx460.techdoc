DTC    C120A    ECU Initial Setting Incomplete

for Preparation Click here


DESCRIPTION

w/ Downhill Assist Control or w/ Crawl Control:
When the master cylinder solenoid (skid control ECU) is replaced, the master cylinder solenoid (skid control ECU) must be informed that the vehicle is equipped with downhill assist control and/or crawl control.

DTC Code
DTC Detection Condition
Trouble Area
C120A
The master cylinder solenoid is not informed that the vehicle is equipped with downhill assist control or crawl control and downhill assist control or crawl control operates.
Calibration not performed

  1. Perform calibration (Click here).

    NOTICE:
    If a DTC is output after replacing the master cylinder solenoid, it is possible that calibration has not been performed or the zero point calibration data was not cleared when calibration was performed.
