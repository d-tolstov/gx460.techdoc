NAVIGATION SYSTEM (for HDD) > Illumination Circuit

for Preparation Click here


DESCRIPTION

Power is supplied to the display and navigation module display and the steering pad switch illumination when the light control switch is in the TAIL or HEAD position.

WIRING DIAGRAM




INSPECTION PROCEDURE

CAUTION:
The vehicle is equipped with an SRS (Supplemental Restraint System) which includes components such as airbags. Before servicing (including removal or installation of parts), be sure to read the Precaution in the SRS section (Click here).

1.CHECK ILLUMINATION

  1. Check if the illumination for the display and navigation display, steering pad switch, glove box or other parts (hazard switch, transmission control switch, etc.) comes on when the light control switch is turned to the HEAD or TAIL position.

    Result 
    Result
    		Proceed to
    Illumination comes on for all components except steering pad switch
    		A
    Illumination comes on for all components except display and navigation module display
    		B
    No illumination comes on (display and navigation module display, hazard switch, glove box, etc.)
    		C
    Illumination comes on for all components except steering pad switch and display and navigation module display
    		D



B
Go to step 6

C
GO TO LIGHTING SYSTEM (Click here)

D
Go to step 7
A


2.CHECK HARNESS AND CONNECTOR (SPIRAL WITH SENSOR CABLE - BATTERY)



  1. Disconnect the G28 spiral with sensor cable sub-assembly connector.

  1. Measure the voltage according to the value(s) in the table below.

    Standard Voltage:
    Tester Connection
    		Switch Condition
    		Specified Condition
    G28-12 (IL+2) - Body ground
    		Light control switch TAIL or HEAD
    		11 to 14 V

    (to Spiral with Sensor Cable Sub-assembly)

REPAIR OR REPLACE HARNESS OR CONNECTOR
OK


3.INSPECT STEERING PAD SWITCH ASSEMBLY


  1. Remove the steering pad switch assembly (Click here).



  1. Connect the positive (+) lead of the battery to terminal 5 (IL+2) and the negative (-) lead of the battery to terminal 8 (EAU) of the steering pad switch assembly connector.

  1. Check if the illumination for the steering pad switch assembly comes on.

    OK:
    Illumination for the steering pad switch assembly comes on.



NG
REPLACE STEERING PAD SWITCH ASSEMBLY (Click here)
OK

    Standard Resistance:
    Tester Connection
    		Condition
    		Specified Condition

    5 (IL+2) - 12 (IL+2)
    		Spiral with sensor cable is turned 2.5 rotations counterclockwise

    Spiral with sensor cable is turned 2.5 rotations clockwise
