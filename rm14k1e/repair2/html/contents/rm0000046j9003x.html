1. REMOVE NO. 1 HEATER TO REGISTER DUCT



  1. Remove the 4 screws <A> and No. 1 heater to register duct.



2. REMOVE NO. 2 HEATER TO REGISTER DUCT

HINT:
Use the same procedure described for the No. 1 heater to register duct.



3. REMOVE NO. 1 SIDE DEFROSTER NOZZLE DUCT



  1. Remove the screw <A>.

  1. Detach the 2 claws and remove the No. 1 side defroster nozzle duct.



4. REMOVE NO. 2 SIDE DEFROSTER NOZZLE DUCT

HINT:
Use the same procedure described for the No. 1 side defroster nozzle duct.



5. REMOVE DEFROSTER NOZZLE ASSEMBLY



  1. Remove the 4 screws <A> and defroster nozzle.



6. REMOVE NO. 3 HEATER TO REGISTER DUCT



  1. Remove the 2 screws <A> and No. 3 heater to register duct.



7. REMOVE INSTRUMENT PANEL CUSHION

  1. Remove the instrument panel cushion.



8. REMOVE NO. 1 DEFROSTER NOZZLE GARNISH


  1. Remove the 6 screws <A> and No. 1 defroster nozzle garnish.





9. REMOVE NO. 2 INSTRUMENT PANEL WIRE

  1. Disconnect the connector.



  1. Detach the 18 clamps and remove the No. 2 instrument panel wire.





10. REMOVE SUNSHINE SENSOR HOLE COVER



  1. Detach the 2 claws and remove the sunshine sensor hole cover.



11. REMOVE AUTOMATIC LIGHT CONTROL SENSOR



  1. Detach the 2 claws and remove the sensor.



12. REMOVE NO. 1 METER HOOD RETAINER



HINT:
Use the same procedure for the other No. 1 meter hood retainer.

  1. Remove the screw <A> and No. 1 meter hood retainer.



13. REMOVE SIDE DEFROSTER DUCT ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Detach the 4 clips and claw and remove the side defroster duct.



14. REMOVE SIDE DEFROSTER DUCT ASSEMBLY RH

HINT:
Use the same procedure described for the LH side.



15. REMOVE NAVIGATION ANTENNA ASSEMBLY (w/ Navigation System)



  1. Detach the 3 clamps.

  1. Remove the 2 screws and navigation antenna.



16. REMOVE INSTRUMENT PANEL PASSENGER AIRBAG ASSEMBLY



  1. Remove the 2 screws.




  1. Detach the 5 hooks (A).
