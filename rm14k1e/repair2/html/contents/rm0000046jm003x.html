1. REMOVE ROOF SIDE AIR OUTLET REGISTER



HINT:
Use the same procedure for all roof side air outlet registers.

  1. Detach the 6 claws and remove the roof side air outlet register.



2. REMOVE NO. 3 ROOF SILENCER PAD



  1. Remove the No. 3 roof silencer pad.



3. REMOVE VANITY LIGHT ASSEMBLY



HINT:
Use the same procedure for the other vanity light.

  1. Detach the 2 claws labeled A and separate the bulb holder from the vanity light as shown in the illustration.

  1. Detach the claw labeled B and remove the vanity light.

4. REMOVE NO. 1 ROOF WIRE

  1. Turn the visor connectors approximately 90° clockwise and remove them from the roof headlining.

  1. Detach each clamp and remove the No. 1 roof wire.



  1. Remove any butyl tape remaining on the roof headlining.

5. REMOVE NO. 2 ANTENNA CORD SUB-ASSEMBLY


  1. Detach the antenna cord from the 11 clamps and the 2 notches on the rear of the roof headlining.



  1. Remove the antenna cord from the roof headlining.

  1. Remove the double-sided tape from the roof headlining.
