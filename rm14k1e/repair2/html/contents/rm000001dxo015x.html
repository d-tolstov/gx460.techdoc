DTC    C1256    Accumulator Low Pressure

for Preparation Click here


DESCRIPTION

Refer to DTC C1254 (Click here).

DTC Code
DTC Detection Condition
Trouble Area
C1256
The fluid pressure inside the accumulator is below the standard value.
  1. Hydraulic circuit
  2. Master cylinder solenoid (Accumulator pressure sensor)

INSPECTION PROCEDURE

NOTICE:
When replacing the master cylinder solenoid, perform calibration (Click here).

HINT:
When DTC C1251, C1253, or C1254 is output together with DTC C1256 inspect and repair the trouble area indicated by DTC C1251, C1253, or C1254 first.

1.RECONFIRM DTC

  1. Clear the DTCs (Click here).

  1. Turn the engine switch off.

  1. Depress the brake pedal more than 40 times.

  1. Turn the engine switch on (IG).

  1. Wait for 99 seconds.

  1. Check if the same DTC is output (Click here).
