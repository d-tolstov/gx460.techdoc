ROOF DRIP SIDE FINISH MOULDING > INSTALLATION

for Preparation Click here


HINT:
  1. Use the same procedure for the RH and LH sides.
  2. The procedure listed below is for the LH side.
  3. When installing the roof drip side finish moulding clip, heat the vehicle body and clip using a heat light.

    Standard:
    Item
    		Temperature
    Vehicle Body
    		40 to 60°C (104 to 140°F)
    Roof Drip Side Finish Moulding Clip
    		20 to 30°C (68 to 86°F)

NOTICE:
Do not heat the vehicle body or clip excessively.



1. INSTALL ROOF DRIP SIDE FINISH MOULDING CLIP

NOTICE:
  1. It is only necessary to perform this procedure when replacing a clip with a new one.
  2. If installing new clips, remove the adhesive remaining where the clips will be installed on the body and clean the body with a non-residue solvent.




  1. Apply a 2.0 to 3.0 mm (0.0787 to 0.118 in.) bead of adhesive (3M DP-105 or equivalent) to new roof drip side finish moulding clips.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Adhesive
    *a
    		2.0 to 3.0 mm Bead of Adhesive

    HINT:
    Adhesive strength (tensile strength): 13.7 MPa (140.0 kgf/cm2, 1991 psi) or more (when the temperature is 23°C (73°F))

  1. Apply primer to the body where the roof drip side finish moulding clip will be installed.

    NOTICE:
    Do not let the primer dry.



  1. While using the position shown in the illustration as a reference, place the roof drip side finish moulding clip onto the roof panel. Then position and install the clip after lightly pressing it so that the adhesive adheres to the primer.

    NOTICE:
    Install the rear roof drip side finish moulding when 40 minutes or more have elapsed after pressing and installing the roof drip side finish moulding clips.

    HINT:
    1. Initial hardening time: 40 minutes
    2. Complete hardening time: 48 hours



2. INSTALL ROOF DRIP SIDE FINISH MOULDING LH

  1. Attach the clip to install the roof drip side finish moulding.



3. INSTALL CENTER NO. 2 ROOF DRIP SIDE FINISH MOULDING LH (w/o Roof Rack)

  1. Install the center No. 2 roof drip side finish moulding.



4. INSTALL CENTER ROOF DRIP SIDE FINISH MOULDING LH (w/o Roof Rack)

  1. Install the center roof drip side finish moulding.



5. INSTALL REAR ROOF DRIP SIDE FINISH MOULDING LH

  1. Attach the clip to install the rear roof drip side finish moulding.



6. INSTALL ROOF DRIP SIDE FINISH MOULDING JOINT COVER LH (w/o Roof Rack)

HINT:
Use the same procedure for all roof drip side finish moulding joint covers.

  1. Attach the 2 clamps to install the roof drip side finish moulding joint cover.
