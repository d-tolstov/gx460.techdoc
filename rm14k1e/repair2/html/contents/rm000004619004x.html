FUEL PUMP > DISASSEMBLY

for Preparation Click here



NOTICE:

Do not disconnect the hoses indicated in the illustration.



1. REMOVE FUEL SENDER GAUGE ASSEMBLY



  1. Disconnect the fuel sender gauge connector.




  1. Press down on the fuel sender gauge claw labeled A. Then slide the fuel sender gauge upward to remove it.
    Text in Illustration 

    		Press Down

    		Slide



2. REMOVE NO. 1 FUEL SUB-TANK



  1. Disconnect the fuel pump connector.




  1. Disconnect the jet pump from the No. 1 fuel sub-tank.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Jet Pump
    *2
    		Protective Tape

  1. Remove the tube part from the No. 1 fuel sub-tank.

    1. Disconnect the tube from the 2 clamps on the No. 1 fuel sub-tank.

    2. Using a small screwdriver, detach the claw from the No. 1 fuel sub-tank.

      HINT:
      Tape the screwdriver tip before use.

      NOTICE:
      Do not damage the No. 1 fuel sub-tank.




  1. Using a screwdriver, detach the 3 claws from the claw holes.

    HINT:
    Tape the screwdriver tip before use.

    NOTICE:
    Do not damage the No. 1 fuel sub-tank.




  1. Using a screwdriver, detach the 3 claws from the claw holes and remove the No. 1 fuel sub-tank.

    HINT:
    Tape the screwdriver tip before use.

    NOTICE:
    Do not damage the No. 1 fuel sub-tank.



3. REMOVE FUEL PUMP



  1. Using a screwdriver, detach the 5 claws from the claw holes and disconnect the fuel pump from the fuel filter case.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Fuel Filter Case

    HINT:
    Tape the screwdriver tip before use.

  1. Disconnect the fuel pump wire harness connector from the fuel pump to remove it.




  1. Remove the O-ring and fuel pump spacer from the fuel pump.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		O-ring
    *2
    		Fuel Pump Spacer



4. REMOVE FUEL MAIN VALVE ASSEMBLY



  1. Remove the fuel main valve from the fuel filter case.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Fuel Filter Case
    *2
    		O-ring

  1. Remove the 2 O-rings from the fuel main valve.
