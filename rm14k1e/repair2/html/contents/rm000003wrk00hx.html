1. ADJUST REAR WASHER NOZZLE SUB-ASSEMBLY



  1. Using a screwdriver, adjust the direction of the rear washer nozzle.

    HINT:
    Use a thin-bladed screwdriver with an approximately 1 mm (0.0394 in.) thick tip.

    NOTICE:
    Do not use a safety pin or other pointed tools. Doing so may damage the inside of the washer nozzle.
