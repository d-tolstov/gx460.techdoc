1. CHECK MASS AIR FLOW METER ASSEMBLY


  1. Check the mass air flow value.

    1. Connect the intelligent tester to the DLC3.

    2. Turn the engine switch on (IG).

    3. Turn the intelligent tester on.

    4. Enter the following menus: Powertrain / Engine / Data List / All Data / MAF.




    5. Blow air into the mass air flow meter and check that the mass air flow value fluctuates.
