    		11 to 14 V

    		Below 1 Ω

    		L - Body ground
    		Battery
    		Always
    		11 to 14 V
    G3-27 (IG+) - Body ground
    		R - Body ground
    		Engine switch signal
    		Engine switch off
    		Below 1 V
    Engine switch on (IG)
    		11 to 14 V
    G3-29 (S) - Body ground
    		V - Body ground
    		Oil pressure switch signal
    		Engine switch on (IG), oil pressure warning light off
    		11 to 14 V
    Engine switch on (IG), oil pressure warning light on
    		Below 1 V
    G3-30 (CHG-) - Body ground

    		Charge warning light signal

    		11 to 14 V
    Engine switch on (IG), charge warning light on

    G3-34 (WLVL) - Body ground
    		W - Body ground
