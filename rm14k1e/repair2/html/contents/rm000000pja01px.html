WIPER AND WASHER SYSTEM > Headlight Cleaner Switch Circuit

for Preparation Click here


DESCRIPTION

This circuit detects the conditions (on or off) of the headlight cleaner switch assembly.

WIRING DIAGRAM




INSPECTION PROCEDURE

1.INSPECT HEADLIGHT CLEANER SWITCH ASSEMBLY

  1. Remove the headlight cleaner switch assembly (Click here).

  1. Measure the resistance according to the value(s) in the table below.

    Standard Resistance:
    Tester Connection
    		Switch Condition
    		Specified Condition
    5 - 8
    		Headlight cleaner switch on
    		Below 1 Ω
    5 - 8
    		Headlight cleaner switch off
    		10 kΩ or higher



NG
REPLACE HEADLIGHT CLEANER SWITCH ASSEMBLY (Click here)
OK


2.CHECK HARNESS AND CONNECTOR (HEADLIGHT CLEANER SWITCH ASSEMBLY - HEADLIGHT CLEANER CONTROL RELAY AND MAIN BODY ECU)

  1. Disconnect the G54 headlight cleaner switch connector.

  1. Disconnect the A32 headlight cleaner control relay connector.

  1. Remove the main body ECU (Click here).

  1. Measure the resistance according to the value(s) in the table below.

    Standard Resistance:
    Tester Connection
    		Condition
    		Specified Condition
    G54-8 - A32-2 (H)
    		Always
    		Below 1 Ω
    G54-5 - A-15 (HRLY)
    		Always
    		Below 1 Ω
    G54-8 - Body ground
    		Always
    		10 kΩ or higher
    G54-5 - Body ground
    		Always
    		10 kΩ or higher



NG
REPAIR OR REPLACE HARNESS OR CONNECTOR
OK

PROCEED TO NEXT SUSPECTED AREA SHOWN IN PROBLEM SYMPTOMS TABLE (Click here)
