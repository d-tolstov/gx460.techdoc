AIR SWITCHING VALVE (for Bank 1) > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here



1. REMOVE V-BANK COVER SUB-ASSEMBLY



  1. Raise the front of the V-bank cover to detach the 2 pins. Then detach the 2 V-bank cover hooks from the No. 1 V-bank cover bracket and remove the V-bank cover.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Pin
    *2
    		Hook
    *3
    		No. 1 V-bank Cover Bracket



2. REMOVE ENGINE ROOM SIDE COVER LH



  1. Remove the 4 clips and engine room side cover LH.



3. DISCONNECT NO. 2 AIR INJECTION SYSTEM HOSE



  1. Disconnect the air switching valve connector.

  1. Disconnect the No. 2 air injection system hose.



4. REMOVE AIR SWITCHING VALVE ASSEMBLY



  1. Remove the bolt and disconnect the wire harness clamp bracket.




  1. Remove the 2 bolts.




  1. Remove the 3 bolts, air switching valve and gasket.

    NOTICE:
    Be careful not to damage the surfaces that contact the gasket.
