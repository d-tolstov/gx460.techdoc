1. DISPOSE OF BRAKE BOOSTER ACCUMULATOR ASSEMBLY



  1. Place the brake booster accumulator assembly in a vise and cover it with cloth.

  1. Using a saw, cut the brake booster accumulator body between A and B as shown in the illustration to discharge the gas inside.

    CAUTION:
    1. Cover the accumulator with a piece of cloth when working because gas may blow out.
    2. Cut the accumulator carefully, not abruptly.
    3. Wear protective glasses.

  1. When the outer body of the brake booster accumulator is cut, gas and liquid discharge.

    HINT:
    The gas is colorless, odorless and non-poisonous.
