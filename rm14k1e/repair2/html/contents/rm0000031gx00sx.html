EXHAUST MANIFOLD > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here



1. REMOVE LOWER FRONT BUMPER COVER



  1. Remove the clip, 5 bolts and lower front bumper cover.



2. REMOVE NO. 1 ENGINE UNDER COVER SUB-ASSEMBLY

  1. Remove the 4 bolts.




  1. Unhook the No. 1 engine under cover from the vehicle body as shown in the illustration.



3. REMOVE REAR ENGINE UNDER COVER ASSEMBLY

  1. Remove the 4 bolts and rear engine under cover.



4. REMOVE FRONT FENDER APRON SEAL LH

  1. Remove the 7 clips and front fender apron seal LH.



5. REMOVE FRONT FENDER APRON SEAL RH

  1. Remove the 4 clips and front fender apron seal RH.



6. REMOVE FRONT NO. 1 FENDER APRON TO FRAME SEAL LH

  1. Remove the 5 clips and front No. 1 fender apron to frame seal LH.



7. REMOVE FRONT NO. 1 FENDER APRON TO FRAME SEAL RH

  1. Remove the 5 clips and front No. 1 fender apron to frame seal RH.



8. REMOVE INTAKE MANIFOLD ASSEMBLY

  1. Remove the intake manifold (Click here).



9. REMOVE GENERATOR ASSEMBLY

  1. Remove the generator (Click here).



10. DISCONNECT COOLER COMPRESSOR ASSEMBLY



  1. Disconnect the connector.

  1. Remove the 3 bolts, nut and cooler compressor.

  1. Remove the stud bolt.



11. REMOVE ENGINE OIL LEVEL DIPSTICK GUIDE

  1. Remove the dipstick.




  1. Remove the bolt and dipstick guide.

  1. Remove the O-ring from the dipstick guide.



12. REMOVE EXHAUST PIPE STOPPER BRACKET

  1. Remove the 2 bolts and exhaust pipe stopper bracket.



13. REMOVE FRONT NO. 2 EXHAUST PIPE ASSEMBLY

  1. Disconnect the heated oxygen sensor connector.

  1. Remove the 2 bolts and 2 nuts.

  1. Disconnect the exhaust pipe support, and then remove the front No. 2 exhaust pipe and 2 gaskets.



14. REMOVE FRONT EXHAUST PIPE ASSEMBLY

  1. Disconnect the heated oxygen sensor connector.

  1. Remove the 2 bolts and 2 compression springs from the center exhaust pipe.

  1. Remove the 2 nuts and front exhaust pipe.

  1. Remove the 2 gaskets.



15. REMOVE PROPELLER SHAFT HEAT INSULATOR WITH BRACKET



  1. Remove the 2 bolts and propeller shaft heat insulator with bracket.



16. REMOVE FRONT PROPELLER SHAFT ASSEMBLY



  1. Place matchmarks on the propeller shaft flange and differential.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Matchmark

  1. Remove the 4 nuts, 4 bolts, 4 washers and front propeller shaft assembly.




  1. Place matchmarks on the propeller shaft flange and transfer flange.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Matchmark

  1. Remove the 4 nuts, 4 washers and front propeller shaft assembly.



17. REMOVE NO. 2 MANIFOLD STAY



  1. Remove the 3 bolts and No. 2 manifold stay.



18. REMOVE NO. 1 EGR PIPE SUB-ASSEMBLY



  1. Remove the 4 nuts and EGR pipe.

  1. Remove the 2 gaskets.



19. REMOVE NO. 2 EXHAUST MANIFOLD HEAT INSULATOR



  1. Remove the 3 bolts and heat insulator.



20. REMOVE EXHAUST MANIFOLD ASSEMBLY LH

  1. Disconnect the air fuel ratio sensor connector.




  1. Remove the 9 nuts, exhaust manifold and 2 gaskets.



21. REMOVE MANIFOLD STAY



  1. Remove the 3 bolts and manifold stay.



22. REMOVE NO. 1 EXHAUST MANIFOLD HEAT INSULATOR



  1. Remove the 3 bolts and heat insulator.



23. REMOVE EXHAUST MANIFOLD ASSEMBLY RH

  1. Disconnect the air fuel ratio sensor connector.




  1. Remove the 9 nuts, exhaust manifold and 2 gaskets.



24. REMOVE AIR FUEL RATIO SENSOR (for Bank 1 Sensor 1)



  1. Using SST, remove the air fuel ratio sensor from the exhaust manifold LH.

    SST
    09224-00010  



25. REMOVE AIR FUEL RATIO SENSOR (for Bank 2 Sensor 1)



  1. Using SST, remove the air fuel ratio sensor from the exhaust manifold RH.

    SST
    09224-00010
