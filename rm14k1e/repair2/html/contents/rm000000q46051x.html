1. INSTALL FUEL INJECTOR ASSEMBLY

  1. Attach the 3 clamps to install the No. 6 wire harness to the fuel delivery pipe.

  1. Attach the 3 clamps to install the No. 7 wire harness to the No. 2 fuel delivery pipe.

  1. Apply gasoline or spindle oil to a new O-ring and install the O-ring to the injector.

    NOTICE:
    Make sure that there is no damage or foreign material in the groove of the injector when installing the O-ring.

  1. Connect the injector connector.




  1. Install the injector to the delivery pipe as shown in the illustration.
    Text in Illustration 
    *a
    		CORRECT
    *b
    		INCORRECT

    NOTICE:
    1. Make sure that there are no scratches or foreign matter in or around the insertion hole of the delivery pipe.
    2. When inserting the injector, be careful not to damage the O-ring.
    3. Attach the part of the injector labeled B between the parts of the delivery pipe labeled A.




  1. Check that each injector is installed to the delivery pipe facing the direction shown in the illustration.

10. INSTALL AIR SWITCHING VALVE ASSEMBLY (for Bank 2)



  1. Install a new gasket and the air switching valve with the 3 bolts.

    Torque:
    24 N*m{ 245 kgf*cm , 18 ft.*lbf }

    Text in Illustration

    *2
    		Air Switching Valve
    *3
    		Claw

    NOTICE:
    Make sure the claws of the gasket are not caught between the air switching valve and No. 3 air tube.

  1. Install the 2 bolts.

    Torque:
    10 N*m{ 102 kgf*cm , 7 ft.*lbf }

  1. Connect the wire harness clamp bracket with the bolt.

    Torque:
    8.0 N*m{ 82 kgf*cm , 71 in.*lbf }

  1. Attach the wire harness clamp and connect the air switching valve connector.

18. INSPECT FOR FUEL LEAK

  1. Make sure that there are no fuel leaks after performing maintenance on the fuel system.

    1. Connect the intelligent tester to the DLC3.

    2. Turn the engine switch on (IG) and turn the intelligent tester on.

      NOTICE:
      Do not start the engine.

    3. Enter the following menus: Powertrain / Engine and ECT / Active Test / Control the Fuel Pump / Speed.

    4. Check that there are no leaks from the fuel system.
      If there are fuel leaks, repair or replace parts as necessary.

    5. Turn the engine switch off.

    6. Disconnect the intelligent tester from the DLC3.
