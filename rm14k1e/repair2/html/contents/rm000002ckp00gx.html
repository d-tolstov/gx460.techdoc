REAR UPPER ARM > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here


HINT:
  1. Use the same procedure for the RH and LH sides.
  2. The procedure listed below is for the LH side.



1. REMOVE REAR WHEEL


2. DISCONNECT REAR HEIGHT CONTROL SENSOR LINK SUB-ASSEMBLY



  1. Disconnect the connector, and then detach the connector.

  1. Detach the clamp.




  1. Place matchmarks on the link and bracket.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Matchmark

  1. Remove the nut and disconnect the sensor link.

  1. Remove the 2 bolts and height control sensor.

    NOTICE:
    Replace the rear height control sensor sub-assembly with a new one if it is dropped.



3. REMOVE REAR UPPER CONTROL ARM ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Remove the nut, washer and bolt from the rear axle housing.

    HINT:
    Turn the nut while holding the bolt.




  1. Remove the nut, washer, bolt and upper control arm assembly.

    HINT:
    Turn the nut while holding the bolt.
