DESCRIPTION

On the SIGNAL CHECK screen, it is possible to check if the signals sent to the parking assist ECU are normal (Click here).

HINT:
  1. On the SIGNAL CHECK screen, "OK" (blue) is displayed for items with a normal inspection result or input state.
  2. On the SIGNAL CHECK screen, "CHK" (red) is displayed for items with an abnormal inspection result or input state.
  3. Displayed items may differ depending on vehicle specifications.
