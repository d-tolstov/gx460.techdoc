DTC    B278A    Short to GND in Immobiliser System Power Source Circuit

for Preparation Click here


DESCRIPTION

When there is a short to GND in the power supply for the transponder key amplifier of the engine switch, the certification ECU stores this DTC.

DTC Code
DTC Detection Condition
Trouble Area
DTC Output Confirmation Operation
B278A
A short to GND in the power supply of the transponder key amplifier of the engine switch (VC5 - VC5) (1 trip detection logic*).
  1. Harness or connector
  1. Engine switch
  1. Certification ECU
With the shift lever in P, the key is held against the engine switch and an engine start operation is performed by pressing and holding the engine switch when the key battery is depleted.
  1. *: Only output while a malfunction is present.

INSPECTION PROCEDURE

NOTICE:
  1. Before replacing the certification ECU, refer to the Service Bulletin.
  2. The fixed 5 V power source voltage in the engine switch is output through a resistance from terminal VC5.
  3. Terminal AGND is grounded through both the engine switch and certification ECU.
  4. After performing repair, perform the operation that fulfills the DTC output confirmation operation, and then confirm that no DTCs are output again.

1.CHECK HARNESS AND CONNECTOR (CERTIFICATION ECU - ENGINE SWITCH)

  1. Disconnect the G26 ECU connector.

  1. Disconnect the G24 switch connector.

  1. Measure the resistance according to the value(s) in the table below.

    Standard Resistance:
