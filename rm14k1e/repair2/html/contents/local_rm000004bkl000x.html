CHARGING SYSTEM > PRECAUTION



  1. Check that the battery cables are connected to the correct terminals.

  1. Disconnect the battery cables when the battery is given a quick charge.

  1. Do not perform tests with a high voltage insulation resistance tester.

  1. Never disconnect the battery while the engine is running.

  1. Check that the charging cable nuts on terminal B of the generator and the engine room relay block are tightened securely.
