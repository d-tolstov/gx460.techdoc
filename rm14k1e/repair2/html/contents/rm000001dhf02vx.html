OUTER MIRROR SWITCH > INSPECTION

for Preparation Click here



1. INSPECT OUTER MIRROR SWITCH ASSEMBLY



  1. Inspect the outer mirror switch.

    1. Select "L" on the mirror select switch.

    2. Measure the resistance according to the value(s) in the table below.

      Standard Resistance:
      Tester Connection
      		Switch Condition
      		Specified Condition
      13 (M+) - 12 (E)
      		Mirror select switch L
      Mirror adjust switch pressed up
      		90 to 110 Ω
      Mirror select switch L
      Mirror adjust switch pressed down
      		437 to 503 Ω
      Mirror select switch L
      Mirror adjust switch pressed left
      		744 to 856 Ω
      Mirror select switch L
      Mirror adjust switch pressed right
      		225 to 275 Ω

      If the result is not as specified, replace the outer mirror switch assembly.

    3. Select "R" on the mirror select switch.

    4. Measure the resistance according to the value(s) in the table below.

      Standard Resistance:
      Tester Connection
      		Switch Condition
      		Specified Condition
      13 (M+) - 12 (E)
      		Mirror select switch L
      Mirror adjust switch pressed up
      		90 to 110 Ω
      Mirror select switch L
      Mirror adjust switch pressed down
      		437 to 503 Ω
      Mirror select switch L
      Mirror adjust switch pressed left
      		744 to 856 Ω
      Mirror select switch L
      Mirror adjust switch pressed right
      		225 to 275 Ω

      If the result is not as specified, replace the outer mirror switch assembly.

  1. Inspect the retract switch.

    1. Measure the resistance according to the value(s) in the table below.

      Standard Resistance:
      Tester Connection
      		Switch Condition
      		Specified Condition
      8 (MR) - 10 (E2)
      		Retract switch in retract position
      		Below 10 Ω
      Retract switch in driving position
      		10 kΩ or higher

      If the result is not as specified, replace the outer mirror switch assembly.

  1. Inspect the switch illumination.
    Apply battery voltage between the terminals of the light and check the operation of the light.

    OK:
    Measurement Condition
    		Specified Condition
    Battery positive (+) → Terminal 6 (ILL+)
    Battery negative (-) → Terminal 15 (ILL-)
    		Light comes on

    If the result is not as specified, replace the outer mirror switch assembly.
