1. REMOVE HEADLIGHT RELAY



  1. Remove the headlight relay from the engine room relay box.



2. INSPECT HEADLIGHT RELAY



  1. Measure the resistance according to the value(s) in the table below.

    Standard Resistance:
    Tester Connection
    		Condition
    		Specified Condition
    3 - 5
    		Battery voltage not applied to terminals 1 and 2
    		10 k立 or higher
    Battery voltage applied to terminals 1 and 2
    		Below 1 立

    If the result is not as specified, replace the headlight relay.



3. INSTALL HEADLIGHT RELAY

  1. Install the headlight relay to the engine room relay box.
