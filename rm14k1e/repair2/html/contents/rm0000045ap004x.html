1. INSPECT BACK WINDOW LOCK ASSEMBLY





Text in Illustration 
*a
Component without harness connected
(Back Window Lock Assembly)
*b
Moves to Open Position
*c
Closed Position
-
-

  1. Apply battery voltage to the back window lock motor and check the operation of the back window lock assembly.

    OK:
    Measurement Condition
    		Specified Condition
    Battery positive (+) → 3 (+)
    Battery negative (-) → 4 (-)
    		Open Position

    If the result is not as specified, replace the back window lock assembly.

  1. Measure the resistance of the back window lock.

    Standard Resistance:
    Tester Connection
    		Condition
    		Specified Condition
    2 (+) - 1 (-)
    		Open Position
    		Below 1 Ω
    Closed Position
    		10 kΩ or higher

    If the result is not as specified, replace the back window lock assembly.
