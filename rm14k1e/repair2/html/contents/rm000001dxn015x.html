DTC    C1253    Pump Motor Relay

for Preparation Click here


DESCRIPTION

The motor relay (semiconductor relay) is built into the master cylinder solenoid and drives the pump motor based on a signal from the skid control ECU.

DTC Code
DTC Detection Condition
Trouble Area
C1253
There is a motor system circuit (motor input circuit) malfunction.
  1. ABS NO. 1 fuse
  2. Motor relay circuit
  3. Master cylinder solenoid (Skid control ECU)

WIRING DIAGRAM




INSPECTION PROCEDURE

NOTICE:
  1. When replacing the master cylinder solenoid, perform calibration (Click here).
  2. Inspect the fuses for circuits related to this system before performing the following inspection procedure.

1.PERFORM ACTIVE TEST USING INTELLIGENT TESTER (MOTOR RELAY)

  1. Turn the engine switch off.

  1. Connect the intelligent tester to the DLC3.

  1. Turn the engine switch on (IG).

  1. Turn the intelligent tester on.

  1. Start the engine.

  1. Enter the following menus: Chassis / ABS/VSC/TRC / Active Test.

    ABS/VSC/TRC 
    Tester Display
    		Test Part
    		Control Range
    		Diagnostic Note
    Motor Relay
    		Motor relay
    		Relay ON/OFF
    		An operating sound of the motor can be heard.

  1. Check the operating sound of the motor individually when operating it with the intelligent tester.

    OK:
    An operating sound of the motor can be heard.

2.RECONFIRM DTC

  1. Clear the DTCs (Click here).

  1. Turn the engine switch off.

  1. Depress the brake pedal more than 40 times.

  1. Turn the engine switch on (IG).

  1. Wait until the pump motor stops.

  1. Depress the brake pedal several times until the pump motor turns on. (Procedure A)

  1. Wait until the pump stops. (Procedure B)

  1. Repeat the above steps (procedure A and B) 3 more times.

  1. Check if the same DTC is output (Click here).

    HINT:
    Reinstall the sensors, connectors, etc. and restore the previous vehicle conditions before rechecking for DTCs.
