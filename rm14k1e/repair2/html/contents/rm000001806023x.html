CONDENSER > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here



1. DRAIN ENGINE COOLANT

  1. Drain the engine coolant (Click here).



2. REMOVE RADIATOR ASSEMBLY

  1. Remove the radiator assembly (Click here).



3. RECOVER REFRIGERANT FROM REFRIGERATION SYSTEM

  1. Start the engine.

  1. Turn the A/C switch on.

  1. Operate the cooler compressor while the engine speed is approximately 1000 rpm for 5 to 6 minutes to circulate the refrigerant and collect the compressor oil remaining in each component into the cooler compressor.

  1. Stop the engine.

  1. Recover the refrigerant from the A/C system using a refrigerant recovery unit.



4. DISCONNECT DISCHARGE HOSE SUB-ASSEMBLY



  1. Remove the bolt and disconnect the discharge hose from the cooler condenser.

    NOTICE:
    1. When removing the bolt, do not allow any tools to contact the pipe.
    2. When removing the bolt, hold a part of the pipe near the connector.

  1. Remove the O-ring from the discharge hose.

    NOTICE:
    Seal the openings of the disconnected parts using vinyl tape to prevent moisture and foreign matter from entering them.



5. DISCONNECT AIR CONDITIONING TUBE ASSEMBLY



  1. Remove the bolt and disconnect the air conditioning tube assembly from the cooler condenser.

    NOTICE:
    1. When removing the bolt, do not allow any tools to contact the pipe.
    2. When removing the bolt, hold a part of the pipe near the connector.

  1. Remove the O-ring from the air conditioning tube assembly.

    NOTICE:
    Seal the openings of the disconnected parts using vinyl tape to prevent moisture and foreign matter from entering them.



6. DISCONNECT NO. 1 COOLER CONDENSER BRACKET



  1. Remove the bolt and disconnect the No. 1 cooler condenser bracket.



7. DISCONNECT NO. 2 COOLER CONDENSER BRACKET



  1. Remove the bolt and disconnect the No. 2 cooler condenser bracket.



8. REMOVE COOLER CONDENSER ASSEMBLY



  1. Remove the 3 bolts and cooler condenser as shown in the illustration.



9. REMOVE COOLER DRYER



  1. Using a 14 mm socket hexagon wrench, remove the cap from the modulator.




  1. Remove the O-ring from the cap.




  1. Using pliers, remove the cooler dryer.
