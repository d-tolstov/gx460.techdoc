DTC    C1422    Master Cylinder Pressure Sensor Zero Point High Malfunction

DTC    C1456    Control Pressure Sensor Zero Point High

for Preparation Click here


DESCRIPTION

Refer to DTCs C1421, C1423, C1424, C1455, C1457 and C1458 (Click here).

DTC Code
DTC Detection Condition
Trouble Area
C1422
When the stop light switch is off, the PM/C1 terminal voltage is 0.86 V or higher for 5 seconds or more.
  1. Stop light switch
  2. Stop light switch circuit
  3. Master cylinder pressure sensor circuit
  4. Master cylinder solenoid (Master cylinder pressure sensor)
C1456
When the stop light switch is off, the PCTRL terminal voltage is 0.86 V or higher for 5 seconds or more.
  1. Stop light switch
  2. Stop light switch circuit
  3. Pressure control sensor circuit
  4. Master cylinder solenoid (Pressure control sensor)

WIRING DIAGRAM

Refer to DTC C1425 (Click here).
