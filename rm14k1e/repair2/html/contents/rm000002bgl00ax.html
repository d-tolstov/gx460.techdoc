HEIGHT CONTROL DRYER > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here


NOTICE:
Before removal, Be sure to disable vehicle height control by pressing the height control OFF switch and confirming that the height control OFF indication is displayed on the meter.



1. DISCONNECT CABLE FROM NEGATIVE BATTERY TERMINAL

CAUTION:
Wait at least 90 seconds after disconnecting the cable from the negative (-) battery terminal to disable the SRS system.

NOTICE:
  1. w/ Navigation System (for HDD):
    After the engine switch is turned off, the display and navigation module display (HDD navigation system) records various types of memory and settings. As a result, after turning the engine switch off, be sure to wait for the time specified in the following table before disconnecting the cable from the negative (-) battery terminal.

    Waiting Time before Disconnecting Cable from Negative (-) Battery Terminal:

    Condition
    		Waiting Time
    Vehicle enrolled in G-BOOK system
    		6 minutes
    Vehicle not enrolled in G-BOOK system
    		1 minute

  2. When disconnecting the cable, some systems need to be initialized after the cable is reconnected (Click here).



2. REMOVE REAR WHEEL LH


3. REMOVE REAR QUARTER PANEL MUDGUARD SUB-ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Remove the 4 screws and rear quarter panel mudguard.



4. REMOVE REAR BUMPER PROTECTOR LH



  1. Remove the clip, 2 screws and rear bumper protector.



5. REMOVE HEIGHT CONTROL COMPRESSOR COVER

  1. Remove the 3 clips and height control compressor cover.



6. REMOVE REAR BUMPER PROTECTOR SUB-ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Remove the screw.

  1. Detach the guide and remove the rear bumper protector.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Guide



7. REMOVE HEIGHT CONTROL FILTER

  1. Slide the 2 clips and disconnect the 2 hoses.




  1. Remove the 2 bolts and height control filter from the height control compressor bracket.



8. DISCONNECT NO. 2 HEIGHT CONTROL TUBE



  1. Disconnect the No. 2 height control tube from the No. 2 height control valve (Click here).

    HINT:
    For the disconnection procedure of the tube (type 2), refer to Precaution of the Suspension Control System section.



9. REMOVE HEIGHT CONTROL COMPRESSOR ASSEMBLY



  1. Disconnect the connector.

  1. Detach the 3 clamps.

  1. Detach the No. 2 height control tube.




  1. Disconnect the connector.

  1. Detach the clamp.




  1. Remove the 3 bolts and height control compressor.



10. REMOVE HEIGHT CONTROL DRYER ASSEMBLY



  1. Disconnect the No. 2 height control tube (Click here).

    HINT:
    For the disconnection procedure of the tube (type 2), refer to Precaution of the Suspension Control System section.




  1. Remove the bolt and height control dryer from the height control compressor.

  1. Remove the O-ring.
