KINETIC DYNAMIC SUSPENSION SYSTEM > ON-VEHICLE INSPECTION

for Preparation Click here



1. INSPECT INDICATOR LIGHT

  1. Check that the KDSS indicator light comes on for 2 seconds.
    If the indicator check result is not normal, proceed to troubleshooting for the Problem Symptoms Table (Click here).



2. INSPECT FLUID PRESSURE

NOTICE:
  1. Perform the inspection on a level surface.
  2. Perform the inspection with the vehicle empty.
  3. Make sure that the wheels are on the ground and facing straight ahead.
  4. Perform the inspection with the vehicle load completely on the suspension.

  1. Turn the engine switch off.

  1. Connect the intelligent tester to the DLC3.

  1. Turn the engine switch on (IG).

  1. Turn the intelligent tester on.

  1. Enter the following menus: Chassis / KDSS / Data List.

  1. Select the item below in the Data List, and read its value displayed on the intelligent tester.

    KDSS
