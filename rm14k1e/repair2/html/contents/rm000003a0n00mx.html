OUTER REAR VIEW MIRROR > REASSEMBLY

for Preparation Click here


HINT:
  1. Use the same procedure for the RH and LH sides.
  2. The procedure listed below is for the LH side.



1. INSTALL SIDE TELEVISION CAMERA ASSEMBLY (w/ Side Monitor System)

  1. Install the side television camera with the 3 screws.

  1. Connect the connector.




  1. Attach the claw as shown in the illustration to install the part labeled A to the part labeled B.

    NOTICE:
    Be careful not to break the claw.



  1. Attach the part labeled C as shown in the illustration.



  1. Install the 5 screws.




  1. Using a T25 "TORX" socket wrench, install the 3 screws.

    HINT:
    1. Clean the threads of the screws with non-residue solvent.
    2. Apply adhesive to the threads of the screws.

      Adhesive:
      Toyota Genuine Adhesive 1324, Three Bond 1324 or equivalent

    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Adhesive




  1. Attach the 8 claws to install the lower cover.

  1. Attach the 4 claws to install the rubber base.

  1. Install the screw.




  1. Apply 2 new pieces of tape so that they overlap.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Tape



2. INSTALL SIDE TURN SIGNAL LIGHT ASSEMBLY LH

  1. Connect the connector.

  1. Install the light with the 3 screws.



3. INSTALL OUTER MIRROR COVER LH


  1. Install the outer mirror cover.




    1. Insert the rib on the outer edge of the cover into the groove of the mirror body.

      NOTICE:
      Do not scratch the turn light.

      Text in Illustration 
      *1
      		Rib




    2. Confirm that the cover and mirror body are aligned properly, and then push the outer end of the cover to attach the outer claws.

      NOTICE:
      1. Make sure that the upper ribs of the cover shown in part A of the illustration are properly attached to the mirror body.
      2. Make sure that the ribs of the mirror body shown in part B of the illustration are not protruding from the cover.

      Text in Illustration 
      *1
      		Rib




    3. While making sure that the rib of the cover fits properly into the groove of the mirror body, squeeze the inner end of the cover and the mirror body together to attach the inner claws.




    4. Push on the cover at the locations of the 8 claws to confirm that the claws are attached properly.



4. INSTALL OUTER MIRROR LH

  1. Connect the 2 connectors.




  1. Attach the 4 claws to install the outer mirror.
