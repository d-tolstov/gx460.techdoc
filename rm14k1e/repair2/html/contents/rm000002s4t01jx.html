WATER PUMP > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here



1. REMOVE WATER INLET SUB-ASSEMBLY WITH THERMOSTAT

  1. Remove the water inlet with thermostat (Click here).



2. REMOVE NO. 1 RADIATOR HOSE



  1. Remove the No. 1 radiator hose.



3. REMOVE FAN SHROUD



  1. Loosen the 4 nuts holding the fluid coupling.

  1. Remove the fan and generator V-ribbed belt (Click here).




  1. Remove the 2 bolts holding the fan shroud.

  1. Remove the 4 nuts of the fluid coupling, and then remove the fan shroud together with the fluid coupling.

    NOTICE:
    Be careful not to damage the radiator core.

  1. Remove the fan pulley from the water pump.



4. REMOVE NO. 1 WATER BY-PASS HOSE



  1. Remove the No. 1 water by-pass hose.



5. DISCONNECT NO. 8 WATER BY-PASS HOSE



  1. Disconnect the No. 8 water by-pass hose.



6. DISCONNECT NO. 5 WATER BY-PASS HOSE



  1. Disconnect the No. 5 water by-pass hose.



7. REMOVE WATER INLET HOUSING



  1. Remove the 3 bolts and water inlet housing.

  1. Remove the gasket from the water pump.



8. REMOVE WATER PUMP PULLEY



  1. Using SST, hold the water pump pulley.

    SST
    09960-10010   (09962-01000, 09963-01000)

  1. Remove the 4 bolts and water pump pulley.



9. REMOVE WATER PUMP ASSEMBLY



  1. Remove the 8 bolts, water pump and gasket.
