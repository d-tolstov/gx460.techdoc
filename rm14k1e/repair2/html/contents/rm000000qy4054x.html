    NEXT


    2.INSPECT BATTERY VOLTAGE

      Standard voltage:
      11 to 14 V

      1. If the voltage is below 11 V, recharge or replace the battery before proceeding.

      HINT:
      A simple method to determine whether the battery is depleted is to operate the horn.


    NEXT


    3.TURN ENGINE SWITCH ON (IG)

      HINT:
      If the engine switch cannot be turned on (IG), communication with the intelligent tester can be confirmed by connecting the intelligent tester to the vehicle and turning a courtesy light switch on and off at intervals of 1.5 seconds or less until communication between the intelligent tester and vehicle begins.


    NEXT
