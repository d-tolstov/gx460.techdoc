1. INSPECT WINDSHIELD WIPER MOTOR ASSEMBLY



  1. Operate the front wipers while spraying washer fluid on the windshield glass. Make sure that the front wipers function properly and the wipers do not come into contact with the vehicle body.

  1. Stop the front wiper motor and link assembly operation.

  1. Check the automatic stop position.

    OK:
    The front wipers stop at the positions shown in the illustration.

    Standard:
    Area
    		Standard Condition
    a
    		35.6 to 55.6 mm (1.40 to 2.19 in.)

    		18.9 to 38.9 mm (0.744 to 1.53 in.)

    If the result is not as specified, remove and reinstall the wiper arm and blade assembly, and then perform the on-vehicle inspection described above to inspect the wiper motor assembly again. If the result is not as specified again, inspect the wiper motor assembly with it removed from the vehicle body (Click here).
