1. INSPECT LOW PITCHED HORN ASSEMBLY



  1. Apply battery voltage and check the operation of the horn according to the table below.

    OK:
    Measurement Condition
    		Specified Condition
    Battery positive (+) → Terminal 1 Battery negative (-) → Body ground
    		Low pitched horn sounds

    If the result is not as specified, replace the low pitched horn assembly.

    		Terminal 1
