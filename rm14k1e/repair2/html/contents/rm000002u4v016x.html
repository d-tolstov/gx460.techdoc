LIN COMMUNICATION SYSTEM > Air Conditioning Control Panel does not Operate

for Preparation Click here


DESCRIPTION

When a switch of the heater control switch board* or No. 2 air conditioning control assembly is operated, the heater control switch board* or No. 2 air conditioning control assembly uses LIN communication to communicate with the air conditioning amplifier. If a switch of the heater control switch board* or No. 2 air conditioning control assembly does not operate properly, a problem with the LIN communication between the heater control switch board* or No. 2 air conditioning control assembly and air conditioning amplifier may be causing the malfunction.
  1. *: w/o Navigation System
