DTC    U1002    Lost Communication with Gateway Module (Power Management1)


DESCRIPTION

WIRING DIAGRAM

INSPECTION PROCEDURE

DISCONNECT CABLE FROM NEGATIVE BATTERY TERMINAL

CHECK FOR CAN BUS WIRE (MAIN WIRE FOR DISCONNECTION, BUS LINE FOR SHORT CIRCUIT)

CHECK FOR OPEN IN CAN BUS MAIN WIRE (NO. 3 JUNCTION CONNECTOR - NO. 1 JUNCTION CONNECTOR)

CHECK FOR OPEN IN CAN BUS MAIN WIRE (NO. 3 JUNCTION CONNECTOR - NO. 4 JUNCTION CONNECTOR)

CONNECT CONNECTOR

CHECK FOR OPEN IN CAN BUS MAIN WIRE (NO. 1 JUNCTION CONNECTOR - POWER MANAGEMENT CONTROL ECU)

CHECK FOR OPEN IN CAN BUS MAIN WIRE (NO. 1 JUNCTION CONNECTOR - NO. 3 JUNCTION CONNECTOR)

CONNECT CONNECTOR

CHECK FOR OPEN IN CAN BUS MAIN WIRE (POWER MANAGEMENT CONTROL ECU - NO. 1 JUNCTION CONNECTOR)

CONNECT CONNECTOR

CHECK FOR OPEN IN CAN BUS MAIN WIRE (NO. 4 JUNCTION CONNECTOR - NO. 5 JUNCTION CONNECTOR)

CHECK FOR OPEN IN CAN BUS MAIN WIRE (NO. 4 JUNCTION CONNECTOR - NO. 3 JUNCTION CONNECTOR)

CONNECT CONNECTOR

CHECK FOR OPEN IN CAN BUS MAIN WIRE (NO. 5 JUNCTION CONNECTOR - NO. 4 JUNCTION CONNECTOR)

CHECK FOR SHORT IN CAN BUS WIRES (NO. 4 JUNCTION CONNECTOR SIDE)

CHECK FOR SHORT IN CAN BUS WIRES (NO. 4 JUNCTION CONNECTOR - SUSPENSION CONTROL ECU)

CHECK FOR SHORT IN CAN BUS WIRES (NO. 4 JUNCTION CONNECTOR - HEADLIGHT SWIVEL ECU)

CHECK FOR SHORT IN CAN BUS WIRES (NO. 4 JUNCTION CONNECTOR - NO. 5 JUNCTION CONNECTOR)

CONNECT CONNECTOR

CHECK FOR SHORT IN CAN BUS WIRES (NO. 3 JUNCTION CONNECTOR - NO. 4 JUNCTION CONNECTOR)

CHECK FOR SHORT IN CAN BUS WIRES (NO. 3 JUNCTION CONNECTOR SIDE)

CHECK FOR SHORT IN CAN BUS WIRES (NO. 3 JUNCTION CONNECTOR - SEAT BELT ECU)

CHECK FOR SHORT IN CAN BUS WIRES (NO. 3 JUNCTION CONNECTOR - STABILIZER CONTROL ECU)

CONNECT CONNECTOR

CHECK FOR SHORT IN CAN BUS WIRES (NO. 1 JUNCTION CONNECTOR - NO. 3 JUNCTION CONNECTOR)

CHECK FOR SHORT IN CAN BUS WIRES (NO. 1 JUNCTION CONNECTOR - DRIVING SUPPORT ECU)

CHECK FOR SHORT IN CAN BUS WIRES (NO. 1 JUNCTION CONNECTOR - PARKING ASSIST ECU OR CLEARANCE WARNING ECU)

CHECK FOR SHORT IN CAN BUS WIRES (NO. 1 JUNCTION CONNECTOR - POWER MANAGEMENT CONTROL ECU)

CONNECT CONNECTOR

CHECK FOR SHORT IN CAN BUS WIRES (SUSPENSION CONTROL ECU)

CONNECT CONNECTOR

CHECK FOR SHORT IN CAN BUS WIRES (HEADLIGHT SWIVEL ECU)

CONNECT CONNECTOR

CHECK FOR SHORT IN CAN BUS WIRES (NO. 5 JUNCTION CONNECTOR)

CONNECT CONNECTOR

CHECK FOR SHORT IN CAN BUS WIRES (SEAT BELT CONTROL ECU)

CONNECT CONNECTOR

CHECK FOR SHORT IN CAN BUS WIRES (STABILIZER CONTROL ECU)

CONNECT CONNECTOR

CHECK FOR SHORT IN CAN BUS WIRES (DRIVING SUPPORT ECU)

CONNECT CONNECTOR

CHECK FOR SHORT IN CAN BUS WIRES (PARKING ASSIST ECU)

CONNECT CONNECTOR

CHECK FOR SHORT IN CAN BUS WIRES (CLEARANCE WARNING ECU)

CONNECT CONNECTOR

CHECK FOR SHORT IN CAN BUS WIRES (POWER MANAGEMENT CONTROL ECU)

CHECK HARNESS AND CONNECTOR (POWER MANAGEMENT CONTROL ECU - BATTERY AND BODY GROUND)
