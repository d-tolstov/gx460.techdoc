1. INSPECT HEATER CONTROL SWITCH BOARD (w/o Navigation System)



  1. Apply battery voltage to the air conditioning control assembly.
    Check that the front passenger side seat belt warning light comes on.

    OK:
    Measurement Condition
    		Specified Condition
    Battery positive (+) → Terminal 1 (IG+)
    Battery negative (-) → Terminal 7 (LAPL)
    		Front passenger side seat belt warning light comes on

    If the result is not as specified, replace the heater control switch board.
