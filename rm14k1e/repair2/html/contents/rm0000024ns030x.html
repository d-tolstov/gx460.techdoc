BACK DOOR GLASS > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here



1. REMOVE REAR NO. 1 SPOILER COVER



  1. Detach the fastening tape and 5 clips and remove the rear No. 1 spoiler cover.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Fastening Tape



2. REMOVE REAR SPOILER COVER RH



  1. Put protective tape around the rear spoiler cover.

  1. Using a screwdriver, detach the 2 clips and remove the rear spoiler cover as shown in the illustration.

    HINT:
    Tape the screwdriver tip before use.

    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape



3. REMOVE REAR SPOILER SUB-ASSEMBLY



  1. Disconnect the connector.




  1. Remove the 6 bolts.




  1. Detach the 3 clips and rear spoiler.



4. REMOVE REAR WIPER ARM



  1. Remove the nut and rear wiper arm.



5. REMOVE REAR WIPER MOTOR ASSEMBLY



  1. Disconnect the connector.




  1. Remove the 3 bolts.

  1. Detach the 2 guides and remove the rear wiper motor.



6. REMOVE BACK WINDOW STAY ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Using a screwdriver, remove the 2 stop rings as shown in the illustration, and then remove the back window stay.

    CAUTION:
    Remove the back window stay while supporting the back door with one hand.

    HINT:
    Tape the screwdriver tip before use.

    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape



7. REMOVE BACK WINDOW STAY ASSEMBLY RH

HINT:
Use the same procedure described for the LH side.



8. REMOVE BACK DOOR GLASS



  1. Disconnect the connector and detach the 2 clamps.




  1. Remove the 4 bolts and back window glass.



9. REMOVE BACK DOOR LOCK STRIKER COVER



  1. Using a moulding remover, detach the 3 claws and remove the lock striker cover.



10. REMOVE BACK WINDOW LOCK STRIKER ASSEMBLY



  1. Remove the 2 nuts and lock striker.



11. REMOVE BACK WINDOW GLASS SPACER



HINT:
Perform the following procedure if replacing the back window glass spacer.

  1. Remove the back window glass spacer.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		No. 2 Back Window Glass Spacer
    *2
    		No. 1 Back Window Glass Spacer



12. REMOVE BACK DOOR HINGE ASSEMBLY LH



HINT:
Perform the following procedure if replacing the back door hinge assembly LH.

  1. Using a T30 "TORX" wrench, remove the 2 screws and back door hinge.



13. REMOVE BACK DOOR HINGE ASSEMBLY RH

HINT:
Use the same procedure described for the LH side.
