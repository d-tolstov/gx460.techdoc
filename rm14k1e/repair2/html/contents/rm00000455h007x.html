FRONT LOWER SUSPENSION ARM > ON-VEHICLE INSPECTION

for Preparation Click here



1. REMOVE FRONT WHEEL


2. INSPECT FRONT SUSPENSION LOWER NO. 1 ARM

  1. Install the hub nuts to the disc.




  1. Using a dial indicator, check the lower ball joint for excessive play by pushing the hub nuts up and down with a force of 294 N (30 kgf, 66 lbf).

    Maximum play:
    0.5 mm (0.02 in.)

    If the play is more than the maximum, replace the lower arm.



3. INSTALL FRONT WHEEL

Torque:
112 N*m{ 1142 kgf*cm , 83 ft.*lbf }
