DTC    B1850/62    Short in Rear Side Squib RH Circuit

DTC    B1851/62    Open in Rear Side Squib RH Circuit

DTC    B1852/62    Short to GND in Rear Side Squib RH Circuit

DTC    B1853/62    Short to B+ in Rear Side Squib RH Circuit

for Preparation Click here


DESCRIPTION

The rear side squib RH circuit consists of the center airbag sensor and the rear seat side airbag RH.
This circuit instructs the SRS to deploy when deployment conditions are met.
These DTCs are stored when a malfunction is detected in the rear side squib RH circuit.

DTC Code
DTC Detection Condition
Trouble Area
B1850/62
One of the following conditions is met:
  1. The center airbag sensor detects a line short circuit signal 5 times in the rear side squib RH circuit during the primary check.
  2. A rear side squib RH malfunction.
  3. A center airbag sensor malfunction.
  1. Floor wire
  2. Rear seat side airbag assembly RH (Rear side squib RH)
  3. Center airbag sensor assembly
B1851/62
One of the following conditions is met:
  1. The center airbag sensor detects an open circuit signal in the rear side squib RH circuit for 2 seconds.
  2. A rear side squib RH malfunction.
  3. A center airbag sensor malfunction.
  1. Floor wire
  2. Rear seat side airbag assembly RH (Rear side squib RH)
  3. Center airbag sensor assembly
B1852/62
One of the following conditions is met:
  1. The center airbag sensor detects a short circuit to ground signal in the rear side squib RH circuit for 0.5 seconds.
  2. A rear side squib RH malfunction.
  3. A center airbag sensor malfunction.
  1. Floor wire
  2. Rear seat side airbag assembly RH (Rear side squib RH)
  3. Center airbag sensor assembly
B1853/62
One of the following conditions is met:
  1. The center airbag sensor detects a short circuit to B+ signal in the rear side squib RH circuit for 0.5 seconds.
  2. A rear side squib RH malfunction.
  3. A center airbag sensor malfunction.
  1. Floor wire
  2. Rear seat side airbag assembly RH (Rear side squib RH)
  3. Center airbag sensor assembly

1.CHECK REAR SEAT SIDE AIRBAG RH (REAR SIDE SQUIB RH)

  1. Turn the engine switch off.

  1. Disconnect the cable from the negative (-) battery terminal, and wait for at least 90 seconds.

  1. Disconnect the connector from the rear seat side airbag RH.

  1. Connect the red wire side of SST (resistance: 2.1 Î©) to connector C.

    CAUTION:
    Never connect the tester to the rear seat side airbag RH (rear side squib RH) for measurement, as this may lead to a serious injury due to airbag deployment.

    NOTICE:
    1. Do not forcibly insert SST into the terminals of the connector when connecting SST.
    2. Insert SST straight into the terminals of the connector.
