DTC    B2412    Headlight Swivel Motor LH Malfunction

DTC    B2413    Headlight Swivel Motor RH Malfunction

for Preparation Click here


DESCRIPTION

Each swivel motor receives a signal from the headlight swivel ECU to operate and sends a signal indicating the direction of the headlight and condition of the headlight swivel motor to the headlight swivel ECU.

DTC Code
DTC Detection Condition
Trouble Area
B2412
  1. Malfunction in the headlight swivel motor LH.
  2. Open or short in the headlight swivel motor LH circuit.
  1. Headlight unit LH (swivel motor)
  2. Headlight swivel ECU assembly
  3. Harness or connector
B2413
  1. Malfunction in the headlight swivel motor RH.
  2. Open or short in the headlight swivel motor RH circuit.
  1. Headlight unit RH (swivel motor)
  2. Headlight swivel ECU assembly
  3. Harness or connector
