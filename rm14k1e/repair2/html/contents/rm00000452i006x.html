DTC    C1453    Reservoir Level Switch Disconnected

DTC    C1454    Reservoir Level Low

for Preparation Click here


DESCRIPTION

The brake fluid level warning switch sends the appropriate signal to the skid control ECU when the brake fluid level drops.

DTC Code
DTC Detection Condition
Trouble Area
C1453
With the ECU terminal IG1 voltage at 10 to 14 V, an open in the brake fluid level warning switch circuit continues for 2 seconds or more.
  1. Brake fluid level
  2. Brake fluid level warning switch (Brake master cylinder reservoir assembly)
  3. Harness or connector
  4. Master cylinder solenoid (Skid control ECU)
C1454
The fluid level of the reservoir is below the LOW level for 40 seconds after the engine switch is turned on (IG), or for 7 seconds during pump motor operation.

INSPECTION PROCEDURE

NOTICE:
When replacing the master cylinder solenoid, perform calibration (Click here).

1.CHECK BRAKE FLUID LEVEL

  1. Turn the engine switch off.

  1. Depress the brake pedal 40 times or more (until the pedal reaction feels light and pedal stroke becomes longer).

  1. Check the amount of fluid in the brake reservoir.

    HINT:
    When the engine switch is turned on (IG), brake fluid is sent to the accumulator and the fluid level decreases by approximately 5 mm (0.197 in.) from the level when the engine switch is off (normal).

    OK:
    Brake fluid level is normal.
