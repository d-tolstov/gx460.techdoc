Meter/Gauge 
Item
Details
Speedometer
Based on a signal received from the wheel speed sensors, the skid control ECU calculates the vehicle speed and transmits data to the combination meter (CAN).
Tachometer
The combination meter receives an engine speed signal from the ECM and the gauge needle operates (CAN).
Engine coolant temperature receiver gauge
The combination meter receives an engine coolant temperature signal from the ECM and the gauge needle operates (CAN).
Fuel receiver gauge
The combination meter receives fuel amount information measured by the fuel sender gauge and the gauge needle operates (Direct line).

Warning/Indicator 
Item
Details
Turn signal indicator light (LH/RH)

Receives a turn signal from the flasher relay (Direct line).
CHARGE warning light

Receives malfunction signals from the generator (Direct line).
DOOR warning light

The open door indicator comes on when a signal is received from the main body ECU (CAN).
Multi-terrain select indicator light*1

Receives multi-terrain select signals from the driving support switch control ECU (CAN).
CRAWL indicator light*2

Receives CRAWL signals from the skid control ECU (CAN).
Kinetic dynamic suspension system indicator light

Receives kinetic dynamic suspension system signals from the stabilizer control ECU (CAN).
2nd START indicator light

Receives a 2nd START switch on signal from the ECM (CAN).
PCS indicator light*3

Receives a PCS indicator light signal from the seat belt control ECU (CAN).
ABS warning light

Receives malfunction signals from the skid control ECU (CAN).
Brake warning light

  1. Comes on when the parking brake switch is on (CAN).
  2. Comes on when the brake fluid level warning switch is on (CAN).
  3. Comes on when the vehicle stability control system is malfunctioning.
MIL

Receives malfunction signals from the ECM (Direct line).
VSC OFF indicator light

Receives VSC OFF signals from the skid control ECU (CAN).
SLIP indicator light

Receives SLIP indicator light signals from the skid control ECU (CAN).
Downhill assist control indicator light*4

Receives downhill assist control signals from the skid control ECU (CAN).
FRONT FOG indicator light

The indicator turns on when a FRONT FOG illumination command signal is received (CAN).
REAR FOG indicator light*5

The indicator turns on when a REAR FOG illumination command signal is received (CAN).
TAIL indicator light

The indicator turns on when a taillight illumination signal is received (CAN).
BEAM indicator light

Receives BEAM indicator light signals from the main body ECU (CAN).
Driver side seat belt warning light

Receives driver seat belt signals (unfastened) from the main body ECU (CAN).
Master warning light

Receives warning signals from sensors and ECUs (CAN).
SRS warning light

Receives malfunction signals from the center airbag sensor (CAN).
Cruise set indicator

Receives a cruise control switch signal from the ECM (CAN).
Eco driving indicator light

Receives an ecology lamp indicator signal from the ECM (CAN).
Cruise indicator

Receives a cruise control switch signal from the ECM (CAN).
Radar cruise indicator light*6

Receives radar cruise indicator signals from the driving support ECU (CAN).
Clearance sonar indicator light*7, *8

Receives clearance sonar indicator light signals from the parking assist ECU*7 or clearance warning ECU*8 (CAN).
AFS OFF indicator light

Receives AFS OFF indicator signals from the headlight swivel ECU (CAN).
FUEL warning light

Receives signals from the fuel sender gauge indicating low or no fuel (Direct line).
4LO indicator light

Receives 4LO indicator light signals from the four wheel drive control ECU (CAN).
Center differential lock indicator light

Receives center differential lock signals from the four wheel drive control ECU.
  1. *1: w/ Multi-terrain Select
  2. *2: w/ CRAWL Control
  3. *3: w/ Pre-crash Safety System
  4. *4: w/ Downhill Assist Control
  5. *5: w/ Rear Fog Light
  6. *6: w/ Dynamic Radar Cruise Control System
  7. *7: w/ Parking Assist Monitor System and/or Side Monitor System
  8. *8: w/o Parking Assist Monitor System and/or Side Monitor System
