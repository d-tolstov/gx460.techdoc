CENTER DIFFERENTIAL LOCK SWITCH > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here



1. REMOVE INSTRUMENT PANEL FINISH PANEL END LH



  1. Detach the 11 clips and remove the instrument panel finish panel end.



2. REMOVE NO. 1 INSTRUMENT PANEL FINISH CUSHION



  1. Put protective tape around the No. 1 instrument panel finish cushion.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape

  1. Using a moulding remover, detach the 4 clips.

  1. Disconnect the connector and remove the No. 1 instrument panel finish cushion.



3. REMOVE LOWER INSTRUMENT PANEL FINISH PANEL ASSEMBLY



  1. Detach the 4 clips.

  1. Disconnect each connector and cooler thermistor and remove the instrument panel finish panel.



4. REMOVE CENTER DIFFERENTIAL LOCK SWITCH



  1. Detach the 2 claws and remove the center differential lock switch.
