FRONT STABILIZER BAR > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here



1. REMOVE FRONT WHEEL


2. REMOVE SIDE STEP ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Disconnect the connector and detach the clamp.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Clamp

  1. Remove the 6 bolts.




  1. Put protective tape around the side step.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape

  1. Using a T30 "TORX" socket wrench, remove the 2 screws.

  1. Remove the 3 screws.

  1. Detach the 6 clips and remove the side step.



3. REMOVE STABILIZER CONTROL VALVE PROTECTOR



  1. Remove the 2 bolts and stabilizer control valve protector.



4. DRAIN SUSPENSION FLUID



  1. Loosen the bleeder plug on the stabilizer control with accumulator housing assembly and drain suspension fluid.

    HINT:
    1. Drain suspension fluid when performing operations related to the hydraulic circuits.
    2. Draining suspension fluid decreases suspension fluid pressure.

  1. Tighten the bleeder plug.

    Torque:
    8.3 N*m{ 85 kgf*cm , 73 in.*lbf }



5. REMOVE LOWER FRONT BUMPER COVER

  1. Remove the clip, 5 bolts and lower front bumper cover.



6. REMOVE NO. 1 ENGINE UNDER COVER SUB-ASSEMBLY

  1. Remove the 4 bolts.




  1. Unhook the No. 1 engine under cover from the vehicle body as shown in the illustration.



7. REMOVE FRONT SUSPENSION MEMBER BRACE SUB-ASSEMBLY



  1. Remove the 6 bolts and 2 member braces from the front frame assembly.



8. REMOVE FRONT STABILIZER END BRACKET



  1. Remove the 4 bolts and 2 brackets from the lower arm.



9. REMOVE FRONT STABILIZER BAR



  1. Remove the 4 bolts, 2 front stabilizer lower brackets and front stabilizer bar.

  1. Remove the 2 front stabilizer link bushes and front stabilizer lower bracket bushes from the stabilizer bar front.



10. REMOVE FRONT FENDER APRON SEAL LH



  1. Remove the 7 clips and front apron seal LH.



11. REMOVE FRAME APRON SEAL BRACKET



  1. Remove the bolt and bracket.



12. DISCONNECT NO. 1 FRONT STABILIZER CONTROL TUBE ASSEMBLY



  1. Using a union nut wrench, disconnect the front stabilizer control tubes.

  1. Remove the bolt.



13. REMOVE FRONT STABILIZER WITH TUBE CYLINDER ASSEMBLY



  1. Remove the bolt, nut and front stabilizer with tube cylinder assembly.

    NOTICE:
    1. Turn the bolt while holding the nut.


    2. Do not loosen or remove the flare nuts labeled A and B in the illustration.
    3. Do not hold the front stabilizer with tube cylinder assembly by the cylinder boot.

  1. Remove the 2 bleeder plug caps and bleeder plugs from the front stabilizer with tube cylinder assembly.



14. REMOVE FRONT STABILIZER LINK ASSEMBLY RH



  1. Remove the bolt, spacer and front stabilizer link assembly.
