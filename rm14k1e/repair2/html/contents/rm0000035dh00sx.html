DTC    C1626    Steering Angle Sensor Failure

for Preparation Click here


DESCRIPTION

This DTC is stored if the parking assist ECU receives a signal via CAN communication from the spiral with sensor cable sub-assembly that indicates an internal malfunction.

DTC Code
DTC Detection Condition
Trouble Area
C1626
A fail flag is transmitted from the steering angle sensor.
  1. Spiral with sensor cable sub-assembly (Steering angle sensor)
  2. Parking assist ECU

INSPECTION PROCEDURE

NOTICE:
  1. When "System initializing" is displayed on the display and navigation module display after the battery terminal disconnected, correct the steering angle neutral point (Click here).
  2. Depending on the parts that are replaced or operations that are performed during vehicle inspection or maintenance, calibration of other systems as well as the parking assist monitor system may be needed (Click here).
