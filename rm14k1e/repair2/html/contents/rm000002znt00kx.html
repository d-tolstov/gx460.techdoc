BACK WINDOW STAY > DISPOSAL

for Preparation Click here


HINT:
  1. Use the same procedure for the RH and LH sides.
  2. The procedure listed below is for the LH side.



1. DISPOSE OF BACK WINDOW STAY ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Horizontally secure the back window stay in a vise with the piston-rod pulled out.

  1. Wear safety glasses. Gradually cut an area between A and B shown in the illustration using a metal saw and gradually release the gas.

    Standard:
    Area
    		Specified Condition
    a
    		80 mm (3.15 in.)
    b
    		20 mm (0.787 in.)

    CAUTION:
    As metal debris may be blown outward by the gas, you must:
    1. Wear protective glasses.
    2. Cover the area being cut.

    NOTICE:
    The gas inside the back window stay is colorless, odorless and harmless. As there is a possibility that metal debris could scatter, cover the back window stay with a piece of cloth or other material.
