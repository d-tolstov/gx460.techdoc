OIL AND OIL FILTER > REPLACEMENT

for Preparation Click here


CAUTION:
  1. Prolonged and repeated contact with engine oil will result in the removal of natural oils from the skin, leading to dryness, irritation and dermatitis. In addition, used engine oil contains potentially harmful contaminants which may cause skin cancer.
  2. Precautions should be taken when replacing engine oil to minimize the risk of your skin making contact with used engine oil. Protective clothing and gloves that cannot be penetrated by oil should be worn. The skin should be washed with soap and water, or use waterless hand cleaner, to remove any used engine oil thoroughly. Do not use gasoline, thinners or solvents.
  3. In order to protect the environment, used oil and used oil filters must be disposed of at designated disposal sites.



1. DRAIN ENGINE OIL

  1. Remove the 2 bolts and engine under cover seal.

  1. Remove the oil filler cap.

  1. Remove the oil pan drain plug and gasket, and then drain the engine oil into a container.

  1. Install a new gasket and the oil pan drain plug.

    Torque:
    40 N*m{ 408 kgf*cm , 30 ft.*lbf }



2. REMOVE LOWER FRONT BUMPER COVER

  1. Remove the clip, 5 bolts and lower front bumper cover.



3. REMOVE NO. 1 ENGINE UNDER COVER SUB-ASSEMBLY

  1. Remove the 4 bolts.




  1. Unhook the No. 1 engine under cover from the vehicle body as shown in the illustration.



4. REMOVE OIL FILTER ELEMENT



  1. Using SST, loosen the oil filter cap 4 revolutions, align the cap ribs vertically and drain the remaining engine oil in the oil filter cap.

    SST
    09228-06501  
    Text in Illustration

    HINT:
    Use a container to catch the draining oil. After the oil filter cap is loosened approximately 4 turns and the cap ribs are vertical, the engine oil drains from the gap between the oil filter cap and oil pan.

    NOTICE:
    Do not remove the oil filter bracket clip when removing the oil filter cap.

  1. Remove the oil filter cap.




  1. Remove the oil filter element and O-ring from the oil filter cap.
    Text in Illustration 

    		O-Ring

    		Oil Filter Element

    NOTICE:
    Be sure to remove the O-ring (for the cap) by hand, without using any tools, to prevent damage to the groove for the O-ring on the cap.



5. INSTALL OIL FILTER ELEMENT

  1. Clean the inside of the oil filter cap, its threads and O-ring groove.

  1. Apply a small amount of engine oil to a new O-ring and install the O-ring to the oil filter cap.

  1. Set a new oil filter element in the oil filter cap.

  1. Remove any dirt or foreign matter from the installation surface and inside of the engine.




  1. Reapply a small amount of engine oil to the O-ring of the oil filter cap. Align the oil filter cap with the oil filter bracket so that the cutout portion of the cap threads is in a position 90° from the groove in the bracket, and then temporarily install the cap.
    Text in Illustration 
    *a
    		Cutout Portion
    *b
    		Groove

    NOTICE:
    Make sure that the O-ring does not get caught between the parts.




  1. Using SST, tighten the oil filter cap.

    SST
    09228-06501  

    Torque:
    25 N*m{ 255 kgf*cm , 18 ft.*lbf }

    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Oil Filter Bracket Clip
    *a
    		No Clearance

    NOTICE:
    1. After tightening the oil filter cap, check for gaps between the installation surfaces.
    2. Do not remove the oil filter bracket clip when installing the oil filter cap.
    3. Do not cross thread the oil filter cap.



6. ADD ENGINE OIL

  1. Add fresh engine oil.

  1. Install the oil filler cap.

  1. Install the engine under cover seal with the 2 bolts.

    Torque:
    29 N*m{ 296 kgf*cm , 21 ft.*lbf }



7. INSPECT FOR OIL LEAK

  1. Start the engine. Make sure that there are no oil leaks from the area that was worked on.



8. CHECK ENGINE OIL LEVEL

  1. Warm up the engine. Then stop the engine and wait for 5 minutes.

  1. Check that the engine oil level is between the low level and full level marks on the dipstick.
    If low, check for leakage and add oil up to the full level mark.

    NOTICE:
    Do not fill engine oil above the full level mark.



9. INSTALL NO. 1 ENGINE UNDER COVER SUB-ASSEMBLY



  1. Hook the No. 1 engine under cover to the vehicle body as shown in the illustration.

  1. Install the 4 bolts.

    Torque:
    29 N*m{ 296 kgf*cm , 21 ft.*lbf }



10. INSTALL LOWER FRONT BUMPER COVER

  1. Install the lower front bumper cover with the 5 bolts and clip.

    Torque:
    8.0 N*m{ 82 kgf*cm , 71 in.*lbf }
