FRONT SHOCK ABSORBER > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here


HINT:
  1. Use the same procedure for the RH and LH sides.
  2. The procedure listed below is for the LH side.



1. REMOVE FRONT WHEEL


2. REMOVE LOWER FRONT BUMPER COVER

  1. Remove the clip, 5 bolts and lower front bumper cover.



3. REMOVE NO. 1 ENGINE UNDER COVER SUB-ASSEMBLY

  1. Remove the 4 bolts.




  1. Unhook the No. 1 engine under cover from the vehicle body as shown in the illustration.



4. REMOVE FRONT SUSPENSION MEMBER BRACE SUB-ASSEMBLY



  1. Remove the 6 bolts and 2 member braces from the front frame assembly.



5. REMOVE FRONT STABILIZER END BRACKET



  1. Remove the 4 bolts and 2 brackets from the lower arm.



6. REMOVE FRONT STABILIZER BAR



  1. Remove the 4 bolts, 2 front stabilizer lower brackets and front stabilizer bar.

  1. Remove the 2 front stabilizer link bushes and front stabilizer lower bracket bushes from the stabilizer bar front.



7. REMOVE FRONT SHOCK ABSORBER WITH COIL SPRING



  1. Detach the 3 clamps and disconnect the connector.

  1. Using a 4 mm hexagon wrench, remove the 2 bolts and absorber control actuator.




  1. Using SST, remove the nut and bracket.

    SST
    09961-00950  




  1. Remove the bolt, nut and washer.




  1. Remove the 3 nuts on the top of the front shock absorber with coil spring.

  1. Remove the front shock absorber with coil spring.
