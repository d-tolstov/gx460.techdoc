DTC    B2287    LIN Communication Master Malfunction

DTC    B278C    Lost Communication with Power Source Control

for Preparation Click here


DESCRIPTION

  1. DTC B2287 is stored when there is an open or short circuit or an ECU communication malfunction between the power management control ECU and certification ECU.
  2. DTC B278C is stored when LIN communication between the certification ECU and power management control ECU stops for 10 seconds or more.

DTC Code
DTC Detection Condition
Trouble Area
B2287
There is an open or short circuit or an ECU communication malfunction between the power management control ECU and certification ECU.
  1. Certification ECU
  2. Power management control ECU
  3. Harness or connector
B278C
No communication between the certification ECU and power management control ECU for 10 seconds or more.
  1. Certification ECU
  2. Power management control ECU
  3. Harness or connector

INSPECTION PROCEDURE

NOTICE:
  1. When using the intelligent tester with the engine switch off to troubleshoot:
    Connect the intelligent tester to the vehicle and turn a courtesy light switch on and off at 1.5 second intervals until communication between the intelligent tester and vehicle begins.
  2. Inspect the fuses for circuits related to this system before performing the following inspection procedure.
  3. Before replacing the certification ECU, refer to the Service Bulletin.

HINT:
When DTC B2287, B278C and B2785 are output simultaneously, perform troubleshooting for DTC B2785 first (Click here).
