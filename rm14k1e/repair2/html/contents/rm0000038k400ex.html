1. INSTALL REAR BUMPER PAD LH (w/ No. 2 Energy Absorber)

HINT:
When installing the rear bumper pad, heat the rear bumper cover surface using a heat light.

Standard:
Item
Temperature
Rear Bumper Cover
20 to 30°C (68 to 86°F)

NOTICE:
Do not heat the rear bumper cover excessively.

  1. When using a new rear bumper cover:

    1. Clean the rear bumper cover surface.

    2. Using a heat light, heat the rear bumper cover surface.

  1. When reusing the rear bumper cover:

    1. Using a heat light, heat the rear bumper cover surface.

    2. Remove the double-sided tape from the rear bumper cover.

    3. Wipe off any tape adhesive residue with cleaner.




  1. Install a new rear bumper pad.

    1. Remove the peeling paper from the face of a new rear bumper pad.

      HINT:
      After removing the peeling paper, keep the exposed adhesive free from foreign matter.

    2. Align the rear bumper pad with the mark-off line of the rear bumper.

    3. Install the rear bumper pad.



2. INSTALL REAR BUMPER PAD RH (w/ No. 2 Energy Absorber)

HINT:
Use the same procedure described for the LH side.
