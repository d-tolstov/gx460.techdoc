INNER REAR VIEW MIRROR > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here



1. REMOVE INNER REAR VIEW MIRROR STAY HOLDER COVER



  1. Detach the 2 claws and slide the inner rear view mirror stay holder cover as shown in the illustration.




  1. Detach the 2 claws and remove the inner rear view mirror stay holder cover.



2. REMOVE INNER REAR VIEW MIRROR ASSEMBLY



  1. Disconnect the connector.

  1. Using a T20 "TORX" socket wrench, remove the screw and inner rear view mirror.
