1. INSPECT AIR SWITCHING VALVE ASSEMBLY



  1. Measure the resistance according to the value(s) in the table below.

    Standard Resistance:
    Tester Connection
    		Condition
    		Specified Condition
    1 - 2
    		20°C (68°F)
    		3.0 to 7.4 Ω
    1 - Body ground
    		Always
    		1 MΩ or higher
    2 - Body ground

    Text in Illustration 
    *a
    		Component without harness connected
    (Air Switching Valve)

    NOTICE:
    When measuring the resistance, do not touch terminals 3, 4 or 5.

    HINT:
    When measuring the resistance, make sure that the surface temperature of the air switching valve assembly is 20°C (68°F).

    If the result is not as specified, replace the air switching valve assembly.




  1. Check the operation of the air switching valve.

    1. Check that air does not flow from port A to port B.
      Text in Illustration 
      *1
      		Air
      *2
      		Port A
      *3
      		Port B

      NOTICE:
      Make sure the applied pressure is 30 kPa (0.3 kgf/cm2, 4.4 psi) or less.

      If the result is not as specified, replace the air switching valve assembly.




    2. Apply battery voltage across terminals 1 and 2.

      NOTICE:
      When applying voltage, do not touch terminals 3, 4 or 5.

    3. Check that air flows from port A to port B.

      		Component without harness connected
