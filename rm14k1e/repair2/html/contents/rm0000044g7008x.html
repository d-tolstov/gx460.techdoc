DTC    B15E7    Harddisk Reading/Writing Malfunction(Low Temperature)

DTC    B15E8    Harddisk Reading/Writing Malfunction(High Temperature)

for Preparation Click here


DESCRIPTION

These DTCs are stored when the temperature is higher than or below the operation range of the hard disk drive built into the display and navigation module display, and hard disk drive read/write errors occur more than a specified number of times.

HINT:
The hard disk drive may not operate normally when the temperature is -20°C (-4°F) or less, or 65°C (149°F) or higher.


DTC Code
DTC Detection Condition
Trouble Area
B15E7
A hard disk drive read/write error at low temperature.
Hard disk drive
B15E8
A hard disk drive read/write error at high temperature.

INSPECTION PROCEDURE

1.CHECK CABIN

  1. Move the vehicle to an area that is at room temperature and allow the cabin temperature to adjust to between -20°C (-4°F) and 65°C (149°F).

    HINT:
    1. The hard disk drive may not operate normally at very high or very low temperatures.
    2. Perform the DTC check at room temperature.


NEXT


2.CHECK FOR DTC

  1. Clear the DTCs (Click here).

  1. Check for DTCs and check if the same trouble occurs again.

    OK:
    No DTCs are output.
