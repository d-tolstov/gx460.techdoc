FRONT LOWER SUSPENSION ARM > DISASSEMBLY

for Preparation Click here



1. REMOVE FRONT NO. 1 LOWER ARM BUSH LH



  1. Using a chisel and hammer, pry the flange of the bush outward.




  1. Using SST and a press, press out the bush.

    SST
    09632-36010  
    09950-00020  
    09950-60010   (09951-00400)



2. REMOVE FRONT NO. 2 LOWER ARM BUSH LH



  1. Using a chisel and hammer, pry the flange of the bush outward.




  1. Using SST and a press, press out the bush.

    SST
    09632-36010  
    09950-00020  
    09950-60010   (09951-00400)



3. REMOVE LOWER BALL JOINT DUST COVER LH



  1. Using a snap ring expander, remove the dust cover set ring and dust cover from the lower arm.

    NOTICE:
    1. Do not damage the ball joint dust cover.
    2. If the ball joint dust cover is damaged, replace it.
