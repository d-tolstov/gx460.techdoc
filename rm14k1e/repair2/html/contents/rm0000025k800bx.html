TRANSFER CASE FRONT OIL SEAL > REPLACEMENT

for Preparation Click here



1. DRAIN TRANSFER OIL



  1. Remove the filler plug and gasket.

  1. Remove the drain plug and gasket, and drain the transfer oil.

  1. Install a new gasket and the drain plug.

    Torque:
    37 N*m{ 377 kgf*cm , 27 ft.*lbf }



2. REMOVE FRONT PROPELLER SHAFT ASSEMBLY

  1. Remove the front propeller shaft (Click here).



3. REMOVE FRONT OUTPUT SHAFT COMPANION FLANGE SUB-ASSEMBLY

  1. Using a chisel and hammer, loosen the staked part of the lock nut.




  1. Using SST to hold the companion flange, remove the lock nut.

    SST
    09330-00021  




  1. Using SST, remove the companion flange.

    SST
    09950-40011   (09951-04020, 09952-04010, 09953-04030, 09954-04010, 09955-04051, 09957-04010, 09958-04011)



4. REMOVE FRONT TRANSFER OUTPUT SHAFT COMPANION FLANGE OIL SEAL

  1. Using a screwdriver and hammer, tap out the oil seal from the companion flange.

    NOTICE:
    Be careful not to damage the oil seal and companion flange contact surfaces.



5. REMOVE TRANSFER CASE FRONT OIL SEAL



  1. Using SST, tap out the oil seal.

    SST
    09308-00010  

    NOTICE:
    Be careful not to damage the oil seal and front case contact surface.



6. INSTALL TRANSFER CASE FRONT OIL SEAL



  1. Coat the lip of a new oil seal with MP grease.

  1. Using SST and a hammer, tap in the oil seal until its metal ring contacts the case.

    SST
    09649-17010  
    09950-70010   (09951-07100)



7. INSTALL FRONT TRANSFER OUTPUT SHAFT COMPANION FLANGE OIL SEAL



  1. Using SST and a hammer, tap in a new oil seal.

    SST
    09950-60010   (09951-00320)
    09950-70010   (09951-07100)

  1. Coat the lip of the oil seal with MP grease.



8. INSTALL FRONT OUTPUT SHAFT COMPANION FLANGE SUB-ASSEMBLY



  1. Apply gear oil to the connecting areas of the companion flange and driven sprocket.

  1. Install the companion flange to the driven sprocket.
    Text in Illustration 

    		Front

  1. Using SST to hold the companion flange, install a new lock nut.

    SST
    09330-00021  

    Torque:
    118 N*m{ 1203 kgf*cm , 87 ft.*lbf }




  1. Using a chisel and hammer, stake the lock nut.
    Text in Illustration 
    *a
    		CORRECT
    *b
    		INCORRECT

    NOTICE:
    1. Securely stake the shaft to the lock nut groove.
    2. Be careful not to damage the parts around the lock nut.
    3. Do not apply excessive force to the shaft.



9. INSTALL FRONT PROPELLER SHAFT ASSEMBLY

  1. Install the front propeller shaft (Click here).



10. ADD TRANSFER OIL

  1. Remove the filler plug and gasket.

  1. Add oil so that the oil level is between 0 to 5.0 mm (0 to 0.196 in.) from the bottom lip of the filler plug hole.

    NOTICE:
    1. Add oil slowly.
    2. Add oil a little at a time, waiting several minutes between each addition of oil.

  1. Wait approximately 5 minutes and check that the oil level has not changed.

  1. Install a new gasket to the filler plug and then tighten the plug.

    Torque:
    37 N*m{ 377 kgf*cm , 27 ft.*lbf }



11. CHECK FOR TRANSFER OIL LEAK
