DTC    C168D    Vehicle Information Unmatched

for Preparation Click here


DESCRIPTION

This DTC is stored if the parking assist ECU judges as a result of its self check that the vehicle information received from the power management control ECU via CAN communication and the vehicle information stored in the parking assist ECU do not match.

DTC Code
DTC Detection Condition
Trouble Area
C168D
Vehicle Information Unmatched
  1. Back camera position setting
  2. Steering angle setting
  3. Side camera position setting*
  4. Parking assist ECU
*: w/ Side Monitor System

INSPECTION PROCEDURE

NOTICE:
  1. When "System initializing" is displayed on the display and navigation module display after the battery terminal disconnected, correct the steering angle neutral point (Click here).
  2. Depending on the parts that are replaced or operations that are performed during vehicle inspection or maintenance, calibration of other systems as well as the parking assist monitor system may be needed (Click here).

1.PERFORM SETTINGS

  1. Perform the following to register the vehicle information into the parking assist ECU.

    1. Refer to Back Camera Position Setting (Click here).

    2. Refer to Steering Angle Setting (Diagnosis system) (Click here).

    3. Refer to Side Camera Position Setting (Click here).
