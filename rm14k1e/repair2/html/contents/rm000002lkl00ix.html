1. INSPECT HEIGHT CONTROL COMPRESSOR (EXHAUST VALVE)



  1. Measure the resistance of the exhaust valve.

    1. Measure the resistance according to the value(s) in the table below.

      Standard Resistance:
      Tester Connection
      		Condition

  1. Check the exhaust valve operation.

    1. Connect a positive (+) lead from the battery to terminal 2 (L), and a negative (-) lead to terminal 1 (B).

    2. Check the operating sound of the height control valve.

      OK:
      It makes an operating sound.

      Text in Illustration 
      *a
      		Component without harness connected
      (Exhaust Valve)
      If the result is not as specified, replace the height control compressor.



2. INSPECT HEIGHT CONTROL COMPRESSOR (COMPRESSOR MOTOR)


  1. Check the compressor operation.




    1. Apply 12 V battery voltage to the compressor motor and check the operation of the motor.

      OK:
      Measurement Condition
      		Specified Value
      12 V battery positive (+) → Terminal 1 (B)
      12 V battery negative (-) → Terminal 2 (E)
      		Motor operates

      Text in Illustration 
      *a
      		Component without harness connected
      (Compressor Motor)

      NOTICE:
      1. Do not operate the height control compressor for 60 seconds or more.
      2. Stop applying voltage immediately if the compressor stops operating because a short will occur and the compressor motor will lock up, causing a flow of excessive current.

      If the result is not as specified, replace the height control compressor.
