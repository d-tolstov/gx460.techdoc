DTC    C1683    Side Camera Feedback Malfunction


DESCRIPTION

WIRING DIAGRAM

INSPECTION PROCEDURE

CHECK FOR DTC

CHECK PARKING ASSIST ECU

CHECK FRONT PASSENGER SIDE TELEVISION CAMERA ASSEMBLY

CHECK HARNESS AND CONNECTOR (PARKING ASSIST ECU - OUTER REAR VIEW MIRROR ASSEMBLY)
