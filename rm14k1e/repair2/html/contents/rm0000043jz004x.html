DESCRIPTION

The DTCs above are output during seat belt control ECU self-diagnosis.
When DTC B2015 or B2016 is stored, DTCs B2026 and B2027 are not stored.
When DTC B2020 or B2021 is stored, DTCs B2031 and B2032 are not stored.
