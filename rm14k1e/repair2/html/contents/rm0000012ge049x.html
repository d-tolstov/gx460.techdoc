METER / GAUGE SYSTEM > Fuel Receiver Gauge Malfunction

for Preparation Click here


DESCRIPTION

  1. The fuel sender gauge has a variable resistance mechanism. The resistance decreases when the fuel amount increases, and the resistance increases when the fuel amount decreases. The fuel receiver gauge changes based on the resistance of the fuel sender gauge.

NOTICE:
When the combination meter is removed or replaced, its connector is disconnected or fuel is added, the fuel receiver gauge display will return to normal within a maximum of 16 minutes after the engine switch is turned on (IG) or when vehicle speed is input.
