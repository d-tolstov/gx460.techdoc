REAR LATERAL CONTROL ROD > INSTALLATION

for Preparation Click here



1. TEMPORARILY INSTALL REAR LATERAL CONTROL ROD ASSEMBLY

  1. Temporarily install the lateral control rod assembly with the bolt and nut.

  1. Temporarily install the bolt.



2. STABILIZE SUSPENSION

  1. Lower the vehicle.

  1. Bounce the vehicle up and down several times to stabilize the suspension.



3. TIGHTEN REAR LATERAL CONTROL ROD ASSEMBLY



  1. Tighten the 2 bolts.

    Torque:
    130 N*m{ 1326 kgf*cm , 96 ft.*lbf }

    HINT:
    Turn the bolt while holding the nut.
