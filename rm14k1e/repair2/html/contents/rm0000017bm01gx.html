FRONT DIFFERENTIAL CARRIER ASSEMBLY > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here



1. REMOVE FRONT WHEELS


2. REMOVE NO. 1 ENGINE UNDER COVER SUB-ASSEMBLY

  1. Remove the 4 bolts.




  1. Unhook the No. 1 engine under cover from the vehicle body as shown in the illustration.



3. REMOVE REAR ENGINE UNDER COVER ASSEMBLY

  1. Remove the 4 bolts and rear engine under cover.



4. DRAIN DIFFERENTIAL OIL

  1. Stop the vehicle on a level surface.

  1. Remove the differential drain plug and gasket.

  1. Drain the oil.

  1. Install a new gasket and the differential drain plug.

    Torque:
    Front differential carrier:
    65 N*m{ 660 kgf*cm , 48 ft.*lbf }
    Rear differential carrier:
    49 N*m{ 500 kgf*cm , 36 ft.*lbf }



5. REMOVE FRONT PROPELLER SHAFT ASSEMBLY

  1. Remove the front propeller shaft (Click here).



6. REMOVE FRONT DRIVE SHAFT ASSEMBLY

  1. Remove the front drive shaft (Click here).



7. REMOVE FRONT STABILIZER LINK ASSEMBLY

  1. Remove the stabilizer link (Click here).



8. REMOVE FRONT SPEED SENSOR

  1. Remove the front speed sensor (Click here).



9. DISCONNECT TIE ROD END SUB-ASSEMBLY LH

  1. Disconnect the tie rod end sub-assembly (Click here).



10. DISCONNECT TIE ROD END SUB-ASSEMBLY RH

  1. Disconnect the tie rod end sub-assembly (Click here).



11. REMOVE FRONT LOWER NO. 1 SUSPENSION ARM SUB-ASSEMBLY LH

  1. Remove the front lower No. 1 suspension arm sub-assembly (Click here).



12. REMOVE FRONT LOWER NO. 1 SUSPENSION ARM SUB-ASSEMBLY RH

HINT:
Use the same procedure described for the LH side.



13. REMOVE FRONT DIFFERENTIAL CARRIER ASSEMBLY



  1. Remove the bolt and disconnect the front differential breather tube bracket.

  1. Support the front differential with a jack.

  1. Remove the front No. 1 differential mount nut.

  1. Remove the 2 front mounting bolts and nut.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Mounting Bolt
    *2
    		Mounting Nut

  1. Disconnect the actuator hose and connector.

  1. Lower the jack and remove the front differential.




  1. Remove the 3 bolts and front No. 1 differential support.




  1. Remove the 2 bolts and front No. 2 differential support.




  1. Remove the 2 bolts and front No. 3 differential support.
