1. INSPECT STOP LIGHT CONTROL RELAY ASSEMBLY



  1. Disconnect the A6 stop light control relay connector.

  1. Measure the voltage according to the value(s) in the table below.

    Standard Voltage:
    Tester Connection
    		Condition
    		Specified Condition
    A6-2 (STP) - Body ground
    		Brake pedal released
    		Below 1 V
    Brake pedal depressed
    		11 to 14 V
    A6-6 (+B) - Body ground
    		Always
    		11 to 14 V

    If the result is not as specified, repair or replace the wire harness or connector.

  1. Measure the resistance according to the value(s) in the table below.

    Standard Resistance:
    Tester Connection
    		Condition
    		Specified Condition
