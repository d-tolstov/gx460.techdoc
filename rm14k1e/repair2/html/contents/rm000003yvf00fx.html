    2.INSPECT BATTERY VOLTAGE

    1. Measure the battery voltage with the engine switch off.

      Standard voltage:
      11 to 14 V

      1. If the voltage is below 11 V, recharge or replace the battery before proceeding to the next step.


    NEXT


    3.BASIC INSPECTION

    1. Turn the engine switch on (IG).

    2. Check whether or not the radio receiver assembly turns on.
