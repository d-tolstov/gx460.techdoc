POWER SEAT CONTROL ECU (for Rear No. 2 Seat) > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here


NOTICE:
  1. If the fold seat control ECU is replaced with a new one, perform initialization (initial position memorization) of the fold seat control ECU (Click here).
  2. If the fold seat control ECU is exchanged with one from another vehicle, perform initialization (initial position reset and initial position memorization) of the fold seat control ECU (Click here).

HINT:
  1. Use the same procedure for the RH and LH sides.
  2. The procedure listed below is for the LH side.



1. REMOVE DECK BOARD ASSEMBLY

  1. Operate the No. 2 fold seat switch and fold down the 2 seatbacks.




  1. Using a screwdriver, detach the 2 claws and open the cover.

    HINT:
    1. Tape the screwdriver tip before use.
    2. Use the same procedure to open the cover on the other side.

    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape




  1. Remove the 2 bolts.

  1. Detach the 4 clips and remove the deck board.



2. REMOVE FOLD SEAT CONTROL ECU



  1. Using a clip remover, remove the 3 clips.




  1. Disconnect the 2 connectors.

  1. Remove the 2 nuts and ECU.
