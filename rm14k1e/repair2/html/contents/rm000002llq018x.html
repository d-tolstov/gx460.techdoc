DTC    B1497/97    BUS IC Communication Malfunction

for Preparation Click here


DESCRIPTION

The air conditioning harness assembly connects the air conditioning amplifier assembly and each servo motor. The air conditioning amplifier assembly supplies power and sends operation instructions to each servo motor through the air conditioning harness assembly. Each servo motor sends the damper position information to the air conditioning amplifier assembly.

DTC Code
DTC Detection Condition
Trouble Area
B1497/97
An open circuit or malfunction in the communication line.
  1. Air conditioning harness assembly
  2. Air conditioning amplifier assembly
