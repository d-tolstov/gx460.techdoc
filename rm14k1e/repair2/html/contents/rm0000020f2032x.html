INSPECTION PROCEDURE

HINT:
After the inspection is completed, clear the DTCs.

1.CHECK THAT DISC IS INSERTED PROPERLY

  1. Check whether or not the disc is inserted upside down.

    OK:
    The disc is properly inserted.

2.CHECK DISC



  1. Check that the disc is not deformed or cracked.

    OK:
    No deformation or cracks on the disc.

3.DISC CLEANING



  1. If dirt is on the disc surface, wipe it clean with a soft cloth from the inside to the outside in a radial direction.

    NOTICE:
    Do not use a conventional record cleaner or antistatic preservative.
