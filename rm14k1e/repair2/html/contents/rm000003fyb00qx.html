1. REMOVE REAR WIPER BLADE



  1. Apply protective tape to the areas shown in the illustration.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape

  1. Turn the engine switch on (ACC).

  1. Turn the rear wiper switch on.

    HINT:
    The wiper motor does not operate if the back door and back door glass are not closed.




  1. Turn the engine switch off when the wiper arm stops at the position shown in the illustration.

    Standard Clearance:
    Area
    		Measurement
    A - B
    		Approximately
    23° to 43°
    C - D
    		approximately
    170.4 to 304.9 mm (6.71 to 12.0 in.)




  1. Raise the rear wiper arm.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape

    NOTICE:
    1. Make sure that the wiper arm does not interfere with the rear spoiler.
    2. If the wiper arm does interfere with the rear spoiler, operate the engine switch again to stop the wiper arm in a near-vertical position.




  1. Raise the wiper blade to the position where the claw detaches with a click sound as shown in the illustration.

    NOTICE:
    Be careful not to damage the claw.




  1. Pull the wiper blade straight toward the left side of the vehicle to remove it from the wiper arm.

    NOTICE:
    Do not lower the wiper arm with the wiper blade removed. The arm tip may damage the back door glass surface.



2. REMOVE REAR WIPER RUBBER



  1. Lift and pull the end of the wiper rubber protrusion from the blade stopper as shown in the illustration.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Stopper
    (Rear End of Blade)
    *a
    		Lightly Push
    (Grip)
    *b
    		Lift
    *c
    		Pull

    NOTICE:
    Do not forcibly pull out the wiper rubber. The backing plates will be deformed or the blade claws will be damaged.

    HINT:
    Lightly pushing the rubber in the middle will allow it to be removed more easily.

  1. Remove the rear wiper rubber backing plates.



3. INSTALL REAR WIPER RUBBER



  1. Install the rear wiper rubber backing plates as shown in the illustration.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Rear Wiper Rubber Backing Plate
    *2
    		Rear Wiper Rubber
    *a
    		Curve

    NOTICE:
    Install the backing plates facing the correct direction.

  1. Insert the wiper rubber from the front end of the wiper blade to the rear end through the second claw.




  1. After pushing the wiper rubber through the rear end claw, allow it to stick out from the rear end stopper.
    Text in Illustration

  1. Slide the wiper rubber through the front end claw.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Front End Claw



4. INSTALL REAR WIPER BLADE



  1. Push the wiper blade straight toward the right side of the vehicle to install it to the wiper arm.

    NOTICE:
    Make sure that the wiper arm does not interfere with the rear spoiler.




  1. attach the claw to install the rear wiper blade as shown in the illustration.

    NOTICE:
    Be careful not to damage the claw.
