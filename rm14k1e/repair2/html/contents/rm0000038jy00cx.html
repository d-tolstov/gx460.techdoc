FRONT BUMPER > DISASSEMBLY

for Preparation Click here



1. REMOVE FRONT BUMPER EXTENSION MOUNTING BRACKET



  1. Remove the 2 screws and front bumper extension mounting bracket.



2. REMOVE FRONT SPOILER LH



  1. Turn the retainers 90° and remove the 2 lower front bumper retainers and front spoiler.



3. REMOVE FRONT SPOILER RH

HINT:
Use the same procedure described for the LH side.



4. REMOVE TYPE 1 HEADLIGHT WASHER NOZZLE SUB-ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Extend the headlight washer nozzle by hand.

    NOTICE:
    Be careful not to damage the headlight washer actuator sub-assembly or headlight washer nozzle.




  1. Detach the 2 claws and remove the headlight cleaner washer nozzle.



5. REMOVE TYPE 1 HEADLIGHT WASHER NOZZLE SUB-ASSEMBLY RH

HINT:
Use the same procedure described for the LH side.



6. REMOVE HEADLIGHT CLEANER HOSE



  1. Disconnect the headlight cleaner hose from the headlight washer actuator sub-assembly.

  1. Detach the 6 clamps and remove the hose from the lower radiator grille.



7. REMOVE HEADLIGHT WASHER ACTUATOR SUB-ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Remove the bolt.




  1. Detach the 2 claws and remove the headlight washer actuator sub-assembly.



8. REMOVE HEADLIGHT WASHER ACTUATOR SUB-ASSEMBLY RH

HINT:
Use the same procedure described for the LH side.



9. REMOVE NO. 2 ENGINE ROOM WIRE


  1. w/ LEXUS Parking Assist-sensor System

    1. Disconnect the 6 connectors.



    2. Detach the 6 clamps and remove the No. 2 engine room wire.





  1. w/o LEXUS Parking Assist-sensor System

    1. Disconnect the 2 connectors.



    2. Detach the 6 clamps and remove the No. 2 engine room wire.





10. REMOVE NO. 1 ULTRASONIC SENSOR (w/ LEXUS Parking Assist-sensor System)



HINT:
Use the same procedure to remove the No. 1 ultrasonic sensor on the other side.

  1. Disconnect the connector.

  1. Detach the 2 claws and remove the No. 1 ultrasonic sensor.



11. REMOVE NO. 1 ULTRASONIC SENSOR RETAINER (w/ LEXUS Parking Assist-sensor System)



HINT:
Use the same procedure to remove the No. 1 ultrasonic sensor retainer on the other side.

  1. Detach the 2 claws and remove the No. 1 ultrasonic sensor retainer.



12. REMOVE FOG LIGHT ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Remove the screw and fog light.

  1. Disconnect the connector.



13. REMOVE FOG LIGHT ASSEMBLY RH

HINT:
Use the same procedure described for the LH side.



14. REMOVE FRONT BUMPER HOLE COVER ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Detach the 3 claws and 2 guides and remove the front bumper hole cover.



15. REMOVE FRONT BUMPER HOLE COVER ASSEMBLY RH

HINT:
Use the same procedure described for the LH side.



16. REMOVE LOWER RADIATOR GRILLE LH



  1. Detach the 8 claws and remove the lower radiator grille.



17. REMOVE LOWER RADIATOR GRILLE RH

HINT:
Use the same procedure described for the LH side.
