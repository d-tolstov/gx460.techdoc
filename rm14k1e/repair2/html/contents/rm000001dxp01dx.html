VEHICLE STABILITY CONTROL SYSTEM > Skid Control Buzzer Circuit

for Preparation Click here


DESCRIPTION

The skid control buzzer sounds upon receiving a signal from the skid control ECU.

WIRING DIAGRAM




INSPECTION PROCEDURE

NOTICE:
When replacing the master cylinder solenoid, perform calibration (Click here).

1.PERFORM ACTIVE TEST USING INTELLIGENT TESTER (DSS SIGNAL BUZZER)

  1. Turn the engine switch off.

  1. Connect the intelligent tester to the DLC3.

  1. Turn the engine switch on (IG).

  1. Turn the intelligent tester on.

  1. Enter the following menus: Chassis / ABS/VSC/TRC / Active Test.

    ABS/VSC/TRC 
    Tester Display
    		Test Part
    		Control Range
    		Diagnostic Note
    DSS Signal Buzzer
    		Skid control buzzer
    		Buzzer ON/OFF
    		The buzzer can be heard.

  1. Check that the skid control buzzer sounds/stops when turning the skid control buzzer on/off by using the intelligent tester.

    Result 
    Result
    		Proceed to
    Buzzer does not sound or sounds constantly
    		A
    Buzzer sounds/stops
    		B

4.CHECK HARNESS AND CONNECTOR (SKID CONTROL BUZZER - SKID CONTROL ECU)

  1. Disconnect the A7 skid control ECU connector.

  1. Disconnect the A9 skid control buzzer connector.

  1. Measure the resistance according to the value(s) in the table below.

    Standard Resistance:
    Tester Connection
    		Condition

    A7-30 (BZ) - A9-1
    		Always
    		Below 1 Ω
    A7-30 (BZ) - Body ground
    		Always
