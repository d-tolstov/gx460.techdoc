DTC    C1437    Lost Communication with ECM

for Preparation Click here


DESCRIPTION

The skid control ECU receives signals from the ECM via the CAN communication system.

DTC Code
DTC Detection Condition
Trouble Area
C1437
When the IG1 terminal voltage is 10 V or higher and the vehicle speed is 15 km/h (9 mph) or more, data cannot be sent from the ECM for 2 seconds or more.
  1. Engine control system
  2. CAN communication system
  3. Master cylinder solenoid (Skid control ECU)
