1. INSPECT HEADLIGHT CLEANER MOTOR AND PUMP ASSEMBLY

  1. Remove the washer jar.

  1. Disconnect the headlight cleaner washer motor and pump connector.

    HINT:
    Make sure that the headlight cleaner washer motor and pump is installed to the washer jar.

  1. Fill the washer jar with washer fluid.




  1. Connect the positive (+) lead of the battery to terminal 2 of the headlight cleaner washer motor and pump, and the negative (-) lead to terminal 1.
    Text in Illustration 
    *a
    		Component without harness connected
    (Headlight Cleaner Motor and Pump Assembly)

  1. Check that washer fluid flows from the washer jar.

    OK:
    Washer fluid flows from the washer jar.

    If the result is not as specified, replace the headlight cleaner motor and pump assembly.
