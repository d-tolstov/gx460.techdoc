HINT:
  1. Use the same procedure for the RH and LH sides.
  2. The procedure listed below is for the LH side.



1. REMOVE REAR TURN SIGNAL LIGHT BULB



  1. Turn the socket in the direction indicated by the arrow and remove it.

  1. Remove the bulb.



2. REMOVE REAR COMBINATION LIGHT SOCKET AND WIRE SUB-ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Detach the 3 clamps.




  1. Turn the cover in the direction indicated by the arrow and disconnect the wire.
