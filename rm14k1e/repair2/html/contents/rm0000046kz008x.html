STEERING KNUCKLE > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here


HINT:
  1. Use the same procedure for the RH and LH sides.
  2. The procedure listed below is for the LH side.



1. REMOVE FRONT SPEED SENSOR LH

  1. Remove the bolt and speed sensor from the knuckle.

    NOTICE:
    Pull out the sensor while trying as much as possible not to rotate it.



2. REMOVE FRONT AXLE HUB SUB-ASSEMBLY LH

  1. Remove the front axle hub (Click here).



3. DISCONNECT TIE ROD END SUB-ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Using SST, disconnect the tie rod end assembly.

    SST
    09610-20012  



4. DISCONNECT FRONT LOWER BALL JOINT ATTACHMENT LH



  1. Remove the 2 bolts and disconnect the front lower ball joint attachment from the axle.



5. REMOVE STEERING KNUCKLE LH

  1. Support the front suspension lower arm LH with a jack.

  1. Remove the clip and nut.




  1. Using SST, disconnect the upper ball joint from the steering knuckle.

    SST
    09628-62011  

    NOTICE:
    Do not the damage the ball joint dust cover.

  1. Remove the steering knuckle.
