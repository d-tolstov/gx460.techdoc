DTC    B15CC    Backup Battery Failure

for Preparation Click here


DESCRIPTION

This DTC is stored when the telematics transceiver detects one of the following:
  1. An open or short in the mayday battery circuit.
  2. The mayday battery voltage drops or the mayday battery malfunctions.
  3. The mayday battery temperature is (temporarily) high.

DTC Code
DTC Detection Condition
Trouble Area
B15CC
A mayday battery malfunction.
  1. Harness or connector
  2. Mayday battery
  3. Telematics transceiver
