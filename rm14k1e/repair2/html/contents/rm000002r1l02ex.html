POWER WINDOW REGULATOR MOTOR (for Front Door) > INSPECTION

for Preparation Click here



1. INSPECT POWER WINDOW REGULATOR MOTOR ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Check that the motor gear rotates smoothly as follows.

    NOTICE:
    Do not apply positive (+) battery voltage to any terminal other than terminal 2 (B) to avoid damaging the pulse sensor inside motor.


    OK:

    Measurement Condition
    		Specified Condition
    1. Connect the positive (+) battery terminal to terminal 2 (B), and connect the negative (-) battery terminal to terminal 1 (GND), and keep them connected for 3 seconds or more.
    2. With terminals 2 (B) and 1 (GND) connected to the battery, connect the negative (-) battery terminal to terminal 10 (UP).
    3. Disconnect and reconnect the negative (-) battery terminal to terminal 10 (UP) within 1 second.
    		Motor gear rotates clockwise (Up)
    1. Connect the positive (+) battery terminal to terminal 2 (B), and connect the negative (-) battery terminal to terminal 1 (GND), and keep them connected for 3 seconds or more.
    2. With terminals 2 (B) and 1 (GND) connected to the battery, connect the negative (-) battery terminal to terminal 7 (DOWN).
    3. Disconnect and reconnect the negative (-) battery terminal to terminal 7 (DOWN) within 1 second.
    		Motor gear rotates counterclockwise (Down)

    1. If the result is not as specified, replace the power window regulator motor assembly LH.

    CAUTION:
    Reset the power window regulator motor (initialize the pulse sensor) after installing the power window regulator motor and regulator assembly to the door.

    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Motor Gear
    *a
    		Clockwise
    *b
    		Counterclockwise
