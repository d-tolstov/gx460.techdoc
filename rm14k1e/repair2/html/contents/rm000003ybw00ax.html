1. REMOVE SYMBOL EMBLEM



HINT:
When removing the symbol emblem, heat the vehicle body and symbol emblem using a heat light.

Standard:
Item
Temperature
Vehicle Body
40 to 60°C (104 to 140°F)
Symbol Emblem
20 to 30°C (68 to 86°F)

NOTICE:
Do not heat the vehicle body or symbol emblem excessively.

  1. Put protective tape around the symbol emblem.

  1. Insert piano wire between the vehicle body and symbol emblem.

    NOTICE:
    Be careful of the boss positions.

  1. Tie objects that can serve as handles (for example, wooden blocks) to the piano wire ends.

  1. Pull the piano wire and scrape off the double-sided tape that holds the symbol emblem to the vehicle body.

    NOTICE:
    Be careful not to damage the vehicle body.

  1. Remove the symbol emblem.
