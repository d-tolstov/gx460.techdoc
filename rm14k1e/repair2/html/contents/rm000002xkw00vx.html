WIRELESS DOOR LOCK BUZZER > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here



1. REMOVE FRONT WHEEL


2. REMOVE SIDE STEP ASSEMBLY LH

  1. Remove the side step assembly LH (Click here).



3. REMOVE FRONT FENDER LINER LH



  1. Remove the 7 screws from the front fender liner LH.




  1. Detach the 14 grommets and remove the front fender liner LH.

    HINT:
    The grommets need to be replaced with new ones because they will break when they are removed.



4. REMOVE WIRELESS DOOR LOCK BUZZER



  1. Disconnect the connector.

  1. Detach the clamp and remove the wireless door lock buzzer.
