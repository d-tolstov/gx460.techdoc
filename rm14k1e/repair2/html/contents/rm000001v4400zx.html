1. DISPOSE OF FRONT SHOCK ABSORBER ASSEMBLY LH

HINT:
Use the same procedure for the other front shock absorber.




  1. Fully extend the shock absorber piston rod and fix the shock absorber in place at an angle in a vise or similar tool.

  1. Using a drill or similar tool, slowly make a hole approximately 50 mm (1.97 in.) from the top end of the shock absorber and discharge the gas inside of it.

    CAUTION:
    1. The gas is colorless, odorless and harmless.
    2. Since the discharged gas may cause chips to fly off, cover the drill with a cloth when making a hole.
