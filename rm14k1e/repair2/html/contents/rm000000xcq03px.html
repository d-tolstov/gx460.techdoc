AIR CONDITIONING SYSTEM > Air Conditioning Compressor Magnetic Clutch Circuit

for Preparation Click here


DESCRIPTION

When the air conditioning amplifier assembly is turned on, a magnet clutch assembly signal is sent from the MGC terminal of the air conditioning amplifier assembly. Then, the A/C COMP relay turns on to operate the magnet clutch assembly.

INSPECTION PROCEDURE

NOTICE:
Inspect the fuses for circuits related to this system before performing the following inspection procedure.

1.INSPECT MAGNET CLUTCH RELAY (A/C COMP)



  1. Remove the A/C COMP relay from the engine room No. 1 relay block, junction block.

  1. Measure the resistance according to the value(s) in the table below.

    Standard Resistance:
    Tester Connection
    		Condition
    		Specified Condition
    3 - 5
    		Battery voltage is not applied to terminals 1 and 2
    		10 kΩ or higher
    Battery voltage is applied to terminals 1 and 2
    		Below 1 Ω



NG
REPLACE MAGNET CLUTCH RELAY (A/C COMP)
OK


2.INSPECT COOLER COMPRESSOR ASSEMBLY



  1. Remove the cooler compressor assembly (Click here).

  1. Disconnect the A magnet clutch connector.

  1. Measure the resistance according to the value(s) in the table below.

    Standard Resistance:
    Tester Connection
    		Condition
    		Specified Condition
    3 (MG+) - A-1
    		Always
    		Below 1 Ω
    3 (MG+) - Body ground
    		Always
    		10 kΩ or higher



NG
REPLACE COOLER COMPRESSOR ASSEMBLY (Click here)
OK


3.INSPECT MAGNETIC CLUTCH ASSEMBLY



  1. Reconnect the A magnet clutch connector.

  1. Apply battery voltage to the magnet clutch assembly and check the operation of the magnet clutch assembly.

    OK:
    Measurement Condition
    		Specified Condition
    Battery positive (+) → Terminal 3 (MG+)
    Battery negative (-) → Body ground
    		Magnet clutch assembly operating sound can be heard, and magnetic clutch hub and rotor lock.



NG
REPLACE MAGNET CLUTCH ASSEMBLY (Click here)
OK

    Standard Voltage:
    Tester Connection
    		Switch Condition
    		Specified Condition
    A/C COMP relay terminal 1 - Body ground

    		11 to 14 V
    A/C COMP relay terminal 5 - Body ground
    		Always
    		11 to 14 V

    *1
    		A/C COMP Relay
    *a
    		Component without relay installed
    (Engine Room No. 1 Relay Block, Junction Block)
