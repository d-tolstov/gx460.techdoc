1. INSTALL MASS AIR FLOW METER ASSEMBLY

  1. Install the mass air flow meter with the 2 screws.

    Torque:
    0.7 N*m{ 7 kgf*cm , 6 in.*lbf }



2. INSTALL AIR CLEANER CAP SUB-ASSEMBLY

  1. Attach the 4 clips and install the air cleaner cap with the bolt.

    HINT:
    When installing the air cleaner hose, align its groove with the protrusion of the throttle body as shown in the illustration.

    Torque:
    5.0 N*m{ 51 kgf*cm , 44 in.*lbf }

  1. Tighten the hose clamp.

    Torque:
    5.0 N*m{ 51 kgf*cm , 44 in.*lbf }

    HINT:
    Make sure the direction of the hose clamp is as shown in the illustration.

  1. Attach the clamp and connect the mass air flow meter connector.
