REAR NO. 2 SEAT INNER BELT ASSEMBLY > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here


CAUTION:
Wear protective gloves. Sharp areas on the parts may injure your hands.

HINT:
  1. Use the same procedure for the RH and LH sides.
  2. The procedure listed below is for the LH side.



1. REMOVE REAR NO. 2 SEAT ASSEMBLY LH

  1. Remove the rear No. 2 seat assembly LH (Click here).



2. REMOVE REAR SEAT CUSHION LOWER HINGE COVER LH



  1. Remove the 2 screws.

  1. Detach the 3 claws and remove the cover.



3. REMOVE REAR NO. 2 SEAT INNER BELT ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Using a T45 "TORX" socket wrench, remove the bolt and inner belt assembly.
