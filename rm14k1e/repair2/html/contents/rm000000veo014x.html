INSTRUMENT PANEL SPEAKER > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here


HINT:
  1. Use the same procedure for the RH and LH sides.
  2. The procedure listed below is for the LH side.



1. DISCONNECT CABLE FROM NEGATIVE BATTERY TERMINAL

CAUTION:
Wait at least 90 seconds after disconnecting the cable from the negative (-) battery terminal to disable the SRS system.

NOTICE:
  1. w/ Navigation System (for HDD):
    After the engine switch is turned off, the display and navigation module display (HDD navigation system) records various types of memory and settings. As a result, after turning the engine switch off, be sure to wait for the time specified in the following table before disconnecting the cable from the negative (-) battery terminal.

    Waiting Time before Disconnecting Cable from Negative (-) Battery Terminal:

    Condition
    		Waiting Time
    Vehicle enrolled in G-BOOK system
    		6 minutes
    Vehicle not enrolled in G-BOOK system
    		1 minute

  2. When disconnecting the cable, some systems need to be initialized after the cable is reconnected (Click here).



2. REMOVE FRONT NO. 1 ASSIST GRIP PLUG LH



HINT:
Use the same procedure for the other front No. 1 assist grip plug.

  1. Using a screwdriver, detach the 2 claws and remove the front No. 1 assist grip plug.

    HINT:
    Tape the screwdriver tip before use.

    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape



3. REMOVE NO. 1 ASSIST GRIP



HINT:
Use the same procedure for the other No. 1 assist grip.

  1. Remove the 2 bolts.

  1. Detach the 2 claws and remove the No. 1 assist grip.



4. REMOVE FRONT PILLAR GARNISH LH



  1. Detach the 3 guides and remove the front pillar garnish.




  1. Protect the curtain shield airbag assembly.

    1. Completely cover the airbag with a cloth or nylon sheet and secure the ends of the cover with adhesive tape as shown in the illustration.

      NOTICE:
      Cover the curtain shield airbag with a protective cover as soon as the front pillar garnish is removed.

      Text in Illustration 
      *1
      		Curtain Shield Airbag Assembly
      *2
      		Adhesive Tape
      *3
      		Protective Cover



5. REMOVE NO. 1 INSTRUMENT PANEL SPEAKER PANEL SUB-ASSEMBLY



  1. Detach the 4 claws, 2 clips and guide and remove the instrument cluster finish panel.



6. REMOVE FRONT NO. 2 SPEAKER ASSEMBLY



  1. Remove the 2 screws.

  1. Remove the front No. 2 speaker and disconnect the speaker connector.

    NOTICE:
    Do not touch the cone of the speaker.



7. REMOVE UPPER INSTRUMENT CLUSTER FINISH PANEL



  1. Put protective tape around the upper instrument cluster finish panel.

  1. Using a screwdriver, detach the 11 clips and remove the upper instrument cluster finish panel.

    HINT:
    Tape the screwdriver tip before use.

    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape



8. REMOVE FRONT NO. 4 SPEAKER ASSEMBLY



  1. Remove the 2 screws.

  1. Remove the front No. 4 speaker and disconnect the speaker connector.

    NOTICE:
    Do not touch the cone of the speaker.
