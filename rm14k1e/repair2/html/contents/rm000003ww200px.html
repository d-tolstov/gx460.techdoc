DTC    C1612    IG Voltage is Low or High

for Preparation Click here


DESCRIPTION

This DTC is stored if the parking assist ECU judges as a result of its self check that the voltage received by terminal IG is low or high.

DTC Code
DTC Detection Condition
Trouble Area
C1612
  1. IG voltage is low
  2. IG voltage is high
  1. Harness or connector
  2. Parking assist ECU
