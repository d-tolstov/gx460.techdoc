1. INSPECT REAR COMBINATION LIGHT LENS AND BODY LH



  1. Check the stop light.

    1. Apply battery voltage to the connector and check the LED illumination condition.

      OK:
      Measurement Condition
      		Specified Condition
      Battery positive (+) → Terminal 1 (STOP)
      Battery negative (-) → Terminal 4 (E)
      		LED illuminates

      If the result is not as specified, replace the rear combination light lens and body.
      Text in Illustration 
      *a
      		Component without harness connected
      (Rear Combination Light Lens and Body)




  1. Check the taillight.

    1. Apply battery voltage to the connector and check the LED illumination condition.

      OK:
      Measurement Condition
      		Specified Condition
      Battery positive (+) → Terminal 2 (TL)
      Battery negative (-) → Terminal 4 (E)
      		LED illuminates

      If the result is not as specified, replace the rear combination light lens and body.

      *a
      		Component without harness connected
