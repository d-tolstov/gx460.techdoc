for Preparation Click here



1. INSTALL WASHER NOZZLE SUB-ASSEMBLY

  1. Connect a new washer nozzle sub-assembly to the washer hose.

  1. Attach the 2 claws to install the washer nozzle sub-assembly.



2. INSPECT WASHER NOZZLE SUB-ASSEMBLY


  1. With the engine running, check the position that the washer fluid hits the windshield.

    Standard:
    Washer fluid hits the windshield in the areas shown in the illustration.

3. ADJUST WASHER NOZZLE SUB-ASSEMBLY


  1. Select a washer nozzle so that the contact area is within the standard. Replace the washer nozzle with the selected one.
