REAR BUMPER > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here



1. REMOVE REAR QUARTER PANEL MUDGUARD LH



  1. Remove the 4 screws and rear quarter panel mudguard.



2. REMOVE REAR QUARTER PANEL MUDGUARD RH

HINT:
Use the same procedure described for the LH side.



3. REMOVE REAR BUMPER PROTECTOR LH



  1. Remove the clip, 2 screws and rear bumper protector.



4. REMOVE REAR BUMPER PROTECTOR RH

HINT:
Use the same procedure described for the LH side.



5. REMOVE JACK BOX HOLE COVER



  1. Using a screwdriver, detach the 2 claws and remove the jack box hole cover.

    HINT:
    Tape the screwdriver tip before use.

    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape



6. REMOVE REAR BUMPER BAR PLATE

  1. Put protective tape around the rear bumper bar plate.




  1. Detach the 14 claws and remove the rear bumper bar plate.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape



7. REMOVE REAR BUMPER COVER



  1. Put protective tape around the rear bumper cover.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape

  1. Remove the 2 clips, 2 screws and 7 bolts.




  1. Detach the 6 claws.

    HINT:
    Use the same procedure for the RH side and LH side.

  1. Disconnect the 3 connectors.

  1. Remove the rear bumper cover.



8. REMOVE NO. 3 REAR BUMPER SIDE SUPPORT



  1. Remove the screw and No. 3 rear bumper side support.



9. REMOVE REAR BUMPER SIDE SUPPORT LH



  1. Remove the 3 screws.

  1. Using a clip remover, detach the 4 claws and remove the rear bumper side support.

    NOTICE:
    Tape the clip remover tip before use.

    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape



10. REMOVE REAR BUMPER SIDE SUPPORT RH

HINT:
Use the same procedure described for the LH side.



11. REMOVE REAR BUMPER BAR REINFORCEMENT LH



  1. Remove the 2 bolts and rear bumper bar reinforcement.



12. REMOVE REAR BUMPER BAR REINFORCEMENT RH

  1. Remove the 3 bolts and rear bumper bar reinforcement.



13. REMOVE REAR COMBINATION LIGHT LENS AND BODY LH



  1. Remove the 2 bolts.

  1. Detach the clip and 2 claws and remove the light.

  1. Disconnect the connector.



14. REMOVE REAR COMBINATION LIGHT LENS AND BODY RH

HINT:
Use the same procedure described for the LH side.



15. REMOVE UPPER REAR BUMPER RETAINER LH



  1. Detach the clip and remove the upper rear bumper retainer.



16. REMOVE UPPER REAR BUMPER RETAINER RH

HINT:
Use the same procedure described for the LH side.



17. REMOVE NO. 2 REAR BUMPER SIDE SUPPORT LH



  1. Remove the bolt and No. 2 rear bumper side support.



18. REMOVE REAR BUMPER SIDE MOUNTING BRACKET LH



  1. Disconnect the connector.

  1. Remove the bolt and rear bumper side moulding bracket.



19. REMOVE REAR BUMPER SIDE MOUNTING BRACKET RH

  1. Remove the bolt and rear bumper side moulding bracket.



20. REMOVE ELECTRICAL KEY ANTENNA



  1. Remove the bolt and electrical key antenna.
