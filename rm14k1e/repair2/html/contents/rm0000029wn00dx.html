1. INSTALL PROPELLER SHAFT ASSEMBLY

  1. Align the matchmarks on the propeller shaft flange and differential flange.

  1. Install the propeller shaft assembly with the 4 bolts, 4 washers and 4 nuts.

    Torque:
    88 N*m{ 899 kgf*cm , 65 ft.*lbf }

  1. Align the matchmarks on the propeller shaft flange and transfer flange.

  1. Install the propeller shaft assembly with the 4 washers and 4 nuts.

    Torque:
    88 N*m{ 899 kgf*cm , 65 ft.*lbf }
