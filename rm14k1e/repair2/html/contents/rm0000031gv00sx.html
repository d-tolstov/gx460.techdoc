1. INSTALL AIR FUEL RATIO SENSOR (for Bank 1 Sensor 1)

  1. Temporarily install the air fuel ratio sensor to the exhaust manifold LH by hand.




  1. Using SST, tighten the air fuel ratio sensor.

    SST
    09224-00010  

    Torque:
    without SST:
    44 N*m{ 449 kgf*cm , 32 ft.*lbf }
    with SST:
    40 N*m{ 408 kgf*cm , 30 ft.*lbf }

    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Fulcrum Length

    HINT:
    1. Use a torque wrench with a fulcrum length of 30 cm (11.8 in.). When using a torque wrench with a fulcrum length that is not 30 cm (11.8 in.), calculate the torque specification for the torque wrench and SST based on the "without SST" torque specification (Click here).
    2. Make sure SST and the wrench are connected in a straight line.

3. INSTALL EXHAUST MANIFOLD ASSEMBLY RH



  1. Install a new gasket to the cylinder head and a new gasket to the No. 3 air tube.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Claw
    *2
    		Tab

    HINT:
    1. Install the exhaust manifold gasket with the gasket tab facing toward the front of the engine.
    2. Install the air tube gasket with the claws of the gasket facing the tube.

  1. Temporarily install the exhaust manifold with 9 new nuts.

  1. Temporarily install the manifold stay with the 3 bolts.




  1. Tighten the 3 bolts in the sequence shown in the illustration.

    Torque:
    40 N*m{ 408 kgf*cm , 30 ft.*lbf }




  1. Uniformly tighten the 7 nuts that are not labeled A. Then tighten the 2 nuts labeled A in the illustration.

    Torque:
    for nut A:
    10 N*m{ 102 kgf*cm , 7 ft.*lbf }
    except nut A:
    21 N*m{ 214 kgf*cm , 15 ft.*lbf }

  1. Connect the air fuel ratio sensor connector.

    HINT:
    Hook the wire harness to the bracket.

10. INSTALL FRONT EXHAUST PIPE ASSEMBLY



  1. Using a plastic-faced hammer and wooden block, tap in a new gasket until its surface is flush with the front exhaust pipe.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Gasket
    *2
    		Wooden Block

    NOTICE:
    1. Be sure to install the gasket so that it faces the correct direction.
    2. Do not reuse the gasket.
    3. Do not damage the gasket.
    4. When connecting the exhaust pipe, do not push in the gasket with the exhaust pipe.

  1. Install a new gasket and the front exhaust pipe to the exhaust manifold RH with 2 new nuts.

    Torque:
    54 N*m{ 554 kgf*cm , 40 ft.*lbf }




  1. Using a vernier caliper, measure the free length of the compression spring.

    Minimum free length:
    42 mm (1.65 in.)

    1. If the free length is less than the minimum, replace the compression spring.

  1. Connect the front exhaust pipe to the center exhaust pipe with the 2 compression springs and 2 bolts.

    Torque:
    43 N*m{ 438 kgf*cm , 32 ft.*lbf }

  1. Connect the heated oxygen sensor connector.

    HINT:
    Hook the wire harness to the bracket.



11. INSTALL FRONT NO. 2 EXHAUST PIPE ASSEMBLY

  1. Connect the front No. 2 exhaust pipe to the exhaust pipe support.
