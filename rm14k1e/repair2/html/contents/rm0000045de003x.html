REAR NO. 2 SEAT ASSEMBLY > INSTALLATION

for Preparation Click here


CAUTION:
Wear protective gloves. Sharp areas on the parts may injure your hands.

NOTICE:
  1. Under either of the following conditions, perform initialization (initial position memorization) of the fold seat control ECU (Click here).
    1. The fold seat control ECU is replaced with a new one.
    2. Initialization (initial position reset) of the fold seat control ECU is performed.
  1. Under any of the following conditions, perform initialization (initial position reset and initial position memorization) of the fold seat control ECU (Click here).
    1. The fold seat control ECU is exchanged with one from another vehicle.
    2. The seatback frame and seat leg are inspected.
    3. The seat is disassembled and the positions of the seatback and seat cushion change with respect to one another.
    4. The seatback frame or seat leg is replaced.
    5. The cable is disconnected from the battery while the seatback or seat cushion is operating.

HINT:
  1. Use the same procedure for the RH and LH sides.
  2. The procedure listed below is for the LH side.



1. INSTALL REAR NO. 2 SEAT ASSEMBLY LH

  1. Place the rear No. 2 seat assembly in the cabin.

    NOTICE:
    Be careful not to damage the vehicle body.

    HINT:
    If installing the seat on the RH side with the LH side installed, install it with the No. 3 rear seat leg cover LH raised.

  1. Temporarily install the 4 bolts.

  1. Tighten the 4 bolts.

    Torque:
    37 N*m{ 377 kgf*cm , 27 ft.*lbf }




  1. Attach the 6 claws and 2 guides to close the 2 covers.
    Text in Illustration

    		Guide

  1. Connect the connector.



2. INSTALL NO. 1 DECK BOARD BRACKET LH

  1. Install the bracket with the 2 bolts.



3. INSTALL DECK BOARD BRACKET REINFORCEMENT

  1. Attach the 2 hooks.

  1. Install the reinforcement with the 2 bolts.



4. INSTALL REAR FLOOR MAT SUPPORT PLATE

  1. Attach the 5 clips and 4 guides.

  1. Attach the 6 claws to install the plate.

  1. Install the 3 bolts.



5. INSTALL REAR NO. 3 FLOOR BOARD



  1. Attach the 2 guides and 4 claws.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Guide

  1. Attach the clip to install the board.



6. INSTALL REAR NO. 4 FLOOR BOARD



  1. Attach the 2 guides and 4 claws.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Guide

  1. Attach the clip to install the board.



7. INSTALL DECK BOARD ASSEMBLY

  1. Attach the 4 clips.

  1. Install the deck board with the 2 bolts.




  1. Attach the 2 claws to close the cover.

    HINT:
    Use the same procedure to close the cover on the other side.



8. PERFORM INITIALIZATION

NOTICE:
  1. Under either of the following conditions, perform initialization (initial position memorization) of the fold seat control ECU (Click here).
    1. The fold seat control ECU is replaced with a new one.
    2. Initialization (initial position reset) of the fold seat control ECU is performed.
  1. Under any of the following conditions, perform initialization (initial position reset and initial position memorization) of the fold seat control ECU (Click here).
    1. The fold seat control ECU is exchanged with one from another vehicle.
    2. The seatback frame and seat leg are inspected.
    3. The seat is disassembled and the positions of the seatback and seat cushion change with respect to one another.
    4. The seatback frame or seat leg is replaced.
    5. The cable is disconnected from the battery while the seatback or seat cushion is operating.
