FRONT DIFFERENTIAL CARRIER OIL SEAL > REPLACEMENT

for Preparation Click here



1. REMOVE FRONT DIFFERENTIAL CARRIER ASSEMBLY

  1. Remove the front differential carrier assembly (Click here).



2. REMOVE FRONT DRIVE PINION COMPANION FLANGE NUT



  1. Using SST and a hammer, loosen the staked part of the nut.

    SST
    09930-00010  




  1. Using SST to hold the companion flange, remove the nut.

    SST
    09330-00021   (09330-00030)
    Text in Illustration 
    *a
    		Turn
    *b
    		Hold



3. REMOVE FRONT DRIVE PINION COMPANION FLANGE SUB-ASSEMBLY



  1. Using SST, remove the companion flange.

    SST
    09950-30012   (09951-03010, 09953-03010, 09954-03010, 09955-03030, 09956-03020)

    NOTICE:
    Before using SST (center bolt), apply hypoid gear oil to its threads and tip.



4. REMOVE FRONT DIFFERENTIAL CARRIER OIL SEAL



  1. Using SST, remove the oil seal.

    SST
    09308-10010  
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Oil Seal
    *2
    		Oil Slinger



5. REMOVE FRONT DIFFERENTIAL DRIVE PINION OIL SLINGER


6. REMOVE FRONT DRIVE PINION REAR TAPERED ROLLER BEARING



  1. Using SST, remove the roller bearing (inner).

    SST
    09556-22010  




  1. Using SST, tap out the roller bearing (outer).

    SST
    09308-00010  



7. REMOVE FRONT DIFFERENTIAL OIL STORAGE RING

  1. Using a screwdriver and hammer, tap out the oil storage ring.



8. REMOVE FRONT DIFFERENTIAL DRIVE PINION BEARING SPACER



  1. Remove the bearing spacer.



9. INSTALL FRONT DIFFERENTIAL DRIVE PINION BEARING SPACER

  1. Install a new bearing spacer.

    HINT:
    Install the spacer so that it is facing in the correct direction.



10. INSTALL FRONT DIFFERENTIAL OIL STORAGE RING

  1. Using a brass bar and hammer, tap in a new oil storage ring.

    NOTICE:
    Be careful not to damage the oil storage ring.



11. INSTALL FRONT DRIVE PINION REAR TAPERED ROLLER BEARING



  1. Using SST and a hammer, install the roller bearing (outer).

    SST
    09316-60011   (09316-00011, 09316-00021)

  1. Install the roller bearing (inner).



12. INSTALL FRONT DIFFERENTIAL DRIVE PINION OIL SLINGER


13. INSTALL FRONT DIFFERENTIAL CARRIER OIL SEAL



  1. Apply MP grease to the lip of a new oil seals.

  1. Using SST and a hammer, tap in the oil seal.

    SST
    09554-22010  

    Oil seal depth:
    3.9 to 4.8 mm (0.154 to 0.188 in.)

    Text in Illustration 
    *a
    		Oil Seal Depth



14. INSTALL FRONT DRIVE PINION COMPANION FLANGE SUB-ASSEMBLY



  1. Place the companion flange on the drive pinion.

  1. Using SST, install the companion flange.

    SST
    09950-30012   (09951-03010, 09953-03010, 09954-03010, 09955-03030, 09956-03020)

    NOTICE:
    Before using SST (center bolt), apply hypoid gear oil to its threads and tip.

  1. Coat the threads of a new nut with hypoid gear oil LSD.




  1. Using SST to hold the companion flange in place, tighten the nut to the correct torque.

    SST
    09330-00021   (09330-00030)

    Torque:
    370 N*m{ 3773 kgf*cm , 273 ft.*lbf } or less



15. INSPECT DIFFERENTIAL DRIVE PINION PRELOAD

  1. Using a torque wrench, measure the preload.

    Standard Preload (at Starting):
    Item
    		Specified Condition
    New bearing
    		0.98 to 1.57 N*m (10 to 16 kgf*cm, 9 to 13 in.*lbf)
    Used bearing
    		0.49 to 0.78 N*m (5 to 7 kgf*cm, 4.3 to 6 in.*lbf)

    If the result is not as specified, adjust the preload.

    Standard Total Preload (at Starting):
    Item
    		Specified Condition
    New bearing
    		1.2 to 2.45 N*m (13 to 24 kgf*cm, 11 to 21 in.*lbf)
    Used bearing
    		0.71 to 1.66 N*m (8 to 16 kgf*cm, 7 to 14 in.*lbf)



16. STAKE FRONT DRIVE PINION COMPANION FLANGE NUT



  1. Using a chisel and hammer, stake the nut.



17. INSTALL FRONT DIFFERENTIAL CARRIER ASSEMBLY

  1. Install the front differential carrier assembly (Click here).
