1. INSPECT MAGNET CLUTCH ASSEMBLY



  1. Check the magnet clutch operation.

    1. Confirm that the magnet clutch hub and magnet clutch rotor lock when the battery positive (+) lead is connected to terminal 3 of the magnet clutch and the negative (-) lead is connected to the ground wire.
      If the operation is not as specified, replace the magnet clutch assembly.

  1. Measure the resistance between terminals 1 and 2.

    Standard resistance:
    65 to 125 Î© at 20°C (68°F)
