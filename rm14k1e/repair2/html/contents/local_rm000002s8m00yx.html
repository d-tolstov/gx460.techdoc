DTC    B2324    Rear Door LH ECU Communication Stop


DESCRIPTION

WIRING DIAGRAM

INSPECTION PROCEDURE

CLEAR DTC

CHECK FOR DTC

CHECK HARNESS AND CONNECTOR (MAIN BODY ECU - REAR POWER WINDOW REGULATOR MOTOR LH)

CHECK HARNESS AND CONNECTOR (REAR POWER WINDOW REGULATOR MOTOR LH - BATTERY AND BODY GROUND)

REPLACE REAR POWER WINDOW REGULATOR MOTOR ASSEMBLY LH

CLEAR DTC

CHECK FOR DTC
