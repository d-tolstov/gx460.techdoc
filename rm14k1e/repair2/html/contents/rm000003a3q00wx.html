REAR AIR CONDITIONING UNIT > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here


NOTICE:
Before beginning this procedure, make sure that the rear air conditioning FOOT mode functions are not operating.



1. REMOVE UPPER RADIATOR SUPPORT SEAL



  1. Remove the 13 clips and upper radiator support seal.



2. RECOVER REFRIGERANT FROM REFRIGERATION SYSTEM

  1. Start the engine.

  1. Turn the A/C switch on.

  1. Operate the cooler compressor while the engine speed is approximately 1000 rpm for 5 to 6 minutes to circulate the refrigerant and collect the compressor oil remaining in each component into the cooler compressor.

  1. Stop the engine.

  1. Recover the refrigerant from the A/C system using a refrigerant recovery unit.



3. DRAIN ENGINE COOLANT

  1. Drain the engine coolant (Click here).



4. DISCONNECT CABLE FROM NEGATIVE BATTERY TERMINAL

CAUTION:
Wait at least 90 seconds after disconnecting the cable from the negative (-) battery terminal to disable the SRS system.

NOTICE:
  1. w/ Navigation System (for HDD):
    After the engine switch is turned off, the display and navigation module display (HDD navigation system) records various types of memory and settings. As a result, after turning the engine switch off, be sure to wait for the time specified in the following table before disconnecting the cable from the negative (-) battery terminal.

    Waiting Time before Disconnecting Cable from Negative (-) Battery Terminal:

    Condition
    		Waiting Time
    Vehicle enrolled in G-BOOK system
    		6 minutes
    Vehicle not enrolled in G-BOOK system
    		1 minute

  2. When disconnecting the cable, some systems need to be initialized after the cable is reconnected (Click here).

    Remove the rear No. 1 seatback assembly (Click here).

19. REMOVE DECK TRIM SIDE PANEL ASSEMBLY RH



  1. Remove the bolt and disconnect the rear No. 1 seat outer belt floor anchor.




  1. Remove the bolt and disconnect the rear No. 2 seat outer belt floor anchor.

  1. Remove the 2 bolts and screw.

  1. Detach the 3 clips and 8 claws.



  1. Disconnect each connector and remove the deck trim side panel.





20. REMOVE REAR QUARTER TRIM PANEL ASSEMBLY RH

HINT:
Use the same procedure described for the LH side.



21. DISCONNECT AIR CONDITIONING TUBE AND ACCESSORY ASSEMBLY



  1. Remove the bolt.

  1. Disconnect the air conditioning tube and accessory assembly.

    NOTICE:
    1. Do not use a screwdriver or similar tool to disconnect the tube.
    2. Seal the openings of the disconnected parts using vinyl tape to prevent moisture and foreign matter from entering them.

  1. Remove the 2 O-rings from the air conditioning tube and accessory assembly.

  1. Using pliers, grip the claws of the clips and slide the 2 clips.

  1. Disconnect the heater water inlet hose and heater water outlet hose.



22. REMOVE REAR SIDE NO. 2 AIR DUCT



  1. Detach the 2 clips.

  1. Detach the clamp and remove the duct.



23. REMOVE REAR SIDE NO. 3 AIR DUCT



  1. Detach the 2 clips and remove the duct.



24. REMOVE REAR SIDE NO. 1 AIR DUCT



  1. Detach the 2 claws and remove the cooler plate.




  1. Swing the bottom of the duct sideways and remove the duct.



25. REMOVE REAR COOLING UNIT ASSEMBLY



  1. Disconnect the 4 connectors and detach the 3 clamps.

  1. Remove the 5 bolts and rear cooling unit.



26. REMOVE CURTAIN SHIELD AIRBAG ASSEMBLY RH

  1. Remove the 17 bolts and curtain shield airbag.
