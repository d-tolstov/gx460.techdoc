DTC    C1A4A    Skid Control Buzzer Circuit

for Preparation Click here


DESCRIPTION

Based on pre-crash control, the driving support ECU warns the driver to be careful by sending a sounding signal to the skid control ECU to sound the skid control buzzer.
When the skid control ECU detects a malfunction in the skid control buzzer circuit, a malfunction signal is sent to the driving support ECU and a DTC is stored. Although the skid control ECU sends a malfunction signal to the driving support ECU, the relevant ABS DTCs are not stored.

DTC Code
DTC Detection Condition
Trouble Area
C1A4A
While the engine switch on (IG), a skid control buzzer circuit malfunction signal is received by the skid control ECU for 1 second.
  1. Skid control buzzer circuit
  2. Master cylinder assembly (Skid control ECU)
  3. Driving support ECU
