VANE PUMP > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here


NOTICE:
  1. When using a vise, do not overtighten it.
  2. When installing the parts indicated by arrows, coat them with power steering fluid (Click here).



1. DISCONNECT CABLE FROM NEGATIVE BATTERY TERMINAL

CAUTION:
Wait at least 90 seconds after disconnecting the cable from the negative (-) battery terminal to disable the SRS system.

NOTICE:
  1. w/ Navigation System for HDD:
    After the ignition switch is turned off, the HDD navigation system requires approximately a minute to record various types of memory and settings. As a result, after turning the ignition switch off, wait a minute or more before disconnecting the cable from the negative (-) battery terminal.
  2. When disconnecting the cable, some systems need to be initialized after the cable is reconnected (Click here).



2. REMOVE FRONT WHEEL RH


3. REMOVE FRONT FENDER APRON SEAL RH

  1. Remove the 4 clips and front fender apron seal RH.



4. REMOVE V-BANK COVER SUB-ASSEMBLY



  1. Raise the front of the V-bank cover to detach the 2 pins. Then detach the 2 V-bank cover hooks from the No. 1 V-bank cover bracket and remove the V-bank cover.
    Text in Illustration

    *2

    		No. 1 V-bank Cover Bracket



5. REMOVE AIR CLEANER CAP AND HOSE



  1. Disconnect the mass air flow meter connector, No. 1 air hose and No. 2 ventilation hose and detach the wire harness clamp.

  1. Loosen the hose clamp.

  1. Detach the 4 clamps and remove the air cleaner cap and hose.



6. REMOVE AIR CLEANER FILTER ELEMENT SUB-ASSEMBLY


7. REMOVE AIR CLEANER CASE



  1. Remove the 3 bolts and air cleaner case.



8. REMOVE RADIATOR SUPPORT UPPER SEAL



  1. Remove the 13 clips and upper radiator support seal.



9. REMOVE FAN AND GENERATOR V BELT



  1. While turning the belt tensioner counterclockwise, align the service hole for the belt tensioner and the belt tensioner fixing position, and then insert a bar with a diameter of 5 mm (0.197 in.) into the service hole to fix the belt tensioner in place.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Bar
    *2
    		Service Hole

    HINT:
    The pulley bolt for the belt tensioner has a left-hand thread.

  1. Remove the fan and generator V belt.



10. DISCONNECT NO. 1 OIL RESERVOIR TO PUMP HOSE



  1. Slide the clip and disconnect the oil reservoir to pump hose from the vane pump.



11. DISCONNECT POWER STEERING OIL PRESSURE SWITCH CONNECTOR



  1. Disconnect the connector.



12. DISCONNECT PRESSURE FEED TUBE



  1. Remove the union bolt and disconnect the pressure feed tube.

  1. Remove the gasket.



13. REMOVE VANE PUMP ASSEMBLY



  1. Remove the 2 bolts and vane pump.
