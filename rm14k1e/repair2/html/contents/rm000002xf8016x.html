1. INSPECT FRONT SEAT CUSHION HEATER ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Check the seat cushion heater.

    1. Measure the resistance according to the value(s) in the table below.

      Standard Resistance:
      Tester Connection
      		Condition
      		Specified Condition
      A-1 - B-1
      		Seat cushion heater temperature 20°C (68°F)
      		3.2 to 3.9 立
      A-4 - B-2
      A-1 - A-4
      A-2 - A-3
      		8 to 12 k立

      If the result is not as specified, replace the seat cushion heater assembly.
      Text in Illustration 
      *1
      		Connector A
      *2
      		Connector B
      *a
      		Component without harness connected
      (Front Seat Cushion Heater)
