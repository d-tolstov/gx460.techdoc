AMBIENT TEMPERATURE SENSOR > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here



1. REMOVE UPPER RADIATOR SUPPORT SEAL



  1. Remove the 13 clips and upper radiator support seal.



2. REMOVE RADIATOR GRILLE

  1. Remove the radiator grille (Click here).



3. REMOVE COOLER THERMISTOR (AMBIENT TEMPERATURE SENSOR)



  1. Disconnect the connector.

  1. Using a screwdriver, detach the clamp and remove the sensor.

    HINT:
    Tape the screwdriver tip before use.
