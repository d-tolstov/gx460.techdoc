SFI SYSTEM > Starter Signal Circuit

for Preparation Click here


DESCRIPTION

While the engine is being cranked, current flows from terminal STAR of the power management control ECU to the park/neutral position switch and also flows to terminal STA of the ECM (STA signal).

INSPECTION PROCEDURE

NOTICE:
Inspect the fuses for circuits related to this system before performing the following inspection procedure.

HINT:
This chart is based on the premise that the engine can crank normally. If the engine cannot crank normally, proceed to Problem Symptoms Table (Click here).
