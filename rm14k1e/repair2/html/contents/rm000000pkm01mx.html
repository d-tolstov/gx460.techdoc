  1. Disconnect the A46 headlight swivel ECU connector.

  2. Measure the resistance and voltage according to the value(s) in the table below.

    Terminal No. (Symbol)

    		Below 1 Ω

CHECK DRIVER SIDE JUNCTION BLOCK ASSEMBLY, MAIN BODY ECU (MULTIPLEX NETWORK BODY ECU)


  1. Remove the main body ECU (Click here).

  2. Measure the resistance and voltage according to the value(s) in the table below.

    Terminal No. (Symbol)
    		Wiring Color
    		Terminal Description
    		Condition
    		Specified Condition
    A-29 (ACC) - Body ground
    		-
    		ACC power supply
    		Engine switch on (ACC)
    		11 to 14 V
    A-30 (BECU) - Body ground
    		-
    		Battery power supply
    		Always
    		11 to 14 V
    A-32 (IG) - Body ground
    		-
    		Ignition power supply
    		Engine switch on (IG)

    		W-B - Body ground
    		Ground
    		Always
    		Below 1 Ω
