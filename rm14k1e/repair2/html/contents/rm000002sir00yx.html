DTC    B2786    No Response from Steering Lock ECU

for Preparation Click here


DESCRIPTION

This DTC is stored when LIN communication between the certification ECU and steering lock actuator assembly (steering lock ECU) stops for 10 seconds or more.

DTC Code
DTC Detection Condition
Trouble Area
B2786
LIN communication between the certification ECU and steering lock ECU stops for 10 seconds or more.
  1. Steering lock actuator assembly (steering lock ECU)
  2. Certification ECU
  3. Harness or connector

WIRING DIAGRAM




INSPECTION PROCEDURE

NOTICE:
  1. When using the intelligent tester with the engine switch off to troubleshoot:
    Connect the intelligent tester to the vehicle and turn a courtesy light switch on and off at 1.5 second intervals until communication between the intelligent tester and vehicle begins.
  2. Inspect the fuses for circuits related to this system before performing the following inspection procedure.
  3. Before replacing the certification ECU or steering lock ECU, refer to the Service Bulletin.

HINT:
When DTC B2786 and B2785 are output simultaneously, perform troubleshooting for DTC B2785 first (Click here).

3.CHECK HARNESS AND CONNECTOR (CERTIFICATION ECU - STEERING LOCK ECU)

  1. Disconnect the G26 certification ECU connector.

  1. Disconnect the G23 steering lock actuator assembly (steering lock ECU) connector.

  1. Measure the resistance according to the value(s) in the table below.

    Standard Resistance:
    Tester Connection
    		Condition
    		Specified Condition

    		Always
    		Below 1 Ω
    G26-29 (LIN) or G23-5 (LIN) - Body ground
    		Always
    		10 kΩ or higher
