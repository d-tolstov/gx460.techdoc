FRONT BRAKE FLEXIBLE HOSE > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here


HINT:
  1. Use the same procedure for the RH and LH sides.
  2. The procedure listed below is for the LH side.



1. REMOVE FRONT WHEEL


2. DRAIN BRAKE FLUID

NOTICE:
Wash off brake fluid immediately if it comes in contact with any painted surface.



3. REMOVE FRONT FLEXIBLE HOSE

  1. Remove the 2 clips.




  1. Disconnect the brake tube from the front flexible hose with a union nut wrench while holding the front flexible hose with a wrench and remove the front flexible hose.

    NOTICE:
    1. Do not bend or damage the brake tube.
    2. Do not allow any foreign matter such as dirt and dust to enter the brake tube from the connecting point.

  1. Remove the front flexible hose.
