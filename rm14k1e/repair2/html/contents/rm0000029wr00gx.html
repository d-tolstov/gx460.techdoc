1. INSTALL REAR PROPELLER SHAFT UNIVERSAL JOINT SPIDER BEARING

HINT:
Use the same procedure for all rear propeller shaft universal joint spider bearing.




  1. Apply MP grease to the universal joint spider and new bearings.

    NOTICE:
    Be careful not to apply too much grease.

  1. Install the universal joint spider to the propeller shaft.




  1. Using SST, install 2 of the bearings to the universal joint spider.

    SST
    09332-25010  

  1. Using SST, adjust both bearings so that the snap ring grooves are at maximum and equal width.

    SST
    09332-25010  




  1. Install 4 new snap rings of equal thickness which will allow no axial play.

    HINT:
    Do not reuse the snap rings.

    NOTICE:
    1. Must use a new retainer ring.
    2. Must use retainer rings of the same thickness as possible on both ends.

  1. Using a hammer, tap the yoke until there is no clearance between the spider bearing outer race and snap ring.

    HINT:
    Install a new spider bearing on the sleeve side in the procedure described above.

3. INSPECT REAR PROPELLER SHAFT UNIVERSAL JOINT SPIDER BEARING

NOTICE:
Be careful not to grip the propeller shaft tube too tightly in a vise as this will cause deformation.

  1. Check the spider bearings for wear or damage.
    If necessary, replace the spider bearing.




  1. Check the spider bearing axial play by turning the yoke with holding the shaft tightly.

    Maximum bearing axial play:
    0 mm (0 in.)

    If the axial play is more than the maximum, replace the spider bearing.
