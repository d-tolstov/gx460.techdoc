DTC    C1782/82    Low Battery Positive Voltage

for Preparation Click here


DESCRIPTION


DTC Code
DTC Detection Condition
Trouble Area
C1782/82
The voltage at terminal IG or B is 10 V or less or 16 V or higher for 0.5 seconds.
  1. Battery
  2. Charging system
  3. AIR SUS fuse
  4. AIR SUS NO. 2 fuse
  5. AVS fuse
  6. Harness or connector
  7. Suspension control ECU
