1. DISPOSE OF REAR SHOCK ABSORBER ASSEMBLY LH

  1. Fully extend the rear shock absorber rod.




  1. Using a drill, make a hole in the cylinder between A and B in the illustration to discharge the gas inside.

    CAUTION:
    Be careful when drilling because shards of metal may fly about. Always use the proper safety equipment.

    NOTICE:
    The gas is colorless, odorless and harmless.
