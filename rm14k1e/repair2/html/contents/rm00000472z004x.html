SEAT HEATER SWITCH (for Front Side) > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here



1. REMOVE INSTRUMENT PANEL FINISH PANEL END LH



  1. Detach the 11 clips and remove the instrument panel finish panel end.



2. REMOVE INSTRUMENT PANEL FINISH PANEL END RH



  1. Detach the 11 clips and remove the instrument panel finish panel end.



3. REMOVE SHIFT LEVER KNOB SUB-ASSEMBLY



  1. Twist the shift lever knob in the direction indicated by the arrow and remove it.



4. REMOVE UPPER CONSOLE PANEL SUB-ASSEMBLY



  1. Put protective tape around the upper console panel.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape




  1. Using a moulding remover, detach the 7 clips.

  1. Disconnect each connector and move the upper console panel in the direction of the arrow to remove it.



5. REMOVE REFRESHING SEAT SWITCH



  1. Disconnect the connector.

  1. Using a screwdriver, detach the 4 claws and remove the switch.

    HINT:
    Use the same procedure for both switches.
