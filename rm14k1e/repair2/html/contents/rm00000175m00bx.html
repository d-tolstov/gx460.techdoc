AIR PUMP > INSTALLATION

for Preparation Click here



1. INSTALL AIR PUMP ASSEMBLY



  1. Attach the 3 fittings of the air pump insulator to install the air pump to the bracket.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Insulator
    *2
    		Air Pump Assembly
    *3
    		Air Pump Bracket

  1. Attach the 2 air pump connector clamps.

  1. Install the air pump with bracket with the 3 bolts.

    Torque:
    20 N*m{ 204 kgf*cm , 15 ft.*lbf }

  1. Connect the air pump connector and attach the wire harness clamp.



2. CONNECT NO. 4 AIR INJECTION SYSTEM HOSE



  1. Connect the No. 4 air injection system hose so that its paint mark aligns with the rib of the air pump as shown in the illustration.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Rib
    *2
    		Paint Mark
    *a
    		Front
    *b
    		Upper Side

    HINT:
    1. Make sure the paint mark of the No. 4 air injection system hose is facing upward.
    2. Make sure the direction of the hose clamp is as shown in the illustration.



3. INSTALL UPPER RADIATOR SUPPORT SEAL

  1. Install the upper radiator support seal with the 13 clips.
