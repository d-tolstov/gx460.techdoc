1. INSTALL FUEL PRESSURE REGULATOR ASSEMBLY

  1. Apply a light coat of gasoline or spindle oil to a new O-ring and install the O-ring to the pressure regulator.

    NOTICE:
    1. Make sure that there are no scratches or foreign matter in or around the insertion hole of the delivery pipe.
    2. When inserting the pressure regulator, be careful not to damage the O-ring.




  1. While turning the pressure regulator left and right, install it to the delivery pipe.

  1. Install the 2 bolts.

    Torque:
    10 N*m{ 102 kgf*cm , 7 ft.*lbf }

  1. Connect the No. 1 air hose to the pressure regulator.

  1. Connect the No. 2 fuel tube to the pressure regulator (Click here).



2. CONNECT CABLE TO NEGATIVE BATTERY TERMINAL

NOTICE:
When disconnecting the cable, some systems need to be initialized after the cable is reconnected (Click here).



3. INSTALL AIR CLEANER CAP AND HOSE



  1. Attach the 4 clamps to install the air cleaner cap and hose.
    Text in Illustration

    *2

    *3

    *a

    HINT:
    When installing the air cleaner hose, align its groove with the protrusion of the throttle body as shown in the illustration.

  1. Tighten the hose clamp.

    Torque:
    5.0 N*m{ 51 kgf*cm , 44 in.*lbf }

    HINT:
    Make sure the direction of the hose clamp is as shown in the illustration.

  1. Connect the mass air flow meter connector, No. 1 air hose and No. 2 ventilation hose and attach the wire harness clamp.



4. INSTALL V-BANK COVER SUB-ASSEMBLY



  1. Attach the 2 V-bank cover hooks to the No. 1 V-bank cover bracket. Then align the 2 V-bank cover grommets with the 2 pins and press down on the V-bank cover to attach the pins.

5. INSPECT FOR FUEL LEAK

  1. Make sure that there are no fuel leaks after performing maintenance on the fuel system.

    1. Connect the intelligent tester to the DLC3.

    2. Turn the engine switch on (IG) and turn the intelligent tester on.

      NOTICE:
      Do not start the engine.

    3. Enter the following menus: Powertrain / Engine and ECT / Active Test / Control the Fuel Pump / Speed.

    4. Check that there are no leaks from the fuel system.
      If there are fuel leaks, repair or replace parts as necessary.

    5. Turn the engine switch off.

    6. Disconnect the intelligent tester from the DLC3.
