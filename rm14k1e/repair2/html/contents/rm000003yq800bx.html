for Preparation Click here



1. CHECK REAR SEAT SIDE AIRBAG ASSEMBLY (VEHICLE NOT INVOLVED IN COLLISION)

  1. Perform a diagnostic system check (Click here).

  1. With the rear seat side airbag installed on the vehicle, perform a visual check. If there are any defects as mentioned below, replace the rear seat with a new one:
    Cuts, minute cracks or marked discoloration on the rear seatback around the rear seat side airbag.



2. CHECK REAR SEAT SIDE AIRBAG ASSEMBLY (VEHICLE INVOLVED IN COLLISION AND AIRBAG HAS NOT DEPLOYED)

  1. Perform a diagnostic system check (Click here).

  1. With the rear seat side airbag removed from the vehicle, perform a visual check. If there are any defects as mentioned below, replace the rear seat side airbag with a new one:

    1. Cuts, minute cracks or marked discoloration on the rear seat side airbag.
    2. Cracks or other damage to the wire harness or connector.

      NOTICE:
      For removal and installation of the rear seat side airbag, be sure to follow the correct procedure.
