1. REMOVE REAR NO. 2 SEAT ASSEMBLY

  1. Remove the rear No. 2 seat assembly (Click here).



2. REMOVE REAR NO. 1 SEAT ASSEMBLY

  1. for 60/40 Split Slide Walk-in Seat Type LH Side:
    Remove the rear No. 1 seat assembly (Click here).

  1. for 60/40 Split Slide Walk-in Seat Type RH Side:
    Remove the rear No. 1 seat assembly (Click here).



3. REMOVE REAR NO. 1 FLOOR STEP COVER



HINT:
Use the same procedure for all rear No. 1 floor step covers.

  1. Using a screwdriver, detach the 2 claws and remove the rear No. 1 floor step cover.

    HINT:
    Tape the screwdriver tip before use.

4. REMOVE QUARTER SCUFF PLATE LH

5. REMOVE QUARTER SCUFF PLATE RH

6. REMOVE REAR DOOR SCUFF PLATE LH



  1. Put protective tape around the rear door scuff plate.

  1. Using a screwdriver, detach the 3 clips, 6 claws and 2 guides and remove the rear door scuff plate.

    HINT:
    Tape the screwdriver tip before use.

17. REMOVE DECK TRIM SIDE PANEL ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Remove the bolt and disconnect the rear No. 1 seat outer belt floor anchor.




  1. Remove the bolt and disconnect the rear No. 2 seat outer belt floor anchor.

  1. Remove the 2 bolts and 2 screws.

  1. Detach the 3 clips and 8 claws.



  1. Disconnect each connector and remove the deck trim side panel.





18. REMOVE DECK TRIM SIDE PANEL ASSEMBLY RH



  1. Remove the bolt and disconnect the rear No. 1 seat outer belt floor anchor.




  1. Remove the bolt and disconnect the rear No. 2 seat outer belt floor anchor.

  1. Remove the 2 bolts and screw.

  1. Detach the 3 clips and 8 claws.



  1. Disconnect each connector and remove the deck trim side panel.





19. REMOVE INDOOR NO. 2 ELECTRICAL KEY ANTENNA ASSEMBLY



  1. Fold back the front floor carpet assembly.

  1. Disconnect the connector.

  1. Detach the 2 claws and remove the indoor No. 2 electrical key antenna assembly.
