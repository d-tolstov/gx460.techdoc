FUEL TANK > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here



1. DISCHARGE FUEL SYSTEM PRESSURE

CAUTION:
  1. Do not disconnect any part of the fuel system until you have discharged the fuel system pressure.
  2. After discharging the fuel pressure, place a cloth or equivalent over fittings as you separate them to reduce the risk of fuel spray on yourself or in the engine compartment.




  1. Disconnect the 2 fuel pump ECU connectors.

  1. Connect the cable to the negative (-) battery terminal.

  1. Start the engine. After the engine has stopped on its own, turn the engine switch off.

    HINT:
    DTC P0171/0174 (system too lean) may be stored.

  1. Crank the engine again, and then check that the engine does not start.

  1. Loosen the fuel tank cap, and then discharge the pressure in the fuel tank completely.

  1. Disconnect the cable from the negative (-) battery terminal.

    NOTICE:
    1. w/ Navigation System (for HDD):
      After the engine switch is turned off, the display and navigation module display (HDD navigation system) records various types of memory and settings. As a result, after turning the engine switch off, be sure to wait for the time specified in the following table before disconnecting the cable from the negative (-) battery terminal.

      Waiting Time before Disconnecting Cable from Negative (-) Battery Terminal:

      Condition
      		Waiting Time
      Vehicle enrolled in G-BOOK system
      		6 minutes
      Vehicle not enrolled in G-BOOK system
      		1 minute

    2. When disconnecting the cable, some systems need to be initialized after the cable is reconnected (Click here).

  1. Connect the 2 fuel pump ECU connectors.



2. REMOVE REAR SEAT ASSEMBLY LH

  1. for 60/40 Split Slide Walk-in Seat Type LH Side:
    Remove the rear seat assembly LH (Click here).



3. REMOVE REAR FLOOR SERVICE HOLE COVER



  1. Remove the 3 screws and rear floor service hole cover.



4. REMOVE NO. 1 FUEL TANK PROTECTOR SUB-ASSEMBLY



  1. Remove the 6 bolts and No. 1 fuel tank protector.



5. DISCONNECT FUEL TANK MAIN TUBE SUB-ASSEMBLY



  1. Disconnect the fuel tank main tube (Click here).



6. DRAIN FUEL

  1. Connect the intelligent tester to the DLC3.

  1. Turn the engine switch on (IG).

    NOTICE:
    Do not start the engine.

  1. Turn the intelligent tester on.

  1. Enter the following menus: Powertrain / Engine and ECT / Active Test / Control the Fuel Pump / Speed.

  1. Operate the fuel pump and drain the fuel from the fuel tank main tube.

    CAUTION:
    1. Do not smoke or be near an open flame when working on the fuel system.
    2. Secure good ventilation.
    3. Keep gasoline away from rubber or leather parts.




  1. Disconnect the fuel pump and sender gauge connector.



7. DISCONNECT CABLE FROM NEGATIVE BATTERY TERMINAL

NOTICE:
  1. w/ Navigation System (for HDD):
    After the engine switch is turned off, the display and navigation module display (HDD navigation system) records various types of memory and settings. As a result, after turning the engine switch off, be sure to wait for the time specified in the following table before disconnecting the cable from the negative (-) battery terminal.

    Waiting Time before Disconnecting Cable from Negative (-) Battery Terminal:

    Condition
    		Waiting Time
    Vehicle enrolled in G-BOOK system
    		6 minutes
    Vehicle not enrolled in G-BOOK system
    		1 minute

  2. When disconnecting the cable, some systems need to be initialized after the cable is reconnected (Click here).



8. DISCONNECT FUEL RETURN TUBE SUB-ASSEMBLY



  1. Disconnect the fuel return tube (Click here).



9. DISCONNECT FUEL CUT OFF TUBE



  1. Disconnect the fuel cut off tube (Click here).



10. DISCONNECT FUEL TANK BREATHER TUBE SUB-ASSEMBLY



  1. Disconnect the fuel tank breather tube (Click here).



11. DISCONNECT FUEL TANK TO FILLER PIPE HOSE



  1. Disconnect the fuel tank to filler pipe hose from the filler pipe.



12. REMOVE FUEL TANK SUB-ASSEMBLY

  1. Place a transmission jack under the fuel tank.




  1. Remove the 2 bolts, 2 clips, 2 pins and 2 fuel tank bands.

  1. Slowly lower the transmission jack slightly.



13. REMOVE FUEL TANK CUSHION



  1. Remove the No. 1, No. 2 and No. 3 fuel tank cushions from the fuel tank.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		No. 1 Fuel Tank Cushion
    *2
    		No. 2 Fuel Tank Cushion
    *3
    		No. 3 Fuel Tank Cushion



14. REMOVE FUEL TANK MAIN TUBE SUB-ASSEMBLY AND FUEL RETURN TUBE SUB-ASSEMBLY



  1. Remove the 2 fuel tube joint clips and pull out the fuel tank main tube and fuel return tube.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Fuel Tube
    *2
    		Fuel Tube Joint
    *3
    		Fuel Tube Joint Clip
    *4
    		O-ring

    NOTICE:
    1. Remove any dirt and foreign matter on the fuel tube joint before performing this work.
    2. Do not allow any scratches or foreign matter on the parts when disconnecting them, as the fuel tube joint contains the O-rings that seal the plug.
    3. Perform this work by hand. Do not use any tools.
    4. Do not forcibly bend, twist or turn the nylon tube.
    5. Protect the disconnected part by covering it with a plastic bag and tape after disconnecting the fuel tubes.




  1. Remove the fuel tank main tube and fuel return tube from the fuel tank.



15. REMOVE FUEL SUCTION WITH PUMP AND GAUGE TUBE ASSEMBLY



  1. Using SST, loosen the retainer.

    SST
    09808-14020  

    HINT:
    Fit the tips of SST onto the ribs of the retainer.

    NOTICE:
    1. When the retainer is loosened, be careful as the pump and gauge tube will spring upward from the force of the spring.
    2. Remove any foreign matter around the fuel suction with pump and gauge tube before this operation.

  1. Remove the retainer.

  1. Remove the fuel suction with pump and gauge tube assembly from the fuel tank.

    NOTICE:
    Be careful not to bend the arm of the fuel sender gauge.

  1. Remove the gasket from the fuel tank.



16. REMOVE NO. 3 FUEL TANK PROTECTOR



  1. Remove the 2 bolts.

  1. Detach the 4 clamps and remove the No. 3 fuel tank protector.



17. REMOVE FUEL TANK TO FILLER PIPE HOSE



  1. Remove the fuel tank to filler pipe hose from the fuel tank.
