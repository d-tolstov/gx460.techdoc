CONDENSER FAN > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here



1. REMOVE UPPER RADIATOR SUPPORT SEAL



  1. Remove the 13 clips and upper radiator support seal.



2. REMOVE RADIATOR GRILLE

  1. Remove the radiator grille (Click here).



3. REMOVE SMOG VENTILATION SENSOR



  1. Disconnect the connector.

  1. Remove the bolt and smog ventilation sensor.



4. REMOVE MILLIMETER WAVE RADAR SENSOR ASSEMBLY



  1. Disconnect the sensor connector.

  1. Remove the 3 bolts and sensor.



5. REMOVE HOOD LOCK RELEASE LEVER PROTECTOR



  1. Detach the 2 clamps and 2 guides and remove the hood lock release lever protector.



6. REMOVE HOOD LOCK ASSEMBLY



  1. Remove the 3 bolts.




  1. Disconnect the connector.

  1. Disconnect the hood lock control cable and remove the hood lock assembly.



7. REMOVE AIR CONDITIONING TUBE ASSEMBLY

  1. Detach the clamp, remove the 2 bolts and the air conditioning tube assembly.

    HINT:
    It is not necessary to completely remove the air conditioning tube. Move the tube so that the shroud blower can be removed.



8. REMOVE SHROUD BLOWER ASSEMBLY



  1. Disconnect the connector.

  1. Remove the bolt and shroud blower as shown in the illustration.
