1. DISPOSE OF HOOD SUPPORT ASSEMBLY

  1. Horizontally hold the hood support in a vise with the piston rod pulled out.




  1. Wear safety glasses. Gradually make a cut between A and B shown in the illustration using a metal saw to release the gas.

    Standard:
    Area
    		Specified Condition
    A - B
    		50 mm (1.97 in.)

    NOTICE:
    Although the gas inside the hood support is colorless, odorless and harmless, there is a possibility that metal debris could scatter. Therefore, cover the hood support with a piece of cloth or other material.
