REAR STABILIZER BAR > INSTALLATION

for Preparation Click here



1. INSTALL REAR STABILIZER LINK ASSEMBLY

  1. Install the rear stabilizer link assembly with the bolt and nut.

    Torque:
    100 N*m{ 1020 kgf*cm , 74 ft.*lbf }

    HINT:
    Turn the nut while holding the bolt.



2. INSTALL REAR STABILIZER CONTROL CYLINDER

  1. Install the 2 bleeder plug caps to the rear stabilizer control cylinder.

  1. Install the rear stabilizer control cylinder with the bolt and nut.

    Torque:
    100 N*m{ 1020 kgf*cm , 74 ft.*lbf }

    NOTICE:
    Do not hold the rear stabilizer control cylinder by the cylinder boot.

    HINT:
    Turn the nut while holding the bolt.



3. CONNECT REAR STABILIZER CONTROL TUBE ASSEMBLY



  1. Connect the rear stabilizer control tube to the rear stabilizer control cylinder with the 2 union bolts and 2 new pressure port gaskets.

    Torque:
    69 N*m{ 704 kgf*cm , 51 ft.*lbf }

    NOTICE:
    Insert the stoppers of the rear stabilizer control tube into the rear stabilizer control cylinder.



4. TEMPORARILY INSTALL REAR STABILIZER BAR SUB-ASSEMBLY



  1. Install the 2 rear stabilizer bushes to the rear stabilizer bar.

    HINT:
    Install the stabilizer bush to the outer side of the stabilizer bush stopper on the stabilizer bar.

    Text in Illustration 

    		Outer Side

  1. Temporarily install the rear stabilizer bar with the 2 bolts and nuts.



5. INSTALL REAR STABILIZER LOWER BRACKET



  1. Support the rear stabilizer bar with a jack.

    NOTICE:
    Place a wooden block between the jack and rear stabilizer bar to prevent damage.




  1. Install the rear stabilizer bar and 2 rear stabilizer lower brackets with the 4 bolts.

    Torque:
    45 N*m{ 459 kgf*cm , 33 ft.*lbf }

    HINT:
    Check that the protrusions on the rear stabilizer bush are positioned within 180° of the rear stabilizer control cylinder and rear stabilizer link.

    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Rear Stabilizer Control Cylinder
    *2
    		Rear Stabilizer Link



6. BLEED SUSPENSION FLUID

  1. Bleed the suspension fluid (Click here).



7. STABILIZE SUSPENSION

  1. Lower the vehicle.

  1. Bounce the vehicle up and down several times to stabilize the suspension.



8. TIGHTEN REAR STABILIZER BAR SUB-ASSEMBLY

  1. Tighten the 2 bolts and 2 nuts.

    Torque:
    110 N*m{ 1122 kgf*cm , 81 ft.*lbf }

    NOTICE:
    Turn the bolt while holding the nut.



9. INSTALL STABILIZER CONTROL VALVE PROTECTOR

  1. Install the stabilizer control valve protector with the 2 bolts.

    Torque:
    29 N*m{ 296 kgf*cm , 21 ft.*lbf }



10. INSTALL SIDE STEP ASSEMBLY LH

  1. Attach the 6 clips to install the side step.

  1. Install the 3 screws.

  1. Using a T30 "TORX" socket wrench, install the 2 screws.

    Torque:
    3.0 N*m{ 31 kgf*cm , 27 in.*lbf }

  1. Install the side step with the 6 bolts.

    Torque:
    18 N*m{ 184 kgf*cm , 13 ft.*lbf }

  1. Connect the connector.

  1. Attach the clamp.



11. INSPECT VEHICLE HEIGHT

  1. Inspect the vehicle height (Click here).
