1. INSPECT COOLER CONDENSER ASSEMBLY

  1. If the fins of the cooler condenser are dirty, clean them with water. Dry the fins with compressed air.

    NOTICE:
    Do not damage the fins of the condenser.

  1. If a fin of the cooler condenser is bent, straighten it using a screwdriver or pliers.



2. CHECK CONDENSER FOR LEAKAGE OF REFRIGERANT

  1. Using a halogen leak detector, check the pipe joints for gas leakage.

  1. If gas leakage is detected in a joint, check the torque of the joint.
