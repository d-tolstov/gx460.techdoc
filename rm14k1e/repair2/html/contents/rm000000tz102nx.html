DESCRIPTION

The headlight cleaner control relay detects the low beam headlights status.

WIRING DIAGRAM




INSPECTION PROCEDURE

NOTICE:
First check that the low beam headlights operate normally.

1.CHECK HEADLIGHT CLEANER CONTROL RELAY (HDLO VOLTAGE)



  1. Disconnect the A32 headlight cleaner control relay connector.

  1. Measure the voltage according to the value(s) in the table below.

    Standard Voltage:
    Tester Connection
    		Switch Condition
    		Specified Condition
    A32-1 (HDLO) - Body ground
    		Headlight dimmer switch in head
    		11 to 14 V
    A32-1 (HDLO) - Body ground
    		Headlight dimmer switch not in head
    		Below 1 V

    Text in Illustration 
    *a
    		Component without harness connected
    (Headlight Cleaner Control Relay)

2.CHECK HARNESS AND CONNECTOR (MAIN BODY ECU - HEADLIGHT CLEANER CONTROL RELAY)

  1. Disconnect the G46 main body ECU.

  1. Disconnect the A32 headlight cleaner control relay connector.

  1. Measure the resistance according to the value(s) in the table below.

    Standard Resistance:
    Tester Connection
    		Condition
    		Specified Condition
    G46-16 (HDLO) - A32-1 (HDLO)
    		Always
    		Below 1 Ω
    G46-16 (HDLO) - Body ground
    		Always
    		10 kΩ or higher
