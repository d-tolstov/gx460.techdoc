1. INSTALL FAN AND GENERATOR V BELT



  1. Set the fan and generator V belt onto every part.

    *7

  1. While turning the belt tensioner counterclockwise, remove the pin.

    NOTICE:
    Make sure that the fan and generator V belt is properly set on each pulley.

  1. Check that the belt fits properly in the ribbed grooves.

    HINT:
    Make sure to check by hand that the belt has not slipped out of the grooves on the bottom of the pulley.



2. INSTALL UPPER RADIATOR SUPPORT SEAL

  1. Install the upper radiator support seal with the 13 clips.



3. INSTALL V-BANK COVER SUB-ASSEMBLY



  1. Attach the 2 V-bank cover hooks to the No. 1 V-bank cover bracket. Then align the 2 V-bank cover grommets with the 2 pins and press down on the V-bank cover to attach the pins.
