DISPLAY AND NAVIGATION ASSEMBLY > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here



1. DISCONNECT CABLE FROM NEGATIVE BATTERY TERMINAL

NOTICE:
  1. w/ Navigation System (for HDD):
    After the engine switch is turned off, the display and navigation module display (HDD navigation system) records various types of memory and settings. As a result, after turning the engine switch off, be sure to wait for the time specified in the following table before disconnecting the cable from the negative (-) battery terminal.

    Waiting Time before Disconnecting Cable from Negative (-) Battery Terminal:

    Condition
    		Waiting Time
    Vehicle enrolled in G-BOOK system
    		6 minutes
    Vehicle not enrolled in G-BOOK system
    		1 minute

  2. When disconnecting the cable, some systems need to be initialized after the cable is reconnected (Click here).



2. REMOVE INSTRUMENT PANEL FINISH PANEL END LH



  1. Detach the 11 clips and remove the instrument panel finish panel end.



3. REMOVE INSTRUMENT PANEL FINISH PANEL END RH



  1. Detach the 11 clips and remove the instrument panel finish panel end.



4. REMOVE FRONT CONSOLE UPPER PANEL GARNISH



  1. Detach the 2 clips and remove the front upper console panel garnish.



5. REMOVE SHIFT LEVER KNOB SUB-ASSEMBLY



  1. Twist the shift lever knob in the direction indicated by the arrow and remove it.



6. REMOVE UPPER CONSOLE PANEL SUB-ASSEMBLY



  1. Put protective tape around the upper console panel.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape




  1. Using a moulding remover, detach the 7 clips.

  1. Disconnect each connector and move the upper console panel in the direction of the arrow to remove it.



7. REMOVE RADIO RECEIVER ASSEMBLY WITH BRACKET


  1. w/o Navigation System:




    1. Remove the 4 bolts.




    2. Pull the radio receiver to detach the 6 clips and rib on the backside of the radio receiver.

    3. Disconnect the connectors and remove the radio receiver.



  1. w/ Navigation System:




    1. Remove the 4 bolts.




    2. Pull the radio receiver to detach the 6 clips and 3 ribs on the backside of the radio receiver.

    3. Disconnect the connectors and remove the radio receiver.



8. REMOVE NO. 2 INSTRUMENT PANEL REGISTER ASSEMBLY



  1. Detach the 8 clips and remove the No. 2 instrument panel register.



9. REMOVE CENTER INSTRUMENT CLUSTER FINISH PANEL GARNISH



  1. Detach the 13 clips and remove the center instrument cluster finish panel garnish together with the instrument cluster finish panel garnish.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Center Instrument Cluster Finish Panel Garnish
    *2
    		Instrument Cluster Finish Panel Garnish



10. REMOVE LOWER INSTRUMENT PANEL FINISH PANEL ASSEMBLY



  1. Detach the 4 clips.

  1. Disconnect each connector and cooler thermistor and remove the instrument panel finish panel.



11. REMOVE DISPLAY AND NAVIGATION MODULE DISPLAY



  1. Remove the 4 bolts.




  1. Pull the display and navigation module display to detach the 7 clips on the backside of the display and navigation module display.

  1. Disconnect the connectors and remove the display and navigation module display.



12. REMOVE NO. 1 RADIO BRACKET



  1. Detach the claw and clamp and remove the connector.




  1. Remove the 3 bolts and No. 1 radio bracket.



13. REMOVE NO. 2 RADIO BRACKET



  1. Remove the 3 bolts and No. 2 radio bracket.



14. REMOVE CENTER INSTRUMENT PANEL REGISTER ASSEMBLY



  1. Detach the 8 claws and remove the center instrument panel register.



15. REMOVE HAZARD WARNING SIGNAL SWITCH ASSEMBLY



  1. Detach the 2 claws and remove the switch.
