DTC    C1337    Diameter of the Tire is not Uniform

for Preparation Click here


DESCRIPTION

The skid control ECU measures the speed of each wheel by receiving signals from the speed sensor. These signals are used for recognizing that all 4 wheels are operating properly. Therefore, all wheel signals must be equal.

DTC Code
DTC Detection Condition
Trouble Area
C1337
Either condition is met:
  1. The diameter of the 2 front wheels and 2 rear wheels is different.
  2. There is a wheel speed sensor fault.
  1. Speed sensor
  2. Speed sensor rotor
  3. Speed sensor circuit
  4. Tire size

INSPECTION PROCEDURE

NOTICE:
When replacing the master cylinder solenoid, perform calibration (Click here).

HINT:
When DTCs C1401 to C1416 are output with DTC C1337, inspect and repair the trouble areas indicated by DTCs C1401 to C1416 first (Click here).

1.CHECK TIRE SIZE

  1. Check tire size and condition of all 4 wheels.

    OK:
    Condition of all 4 wheels is normal and tire size is same.
