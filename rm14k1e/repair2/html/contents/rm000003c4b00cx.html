1. INSPECT COMBINATION SWITCH ASSEMBLY (CRAWL CONTROL SWITCH)



  1. Measure the resistance according to the value(s) in the table below.

    Standard Resistance:
    Tester Connection
    		Switch Condition
    		Specified Condition
    5 (UP) - 9 (E)
    		SPEED (UP): Pressed
    		Below 1 Ω
    SPEED (UP): Not Pressed
    		10 kΩ or higher
    3 (DN) - 9 (E)
    		SPEED (DOWN): Pressed
    		Below 1 Ω
    SPEED (DOWN): Not Pressed
    		10 kΩ or higher
    6 (ECU) - 9 (E)
    		ON/OFF: Pressed
    		Below 1 Ω
    ON/OFF: Not pressed
    		10 kΩ or higher

    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Speed Selector Switch
    *2

    *a

    If the result is not as specified, replace the combination switch assembly (crawl control switch).
