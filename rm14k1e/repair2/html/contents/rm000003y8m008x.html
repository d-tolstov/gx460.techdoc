DTC    C1621    Back Camera Power Supply Failure

DTC    C1622    Open or Short Circuit in Back Camera Signal

for Preparation Click here


DESCRIPTION

This DTC is stored if the parking assist ECU judges as a result of its self check that the signals or signal lines between the parking assist ECU and the rear television camera assembly are not normal.

DTC Code
DTC Detection Condition
Trouble Area
C1621
Rear television camera power supply failure
  1. Harness or connector
  2. Rear television camera assembly
  3. Parking assist ECU
C1622
Open or Short Circuit in rear television camera signal
  1. Harness or connector
  2. Rear television camera assembly
  3. Parking assist ECU
