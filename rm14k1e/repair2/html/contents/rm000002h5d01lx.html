POWER STEERING OIL PRESSURE SWITCH > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here



1. REMOVE FAN AND GENERATOR V BELT

  1. Remove the fan and generator V belt (Click here).



2. REMOVE FRONT WHEEL


3. REMOVE FRONT FENDER APRON SEAL RH

  1. Remove the 4 clips and front fender apron seal RH.



4. DRAIN POWER STEERING FLUID


5. REMOVE POWER STEERING OIL PRESSURE SWITCH



  1. Disconnect the power steering oil pressure switch connector.

  1. Remove the power steering oil pressure switch.

  1. Remove the O-ring from the power steering oil pressure switch.
