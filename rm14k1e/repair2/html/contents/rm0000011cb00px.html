PARKING BRAKE ASSEMBLY > DISASSEMBLY

for Preparation Click here


HINT:
  1. Use the same procedure for the RH and LH sides.
  2. The procedure listed below is for the LH side.



1. REMOVE REAR WHEEL


2. DISCONNECT REAR DISC BRAKE CYLINDER ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Remove the 2 bolts and disconnect the rear disc brake cylinder.

    NOTICE:
    1. Do not disconnect the flexible hose from the disc brake cylinder.
    2. Do not twist or bend the flexible hose.



3. REMOVE REAR DISC



  1. Place matchmarks on the rear disc and axle hub if planning to reuse the disc.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Matchmark




  1. Turn the shoe adjuster until the disc turns freely, and then remove the disc.
    Text in Illustration 

    		Shoe Adjuster Contracts

    		Shoe Adjuster Expands



4. REMOVE PARKING BRAKE SHOE RETURN TENSION SPRING



  1. Remove the 2 parking brake shoe return tension springs.



5. DISCONNECT NO. 1 PARKING BRAKE SHOE ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Remove the parking brake shoe hold down spring cup and parking brake shoe hold down spring to disconnect the No. 1 parking brake shoe assembly from the backing plate.



6. REMOVE PARKING BRAKE SHOE STRUT LH



  1. Remove the parking brake shoe strut LH and parking brake shoe strut compression spring.



7. DISCONNECT NO. 2 PARKING BRAKE SHOE ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Remove the parking brake shoe hold down spring cup and parking brake shoe hold down spring to disconnect the No. 2 parking brake shoe assembly from the backing plate.



8. REMOVE PARKING BRAKE SHOE ADJUSTING SCREW SET



  1. Remove the parking brake shoe adjusting screw set.



9. REMOVE NO. 1 PARKING BRAKE SHOE ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Disconnect the parking brake shoe return tension spring to remove the No. 1 parking brake shoe assembly.



10. REMOVE PARKING BRAKE SHOE RETURN TENSION SPRING



  1. Remove the parking brake shoe return tension spring from the No. 2 parking brake shoe assembly.



11. REMOVE NO. 2 PARKING BRAKE SHOE ASSEMBLY WITH PARKING BRAKE SHOE LEVER



  1. Using needle-nose pliers, disconnect the No. 3 parking brake cable assembly from the parking brake shoe lever as shown in the illustration.

    NOTICE:
    Be careful not to damage the No. 3 parking brake cable assembly.



12. REMOVE PARKING BRAKE SHOE LEVER



  1. Remove the C-washer, shim and parking brake shoe lever from the No. 2 parking brake shoe assembly as shown in the illustration.



13. REMOVE PARKING BRAKE SHOE HOLD DOWN SPRING PIN

  1. Remove the parking brake shoe hold down spring pin (for front side).

  1. Remove the parking brake shoe hold down spring pin (for rear side).
