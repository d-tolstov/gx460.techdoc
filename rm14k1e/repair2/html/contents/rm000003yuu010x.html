DTC    B15ED    Backup Battery Correspondence Not Detected

for Preparation Click here


DESCRIPTION

If the telematics transceiver receives a signal indicating that the mayday battery is not compatible as a result of the self-check of the mayday battery, this DTC will be stored.

DTC Code
DTC Detection Condition
Trouble Area
B15ED
The installed mayday battery is not compatible.
  1. Mayday battery
  2. Telematics transceiver

INSPECTION PROCEDURE

HINT:
When replacing the telematics transceiver, perform the vehicle contract setting (Click here).

  1. Clear the DTCs (Click here).
