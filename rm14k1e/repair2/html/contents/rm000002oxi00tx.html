WIPER AND WASHER SYSTEM > Headlight Cleaner Motor and Relay Circuit

for Preparation Click here


DESCRIPTION

This circuit provides power to the headlight cleaner control relay.
The headlight cleaner control relay sends the signal from the headlight cleaner switch assembly, etc. to the headlight cleaner motor and pump assembly to operate the headlight cleaner system.
