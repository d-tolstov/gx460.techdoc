DTC    B2603    Tilt and Telescopic Manual Switch Circuit Malfunction


DESCRIPTION

WIRING DIAGRAM

INSPECTION PROCEDURE

READ VALUE USING INTELLIGENT TESTER (TILT UP/DOWN SWITCH, TELESCO SHORT/LONG SWITCH)

CONFIRM DTC

CHECK HARNESS AND CONNECTOR (MULTIPLEX TILT AND TELESCOPIC ECU - HEADLIGHT DIMMER SWITCH)

CHECK MULTIPLEX TILT AND TELESCOPIC ECU (VC TERMINAL VOLTAGE)

CHECK TILT AND TELESCOPIC SWITCH
