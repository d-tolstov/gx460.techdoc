INSPECTION PROCEDURE

1.CHECK DISPLAY SETTING



  1. Enter the display adjustment screen by pressing the "DISP" switch.

  1. Reset the display settings (contrast and brightness) and check that the screen appears normal.

    OK:
    The display returns to normal.



NG
Go to step 2
OK

END 

2.CHECK CABIN TEMPERATURE

  1. Check that the cabin temperature is higher than -20°C (-4.0°F).

    OK:
    The cabin temperature is higher than -20°C (-4.0°F).



NG
HEAT CABIN AND RECHECK TEMPERATURE
OK


3.CHECK COLOR BAR



  1. Enter the "Display Check" mode and select "Color Bar Check" (Click here).

  1. Check that the color bars match the displayed names.

    OK:
    Color bars match the displayed names.



NG
REPLACE DISPLAY AND NAVIGATION MODULE DISPLAY (Click here)
OK
