SIDE TURN SIGNAL LIGHT ASSEMBLY > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here


HINT:
  1. Use the same procedure for the RH and LH sides.
  2. The procedure listed below is for the LH side.



1. REMOVE OUTER REAR VIEW MIRROR LH

  1. Remove the outer rear view mirror LH (Click here).



2. REMOVE OUTER REAR VIEW MIRROR ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Push the upper part of the mirror surface and tilt it.

  1. Using a moulding remover, detach the 4 claws and separate the outer mirror from the mirror body.

    HINT:
    Apply protective tape to the outer mirror to prevent it from being damaged.

    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape




  1. Disconnect the 2 connectors and remove the outer mirror.



3. REMOVE OUTER MIRROR COVER LH



HINT:
Be sure to detach the claws of the outer mirror cover in the order shown in the illustration.




  1. Using 2 screwdrivers, detach the 2 claws.

    HINT:
    Tape the screwdriver tip before use.

    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape



  1. Insert a screwdriver into the slot as shown in the illustration and push on the outer mirror body to create a space between the outer mirror body and outer mirror cover.

    NOTICE:
    Be careful not to break the ribs.

    HINT:
    Tape the screwdriver tip before use.





    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape
    		*2
    		Rib
    *a
    		Inner Side of Outer Mirror Cover
    		-
    		-




  1. Insert a moulding remover into the space made between the outer mirror body and outer mirror cover.

    NOTICE:
    Do not insert the moulding remover more than 4 mm (0.157 in.).

    HINT:
    Tape the moulding remover before use.

    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape




  1. Slide the moulding remover downwards as shown in the illustration to detach the 2 claws.

    NOTICE:
    1. Do not insert the moulding remover more than 4 mm (0.157 in.).
    2. Do not slide the moulding remover past the point approximately 40 mm (1.57 in.) from the bottom edge of the outer mirror cover as the outer mirror body will become damaged.

    Standard:
    Area
    		Specified Condition
    a
    		40.0 mm (1.57 in.)

  1. Remove the moulding remover.




  1. Insert a moulding remover between the outer mirror cover and outer mirror body as shown in the illustration and detach the claw.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape




  1. Using a screwdriver, detach the claw.

    HINT:
    Tape the screwdriver tip before use.

    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape




  1. Detach the 2 claws and remove the outer mirror cover.

    NOTICE:
    When removing the cover, be careful not to damage the side turn signal light assembly or cover.



4. REMOVE SIDE TURN SIGNAL LIGHT ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Remove the 3 screws and light.

  1. Disconnect the connector.
