HOOD LOCK CONTROL CABLE ASSEMBLY > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here



1. REMOVE ENGINE ROOM SIDE COVER LH



  1. Detach the 4 clips and remove the engine room side cover.



2. REMOVE UPPER RADIATOR SUPPORT SEAL



  1. Remove the 13 clips and upper radiator support seal.



3. REMOVE RADIATOR GRILLE

  1. Remove the radiator grille (Click here).



4. REMOVE MILLIMETER WAVE RADAR SENSOR ASSEMBLY (w/ Dynamic Radar Cruise Control System)



  1. Disconnect the sensor connector.

  1. Remove the 3 bolts and sensor.



5. REMOVE HOOD LOCK RELEASE LEVER PROTECTOR



  1. Detach the 2 clamps and 2 guides and remove the hood lock release lever protector.



6. REMOVE LOW PITCHED HORN ASSEMBLY



  1. Disconnect the connector.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Connector

  1. Remove the bolt and low pitched horn.



7. REMOVE HIGH PITCHED HORN ASSEMBLY



  1. Disconnect the connector.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Connector

  1. Remove the bolt and high pitched horn.



8. REMOVE HOOD LOCK CONTROL CABLE COVER



  1. Remove the 2 screws.

  1. Detach the claw and remove the hood lock control cable cover.



9. REMOVE HOOD LOCK ASSEMBLY



  1. Remove the 3 bolts.




  1. Disconnect the connector.

  1. Disconnect the hood lock control cable and remove the hood lock assembly.



10. REMOVE HOOD LOCK CONTROL LEVER SUB-ASSEMBLY



  1. Detach the clamp as shown in the illustration.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Connector Box
    *2
    		Hood Lock Control Cable
    *3
    		Cowl Side Trim




  1. Detach the 3 claws.




  1. Remove the hood lock control cable assembly and hood lock control lever sub-assembly.



11. REMOVE HOOD LOCK CONTROL CABLE ASSEMBLY

  1. Using a screwdriver, detach the clamps shown in the illustration.

    HINT:
    Tape the screwdriver tip before use.



  1. Pull the hood lock control cable assembly from the engine compartment and remove it.
