ACCELERATOR PEDAL > ON-VEHICLE INSPECTION

for Preparation Click here



1. INSPECT ACCELERATOR PEDAL POSITION SENSOR

  1. Connect the intelligent tester to the DLC3.

  1. Turn the engine switch on (IG).

  1. Turn the intelligent tester on.

  1. Enter the following menus: Powertrain / Engine and ECT / Data List / ALL Date / Accel Sensor Out No. 1 and Accel Sensor Out No. 2.

  1. Read the value displayed on the intelligent tester.

    Standard Voltage:
    Accel Sensor Out No. 1:
    Condition
    		Specified Condition
    Accelerator pedal is released
    		0.5 to 1.1 V
    Accelerator pedal is depressed
    		2.6 to 4.5 V
    Accel Sensor Out No. 2:
    Condition
    		Specified Condition
    Accelerator pedal is released
    		1.2 to 2.0 V
    Accelerator pedal is depressed
    		3.4 to 4.7 V

    If the result is not as specified, check the accelerator pedal position sensor, wire harness or ECM.
