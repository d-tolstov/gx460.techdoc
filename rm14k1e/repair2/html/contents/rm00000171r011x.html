1. CHECK BRAKE BOOSTER PUMP ASSEMBLY



  1. Apply battery voltage to the brake booster pump cables, and check the operation of the pump motor.

    OK:
    Measurement Condition
    		Specified Condition
    Battery positive (+) voltage → Red cable terminal
    		Pump motor operates
    Battery negative (-) voltage → Black cable terminal

    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Red Cable
    *2
    		Black Cable
    If the result is not as specified, replace the brake booster pump assembly.
