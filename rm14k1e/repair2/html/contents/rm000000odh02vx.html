1. INSPECT SHIFT SOLENOID VALVE SL1 AND SL2



  1. Measure the resistance according to the value(s) in the table below.

    Standard Resistance:
    Tester Connection
    		Condition
    		Specified Condition
    1 - 2
    		20°C (68°F)
    		5.0 to 5.6 Ω

    Text in Illustration 
    *a
    		Component without harness connected
    (Shift Solenoid Valve)

  1. Apply 12 V battery voltage to the shift solenoid valve and check that the valve moves and makes an operating noise.

    OK:
    Measurement Condition
    		Specified Condition
    Battery positive (+) with a 21 W bulb → Terminal 1
    Battery negative (-) → Terminal 2
    		Valve moves and makes an operating noise

    If the result is not as specified, replace the solenoid valve.
