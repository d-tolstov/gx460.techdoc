POWER MIRROR CONTROL SYSTEM > Mirror Heater does not Operate with Rear Defogger Switch

for Preparation Click here


DESCRIPTION

When the mirror heater switch is operated, the mirror heater signal is sent to the air conditioning amplifier assembly and then to each outer mirror control ECU assembly via CAN communication. Based on the signal, each outer mirror control ECU assembly operates the mirror heater.
