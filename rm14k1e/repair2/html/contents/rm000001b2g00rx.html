REAR SPEED SENSOR > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here



1. REMOVE REAR WHEEL


2. REMOVE REAR SPEED SENSOR LH



  1. Disconnect the speed sensor connector.

  1. Remove the nut and speed sensor.

    NOTICE:
    Pull out the sensor while trying as much as possible not to rotate it.



3. REMOVE REAR SPEED SENSOR RH

HINT:
Use the same procedure described for the LH side.



4. REMOVE SKID CONTROL SENSOR WIRE




  1. Disconnect the connector.

  1. Detach the connector.

  1. Detach the 8 clamps.

  1. Remove the 2 bolts and 2 sensor clamps.
