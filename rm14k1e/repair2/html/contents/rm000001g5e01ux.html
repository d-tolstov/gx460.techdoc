REAR NO. 2 SEAT OUTER BELT ASSEMBLY > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here


HINT:
  1. Use the same procedure for the RH and LH sides.
  2. The procedure listed below is for the LH side.



1. REMOVE REAR NO. 2 SEAT ASSEMBLY LH

  1. Remove the rear No. 2 seat assembly LH (Click here).



2. REMOVE REAR NO. 1 FLOOR STEP COVER



HINT:
Use the same procedure for all rear No. 1 floor step covers.

  1. Using a screwdriver, detach the 2 claws and remove the rear No. 1 floor step cover.

    HINT:
    Tape the screwdriver tip before use.

    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape



3. REMOVE QUARTER SCUFF PLATE LH



  1. Remove the 2 bolts and quarter scuff plate.



4. REMOVE REAR DOOR SCUFF PLATE LH



  1. Put protective tape around the rear door scuff plate.

  1. Using a screwdriver, detach the 3 clips, 6 claws and 2 guides and remove the rear door scuff plate.

    HINT:
    Tape the screwdriver tip before use.

    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape



5. REMOVE REAR DOOR OPENING TRIM WEATHERSTRIP LH

  1. Partially remove the rear door opening trim weatherstrip so that the front quarter trim panel assembly LH and deck trim side panel assembly LH can be removed (Click here).



6. REMOVE REAR NO. 1 SEAT OUTER LAP BELT ANCHOR COVER



HINT:
Use the same procedure for the other rear No. 1 seat outer lap belt anchor cover.

  1. Detach the 3 claws and remove the rear No. 1 seat outer lap belt anchor cover.



7. REMOVE NO. 1 LUGGAGE COMPARTMENT TRIM HOOK



HINT:
Use the same procedure for the other No. 1 luggage compartment trim hook.

  1. Remove the luggage compartment trim hook by turning it clockwise.



8. REMOVE NO. 1 TONNEAU COVER HOLDER CAP (w/o Tonneau Cover)



HINT:
Use the same procedure for the other No. 1 tonneau cover holder cap.

  1. Using a screwdriver, detach the 2 claws and remove the No. 1 tonneau cover holder cap.

    HINT:
    Tape the screwdriver tip before use.

    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape



9. REMOVE FRONT DECK SIDE TRIM COVER (w/ Tonneau Cover)



HINT:
Use the same procedure for the other front deck side trim cover.

  1. Using a screwdriver, detach the 2 claws and remove the front deck side trim cover.

    HINT:
    Tape the screwdriver tip before use.

    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape



10. REMOVE ASSIST GRIP PLUG



HINT:
Use the same procedure for all assist grip plugs.

  1. Using a screwdriver, detach the 2 claws and remove the assist grip plug.

    HINT:
    Tape the screwdriver tip before use.

    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape



11. REMOVE ASSIST GRIP SUB-ASSEMBLY



HINT:
Use the same procedure for the other assist grip.

  1. Remove the 2 bolts and assist grip.



12. REMOVE DECK TRIM SIDE PANEL ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Remove the bolt and disconnect the rear No. 1 seat outer belt floor anchor.




  1. Remove the bolt and disconnect the rear No. 2 seat outer belt floor anchor.




  1. for RH Side:
    Remove the bolt and disconnect the rear No. 2 seat outer belt floor anchor.

  1. Remove the 2 bolts and 2 screws.

  1. Detach the 3 clips and 8 claws.



  1. Disconnect each connector and remove the deck trim side panel.





13. REMOVE REAR QUARTER TRIM PANEL ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Detach the 7 clips and 2 guides.

  1. Disconnect the connector.




  1. Detach the 4 clips and 2 guides, pass the rear No. 2 seat outer belt floor anchor through the rear quarter trim panel and remove the rear quarter trim panel.



14. REMOVE STEREO COMPONENT AMPLIFIER ASSEMBLY WITH BRACKET (for LH Side, w/ Woofer)



  1. Disconnect the 4 connectors.

  1. Remove the 3 bolts and stereo component amplifier assembly with bracket.



15. REMOVE NO. 1 SPEAKER WITH BOX ASSEMBLY (for LH Side, w/ Woofer)



  1. Disconnect the connector.

  1. Remove the 5 bolts and No. 1 speaker with box.



16. REMOVE REAR NO. 2 SEAT OUTER BELT ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Remove the bolt and rear No. 1 seat outer belt shoulder anchor.




  1. Remove the bolt and rear No. 2 seat outer belt assembly.



17. REMOVE REAR SEAT OUTER BELT ANCHOR PLATE SUB-ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Remove the bolt and anchor plate.



18. REMOVE SEAT BELT ANCHOR PLATE ASSEMBLY (for RH Side)



  1. Remove the 2 bolts and anchor plate.
