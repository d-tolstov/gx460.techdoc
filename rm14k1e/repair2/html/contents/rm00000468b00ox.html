REAR NO. 1 SEAT ASSEMBLY (for 60/40 Split Slide Walk-in Seat Type LH Side) > DISASSEMBLY

for Preparation Click here


CAUTION:
Wear protective gloves. Sharp areas on the parts may injure your hands.



1. REMOVE REAR SEAT UPPER RECLINING COVER LH



  1. Using a screwdriver, detach the 2 claws and remove the cover.

    HINT:
    Tape the screwdriver tip before use.

    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape



2. REMOVE RECLINING ADJUSTER RELEASE HANDLE LH



  1. Remove the 3 screws and handle.



3. REMOVE CENTER SEAT HINGE COVER RH



  1. Using a clip remover, remove the clip.

  1. Detach the 2 guides and remove the cover.



4. REMOVE REAR SEAT CUSHION MOULDING LH



  1. Using a clip remover, detach the clip.

  1. Using a moulding remover, detach the 3 claws.

  1. Move the moulding in the direction of the arrow to detach the guide and remove the moulding.



5. REMOVE REAR SEATBACK LOCK STRIKER COVER LH



  1. Using a clip remover, detach the 3 clips and remove the cover.



6. REMOVE SEAT CUSHION COVER WITH PAD



  1. Detach the hooks.




  1. w/ Seat Heater System:

    1. Disconnect the 2 seat heater connectors.

    2. Detach the 2 clamps and disconnect the seat heater wire harness.




  1. Detach the hook and remove the seat cushion cover with pad.



7. REMOVE REAR NO. 2 SEAT PROTECTOR



  1. Remove the 2 protectors from the cushion pad.



8. REMOVE SEPARATE TYPE REAR SEAT CUSHION COVER


  1. Remove the hog rings and seat cushion cover from the seat cushion pad.





9. REMOVE REAR SEAT CUSHION HEATER ASSEMBLY (w/ Seat Heater System)



  1. Cut off the tack pins and remove the seat cushion heater from the seat cushion cover.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Tack Pin



10. REMOVE REAR SEAT CUSHION EDGE PROTECTOR

  1. Remove the protector from the cushion spring.



11. REMOVE CENTER SEATBACK ASSEMBLY



  1. Remove the 2 bolts and center seatback.



12. REMOVE REAR SEAT CENTER HEADREST ASSEMBLY

  1. Remove the headrest.



13. REMOVE CENTER SEAT HINGE COVER



  1. Using a clip remover, remove the clip.

  1. Detach the 2 guides and remove the cover.



14. REMOVE REAR SEAT RECLINING BRACKET LH



  1. Using a T45 "TORX" socket wrench, remove the "TORX" bolt, bush and bracket.



15. REMOVE REAR SEAT RECLINING BRACKET RH

  1. Using a T45 "TORX" socket wrench, remove the "TORX" bolt, bush and bracket.



16. REMOVE REAR SEATBACK STAY COVER LH



  1. Using a moulding remover, detach the 3 claws.

  1. Detach the guide and remove the cover.



17. REMOVE REAR SEATBACK STAY COVER RH

  1. Using a moulding remover, detach the 3 claws.

  1. Detach the guide and remove the cover.



18. REMOVE REAR SEAT CENTER ARMREST ASSEMBLY



  1. Using a T45 "TORX" socket wrench, remove the 2 "TORX" bolts and 2 bushes.




  1. Remove the nut and armrest support.




  1. Remove the armrest as shown in the illustration.



19. REMOVE REAR SEAT CENTER ARMREST CUP HOLDER SUB-ASSEMBLY



  1. Remove the 2 screws.




  1. Using a moulding remover, detach the 2 claws and remove the cup holder.



20. REMOVE REAR SEATBACK COVER



  1. Using a screwdriver, detach the 4 claws and remove the cover.

    HINT:
    Tape the screwdriver tip before use.



21. REMOVE REAR SEATBACK LOCK COVER



  1. Using a moulding remover, detach the claw.

  1. Move the cover in the direction of the arrow to detach the clip and 2 guides, and then remove the cover.



22. REMOVE REAR SEATBACK BOARD SUB-ASSEMBLY



  1. Using a clip remover, detach the 5 clips and remove the board.



23. REMOVE SEAT ARMREST COVER



  1. Detach the hooks.




  1. Remove the cover with pad.




  1. Remove the cover from the pad.



24. REMOVE REAR SEAT HEADREST SUPPORT



  1. Detach the 4 claws and remove the 2 supports.



25. REMOVE NO. 1 SEATBACK COVER



  1. Using a moulding remover, detach the 2 claws.




  1. Detach the guide and remove the cover.



26. REMOVE REAR SEAT LOCK ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Detach the clamp and disconnect the wire.

  1. Remove the 2 bolts and lock.



27. REMOVE REAR NO. 1 SEAT LOCK CONTROL LEVER



  1. Remove the lever as shown in the illustration.



28. REMOVE FOLD SEATBACK WIRE



  1. Detach the 2 claws and remove the wire.



29. REMOVE COAT HOOK CAP



  1. Using a screwdriver, detach the 2 claws and guide, and then remove the cap.

    HINT:
    Tape the screwdriver tip before use.

    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape



30. REMOVE CENTER NO. 2 SEATBACK COVER



  1. Remove the 3 screws and cover.



31. REMOVE REAR NO. 1 SEAT INNER BELT ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Remove the bolt and inner belt and disconnect the center outer belt.



32. REMOVE RECLINING ADJUSTER KNOB CAP LH



  1. Using a screwdriver, detach the claw and guide, and then remove the cap.

    HINT:
    Tape the screwdriver tip before use.

    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape



33. REMOVE REAR SEAT LOCK CONTROL LEVER SUB-ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Remove the 2 screws.




  1. Disconnect the cable in the order shown in the illustration and remove the lever.



34. REMOVE SEAT BELT ANCHOR COVER CAP



  1. Using a screwdriver, detach the 2 claws and remove the cap.

    HINT:
    Tape the screwdriver tip before use.

    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape



35. REMOVE REAR SEAT SHOULDER BELT COVER



  1. Detach the 2 claws and 2 guides, and then remove the cover.

  1. Disconnect the outer belt from the cover.



36. REMOVE REAR SEATBACK COVER



  1. Using a screwdriver, detach the 4 claws and remove the cover.

    HINT:
    Tape the screwdriver tip before use.



37. REMOVE REAR SEATBACK BOARD CARPET ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Using a clip remover, detach the 7 clips and remove the carpet.



38. REMOVE REAR NO. 1 SEAT HEADREST SUPPORT ASSEMBLY



  1. Detach the 4 claws and remove the 2 supports.



39. REMOVE SEATBACK COVER WITH PAD



  1. Remove the nut, detach the 2 guides and disconnect the seatback cover bracket from the seatback frame.




  1. Remove the hog rings and detach the hooks, and then remove the seatback cover with pad.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Hook
    *2
    		Hog Ring




  1. Disconnect the outer belt from the seatback cover with pad.



40. REMOVE SEPARATE TYPE REAR SEATBACK COVER



  1. Disconnect the seatback cover bracket from the seatback pad.




  1. w/ Seat Heater System:
    Disconnect the seatback heater wire harness from the seatback pad.




  1. Remove the hog rings and cover from the seatback pad.



41. REMOVE REAR SEATBACK HEATER ASSEMBLY (w/ Seat Heater System)



  1. Cut off the tack pins which fasten the seatback heater to the seatback cover, and then remove the seatback heater from the seatback cover.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Tack Pin



42. REMOVE REAR SEATBACK EDGE PROTECTOR

  1. Remove the protector from the seatback frame.



43. REMOVE REAR SEAT INNER RECLINING COVER LH



  1. Remove the 2 screws.

  1. Detach the guide and remove the cover.



44. REMOVE RECLINING ADJUSTER INSIDE COVER LH



  1. Remove the 2 screws.

  1. Detach the 2 claws and remove the cover.



45. REMOVE REAR SEAT REAR CUSHION MOULDING LH



  1. Using a clip remover, detach the clip.

  1. Using a moulding remover, detach the 4 claws.

  1. Move the moulding in the direction of the arrow to detach the guide and remove the moulding.



46. REMOVE CAP



  1. Detach the 2 claws and remove the cap.



47. REMOVE REAR SEAT RECLINING COVER INNER LH



  1. Detach the claw and guide, and then remove the cover.



48. REMOVE REAR SEAT SHOULDER BELT GUIDE



  1. Detach the claw and guide, and then remove the belt guide.



49. REMOVE NO. 1 SEAT 3 POINT TYPE BELT ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Remove the nut and bolt, and then detach the 2 claws and remove the seat belt.



50. REMOVE REAR SEAT RECLINING CONTROL CABLE


  1. Cut off the 4 cable ties.





  1. Detach the 3 cable clamps and disconnect the cable, and then remove the cable.





51. REMOVE REAR NO. 2 SEAT RECLINING CONTROL CABLE


  1. Detach the 3 cable clamps and disconnect the cable, and then remove the cable.





52. REMOVE REAR BACK LOCK CONTROL LEVER SUB-ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Remove the 2 nuts and lever.



53. REMOVE SEAT HEATER CONTROL SUB-ASSEMBLY (w/ Seat Heater System)



  1. Disconnect the connector.

  1. Detach the 2 clamps and remove the seat heater control.



54. REMOVE REAR SEAT WIRE LH (w/ Seat Heater System)



  1. Detach the claw and disconnect the seat wire connector from the seat track rail.

  1. Detach the clamp and remove the seat wire from the seat cushion frame.



55. REMOVE REAR SEAT CUSHION FRAME SUB-ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Cut off the cable tie.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Cable tie
    *2
    		Clamp

  1. Detach the claw and disconnect the side airbag connector from the seat track rail.

  1. Detach the 2 airbag wire harness clamps and disconnect the airbag wire harness from the seat cushion frame.




  1. Using a T45 "TORX" socket wrench, remove the 2 "TORX" bolts.

  1. Remove the 2 bolts and seatback frame.




  1. Remove the 6 nuts and seat cushion frame.



56. REMOVE REAR SEAT TRACK ADJUSTING HANDLE LH



  1. Slide the slide rail to the rearmost position.

  1. Using a small screwdriver, push down the handle stopper to detach the claw and remove the adjusting handle.

    HINT:
    Insert the screwdriver into the hole shown in the illustration while looking into the hole.
