STEERING GEAR > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here



1. PLACE FRONT WHEELS FACING STRAIGHT AHEAD


2. REMOVE FRONT WHEELS


3. REMOVE ENGINE ASSEMBLY

  1. Remove the engine assembly (Click here).



4. DISCONNECT NO. 2 STEERING INTERMEDIATE SHAFT

  1. Disconnect the No. 2 steering intermediate shaft (Click here).



5. DISCONNECT TIE ROD END SUB-ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Using SST, disconnect the tie rod end assembly.

    SST
    09610-20012  



6. DISCONNECT TIE ROD END SUB-ASSEMBLY RH

HINT:
Use the same procedures described for the LH side.



7. DISCONNECT PRESSURE FEED TUBE ASSEMBLY



  1. Remove the clamp and disconnect the pressure feed tube (return tube side) from the steering gear.




  1. Using a union nut wrench, disconnect the pressure feed tube (pressure feed tube side) from the steering gear.




  1. Remove the 2 bolts and disconnect the pressure feed tube clamp.



8. REMOVE RACK AND PINION POWER STEERING GEAR ASSEMBLY



  1. Remove the 2 bolts, 2 nuts, 2 washers and steering gear assembly.
