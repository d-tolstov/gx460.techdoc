5. ADJUST CAMBER AND CASTER

HINT:
After the camber has been adjusted, inspect the toe-in.

  1. Loosen the front adjusting cam bolt and rear adjusting cam nut.



  1. Turn the front adjusting cam and rear adjusting cam in both directions, and adjust the camber and caster to the center value.

    HINT:
    1. The camber and caster should be as close as possible to the center value.
    2. Adjust the camber and caster within +/- 30'.

6. INSPECT TOE-IN



  1. Bounce the vehicle up and down at the corners to stabilize the suspension and inspect the toe-in.

    Vehicle Model

    C - D: 2.0 +/-2 mm (0.08 +/-0.08 in.)
    URJ150L-GKTZKV
    		A + B: 0°4' +/-0°10' (0.07° +/-0.16°)
    C - D: 1.9 +/-2 mm (0.07 +/-0.08 in.)
    URJ150L-GKTZKW
    		A + B: 0°4' +/-0°10' (0.07° +/-0.16°)
    C - D: 2.0 +/-2 mm (0.08 +/-0.08 in.)

    If the toe-in is not within the specified range, adjust it at the rack ends.



7. ADJUST TOE-IN

  1. Detach the rack boot set clips.

  1. Loosen the tie rod end lock nuts.

  1. Turn the right and left rack ends by an equal amount to adjust the toe-in to the center value.




  1. Check that the lengths of the right and left rack ends are approximately the same.

    Standard difference:
    0 +/-1 mm (0 +/-0.0394 in.) or less

    Text in Illustration 
    *a
    		Length

    		Lock Nut

  1. Tighten the tie rod end lock nuts.

    Torque:
    88 N*m{ 897 kgf*cm , 65 ft.*lbf }

  1. Place the boots on the seats and install the clips.

    HINT:
    Make sure that the boots are not twisted.
