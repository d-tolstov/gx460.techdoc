EXHAUST PIPE > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here



1. REMOVE REAR ENGINE UNDER COVER ASSEMBLY

  1. Remove the 4 bolts and rear engine under cover.



2. REMOVE REAR QUARTER PANEL MUDGUARD RH

  1. Remove the 4 screws and rear quarter panel mudguard.



3. REMOVE REAR BUMPER PROTECTOR SUB-ASSEMBLY RH

  1. Remove the 2 screws and grommet.




  1. Detach the guide and remove the rear bumper protector.

  1. Disconnect the wire harness from the rear bumper protector.



4. REMOVE TAILPIPE ASSEMBLY

  1. Remove the 2 bolts and disconnect the tailpipe from the exhaust pipe support.

  1. Remove the gasket.



5. REMOVE CENTER EXHAUST PIPE ASSEMBLY

  1. Remove the 2 bolts and 2 compression springs, and then disconnect the center exhaust pipe from the 3 exhaust pipe supports.

  1. Remove the gasket.



6. REMOVE EXHAUST PIPE STOPPER BRACKET

  1. Remove the 2 bolts and exhaust pipe stopper bracket.



7. REMOVE FRONT NO. 2 EXHAUST PIPE ASSEMBLY

  1. Disconnect the heated oxygen sensor connector.

  1. Remove the 2 bolts and 2 nuts.

  1. Disconnect the exhaust pipe support, and then remove the front No. 2 exhaust pipe and 2 gaskets.



8. REMOVE FRONT EXHAUST PIPE ASSEMBLY

  1. Disconnect the heated oxygen sensor connector.

  1. Remove the 2 nuts and front exhaust pipe.

  1. Remove the gasket.



9. REMOVE HEATED OXYGEN SENSOR (for Bank 1 Sensor 2)



  1. Disconnect the heated oxygen sensor connector.

  1. Using SST, remove the heated oxygen sensor from the front No. 2 exhaust pipe.

    SST
    09224-00010  



10. REMOVE HEATED OXYGEN SENSOR (for Bank 2 Sensor 2)



  1. Detach the 2 clamps and disconnect the heated oxygen sensor connector.




  1. Using SST, remove the heated oxygen sensor from the front exhaust pipe.

    SST
    09224-00010
