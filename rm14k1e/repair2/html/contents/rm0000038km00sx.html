FRONT DOOR BELT MOULDING > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here


HINT:
  1. Use the same procedure for the RH and LH sides.
  2. The procedure listed below is for the LH side.



1. DISCONNECT CABLE FROM NEGATIVE BATTERY TERMINAL

NOTICE:
  1. w/ Navigation System (for HDD):
    After the engine switch is turned off, the display and navigation module display (HDD navigation system) records various types of memory and settings. As a result, after turning the engine switch off, be sure to wait for the time specified in the following table before disconnecting the cable from the negative (-) battery terminal.

    Waiting Time before Disconnecting Cable from Negative (-) Battery Terminal:

    Condition
    		Waiting Time
    Vehicle enrolled in safety connect system
    		6 minutes
    Vehicle not enrolled in safety connect system
    		1 minute

  2. When disconnecting the cable, some systems need to be initialized after the cable is reconnected (Click here).



2. REMOVE FRONT DOOR LOWER FRAME BRACKET GARNISH LH



  1. Detach the 2 clips and remove the front door lower frame bracket garnish LH.



3. REMOVE FRONT DOOR INSIDE HANDLE BEZEL PLUG LH



  1. Using a moulding remover, detach the 3 claws and remove the inside handle bezel as shown in the illustration.



4. REMOVE FRONT ARMREST ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Using moulding remover B, detach the 7 claws and 7 guides, and remove the front armrest assembly as shown in the illustration.



5. REMOVE POWER WINDOW REGULATOR MASTER SWITCH ASSEMBLY WITH FRONT DOOR ARMREST BASE PANEL



  1. Using a moulding remover B, detach the 2 clips, 4 claws and guide as shown in the illustration.

  1. Disconnect the connector and remove the power window regulator master switch assembly with front door armrest base panel.



6. REMOVE FRONT DOOR TRIM BOARD SUB-ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Remove the 2 screws.

  1. Remove the 12 clips.




  1. Pull out the front door trim board sub-assembly in the direction indicated by the arrow in the illustration.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Reference Boss

  1. Raise the front door trim board sub-assembly to detach the 4 claws and remove the front door trim board sub-assembly together with the front door inner glass weatherstrip LH.

  1. Disconnect the 2 connectors.




  1. Disconnect the front door lock remote control cable assembly and front door inside locking cable assembly.



7. REMOVE FRONT DOOR INNER GLASS WEATHERSTRIP LH



  1. Using a screwdriver, detach the 4 claws and remove the front door inner glass weatherstrip from the front door trim board sub-assembly as shown in the illustration.



8. REMOVE FRONT DOOR SERVICE HOLE COVER LH



  1. Remove the bolt.

  1. Detach the 2 clamps, move the wire harness out of the way and remove the service hole cover.

    HINT:
    Remove any remaining butyl tape from the door.



9. REMOVE OUTER REAR VIEW MIRROR ASSEMBLY LH




  1. w/ Side Monitor System:
    Disconnect the 2 connectors and detach the clamp.

  1. w/o Side Monitor System:
    Disconnect the connector and detach the clamp.

  1. Remove the 3 nuts.

  1. Detach the claw and remove the outer rear view mirror.



10. REMOVE FRONT DOOR GLASS SUB-ASSEMBLY LH

  1. Connect the cable to the negative (-) battery terminal.

  1. Connect the power window regulator master switch assembly and move the front door glass sub-assembly so that the door glass bolts can be seen.

  1. Disconnect the cable from the negative (-) battery terminal and power window regulator master switch assembly.

    NOTICE:
    1. w/ Navigation System (for HDD):
      After the engine switch is turned off, the display and navigation module display (HDD navigation system) records various types of memory and settings. As a result, after turning the engine switch off, be sure to wait for the time specified in the following table before disconnecting the cable from the negative (-) battery terminal.

      Waiting Time before Disconnecting Cable from Negative (-) Battery Terminal :

      Condition
      		Waiting Time
      Vehicle enrolled in G-BOOK system
      		6 minutes
      Vehicle not enrolled in G-BOOK system
      		1 minute

    2. When disconnecting the cable, some systems need to be initialized after the cable is reconnected (Click here).




  1. Remove the 2 bolts.

    NOTICE:
    After the bolts are removed, do not allow the door glass to fall.




  1. Remove the front door glass sub-assembly as indicated by the arrows in the order shown in the illustration.

    NOTICE:
    Do not damage the door glass.



11. REMOVE FRONT DOOR GLASS RUN LH



  1. Remove the front door glass run.



12. REMOVE FRONT DOOR BELT MOULDING LH



  1. Put protective tape around the belt moulding.

  1. Detach the 6 claws and remove the front door belt moulding.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape
