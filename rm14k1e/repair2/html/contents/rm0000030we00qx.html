AIR SWITCHING VALVE (for Bank 1) > INSTALLATION

for Preparation Click here



1. INSTALL AIR SWITCHING VALVE ASSEMBLY



  1. Install a new gasket and the air switching valve with the 3 bolts.

    Torque:
    24 N*m{ 245 kgf*cm , 18 ft.*lbf }

    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		No. 4 Air Tube
    *2
    		Air Switching Valve
    *3
    		Claw

    NOTICE:
    Make sure the claws of the gasket are not caught between the air switching valve and No. 4 air tube.

  1. Install the 2 bolts.

    Torque:
    10 N*m{ 102 kgf*cm , 7 ft.*lbf }

  1. Connect the wire harness clamp bracket with the bolt.

    Torque:
    8.0 N*m{ 82 kgf*cm , 71 in.*lbf }



2. CONNECT NO. 2 AIR INJECTION SYSTEM HOSE



  1. Align the paint mark with the projection and connect the No. 2 air injection system hose.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Projection
    *2
    		Paint Mark

    HINT:
    Make sure the direction of the hose clamp is as shown in the illustration.

  1. Connect the air switching valve connector.



3. INSTALL ENGINE ROOM SIDE COVER LH

  1. Install the engine room side cover LH with the 4 clips.



4. INSTALL V-BANK COVER SUB-ASSEMBLY



  1. Attach the 2 V-bank cover hooks to the No. 1 V-bank cover bracket. Then align the 2 V-bank cover grommets with the 2 pins and press down on the V-bank cover to attach the pins.
    Text in Illustration

    		Pin
    *2
    		Grommet
    *3
    		Hook
    *4
    		No. 1 V-bank Cover Bracket
