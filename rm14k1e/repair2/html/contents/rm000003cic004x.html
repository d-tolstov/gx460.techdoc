ROOF RACK > INSTALLATION

for Preparation Click here


HINT:
  1. Use the same procedure for the RH and LH sides.
  2. The procedure listed below is for the LH side.



1. INSTALL NO. 2 ROOF CARRIER SEAL

HINT:
When installing the No. 2 roof carrier seal, heat the roof rack assembly surface using a heat light.

Standard:
Item
Temperature
Roof Rack Assembly
20 to 30°C (68 to 86°F)

NOTICE:
Do not heat the roof rack assembly excessively.

  1. Clean the roof rack assembly surface.

    1. Using a heat light, heat the roof rack assembly surface.

    2. Remove the double-sided tape from the roof rack assembly surface.

    3. Wipe off any tape adhesive residue with cleaner.

  1. Install a new No. 2 roof carrier seal.

    1. Remove the peeling paper from the face of a new No. 2 roof carrier seal.

      HINT:
      After removing the peeling paper, keep the exposed adhesive free from foreign matter.

    2. Install the 6 No. 2 roof carrier seals.

      HINT:
      Press the No. 2 roof carrier seal firmly to install it.



2. INSTALL ROOF RACK ASSEMBLY LH

  1. Clean the threads of the bolts with non-residue solvent (when reusing the bolts).

  1. Apply adhesive to the threads of the bolts (when reusing the bolts).

    Adhesive:
    Toyota Genuine Adhesive 1324, Three Bond 1324 or equivalent

  1. Install the roof rack with the 6 bolts.

    Torque:
    31 N*m{ 316 kgf*cm , 23 ft.*lbf }



3. INSTALL NO. 1 ROOF SIDE GARNISH LID LH

HINT:
Use the same procedure for all No. 1 roof side garnish lids.

  1. Attach the 2 guides and 2 claws to install the No. 1 roof side garnish lid.
