1. INSPECT REAR WIPER MOTOR ASSEMBLY

  1. Operate the rear wiper motor assembly.

    HINT:
    The wiper motor does not operate if the back door and back door glass are not closed.

  1. Stop the rear wiper motor assembly operation.




  1. Align the blade tip with the mark on the glass as shown in the illustration.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Ceramic Dot
    *a
    		Upper Limit
    *b
    		Lower Limit

  1. Check the automatic stop position.

    HINT:
    After the rear wiper motor is stopped, check the automatic stop position after lifting the wiper blade 2 times.

    Standard Clearance:
    Area
    		Specified Condition
    a
    		10 mm (0.394 in.)
    b
    		10 mm (0.394 in.)
