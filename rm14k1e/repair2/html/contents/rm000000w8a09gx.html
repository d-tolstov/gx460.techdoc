DTC    P2716    Pressure Control Solenoid "D" Electrical (Shift Solenoid Valve SLT)

for Preparation Click here


DESCRIPTION

Refer to DTC P0894 (Click here).

DTC Code
DTC Detection Condition
Trouble Area
P2716
Open or short is detected in the shift solenoid valve SLT circuit for 1 second or more while driving (1-trip detection logic).
  1. Open or short in shift solenoid valve SLT circuit
  2. Shift solenoid valve SLT
  3. ECM

MONITOR DESCRIPTION

When an open or short in the shift solenoid valve SLT circuit is detected, the ECM interprets this as a fault. The ECM will turn on the MIL and store the DTC.
