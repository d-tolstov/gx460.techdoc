1. INSPECT REAR HEADER SPEAKER ASSEMBLY



  1. Measure the resistance according to the value(s) in the table below.

    Standard Resistance:
    Tester Connection
    		Condition
    		Specified Condition
    1 - 2
    		Always
    		4.6 to 7.0 Ω

    If the result is not as specified, replace the rear header speaker assembly.
    Text in Illustration 
    *a
    		Component without harness connected
    (Rear Header Speaker Assembly)
