SEAT BELT WARNING SYSTEM DESCRIPTION
If a seat belt is not fastened, this system flashes the seat belt warning light or sounds the seat belt warning buzzer as a reminder.

  1. Driver side seat belt warning light:
    When the driver side seat belt is not fastened with the engine switch on (IG), the driver side seat belt warning light in the combination meter blinks to inform the driver. The center airbag sensor detects the driver side seat belt status and sends signals to the main body ECU, and then the main body ECU sends signals to the combination meter using the CAN.

  2. Front passenger side seat belt warning light:
    When the front passenger side seat belt is not fastened with the engine switch on (IG) and the front passenger seat occupied, the front passenger side seat belt warning light of the heater control switch board*1 or display and navigation module display*2 blinks. The heater control switch board*1 or display and navigation module display*2 displays the front passenger side seat belt status and whether the front passenger seat is occupied.

    1. *1: w/o Navigation System
    2. *2: w/ Navigation System

  3. Seat belt warning buzzer (for Middle East):
    When the engine switch is turned on (IG) and 1.8 seconds elapse while the driver side seat belt is not fastened, the seat belt warning buzzer in the combination meter sounds 5 times at an interval of 1.2 seconds. When 13.8 seconds have elapsed after turning the engine switch on (IG), the shift lever is in D and the vehicle is driven at approximately 20 km/h (12 mph) while the driver or front passenger side seat belt is unfastened, the seat belt warning buzzer in the combination meter sounds at an interval of 1.2 seconds for 9.6 seconds. After that, the buzzer sounds at an interval of 0.4 seconds for 20 seconds.

  4. Seat belt warning buzzer (except Middle East):
    When the vehicle is driven at approximately 20 km/h (12 mph) while the driver or front passenger side seat belt is unfastened, the seat belt warning buzzer in the combination meter sounds at an interval of 1.2 seconds for 30 seconds. After that, the buzzer sounds at an interval of 0.4 seconds for 90 seconds.


FUNCTION OF MAIN COMPONENT
The following functions are available.

Components
Function
Front seat inner belt LH (driver side buckle switch)
Informs the center airbag sensor of the driver side seat belt condition (fastened or unfastened).
Front seat inner belt RH (front passenger side buckle switch)
Informs the combination meter of the front passenger side seat belt condition (fastened or unfastened).
Occupant detection sensor
Informs the combination meter of the front passenger seat condition (occupied or unoccupied).
Driver side seat belt warning light
If the driver side seat belt is unfastened and the engine switch on (IG), the combination meter flashes the seat belt warning light in the combination meter as a reminder.
Front passenger side seat belt warning light
When the front passenger seat is occupied and the engine switch on (IG), if the front passenger side seat belt is unfastened, the combination meter flashes the front passenger side seat belt warning light of the heater control switch board*1 or display and navigation module display*2 as a reminder.
Seat belt warning buzzer
If the vehicle is driven at approximately 20 km/h (12.5 mph) or more with either of the following conditions met, the combination meter sounds the buzzer as a reminder: 1) the driver side seat belt is unfastened, or 2) the front passenger side seat belt is unfastened with the front passenger seat occupied.
  1. *1: w/o Navigation System
  2. *2: w/ Navigation System
