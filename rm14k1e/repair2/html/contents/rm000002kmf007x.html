1. INSPECT ABSORBER CONTROL ACTUATOR



  1. Measure the resistance according to the value(s) in the table below.

    Standard Resistance:
    for LH 
    Tester Connection
    		Condition
    		Specified Condition
    1 (FAL+) - 2 (FAL-)
    		Always
    		6.4 to 7.2 立
    3 (FBL+) - 4 (FBL-)
    		6.4 to 7.2 立

    Text in Illustration 
    *a
    		Component without harness connected
    (Absorber Control Actuator LH)
    If the result is not as specified, replace the absorber control actuator.
