REAR BUMPER > DISASSEMBLY

for Preparation Click here



1. REMOVE NO. 1 REAR BUMPER PLATE



  1. Put protective tape around the No. 1 rear bumper plate.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape

  1. Detach the 19 claws and remove the No. 1 rear bumper plate.



2. REMOVE REAR BUMPER ENERGY ABSORBER



  1. Detach the 5 double-sided tapes and remove the rear bumper energy absorber from the rear bumper cover.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Double-sided Tape



3. REMOVE REAR BUMPER CENTER BRACKET



  1. Remove the 2 outside moulding retainers and 2 retainers.

  1. Detach the 4 claws and remove the rear bumper center bracket.



4. REMOVE NO. 5 FLOOR WIRE (w/ LEXUS Parking Assist-sensor System)



  1. w/o Rear Fog Light:

    1. Disconnect the 3 connectors.

    2. Detach the 6 clamps and remove the No. 5 floor wire.




  1. w/ Rear Fog Light:

    1. Disconnect the 4 connectors.

    2. Detach the 6 clamps and remove the No. 5 floor wire.



5. REMOVE NO. 5 FLOOR WIRE (w/o LEXUS Parking Assist-sensor System)



  1. w/o Rear Fog Light:

    1. Disconnect the 2 connectors.

    2. Detach the 4 clamps and remove the No. 5 floor wire.




  1. w/ Rear Fog Light:

    1. Disconnect the 3 connectors.

    2. Detach the 4 clamps and remove the No. 5 floor wire.



6. REMOVE NO. 4 FLOOR WIRE (w/ LEXUS Parking Assist-sensor System)

HINT:
Use the same procedure described for the No. 5 floor wire.



7. REMOVE NO. 4 FLOOR WIRE (w/o LEXUS Parking Assist-sensor System)

HINT:
Use the same procedure described for the No. 5 floor wire.



8. REMOVE ULTRASONIC SENSOR CLIP (w/ LEXUS Parking Assist-sensor System)



HINT:
Use the same procedure to remove the ultrasonic sensor clip on the other side.

  1. Detach the 4 claws and remove the ultrasonic sensor clip.



9. REMOVE NO. 1 ULTRASONIC SENSOR (w/ LEXUS Parking Assist-sensor System)



HINT:
Use the same procedure to remove the No. 1 ultrasonic sensor on the other side.

  1. Disconnect the connector.

  1. Detach the 2 claws and remove the No. 1 ultrasonic sensor.



10. REMOVE NO. 1 ULTRASONIC SENSOR RETAINER (w/ LEXUS Parking Assist-sensor System)



HINT:
Use the same procedure to remove the No. 1 ultrasonic sensor retainer on the other side.

  1. Detach the 2 claws and remove the No. 1 ultrasonic sensor retainer.



11. REMOVE NO. 2 ULTRASONIC SENSOR RETAINER (w/ LEXUS Parking Assist-sensor System)



HINT:
Use the same procedure to remove the No. 2 ultrasonic sensor retainer on the other side.

  1. Detach the 2 claws and remove the No. 2 ultrasonic sensor retainer.



12. REMOVE REAR BUMPER PROTECTOR SUB-ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Remove the screw.

  1. Detach the guide and remove the rear bumper protector.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Guide



13. REMOVE REAR BUMPER PROTECTOR SUB-ASSEMBLY RH

HINT:
Use the same procedure described for the LH side.



14. REMOVE BACK UP LIGHT ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Remove the 2 nuts.

  1. Detach the 2 claws and clamp and remove the light.

  1. Disconnect the connector.



15. REMOVE BACK UP LIGHT ASSEMBLY RH

HINT:
Use the same procedure described for the LH side.



16. REMOVE REAR BUMPER PAD



  1. Remove the 4 rear bumper pads.



17. REMOVE REAR BUMPER PAD LH (w/ No. 2 Energy Absorber)



  1. Remove the rear bumper pad.



18. REMOVE REAR BUMPER PAD RH (w/ No. 2 Energy Absorber)

HINT:
Use the same procedure described for the LH side.
