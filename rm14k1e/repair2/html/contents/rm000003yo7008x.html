1. INSPECT MAP LIGHT ASSEMBLY



  1. Measure the resistance according to the value(s) in the table below.

    Standard Resistance:
    Tester Connection
    		Switch Condition
    		Specified Condition
    25 (DOWN) - 15 (GND1)
    		Spot light switch off
    		10 kΩ or higher
    Spot light switch on
    		Below 1 Ω
    25 (DOWN) - 1 (CTY)
    		Not door position
    		10 kΩ or higher
    Door position
    		Below 1 Ω
    25 (DOWN) - 2 (RILL)
    		Room light switch off
    		10 kΩ or higher
    Room light switch on
    		Below 1 Ω

    If the result is not as specified, replace the map light assembly.
