DTC    U1002    Lost Communication with Gateway Module (MS Bus)


DESCRIPTION

WIRING DIAGRAM

INSPECTION PROCEDURE

DISCONNECT CABLE FROM NEGATIVE BATTERY TERMINAL

CHECK CAN BUS WIRE (MAIN WIRE FOR DISCONNECTION, BUS LINE FOR SHORT CIRCUIT)

CHECK FOR OPEN IN CAN BUS MAIN WIRE (NO. 1 JUNCTION CONNECTOR - NO. 3 JUNCTION CONNECTOR)

CHECK FOR OPEN IN CAN BUS MAIN WIRE (NO. 1 JUNCTION CONNECTOR - NO. 6 JUNCTION CONNECTOR)

CONNECT CONNECTOR

CHECK FOR OPEN IN CAN BUS MAIN WIRE (NO. 3 JUNCTION CONNECTOR - NO. 1 JUNCTION CONNECTOR)

CHECK FOR OPEN IN CAN BUS MAIN WIRE (NO. 3 JUNCTION CONNECTOR - MAIN BODY ECU)

CONNECT CONNECTOR

CHECK FOR OPEN IN CAN BUS MAIN WIRE (NO. 6 JUNCTION CONNECTOR - NO. 1 JUNCTION CONNECTOR)

CONNECT CONNECTOR

CHECK FOR OPEN IN CAN BUS MAIN WIRE (MAIN BODY ECU - NO. 3 JUNCTION CONNECTOR)

CHECK FOR SHORT IN CAN BUS WIRES (NO. 3 JUNCTION CONNECTOR SIDE)

CHECK FOR SHORT IN CAN BUS WIRES (NO. 3 JUNCTION CONNECTOR - FRONT POWER SEAT SWITCH LH)

CHECK FOR SHORT IN CAN BUS WIRES (NO. 3 JUNCTION CONNECTOR - MULTIPLEX TILT AND TELESCOPIC ECU)

CHECK FOR SHORT IN CAN BUS WIRES (NO. 3 JUNCTION CONNECTOR - OUTER MIRROR CONTROL ECU LH)

CHECK FOR SHORT IN CAN BUS WIRES (NO. 3 JUNCTION CONNECTOR - MAIN BODY ECU)

CONNECT CONNECTOR

CHECK FOR SHORT IN CAN BUS WIRES (NO. 1 JUNCTION CONNECTOR - NO. 3 JUNCTION CONNECTOR)

CHECK FOR SHORT IN CAN BUS WIRES (NO. 1 JUNCTION CONNECTOR - DRIVING SUPPORT SWITCH CONTROL ECU)

CHECK FOR SHORT IN CAN BUS WIRES (NO. 1 JUNCTION CONNECTOR - OUTER MIRROR CONTROL ECU RH)

CHECK FOR SHORT IN CAN BUS WIRES (NO. 1 JUNCTION CONNECTOR - NO. 6 JUNCTION CONNECTOR)

CONNECT CONNECTOR

CHECK FOR SHORT IN CAN BUS WIRES (FRONT POWER SEAT SWITCH LH)

CONNECT CONNECTOR

CHECK FOR SHORT IN CAN BUS WIRES (MULTIPLEX TILT AND TELESCOPIC ECU)

CONNECT CONNECTOR

CHECK FOR SHORT IN CAN BUS WIRES (OUTER MIRROR CONTROL ECU LH)

CONNECT CONNECTOR

CHECK FOR SHORT IN CAN BUS WIRES (MAIN BODY ECU)

CONNECT CONNECTOR

CHECK FOR SHORT IN CAN BUS WIRES (DRIVING SUPPORT SWITCH CONTROL ECU)

CONNECT CONNECTOR

CHECK FOR SHORT IN CAN BUS WIRES (OUTER MIRROR CONTROL ECU RH)

CONNECT CONNECTOR

CHECK FOR SHORT IN CAN BUS WIRES (NO. 6 JUNCTION CONNECTOR)

CHECK HARNESS AND CONNECTOR (MAIN BODY ECU - BATTERY AND GROUND)
