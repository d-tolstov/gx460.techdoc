ILLUMINATION CONTROL SYSTEM (Illuminated Entry System)

  1. The illuminated entry system has the following control functions:

    Control
    		Outline
    		Lighting
    Actuation Area-linked
    		When a registered key enters any actuation area around the doors, the lights that operate turn on.
    1. Engine switch illumination
    2. Map light assembly
    3. No. 1 room light assembly
    4. No. 2 room light assembly
    5. Side step light
    Door Lock/Unlock-linked
    		When any door is unlocked, the lights that operate turn on.
    1. Engine switch illumination
    2. Map light assembly
    3. No. 1 room light assembly
    4. No. 2 room light assembly
    5. Side step light
    6. Interior foot lights
    7. Inside handle illumination
    When all doors are locked, the lights that operate turn off.
    Door Open/Close-linked
    		When any door is opened, the lights that operate turn on.
    1. Engine switch illumination
    2. Map light assembly
    3. No. 1 room light assembly
    4. No. 2 room light assembly
    5. Side step light
    6. Interior foot lights
    7. Inside handle illumination
    8. Door courtesy lights
    When all doors are closed, the lights that operate turn off.
    Engine Switch-linked
    		When the engine switch is turned from on (ACC or IG) to off, the lights that operate turn on.
    1. Engine switch illumination
    2. Map light assembly
    3. No. 1 room light assembly
    4. No. 2 room light assembly
    When the engine switch is turned from off to on (ACC or ON), the lights that operate turn on.
    		Center console spot light
    Battery Saving
    		When the doors remain in the same condition for 20 minutes and the key is outside the vehicle exterior detection area, the illumination of the items listed to the right turns off.
    1. Engine switch illumination
    2. Map light assembly
    3. No. 1 room light assembly
    4. No. 2 room light assembly
    5. Side step light
    6. Interior foot lights
    7. Inside handle illumination
