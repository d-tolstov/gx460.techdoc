REAR AXLE SHAFT > DISASSEMBLY

for Preparation Click here



1. REMOVE REAR AXLE SHAFT SNAP RING LH



  1. Using a snap ring expander, remove the snap ring.



2. REMOVE REAR AXLE SHAFT LH



  1. Using SST and a press, press out the rear axle shaft.

    SST
    09521-25023  
    09521-25011  




  1. Grind the rear axle bearing retainer using a grinder, and then remove it with a chisel.

  1. Remove the shaft washer from the axle shaft.



3. REMOVE REAR AXLE HUB AND BEARING ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Temporarily install 4 nuts to the housing bolts.

    NOTICE:
    Do not use any nuts removed from the vehicle, as they may be damaged.

  1. Using a hammer, remove the 4 housing bolts and rear axle hub and bearing.

  1. Remove the 4 nuts.



4. REMOVE BRAKE DRUM OIL DEFLECTOR LH



  1. Using SST, remove the 6 hub bolts.

    SST
    09650-17011  

    NOTICE:
    1. Do not deform the oil deflector.
    2. Make sure to align the notch of SST with the flange of the oil deflector.

  1. Remove the deflector and deflector gasket from the rear axle shaft.
