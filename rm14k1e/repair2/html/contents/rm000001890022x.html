1. REMOVE GENERATOR PULLEY

  1. Mount the generator in a vise between aluminum plates.




  1. Install SST 1-A to the pulley shaft.

    SST
    09820-63011   (09820-06010, 09820-06021)

    HINT:
    SST 1-A and B
    		09820-06010
    SST 2
    		09820-06021

  1. Insert SST 1-B to SST 1-A.

  1. Hold SST 1-A with a torque wrench and turn SST 1-B clockwise with the specified torque.

    Torque:
    39 N*m{ 398 kgf*cm , 29 ft.*lbf }

    Text in Illustration 
    *a
    		Hold

    		Turn

    NOTICE:
    Check that SST is secured on the rotor shaft.




  1. Insert SST 2 and attach it to the pulley nut.
    Text in Illustration 

    		Insert




  1. To loosen the pulley nut, turn SST 1-A in the direction shown in the illustration.

    NOTICE:
    To prevent damage to the rotor shaft, do not loosen the pulley nut more than one-half turn.

    Text in Illustration 
    *a
    		Hold
    *b
    		Turn

  1. Remove SST 2 from the generator.




  1. Turn SST 1-B in the direction shown in the illustration, and then remove SST 1-A and SST 1-B.
    Text in Illustration 
    *a
    		Hold

    		Turn

  1. Remove the pulley nut and pulley.



2. REMOVE GENERATOR REAR END COVER



  1. Remove the 3 nuts and generator rear end cover.



3. REMOVE TERMINAL INSULATOR



  1. Remove the terminal insulator.



4. REMOVE GENERATOR BRUSH HOLDER ASSEMBLY



  1. Remove the 2 screws and brush holder.



5. REMOVE GENERATOR COIL ASSEMBLY



  1. Remove the 4 bolts.




  1. Using SST, remove the generator coil assembly.

    SST
    09950-40011   (09951-04020, 09952-04010, 09953-04020, 09954-04010, 09955-04071, 09957-04010, 09958-04011)
    Text in Illustration 
    *a
    		Hold

    		Turn



6. REMOVE GENERATOR ROTOR ASSEMBLY



  1. Remove the generator washer.




  1. Remove the generator rotor assembly.



7. INSPECT GENERATOR DRIVE END FRAME BEARING



  1. Check that the drive end frame bearing is not rough or worn.
    If necessary, replace the generator drive end frame bearing.



8. REMOVE GENERATOR DRIVE END FRAME BEARING



  1. Remove the 4 screws and retainer plate.




  1. Using SST and a hammer, tap out the bearing.

    SST
    09950-60010   (09951-00250)
    09950-70010   (09951-07100)
