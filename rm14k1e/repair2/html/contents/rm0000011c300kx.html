PARKING BRAKE PEDAL > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here



1. DISCONNECT CABLE FROM NEGATIVE BATTERY TERMINAL

CAUTION:
Wait at least 90 seconds after disconnecting the cable from the negative (-) battery terminal to disable the SRS system.

NOTICE:
  1. w/ Navigation System (for HDD):
    After the engine switch is turned off, the display and navigation module display (HDD navigation system) records various types of memory and settings. As a result, after turning the engine switch off, be sure to wait for the time specified in the following table before disconnecting the cable from the negative (-) battery terminal.

    Waiting Time before Disconnecting Cable from Negative (-) Battery Terminal:

    Condition
    		Waiting Time
    Vehicle enrolled in G-BOOK system
    		6 minutes
    Vehicle not enrolled in G-BOOK system
    		1 minute

  2. When disconnecting the cable, some systems need to be initialized after the cable is reconnected (Click here).



2. REMOVE LOWER NO. 1 INSTRUMENT PANEL AIRBAG ASSEMBLY

  1. Remove the lower No. 1 instrument panel airbag assembly (Click here).



3. REMOVE REAR CONSOLE BOX ASSEMBLY

  1. Remove the rear console box assembly (Click here).



4. REMOVE CENTER AIRBAG SENSOR ASSEMBLY



  1. Disconnect the connector.

  1. Remove the 3 bolts and airbag sensor.



5. REMOVE YAW RATE AND ACCELERATION SENSOR



  1. Remove the 2 bolts and yaw rate and acceleration sensor.

  1. Disconnect the connector.

  1. Detach the clamp from the sensor bracket.



6. REMOVE TURN SIGNAL FLASHER ASSEMBLY



  1. Disconnect the connector.

  1. Remove the nut and turn signal flasher.



7. REMOVE PARKING BRAKE CONTROL PEDAL ASSEMBLY



  1. Release the parking brake pedal.

  1. Disconnect the parking brake switch connector.

  1. Remove the 3 nuts and parking brake control pedal assembly.



8. REMOVE TRANSMISSION FLOOR SHIFT ASSEMBLY

  1. Remove the transmission floor shift assembly (Click here).



9. REMOVE NO. 1 PARKING BRAKE CABLE ASSEMBLY



  1. Remove the lock nut and adjusting nut.

  1. Remove the clip.

  1. Pull up the parking pedal claw.

  1. Remove the No. 1 parking brake cable assembly.



  1. Remove the 4 nuts and 3 bolts from the No. 1 parking brake cable assembly.






  1. Remove the lock nut, adjusting nut and No. 1 parking brake cable assembly from the parking brake equalizer.

  1. Disconnect the No. 1 parking brake cable No. 3 clamp from the No. 1 parking brake cable.



10. REMOVE PARKING PEDAL PAD

  1. Remove the parking pedal pad.
