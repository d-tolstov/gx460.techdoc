FRONT CONSOLE BOX (w/ Refrigerated Cool Box) > DISASSEMBLY

for Preparation Click here



1. REMOVE CONSOLE COMPARTMENT DOOR SUB-ASSEMBLY



  1. Remove the 6 screws and console compartment door sub-assembly.



2. REMOVE COOLING BOX ASSEMBLY



  1. Remove the 6 screws.

  1. Detach the 12 claws and remove the cooling box assembly.



3. REMOVE AIR CONDITIONING AMPLIFIER ASSEMBLY



  1. Remove the 2 screws.

  1. Disconnect the connector and remove the air conditioning amplifier assembly.



4. REMOVE NO. 3 COOLER UNIT DRAIN HOSE



  1. Remove the No. 3 cooler unit drain hose.



5. REMOVE NO. 1 COOLER WIRING HARNESS SUB-ASSEMBLY

  1. Remove the packing.




  1. Disconnect the 3 connectors and remove the No. 1 cooler wiring harness sub-assembly.



6. REMOVE COOLER CONTROL SWITCH SUB-ASSEMBLY



  1. Detach the 2 claws and remove the cooler control switch sub-assembly.



7. REMOVE COOLING UNIT DOOR

  1. Remove the packing.




  1. Remove the 8 screws, 2 bolts and cooling unit door.



8. REMOVE NO. 2 COOLER EVAPORATOR SUB-ASSEMBLY



  1. Remove the 3 bolts.

  1. Remove the 5 holding springs.




  1. Remove the 4 bolts.




  1. Remove the cooling unit case.




  1. Remove the cooler evaporator assembly.




  1. Detach the 2 claws.




  1. Loosen the 2 nuts and remove the liquid tube, cooler magnet valve assembly and No. 2 evaporator sub-assembly.
