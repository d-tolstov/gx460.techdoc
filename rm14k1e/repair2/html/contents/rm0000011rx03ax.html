REAR DOOR > DISASSEMBLY

for Preparation Click here


HINT:
  1. Use the same procedure for the RH and LH sides.
  2. The procedure listed below is for the LH side.



1. DISCONNECT CABLE FROM NEGATIVE BATTERY TERMINAL

NOTICE:
  1. w/ Navigation System (for HDD):
    After the engine switch is turned off, the display and navigation module display (HDD navigation system) records various types of memory and settings. As a result, after turning the engine switch off, be sure to wait for the time specified in the following table before disconnecting the cable from the negative (-) battery terminal.

    Waiting Time before Disconnecting Cable from Negative (-) Battery Terminal :

    Condition
    		Waiting Time
    Vehicle enrolled in G-BOOK system
    		6 minutes
    Vehicle not enrolled in G-BOOK system
    		1 minute

  2. When disconnecting the cable, some systems need to be initialized after the cable is reconnected (Click here).



2. REMOVE REAR DOOR INSIDE HANDLE BEZEL PLUG LH



  1. Using moulding remover A, detach the 3 claws and remove the rear door inside handle bezel plug as shown in the illustration.



3. REMOVE REAR ARMREST ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Using moulding remover B, detach the 7 claws and 7 guides and remove the rear armrest assembly as shown in the illustration.



4. REMOVE REAR POWER WINDOW REGULATOR SWITCH ASSEMBLY WITH REAR DOOR ARMREST BASE PANEL



  1. Using a moulding remover B, detach the 2 clips, 4 claws and guide as shown in the illustration.

  1. Disconnect the connector and remove the rear power window regulator switch assembly with rear door armrest base panel.



5. REMOVE REAR DOOR TRIM BOARD SUB-ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Remove the 2 screws.

  1. Using a clip remover, detach the 9 clips.




  1. Pull out the rear door trim board sub-assembly in the direction indicated by the arrow in the illustration.

  1. Raise the rear door trim board sub-assembly to detach the 4 claws and remove the rear door trim board sub-assembly together with the rear door inner glass weatherstrip.




  1. Disconnect the rear door lock remote control cable assembly and rear door inside locking cable assembly as shown in the illustration.

  1. Disconnect each connector.




  1. Using a screwdriver, detach the claw and remove the clamp.



6. REMOVE REAR DOOR INNER GLASS WEATHERSTRIP LH



  1. Using a screwdriver, detach the 3 claws and remove the rear door inner glass weatherstrip from the rear door trim board sub-assembly as shown in the illustration.



7. REMOVE REAR POWER WINDOW REGULATOR SWITCH ASSEMBLY



  1. Detach the 2 claws and remove the rear power window regulator switch assembly.



8. REMOVE NO. 2 COURTESY LIGHT ASSEMBLY



  1. Detach the 2 claws and remove the light.

  1. Disconnect the connector.



9. REMOVE REAR DOOR INSIDE HANDLE ILLUMINATION LIGHT ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Disconnect the connector.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape

  1. Using a screwdriver, detach the claw and remove the light.

    HINT:
    Tape the screwdriver tip before use.



10. REMOVE REAR DOOR INSIDE HANDLE SUB-ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Remove the 14 screws.




  1. Move the assist grip together with the inside handle sub-assembly to detach the 9 guides.




  1. Slide the assist grip together with the inside handle sub-assembly to make a space between the ornament panel and assist grip.




  1. Rotate the assist grip together with the inside handle sub-assembly to detach the 2 guides on the rear of the trim, and then pull out the assist grip together with the inside handle sub-assembly.




  1. Detach the 5 claws and remove the inside handle sub-assembly.



11. REMOVE REAR NO. 2 SPEAKER ASSEMBLY (for 14 Speakers)



  1. Remove the 2 screws and No. 2 speaker.

    NOTICE:
    Do not touch the cone of the speaker.



12. REMOVE REAR SPEAKER ASSEMBLY



  1. Disconnect the connector.

  1. Remove the 3 screws.

  1. Detach the 2 claws and remove the rear speaker.

    NOTICE:
    Do not touch the cone of the speaker.



13. REMOVE REAR DOOR SERVICE HOLE COVER LH



  1. Disconnect the connector.

  1. Detach the 3 clamps, move the wire harness out of the way and remove the service hole cover.

    HINT:
    Remove any remaining butyl tape from the door.



14. REMOVE REAR DOOR GLASS RUN LH



  1. Remove the rear door glass run.



15. REMOVE REAR DOOR FRAME GARNISH LH



  1. Detach the clip and remove the rear door frame garnish.



16. REMOVE REAR DOOR WINDOW DIVISION BAR SUB-ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Loosen the temporary bolt.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Temporary Bolt

  1. Remove the 2 bolts and screw.

  1. Remove the rear door window division bar sub-assembly.

  1. Remove the temporary bolt from the rear door window division bar sub-assembly.



17. REMOVE REAR DOOR QUARTER WINDOW GLASS LH



  1. Remove the rear door quarter window glass and rear door quarter window weatherstrip as a unit as shown in the illustration.



18. REMOVE REAR DOOR QUARTER WINDOW WEATHERSTRIP LH



  1. Remove the rear door quarter window weatherstrip from the rear door quarter window glass.



19. REMOVE REAR DOOR GLASS SUB-ASSEMBLY LH

  1. Connect the cable to the negative (-) battery terminal and rear power window regulator motor connector.

  1. Connect the power window regulator switch assembly and move the rear door glass sub-assembly so that the door glass bolts can be seen.

  1. Disconnect the power window regulator switch assembly and rear power window regulator motor connectors.

  1. Disconnect the cable from the negative (-) battery terminal.

    NOTICE:
    1. w/ Navigation System (for HDD):
      After the engine switch is turned off, the display and navigation module display (HDD navigation system) records various types of memory and settings. As a result, after turning the engine switch off, be sure to wait for the time specified in the following table before disconnecting the cable from the negative (-) battery terminal.

      Waiting Time before Disconnecting Cable from Negative (-) Battery Terminal :

      Condition
      		Waiting Time
      Vehicle enrolled in G-BOOK system
      		6 minutes
      Vehicle not enrolled in G-BOOK system
      		1 minute

    2. When disconnecting the cable, some systems need to be initialized after the cable is reconnected (Click here).




  1. Remove the rear door glass sub-assembly from the rear door window regulator sub-assembly as shown in the illustration.

    NOTICE:
    After the bolts are removed, do not allow the door glass to fall.




  1. Remove the rear door glass sub-assembly as indicated by the arrows in the order shown in the illustration.

    NOTICE:
    Do not damage the door glass.



20. REMOVE REAR DOOR GLASS CHANNEL SUB-ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Using a screwdriver, remove the rear door glass channel sub-assembly.

    HINT:
    Tape the screwdriver tip before use.

21. REMOVE REAR DOOR GLASS CHANNEL FILLER

  1. Remove the rear door glass channel filler.



22. REMOVE REAR DOOR WINDOW REGULATOR SUB-ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Loosen the temporary bolt.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Temporary Bolt

    NOTICE:
    Do not remove the temporary bolt. If the temporary bolt is removed, the rear door window regulator may fall and cause damage.

  1. Remove the 3 bolts.

  1. Remove the rear door window regulator assembly.

  1. Remove the temporary bolt from the rear door window regulator assembly.



23. REMOVE POWER WINDOW REGULATOR MOTOR ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Using a T25 "TORX" socket wrench, remove the 3 screws and power window regulator motor.



24. REMOVE REAR DOOR LOCK ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Using a T30 "TORX" wrench, remove the 3 screws.
    Text in Illustration 

    		Slide

  1. Move the rear door lock assembly downward, pull the release plate out of the rear door outside handle frame, and remove the rear door lock assembly and cables as a unit.

  1. Remove the door lock wiring harness seal from the rear door lock assembly.



25. REMOVE REAR DOOR LOCK REMOTE CONTROL CABLE ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Using a screwdriver, detach the claw.

    HINT:
    Tape the screwdriver tip before use.




  1. Remove the rear door lock remote control cable assembly.



26. REMOVE REAR DOOR INSIDE LOCKING CABLE ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Using a screwdriver, detach the 3 claws.

    HINT:
    Tape the screwdriver tip before use.




  1. Remove the rear door inside locking cable assembly.



27. REMOVE REAR DOOR OUTSIDE HANDLE COVER LH



  1. Using a T30 "TORX" socket wrench, loosen the screw.

    HINT:
    The screw cannot be removed because it is integrated into the rear door outside handle frame sub-assembly.

  1. Detach the claw and remove the rear door outside handle cover.



28. REMOVE REAR DOOR OUTSIDE HANDLE ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Move the lever in the direction indicated by the arrow in the illustration.




  1. Remove the rear door outside handle assembly as shown in the illustration.



29. REMOVE REAR DOOR NO. 1 OUTSIDE HANDLE PAD LH



  1. Detach the 3 claws and remove the rear door No. 1 outside handle pad.



30. REMOVE REAR DOOR NO. 2 OUTSIDE HANDLE PAD LH



  1. Detach the 2 claws and remove the rear door No. 2 outside handle pad.



31. REMOVE REAR DOOR OUTSIDE HANDLE FRAME SUB-ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Using a T30 "TORX" socket wrench, loosen the screw.

  1. Slide the rear door outside handle frame sub-assembly to detach the door handle nut and claw of the rear door outside handle frame sub-assembly, and then remove the rear door outside handle frame sub-assembly.



32. REMOVE REAR DOOR NO. 2 WEATHERSTRIP LH



  1. Using a clip remover, detach the 6 clips and guide and remove the rear door No. 2 weatherstrip as shown in the illustration.



33. REMOVE REAR DOOR NO. 3 WEATHERSTRIP LH

HINT:
When removing the rear door No. 3 weatherstrip, heat the vehicle body and No. 3 rear door weatherstrip using a heat light.

NOTICE:
Do not heat the vehicle body or rear door No. 3 weatherstrip excessively.




  1. Using a clip remover, detach the clip.

  1. Using a heat light, heat the rear door No. 3 weatherstrip.

    Heating Temperature:
    Item
    		Temperature
    Vehicle body
    		40 to 60°C (104 to 140°F)
    rear door No. 3 weatherstrip
    		20 to 30°C (68 to 86°F)

  1. Remove the rear door No. 3 weatherstrip.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Double-sided Tape

  1. Remove the bolt, 2 nuts and rear door check assembly.



35. REMOVE REAR DOOR WEATHERSTRIP LH



  1. Using a clip remover, detach the 19 clips and guide and remove the rear door weatherstrip.



36. REMOVE REAR DOOR BELT MOULDING LH
