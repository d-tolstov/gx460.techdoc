FRONT WIPER MOTOR > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here



1. DISCONNECT CABLE FROM NEGATIVE BATTERY TERMINAL

NOTICE:
  1. w/ Navigation System (for HDD):
    After the engine switch is turned off, the display and navigation module display (HDD navigation system) records various types of memory and settings. As a result, after turning the engine switch off, be sure to wait for the time specified in the following table before disconnecting the cable from the negative (-) battery terminal.

    Waiting Time before Disconnecting Cable from Negative (-) Battery Terminal :

    Condition
    		Waiting Time
    Vehicle enrolled in G-BOOK system
    		6 minutes
    Vehicle not enrolled in G-BOOK system
    		1 minute

  2. When disconnecting the cable, some systems need to be initialized after the cable is reconnected (Click here).



2. REMOVE UPPER RADIATOR SUPPORT SEAL



  1. Remove the 13 clips and upper radiator support seal.



3. REMOVE ENGINE ROOM SIDE COVER LH



  1. Detach the 4 clips and remove the engine room side cover.



4. REMOVE ENGINE ROOM COVER SIDE



  1. Detach the 4 clips and remove the engine room side cover.



5. REMOVE FRONT FENDER SIDE PANEL PROTECTOR LH



  1. Detach the 3 clips and guide, and remove the front fender side panel protector.



6. REMOVE FRONT FENDER SIDE PANEL PROTECTOR RH

HINT:
Use the same procedure for the RH side and LH side.



7. REMOVE FRONT FENDER TO COWL SIDE SEAL LH



  1. Detach the 5 claws and remove the front fender to cowl side seal LH.



8. REMOVE FRONT FENDER TO COWL SIDE SEAL RH

HINT:
Use the same procedure for the RH side and LH side.



9. REMOVE FRONT WIPER ARM HEAD CAP



  1. Using a screwdriver, detach the 3 claws and remove the wiper arm head cap.

    HINT:
    1. Tape the screwdriver tip before use.
    2. Use the same procedure for the RH and LH sides.

    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape



10. REMOVE FRONT WIPER ARM AND BLADE ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Remove the nut and front wiper arm and blade assembly LH.



11. REMOVE FRONT WIPER ARM AND BLADE ASSEMBLY RH



  1. Remove the nut and front wiper arm and blade assembly RH.



12. REMOVE HOOD TO COWL TOP SEAL



  1. Detach the 7 claws and 2 clips and remove the hood to cowl top seal.



13. REMOVE CENTER NO. 2 COWL TOP VENTILATOR LOUVER



  1. Detach the 2 claws and 3 guides and remove the center No. 2 cowl top ventilator louver.



14. REMOVE COWL TOP VENTILATOR LOUVER SUB-ASSEMBLY



  1. Remove the 2 clips.

  1. Detach the 6 claws and guide (A).

  1. Disengage the 10 guides (B) and pull out the cowl top ventilator louver sub-assembly.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Guide A
    *2
    		Guide B



15. REMOVE WINDSHIELD WIPER MOTOR AND LINK



  1. Detach the clamp and disconnect the connector.




  1. Remove the 2 bolts.




  1. Detach the grommet and remove the windshield wiper motor and link as shown in the illustration.

    NOTICE:
    Be careful not to damage the windshield when removing the windshield wiper motor and link assembly.

    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Grommet



16. REMOVE WINDSHIELD WIPER MOTOR ASSEMBLY



  1. Using a screwdriver, separate the windshield wiper link rod from the crank arm pivot of the front wiper crank as shown in the illustration.

    HINT:
    Tape the screwdriver tip before use.

    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape




  1. Disconnect the connector.

  1. Using a T30 ''TORX'' socket, remove the 2 bolts and windshield wiper motor.



17. REMOVE WIPER MOTOR WIRE



  1. Detach the claw and remove the wiper motor wire.
