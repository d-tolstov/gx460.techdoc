DTC    C1882/82    Power Supply Voltage Malfunction

for Preparation Click here


DESCRIPTION

The stabilizer control ECU recognizes the engine switch on (IG) signal based on the voltage input to the IG terminal.

DTC Code
DTC Detection Condition
Trouble Area
C1882/82
The IG terminal voltage is 10 V or less or 16 V or higher for 0.5 seconds
  1. Battery
  2. Charging system
  3. KDSS fuse
  4. Harness or connector
  5. Stabilizer control ECU
