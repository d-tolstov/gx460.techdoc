DRIVE BELT > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here



1. REMOVE V-BANK COVER SUB-ASSEMBLY



  1. Raise the front of the V-bank cover to detach the 2 pins. Then detach the 2 V-bank cover hooks from the No. 1 V-bank cover bracket and remove the V-bank cover.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Pin
    *2
    		Hook
    *3
    		No. 1 V-bank Cover Bracket



2. REMOVE UPPER RADIATOR SUPPORT SEAL



  1. Remove the 13 clips and upper radiator support seal.



3. REMOVE FAN AND GENERATOR V BELT



  1. While turning the belt tensioner counterclockwise, align the service hole for the belt tensioner and the belt tensioner fixing position, and then insert a bar with a diameter of 5 mm (0.197 in.) into the service hole to fix the belt tensioner in place.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Bar
    *2
    		Service Hole

    HINT:
    The pulley bolt for the belt tensioner has a left-hand thread.

  1. Remove the fan and generator V belt.
