IGNITION COIL AND SPARK PLUG > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here



1. REMOVE V-BANK COVER SUB-ASSEMBLY



  1. Raise the front of the V-bank cover to detach the 2 pins. Then detach the 2 V-bank cover hooks from the No. 1 V-bank cover bracket and remove the V-bank cover.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Pin
    *2
    		Hook
    *3
    		No. 1 V-bank Cover Bracket



2. REMOVE AIR CLEANER CAP AND HOSE



  1. Disconnect the mass air flow meter connector, No. 1 air hose and No. 2 ventilation hose and detach the wire harness clamp.

  1. Loosen the hose clamp.

  1. Detach the 4 clamps and remove the air cleaner cap and hose.



3. REMOVE AIR CLEANER FILTER ELEMENT SUB-ASSEMBLY


4. REMOVE AIR CLEANER CASE SUB-ASSEMBLY



  1. Remove the 3 bolts and air cleaner case.



5. REMOVE IGNITION COIL ASSEMBLY


  1. for Bank 1:
    Remove the 4 ignition coils.




    1. Detach the clamp and disconnect the engine wire.

    2. Disconnect the 4 ignition coil connectors.




    3. Remove the 4 bolts and 4 ignition coils.



  1. for Bank 2:
    Remove the 4 ignition coils.




    1. Detach the 2 clamps and disconnect the engine wire.

    2. Disconnect the 4 ignition coil connectors.




    3. Detach the clamp and disconnect the engine wire.




    4. Remove the 2 bolts and disconnect the No. 4 water by-pass pipe.




    5. Remove the 4 bolts and 4 ignition coils.



6. REMOVE SPARK PLUG

  1. Using a 16 mm plug wrench, remove the 8 spark plugs.
