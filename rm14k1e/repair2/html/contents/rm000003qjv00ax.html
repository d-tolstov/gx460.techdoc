1. INSPECT HAZARD WARNING SIGNAL SWITCH ASSEMBLY



  1. Measure the resistance according to the value(s) in the table below.

    Standard Resistance:
    Tester Connection
    		Switch Condition
    		Specified Condition
    4 (B) - 1 (E)
    		Hazard warning signal switch off
    		10 kΩ or higher
    Hazard warning signal switch on
    		Below 1 Ω

  1. Apply battery voltage to the connector and check the LED illumination condition.

    OK:
    Measurement Condition
    		Specified Condition
    Battery positive (+) → Terminal 3 (ILL+)
    Battery negative (-) → Terminal 2 (ILL-)
    		LED illuminates

    If the result is not as specified, there may be a malfunction in the hazard warning signal switch.

    		Component without harness connected
