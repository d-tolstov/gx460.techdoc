BACK WINDOW LOCK > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here



1. REMOVE BACK DOOR CENTER GARNISH



  1. Detach the 4 clips and remove the back door center garnish.



2. REMOVE BACK DOOR SIDE GARNISH LH



  1. Detach the 2 clips and claw and remove the back door side garnish.



3. REMOVE BACK DOOR SIDE GARNISH RH



  1. Detach the 2 clips and claw and remove the back door side garnish.



4. REMOVE BACK DOOR TRIM COVER



  1. Remove the back door trim cover as shown in the illustration.



5. REMOVE UPPER TOOL BOX PANEL SUB-ASSEMBLY



  1. Remove the upper tool box panel sub-assembly as shown in the illustration.



6. REMOVE TOOL CASE



  1. Detach the 2 claws and remove the tool case.



7. REMOVE BACK DOOR LOCK COVER



  1. Using moulding remover B, detach the clip and 6 claws and remove the back door lock cover.



8. REMOVE BACK DOOR TRIM PANEL ASSEMBLY



  1. Detach the claw and remove the cover.

    HINT:
    Tape the screwdriver tip before use.




  1. Remove the screw.




  1. Remove the bolt.

  1. Detach the 15 clips and remove the back door trim panel assembly.



9. REMOVE BACK WINDOW LOCK ASSEMBLY



  1. Disconnect the connector.

  1. Remove the 3 bolts and back window lock assembly.
