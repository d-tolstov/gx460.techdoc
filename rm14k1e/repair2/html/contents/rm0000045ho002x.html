STEP LIGHT ASSEMBLY > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here


HINT:
  1. Use the same procedure for the RH and LH sides.
  2. The procedure listed below is for the LH side.



1. REMOVE SIDE STEP ASSEMBLY LH

  1. Remove the side step assembly LH (Click here).



2. REMOVE STEP LIGHT ASSEMBLY



  1. Remove the 4 bolts and 2 lights.



3. REMOVE STEP LIGHT BULB



  1. Turn the socket in the direction indicated by the arrow and remove it.

  1. Remove the bulb.



4. REMOVE STEP PANEL WIRE LH



  1. Detach the 10 clamps and remove the wire.
