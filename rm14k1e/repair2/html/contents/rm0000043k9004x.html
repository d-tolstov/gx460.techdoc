DTC    B2075    Passenger Side Seat Belt Buckle Switch Circuit Malfunction

for Preparation Click here


DESCRIPTION

This DTC is stored when there is a malfunction in the front seat inner belt buckle switch circuit for the passenger side. The seat belt control ECU receives buckle switch condition information from the front seat inner belt and controls the pre-crash safety system.

DTC Code
DTC Detection Condition
Trouble Area
B2075
A malfunction in the front seat inner belt (buckle switch) circuit.
  1. Front seat inner belt assembly RH
  2. Harness or connector
  3. Seat belt control ECU
