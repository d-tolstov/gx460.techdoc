DTC    B2611    Telescopic Position Sensor or Telescopic Motor Circuit Malfunction


DESCRIPTION

WIRING DIAGRAM

INSPECTION PROCEDURE

PERFORM ACTIVE TEST USING INTELLIGENT TESTER (TELESCO OPERATION)

CHECK HARNESS AND CONNECTOR (MULTIPLEX TILT AND TELESCOPIC ECU - BATTERY)

CHECK HARNESS AND CONNECTOR (MULTIPLEX TILT AND TELESCOPIC ECU - BODY GROUND)

CHECK TELESCOPIC MOTOR

CHECK HARNESS AND CONNECTOR (MULTIPLEX TILT AND TELESCOPIC ECU - BODY GROUND)

CHECK MULTIPLEX TILT AND TELESCOPIC ECU (VCE TERMINAL VOLTAGE)

CHECK TELESCOPIC POSITION SENSOR

CHECK STEERING COLUMN ASSEMBLY
