1. INSPECT HOOD LOCK ASSEMBLY (ENGINE HOOD COURTESY SWITCH)



  1. Measure the resistance according to the value(s) in the table below.

    Standard Resistance:
    Tester Connection
    		Condition
    		Specified Condition
    1 (+) - 4 (E)
    		Unlock position
    		Below 1 Ω
    1 (+) - 4 (E)
    		Lock position
    		10 kΩ or higher
    2 (HWM+) - 3 (HWM-)
    		Always
    		Below 1 Ω

    If the result is not as specified, replace the hood lock assembly.
