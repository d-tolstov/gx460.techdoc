TRANSFER ADAPTER OIL SEAL > REPLACEMENT

for Preparation Click here



1. REMOVE TRANSFER ASSEMBLY

  1. Remove the transfer (Click here).



2. REMOVE TRANSFER CASE ADAPTER REAR OIL SEAL



  1. Using SST, tap out the oil seal.

    SST
    09308-00010  



3. INSTALL TRANSFER CASE ADAPTER REAR OIL SEAL

  1. Coat the lip of a new oil seal with ATF.




  1. Using SST and a hammer, tap in the oil seal.

    SST
    09223-00010  

    Standard depth:
    0 to 0.5 mm (0 to 0.0197 in.)



4. INSTALL TRANSFER ASSEMBLY

  1. Install the transfer (Click here).
