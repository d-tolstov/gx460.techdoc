1. CUSTOMIZE ENTRY AND START SYSTEM (for Start Function)

HINT:
The following items can be customized.

NOTICE:
  1. When the customer requests a change in a function, first make sure that the function can be customized.
  2. Be sure to make a note of the current setting before customizing.
  3. When troubleshooting a function, first make sure that the function is set to the default setting.

  1. Customizing with intelligent tester

    1. Connect the intelligent tester to the DLC3.

    2. Turn the engine switch on (IG).

    3. Turn the intelligent tester on.

    4. Enter the following menus: Utility / Customize / Entry & Start or Warning.

    5. Select the setting by referring to the table below.

      Tester Display
      		Default
      		Content
      		Setting
      		Relevant ECU
      Auto Entry Cancel SW
      		OFF
      		Function that enables or disables the entry and start system.
      		ON or OFF
      		Certification ECU
      Ignition Available Area
      		All
      		Function that changes the area that the key must be in before the engine switch can be operated.
      		Front or All
      		Certification ECU
      Key Low Battery Warning (Warn when key battery becomes weak)
      		ON
      		Function that enables or disables the sounding of the buzzer when the key battery is low and the engine switch is turned off after being on (IG) for 20 minutes or more.
      		ON or OFF
      		Certification ECU

  1. Customizing with Multi-information display

    1. Turn the engine switch on (IG).

    2. With the vehicle stationary or at a vehicle speed of less than 8 km/h (4.9 mph), press and hold the menu switch on the steering pad switch to display the meter customize mode screen.

    3. Select the setting by referring to the table below.

      Display
      		Default
      		Content
      		Setting
      		Relevant ECU
      ACCESS SYSTEM WITH ELEC. KEY
      		ON
      		Function that enables or disables the entry and start system.
      		ON or OFF
      		Certification ECU

  1. Customizing key cancel function

    1. The key cancel operation disables the following functions:

      1. Entry Unlock/Lock
      2. Key Lock-in Prevention
      3. Entry Glass Hatch Open
      4. Entry Warning
      5. Entry Ignition

      NOTICE:
      Because the key (card type) does not have a wireless transmitter function, it cannot be used to perform the key cancel operation.

    2. Make sure the following conditions are met:

      1. The engine switch is off.
      2. The driver side door is closed.
      3. The driver side door is unlocked.

    3. Press the unlock button of the key.

    4. Open the driver side door within 5 seconds of completing the step above (driver side door: closed at beginning of step → opened).

    5. With the driver side door open, press the unlock button of the key 2 times within 5 seconds of completing the step above.

      NOTICE:
      If the driver side door is closed before or while pressing the unlock button, the entry cancel setting mode will end.

    6. Close and open the driver side door twice within 30 seconds of completing the step above (driver side door: open at beginning of step → closed → opened → closed → opened).

    7. With the driver side door open, press the unlock button of the key 2 times within 30 seconds of completing the step above.

      NOTICE:
      If the driver side door is closed before or while pressing the unlock button, the entry cancel setting mode will end.

    8. Close and open the driver side door within 30 seconds of completing the step above (driver side door: open at beginning of step → closed → opened).

    9. Close the driver side door within 5 seconds of completing the above step.

    10. Check that the wireless buzzer sounds twice (short beeps) to confirm that the entry and start system has been canceled.

    11. To return the system to the original condition, perform the procedure again. When the original condition is restored, the wireless door lock buzzer sounds once.
