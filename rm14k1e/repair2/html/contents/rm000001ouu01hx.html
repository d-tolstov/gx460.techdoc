HEADLIGHT CLEANER ACTUATOR > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here



1. REMOVE RADIATOR GRILLE

  1. Remove the radiator grille (Click here).



2. REMOVE FRONT BUMPER COVER

  1. Put protective tape around the front bumper cover.

  1. Remove the 2 bolts labeled A and 2 bolts labeled B.




  1. Remove the 2 screws and 4 clips.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape
    *2
    		Bolt A
    *3
    		Bolt B




  1. Remove the 2 screws.

    HINT:
    Use the same procedure for the RH and LH sides.




  1. Detach the 7 claws.

    HINT:
    Use the same procedure for the RH and LH sides.

  1. w/ LEXUS Parking Assist-sensor System:
    Disconnect the connector.

  1. Disconnect the headlight cleaner hose and 2 fog light connectors.

  1. Remove the front bumper cover.



3. REMOVE TYPE 1 HEADLIGHT WASHER NOZZLE SUB-ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Extend the headlight washer nozzle by hand.

    NOTICE:
    Be careful not to damage the headlight washer actuator sub-assembly or headlight washer nozzle.




  1. Detach the 2 claws and remove the headlight cleaner washer nozzle.



4. REMOVE TYPE 1 HEADLIGHT WASHER NOZZLE SUB-ASSEMBLY RH

HINT:
Use the same procedure described for the LH side.



5. REMOVE HEADLIGHT CLEANER HOSE



  1. Disconnect the headlight cleaner hose from the headlight washer actuator sub-assembly.

  1. Detach the 6 clamps and remove the hose from the lower radiator grille.



6. REMOVE HEADLIGHT WASHER ACTUATOR SUB-ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Remove the bolt.




  1. Detach the 2 claws and remove the headlight washer actuator sub-assembly.



7. REMOVE HEADLIGHT WASHER ACTUATOR SUB-ASSEMBLY RH

HINT:
Use the same procedure described for the LH side.
