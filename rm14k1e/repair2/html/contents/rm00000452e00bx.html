DTC    C1409    Front Speed Sensor RH Performance

DTC    C1410    Front Speed Sensor LH Performance

for Preparation Click here


DESCRIPTION

Refer to DTCs C1401 and C1402 (Click here).

DTC Code
DTC Detection Condition
Trouble Area
C1409
C1410
One of the following conditions is met:
  1. When the vehicle is driven in reverse at a speed of 3 km/h (2 mph) or more and 3 of the wheel sensors detect the reverse signal, the other sensor detects a high frequency pulse 75 times while the engine switch is on (IG).
  2. At a vehicle speed of 10 km/h (6 mph) or more, the vehicle speed sensor output drops by 50% for 5 seconds.
  3. The lowest wheel speed is 10 km/h (6 mph) or more and the difference between the highest and lowest wheel speed values is 2 km/h (1 mph) or less for 15 seconds.
  4. At a vehicle speed of 30 km/h (19 mph) or more, one wheel direction is different from the other 3 wheels for 1 second.
  5. At a vehicle speed of 100 km/h (62 mph), a reverse signal is output for 1 second or more.
  6. At a vehicle speed of 30 km/h (19 mph) or more, the rotation direction of the one of the wheels is not detected normally and the rotation direction of the other 3 wheels is not the same for 1 second.
Front speed sensor RH/LH

HINT:
  1. DTC C1409 is for the front speed sensor RH.
  2. DTC C1410 is for the front speed sensor LH.
