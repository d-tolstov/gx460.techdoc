FRONT AXLE HUB > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here


HINT:
  1. Use the same procedure for the RH and LH sides.
  2. The procedure listed below is for the LH side.



1. REMOVE FRONT WHEEL


2. REMOVE DISC BRAKE CYLINDER ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Using a union nut wrench, disconnect the brake tube from the disc brake cylinder assembly.

    HINT:
    Use a container to catch brake fluid as it drains out.




  1. Remove the 2 bolts and disc brake cylinder assembly.



3. REMOVE FRONT DISC



  1. Place matchmarks on the disc and axle hub if planning to reuse the disc.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Matchmark

  1. Remove the front disc.



4. REMOVE FRONT AXLE HUB GREASE CAP

  1. Using a screwdriver and hammer, remove the front axle hub grease cap.

    NOTICE:
    Do not damage the axle hub.



5. REMOVE FRONT AXLE SHAFT NUT



  1. Remove the cotter pin and lock cap.

  1. Remove the front axle shaft nut.



6. REMOVE FRONT AXLE HUB SUB-ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Remove the 4 bolts.

  1. Using a plastic-faced hammer, tap out the front drive shaft from the front axle hub.

    NOTICE:
    Be careful not to damage the drive shaft boot.

  1. Remove the front axle hub and front disc brake dust cover.

  1. Remove the O-ring.
