ENGINE > GENERAL MAINTENANCE


HINT:
Work in a well-lighted area. Turn the front wheels fully to the right or left before beginning the inspection.



1. INSPECT DRIVE BELT

  1. Inspect the drive belt (Click here).



2. INSPECT SPARK PLUGS

  1. Inspect the spark plugs (Click here).



3. REPLACE ENGINE OIL AND OIL FILTER

  1. Inspect the engine oil and oil filter (Click here).



4. INSPECT ENGINE COOLANT

  1. Inspect the engine coolant (Click here).



5. REPLACE ENGINE COOLANT

  1. Replace the engine coolant (Click here).



6. INSPECT EXHAUST PIPES AND MOUNTINGS

  1. Visually check the pipes, hangers and connections for severe corrosion, leaks or damage.



7. INSPECT AIR CLEANER FILTER

  1. Remove the air cleaner filter.

  1. Visually check that the air cleaner filter is not excessively damaged or oily.

  1. Replace the air cleaner filter with a new one if necessary.



8. INSPECT AIR CLEANER CASE

  1. Check if foreign matter has accumulated in the bottom of the air cleaner case. If foreign matter has accumulated in the bottom of the air cleaner case, clean the air cleaner case.

    NOTICE:
    When cleaning the air cleaner case, prevent any foreign matter from entering into the engine.



9. INSPECT FUEL TANK CAP GASKET

  1. Inspect the fuel tank cap gasket (Click here).



10. INSPECT FUEL LINES, FUEL LINE CONNECTIONS, FUEL TANK VAPOR VENT SYSTEM HOSES AND FUEL TANK BAND

  1. Visually check the fuel lines and hoses for cracks, leakage, loose connections and deformation. Also, visually check the tank band for looseness.



11. INSPECT BATTERY ELECTROLYTE LEVEL

  1. Inspect the battery electrolyte level (Click here).



12. REPLACE FUEL FILTER

  1. Replace the fuel filter (Click here).



13. INSPECT CHARCOAL CANISTER

  1. Inspect the charcoal canister (Click here).
