GENERAL

  1. This system has a side television camera assembly built into the front passenger side outer rear view mirror assembly and driver side outer rear view mirror assembly* to display the passenger side view and driver side view* of the vehicle on the accessory meter assembly.

  2. This system consists of the following components: steering pad switch assembly (Wide view front and side monitor switch) and combination meter assembly.

    1. Front passenger side television camera assembly

    2. Driver side television camera assembly*

    3. Parking assist ECU

    4. Accessory meter assembly

    5. Steering pad switch assembly (Wide view front and side monitor switch)

    6. Spiral with sensor cable sub-assembly

    7. Driving support switch control ECU

    8. Skid control ECU

    9. ECM

  3. This system is equipped with a self-diagnosis system, which is operated on a designated window that appears on the display panel, just as in the navigation system.


FUNCTION OF COMPONENTS

  1. The parking assist ECU controls the system by using information from the following components.

    Item
    		Function
    1. Front Passenger Side Television Camera Assembly
    2. Driver Side Television Camera Assembly*
    1. Built into the front passenger side outer rear view mirror assembly and driver side outer rear view mirror assembly* to transmit the passenger side view and driver side view* of the vehicle to the parking assist ECU.
    2. Has a color video camera that uses a Charge-coupled Device (CCD) and a wide-angle lens.
    Parking Assist ECU
    1. Transmits video signals which contain a composite consisting of the passenger side view and driver side view* of the vehicle taken with the television camera to the accessory meter assembly.
    2. Performs overall control of the system by receiving signals from the sensors and accessory meter assembly.
    Accessory Meter Assembly
    		Receives the video signals containing a composite of the front passenger side view and driver side view* of the vehicle from the parking assist ECU and displays them on the display panel.
    ECM
    1. Transmits the destination signal to the parking assist ECU through CAN communication.
    2. Transmits the shift position signal to the parking assist ECU through CAN communication.
    3. Transmits the steering wheel information signal to the parking assist ECU through CAN communication.
    4. Transmits the transmission information signal to the parking assist ECU through CAN communication.
    Outer Mirror Switch Assembly
    1. Transmits the outer mirror switch signal to the parking assist ECU.
    Spiral with Sensor Cable Sub-assembly
    		Transmits a steering angle sensor signal to the parking assist ECU.
    Steering Pad Switch Assembly
    		Transmits the wide view front and side monitor switch signal to the parking assist ECU through CAN communication.
    Skid Control ECU
    		Transmits a vehicle speed signal to the parking assist ECU through CAN communication.


OPERATION EXPLANATION

  1. The wide view front and side monitor switch on signal is sent from the steering pad switch assembly to the parking assist ECU.
    After receiving the wide view front and side monitor switch on signal, the parking assist ECU switches the display signal for the accessory meter assembly from the meter and gauge system to the side monitor system.
