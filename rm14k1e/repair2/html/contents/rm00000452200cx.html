VEHICLE STABILITY CONTROL SYSTEM > TRC OFF Indicator Light Remains ON

for Preparation Click here


DESCRIPTION

The skid control ECU is connected to the combination meter via the CAN communication system. Pressing the VSC OFF switch turns off traction control and pressing and holding this switch turns off traction control and VSC. If traction control turns off, the TRC OFF message is displayed on the multi-information display.
When the temperature inside the hydraulic brake booster becomes too high, the TRC OFF message is displayed on the multi-information display and traction control is stopped until the temperature decreases.
When the transfer is in L4, VSC is prohibited and the VSC OFF indicator and TRC OFF indicator turn on.
