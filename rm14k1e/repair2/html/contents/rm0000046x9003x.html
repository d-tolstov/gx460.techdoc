1. INSTALL LOWER BACK DOOR OUTSIDE GARNISH

  1. Attach the 2 claws to install the lower back door outside garnish.

  1. Install the 6 screws.



2. INSTALL NO. 2 BACK DOOR OUTSIDE GARNISH

  1. Attach the 2 claws to install the No. 2 back door outside garnish.

  1. Install the 8 screws.



3. INSTALL BACK DOOR OUTSIDE GARNISH PROTECTOR

HINT:
When installing the back door outside garnish protector, heat the back door outside garnish sub-assembly surface using a heat light.

Standard:
Item
Temperature
Back Door Outside Garnish Sub-assembly
20 to 30°C (68 to 86°F)

NOTICE:
Do not heat the back door outside garnish sub-assembly excessively.



  1. For upper side:

    1. Clean the back door outside garnish sub-assembly surface.

      1. Using a heat light, heat the back door outside garnish sub-assembly surface.
      2. Remove the double-sided tape from the back door outside garnish sub-assembly surface.
      3. Wipe off any tape adhesive residue with cleaner.




    2. Install a new back door outside garnish protector.

      1. Remove the peeling paper from the face of a new back door outside garnish protector.

        HINT:
        After removing the peeling paper, keep the exposed adhesive free from foreign matter.

      2. Install the back door outside garnish protector as shown in the illustration.

        HINT:
        Press the back door outside garnish protector firmly to install it.



  1. For left and right side:

    HINT:
    Use the same procedure for both back door outside garnish protectors.


    1. Clean the back door outside garnish sub-assembly surface.

      1. Using a heat light, heat the back door outside garnish sub-assembly surface.
      2. Remove the double-sided tape from the back door outside garnish sub-assembly surface.
      3. Wipe off any tape adhesive residue with cleaner.




    2. Install a new back door outside garnish protector.

      1. Remove the peeling paper from the face of a new back door outside garnish protector.

        HINT:
        After removing the peeling paper, keep the exposed adhesive free from foreign matter.

      2. Install the back door outside garnish protector as shown in the illustration.

        HINT:
        Press the back door outside garnish protector firmly to install it.



  1. For lower side:

    HINT:
    Use the same procedure for both back door outside garnish protectors.


    1. Clean the back door outside garnish sub-assembly surface.

      1. Using a heat light, heat the back door outside garnish sub-assembly surface.
      2. Remove the double-sided tape from the back door outside garnish sub-assembly surface.
      3. Wipe off any tape adhesive residue with cleaner.




    2. Install a new back door outside garnish protector.

      1. Using a heat light, heat a new back door outside garnish protector and the back door outside garnish sub-assembly surface.
      2. Remove the peeling paper from the face of the back door outside garnish protector.

        HINT:
        After removing the peeling paper, keep the exposed adhesive free from foreign matter.

      3. Install the back door outside garnish protector as shown in the illustration.

        HINT:
        Press the back door outside garnish protector firmly to install it.
