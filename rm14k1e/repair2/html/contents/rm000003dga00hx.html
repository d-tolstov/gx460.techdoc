LIN COMMUNICATION SYSTEM > Rain Sensor LIN Communication Malfunction

for Preparation Click here


DESCRIPTION

When the auto wiper system is operated, the windshield wiper switch assembly uses LIN communication to communicate with the rain sensor. If the auto wiper system does not operate properly, a problem with the LIN communication between the windshield wiper switch assembly and rain sensor may be causing the malfunction.

WIRING DIAGRAM




INSPECTION PROCEDURE

1.CHECK HARNESS AND CONNECTOR (WINDSHIELD WIPER SWITCH - RAIN SENSOR)

  1. Disconnect the G50 windshield wiper switch assembly connector.

  1. Disconnect the W6 rain sensor connector.

  1. Measure the resistance according to the value(s) in the table below.

    Standard Resistance:
    Tester Connection

    		Specified Condition

    		Always
    		Below 1 Ω
    G50-5 (MPX1) or W6-3 (MPX) - Body ground
    		Always
    		10 kΩ or higher
