REAR CENTER SEAT OUTER BELT ASSEMBLY (for 60/40 Split Slide Walk-in Seat Type LH Side) > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here


CAUTION:
Wear protective gloves. Sharp areas on the parts may injure your hands.



1. REMOVE REAR NO. 1 SEAT ASSEMBLY LH

  1. Remove the rear No. 1 seat assembly LH (Click here).



2. REMOVE REAR SEAT UPPER RECLINING COVER LH



  1. Using a screwdriver, detach the 2 claws and remove the cover.

    HINT:
    Tape the screwdriver tip before use.

    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape



3. REMOVE RECLINING ADJUSTER RELEASE HANDLE LH



  1. Remove the 3 screws and handle.



4. REMOVE CENTER SEAT HINGE COVER RH



  1. Using a clip remover, remove the clip.

  1. Detach the 2 guides and remove the cover.



5. REMOVE REAR SEAT CUSHION MOULDING LH



  1. Using a clip remover, detach the clip.

  1. Using a moulding remover, detach the 3 claws.

  1. Move the moulding in the direction of the arrow to detach the guide and remove the moulding.



6. REMOVE REAR SEATBACK LOCK STRIKER COVER LH



  1. Using a clip remover, detach the 3 clips and remove the cover.



7. REMOVE SEAT CUSHION COVER WITH PAD



  1. Detach the hooks.




  1. w/ Seat Heater System:

    1. Disconnect the 2 seat heater connectors.

    2. Detach the 2 clamps and disconnect the seat heater wire harness.




  1. Detach the hook and remove the seat cushion cover with pad.



8. REMOVE CENTER SEATBACK ASSEMBLY



  1. Remove the 2 bolts and center seatback.



9. REMOVE REAR NO. 1 SEAT INNER BELT ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Remove the bolt and inner belt and disconnect the 3 point type belt anchor.



10. REMOVE RECLINING ADJUSTER KNOB CAP LH



  1. Using a screwdriver, detach the claw and guide, and then remove the cap.

    HINT:
    Tape the screwdriver tip before use.

    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape



11. REMOVE REAR SEAT LOCK CONTROL LEVER SUB-ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Remove the 2 screws.




  1. Disconnect the cable in the order shown in the illustration and remove the lever.



12. REMOVE SEAT BELT ANCHOR COVER CAP



  1. Using a screwdriver, detach the 2 claws and remove the cap.

    HINT:
    Tape the screwdriver tip before use.

    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape



13. REMOVE REAR SEAT SHOULDER BELT COVER



  1. Detach the 2 claws and 2 guides, and then remove the cover.

  1. Disconnect the outer belt from the cover.



14. REMOVE REAR SEATBACK COVER



  1. Using a screwdriver, detach the 4 claws and remove the cover.

    HINT:
    Tape the screwdriver tip before use.



15. REMOVE REAR SEATBACK BOARD CARPET ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Using a clip remover, detach the 7 clips and remove the carpet.



16. REMOVE REAR NO. 1 SEAT HEADREST SUPPORT ASSEMBLY



  1. Detach the 4 claws and remove the 2 supports.



17. REMOVE SEATBACK COVER WITH PAD



  1. Remove the nut, detach the 2 guides and disconnect the seatback cover bracket from the seatback frame.




  1. Remove the hog rings and detach the hooks, and then remove the seatback cover with pad.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Hook
    *2
    		Hog Ring




  1. Disconnect the outer belt from the seatback cover with pad.



18. REMOVE REAR SEAT SHOULDER BELT GUIDE



  1. Detach the claw and guide, and then remove the belt guide.



19. REMOVE REAR SEATBACK EDGE PROTECTOR

  1. Remove the protector from the seatback frame.



20. REMOVE NO. 1 SEAT 3 POINT TYPE BELT ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Remove the nut and bolt, and then detach the 2 claws and remove the seat belt.
