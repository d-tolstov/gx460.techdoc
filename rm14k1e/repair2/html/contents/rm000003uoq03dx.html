INSPECTION PROCEDURE

HINT:
Even if the portable player can play audio content, it may not be able to play via the in-vehicle device. This does not necessarily indicate a malfunction of the in-vehicle device.

1.CHECK OPERATION

  1. Check if the portable player operates normally.

    OK:
    The portable player operates normally.



NG
MALFUNCTION IN PORTABLE PLAYER
OK


2.CHECK AUDIO SETTINGS



  1. Enter the "Sound Settings" screen by pressing the "Sound" switch on the audio display.

  1. Set volume, fader and balance to the initial values and check that sound is normal.

    OK:
    Audio system returns to normal.

    HINT:
    Sound quality adjustment measures vary according to the type of the amplifier.



NG
TURN VOLUME UP AND SET MUTE OFF
OK


3.CHECK USING ANOTHER "BLUETOOTH" AUDIO COMPATIBLE VEHICLE OF SAME MODEL

  1. Check if the "Bluetooth" audio player plays normally on another "Bluetooth" audio compatible vehicle of the same model.

    OK:
    "Bluetooth" audio plays normally.



NG
MALFUNCTION IN PORTABLE PLAYER
OK
