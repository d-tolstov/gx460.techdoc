RELAY (w/ Headlight Cleaner System) > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here



1. REMOVE RADIATOR GRILLE

  1. Remove the radiator grille (Click here).



2. REMOVE FRONT BUMPER COVER

  1. Put protective tape around the front bumper cover.

  1. Remove the 2 bolts labeled A and 2 bolts labeled B.




  1. Remove the 2 screws and 4 clips.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape
    *2
    		Bolt A
    *3
    		Bolt B




  1. Remove the 2 screws.

    HINT:
    Use the same procedure for the RH and LH sides.




  1. Detach the 7 claws.

    HINT:
    Use the same procedure for the RH and LH sides.

  1. w/ LEXUS Parking Assist-sensor System:
    Disconnect the connector.

  1. Disconnect the headlight cleaner hose and 2 fog light connectors.

  1. Remove the front bumper cover.



3. REMOVE HEADLIGHT CLEANER CONTROL RELAY



  1. Disconnect the connector.

  1. Remove the headlight cleaner control relay.
