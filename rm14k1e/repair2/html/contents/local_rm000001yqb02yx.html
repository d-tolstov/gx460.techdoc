DTC    74-40    Short in Speaker Circuit


DESCRIPTION

WIRING DIAGRAM

INSPECTION PROCEDURE

CHECK FOR DTC

CHECK FOR DTC

CHECK HARNESS AND CONNECTOR (STEREO COMPONENT AMPLIFIER - SPEAKER)

INSPECT FRONT NO. 3 SPEAKER ASSEMBLY RH

INSPECT FRONT NO. 1 SPEAKER ASSEMBLY RH

CHECK HARNESS AND CONNECTOR (FRONT NO. 3 SPEAKER RH - FRONT NO. 1 SPEAKER RH)

REPLACE FRONT NO. 3 SPEAKER ASSEMBLY RH

INSPECT FRONT NO. 3 SPEAKER ASSEMBLY LH

INSPECT FRONT NO. 1 SPEAKER ASSEMBLY LH

CHECK HARNESS AND CONNECTOR (FRONT NO. 3 SPEAKER LH - FRONT NO. 1 SPEAKER LH)

REPLACE FRONT NO. 3 SPEAKER ASSEMBLY LH

INSPECT FRONT NO. 4 SPEAKER ASSEMBLY

CHECK HARNESS AND CONNECTOR (STEREO COMPONENT AMPLIFIER - SPEAKERS)

INSPECT FRONT NO. 2 SPEAKER ASSEMBLY RH

INSPECT FRONT NO. 2 SPEAKER ASSEMBLY LH

INSPECT REAR NO. 1 SPEAKER ASSEMBLY RH

INSPECT REAR NO. 1 SPEAKER ASSEMBLY LH
