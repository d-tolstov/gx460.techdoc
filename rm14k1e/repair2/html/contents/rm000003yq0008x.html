FRONT PASSENGER AIRBAG ASSEMBLY > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here



1. REMOVE INSTRUMENT PANEL SAFETY PAD SUB-ASSEMBLY

  1. Remove the instrument panel safety pad sub-assembly (Click here).



2. REMOVE NO. 2 INSTRUMENT PANEL WIRE



  1. Disconnect the 2 connectors.

    NOTICE:
    When handling the airbag connector, take care not to damage the airbag wire harness.

  1. Detach the clamp and remove the wire.



3. REMOVE INSTRUMENT PANEL PASSENGER AIRBAG ASSEMBLY



  1. Remove the 2 screws.




  1. Detach the 5 hooks (A).
    Text in Illustration 
    *a
    		Hook A
    *b
    		Hook B

  1. Detach the 5 hooks (B) and remove the instrument panel passenger airbag.
