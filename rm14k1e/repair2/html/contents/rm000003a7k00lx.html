RELAY (for Windshield Deicer) > ON-VEHICLE INSPECTION

for Preparation Click here



1. REMOVE DEICER RELAY

  1. Remove the deicer relay from the engine room relay block.



2. INSPECT DEICER RELAY



  1. Measure the resistance according to the value(s) in the table below.

    Standard Resistance:
    Tester Connection
    		Condition
    		Specified Condition
    3 - 5
    		Battery voltage not applied to terminals 1 and 2
    		10 kΩ or higher
    3 - 5
    		Battery voltage applied to terminals 1 and 2
    		Below 1 Ω

    If the result is not as specified, replace the relay.



3. INSTALL DEICER RELAY

  1. Install the deicer relay to the engine room relay block.
