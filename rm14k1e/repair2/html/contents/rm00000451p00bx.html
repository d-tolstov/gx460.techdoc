1.INSPECT ABS WARNING LIGHT

  1. Disconnect the 2 skid control ECU connectors.

  1. Turn the engine switch on (IG).

  1. Check that the ABS warning light comes on.

    OK:
    ABS warning light comes on.

2.CHECK CAN COMMUNICATION LINE

  1. Turn the engine switch off.

  1. Connect the intelligent tester to the DLC3.

  1. Turn the engine switch on (IG).

  1. Turn the intelligent tester on.

  1. Select CAN Bus Check from the System Selection Menu screen and follow the prompts on the screen to inspect the CAN bus (Click here).

    OK:
    CAN Bus Check indicates no malfunctions in CAN communication.
