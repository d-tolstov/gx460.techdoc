REAR POWER SEAT CONTROL SYSTEM > Reclining Motor Circuit

for Preparation Click here


DESCRIPTION

The fold seat control ECU receives switch operation signals from the No. 1 and No. 2 fold seat switches and rear power seat switch and activates the power seat motors. At this time, the Hall IC detects the actuation of the seatback and sends a seatback actuation signal to the fold seat control ECU. The fold seat control ECU uses signals from the Hall IC to detect if an object is caught or if any other abnormal condition has occurred.
