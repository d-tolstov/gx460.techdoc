DTC    C1252    Brake Booster Pump Motor on Time Abnormally Long

for Preparation Click here


DESCRIPTION

The motor relay (semiconductor relay) is built into the master cylinder solenoid and drives the pump motor based on a signal from the skid control ECU.

DTC Code
DTC Detection Condition
Trouble Area
C1252
The motor operates for 3 minutes or more.
  1. Brake booster with accumulator pump assembly
  2. Pump motor wire harness condition
  3. Master cylinder solenoid (Skid control ECU)
  4. Hydraulic brake booster assembly

HINT:
The pump motor continues operation for the first 3 minutes, and then starts and stops repeatedly.


INSPECTION PROCEDURE

NOTICE:
When replacing the master cylinder solenoid, perform calibration (Click here).

HINT:
