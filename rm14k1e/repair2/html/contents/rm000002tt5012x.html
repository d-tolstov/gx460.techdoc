REAR SEAT ENTERTAINMENT SYSTEM > Sound from Wireless Headphones is Difficult to Hear, cannot be Heard, or Quality is Poor

for Preparation Click here


INSPECTION PROCEDURE

1.CHECK WIRELESS HEADPHONE OPERATION

  1. Check whether only one of the wireless headphones does not operate, or whether both wireless headphones do not operate.
    Result 
    Result
    		Proceed to
    Sound is abnormal from the wireless headphones of either the television display assembly RH or LH
    		A
    Sound is abnormal from the wireless headphones of both the television display assembly RH and LH
    		B



B
PROCEED TO NEXT SUSPECTED AREA SHOWN IN PROBLEM SYMPTOMS TABLE (Click here)
A


2.CHECK WIRELESS HEADPHONES (BATTERY)

  1. Check that the dry-cell battery of the wireless headphones is not depleted.

    OK:
    Battery is not depleted.



NG
REPLACE WIRELESS HEADPHONE BATTERY
OK


3.CHECK WIRELESS HEADPHONE SETTING

  1. Check the wireless headphone settings.

    1. Check that the volume is not set to "0".

    2. Check that "MUTE" is off.

      OK:
      The volume is not set to "0" and "MUTE" is off.



NG
SET VOLUME TO HIGH AND TURN MUTE OFF
OK


4.CLEAN INFRARED RAY RECEIVING PORTION



  1. Clean the infrared ray receiving portion on the television display assembly.

  1. Check whether the same malfunction occurs.

    OK:
    The function returns to normal.



NG
Go to step 5
OK

END 

5.REPLACE WIRELESS HEADPHONES

  1. Replace the wireless headphones with a new or normally functioning pair of headphones and check if the same problem occurs again.

    OK:
    Same problem does not occur.



NG
PROCEED TO NEXT SUSPECTED AREA SHOWN IN PROBLEM SYMPTOMS TABLE (Click here)
OK

END (WIRELESS HEADPHONES ARE DEFECTIVE)
