DTC    B2662    Lost Communication with Clock/Outside Temperature Display


DESCRIPTION

WIRING DIAGRAM

INSPECTION PROCEDURE

CLEAR DTC

CHECK FOR DTC

CHECK HARNESS AND CONNECTOR (DRIVING SUPPORT SWITCH CONTROL ECU - ACCESSORY METER)

CHECK HARNESS AND CONNECTOR (ACCESSORY METER - BATTERY AND BODY GROUND)

REPLACE ACCESSORY METER ASSEMBLY

CLEAR DTC

CHECK FOR DTC
