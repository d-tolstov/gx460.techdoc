DTC    B15D0    MOST Communication Malfunction

for Preparation Click here


DESCRIPTION

  1. This DTC is stored when the MOST network connections cannot be established after the master unit is activated.

DTC Code
DTC Detection Condition
Trouble Area
B15D0
The MOST network connections cannot be established.
  1. Harness or connector
  2. Display and navigation module display
  3. Stereo component amplifier assembly
  4. Radio receiver assembly
  5. Multi-display controller sub-assembly*
  1. *: w/ Rear Seat Entertainment System

HINT:
  1. The MOST network connections cannot be established.
  2. Errors may occur in MOST communication between devices due to problems such as electrical noise.
