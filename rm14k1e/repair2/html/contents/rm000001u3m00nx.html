CAUTION:
Care must be taken to replace each part properly as it could affect the performance of the brake system and result in a driving hazard. Replace the parts with parts having the same part number or equivalent.

NOTICE:
  1. It is very important to keep parts and the area clean when repairing the brake system.
  2. If the vehicle is equipped with a mobile communication system, refer to the Precaution in the Introduction section.
