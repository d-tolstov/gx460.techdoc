RAIN SENSOR > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here



1. REMOVE NO. 1 RAIN SENSOR COVER



  1. Detach the 2 claws and remove the rain sensor cover.



2. REMOVE RAIN SENSOR



  1. Disconnect the connector.




  1. Release the stopper by pulling it down.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Stopper

  1. Remove the rain sensor as shown in the illustration.



3. CLEAN WINDSHIELD GLASS

NOTICE:
  1. The rain sensor tape can be reused, however, replace it with a new piece of tape if it is dirty or damaged.
  2. Check that the rain sensor tape does not remain on the windshield glass. If it does, remove it before installing the rain sensor.
