1. REMOVE SHIFT POSITION INDICATOR



  1. Detach the connector clamp.

  1. Disconnect the shift lock solenoid connector from the shift lock control ECU.




  1. Detach the 3 claws and remove the shift lock control ECU from the transmission floor shift.
