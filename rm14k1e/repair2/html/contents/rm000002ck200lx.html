REAR SHOCK ABSORBER > INSTALLATION

for Preparation Click here


HINT:
  1. Use the same procedure for the RH and LH sides.
  2. The procedure listed below is for the LH side.
  3. A bolt without a torque specification is shown in the standard bolt chart (Click here).



1. TEMPORARILY INSTALL REAR SHOCK ABSORBER ASSEMBLY LH

  1. Install the cushion No. 2 to the rear shock absorber assembly LH.





  1. Install the cushion No. 1 and rear shock absorber assembly LH with a new nut.

    Torque:
    25 N*m{ 255 kgf*cm , 18 ft.*lbf }

    NOTICE:
    Be sure to fit the positioning protrusion of the bracket into the hole in the frame.




  1. Temporarily install the rear shock absorber assembly LH with the bolt.

  1. Connect the absorber control actuator connector.



2. INSTALL REAR WHEEL

Torque:
112 N*m{ 1142 kgf*cm , 83 ft.*lbf }



3. STABILIZE SUSPENSION

  1. Lower the vehicle.

  1. Bounce the vehicle up and down several times to stabilize the suspension.



4. TIGHTEN REAR SHOCK ABSORBER ASSEMBLY LH

  1. Tighten the bolt.

    Torque:
    98 N*m{ 999 kgf*cm , 72 ft.*lbf }
