DTC    P060E    Internal Control Module Throttle Position Performance

for Preparation Click here


DESCRIPTION

The ECM monitors the input signals of the throttle position sensor No. 1 and stop light switch. As the ECM monitors the input signals of the throttle position sensor No. 1 and the STP signals of the stop light switch, if the input signals and control signals deviate, a DTC is stored.

DTC No.
DTC Detection Condition
Trouble Area
P060E
Either condition is met (1 trip detection logic):
  1. There is an ECM main CPU error.
  2. There is an ECM sub CPU error.
ECM

MONITOR STRATEGY


Required sensors/Components (Main)
ECM
Frequency of operation
Once per driving cycle

TYPICAL ENABLING CONDITIONS


DMA communication error
Not detected

TYPICAL MALFUNCTION THRESHOLDS


When one of following conditions is met:
Condition 1 or 2
1. Difference of main throttle position and sub throttle position
0.3 V or more
2. Stop light switch signal (CPU) and stop light switch signal (Monitor IC)
Different

CONFIRMATION DRIVING PATTERN

  1. Turn the engine switch off.
  2. Disconnect the cable from the negative (-) battery terminal and wait for 1 minute.
  3. Connect the cable to the negative (-) battery terminal.
  4. Turn the engine switch on (IG) and wait for 16 seconds or more.

INSPECTION PROCEDURE

1.CHECK FOR DTC

  1. Connect the intelligent tester to the DLC3.

  1. Turn the engine switch on (IG).

  1. Turn the tester on.

  1. Clear DTCs (Click here).

  1. Turn the tester off.

  1. Turn the engine switch off.

  1. Disconnect the intelligent tester.

  1. Disconnect the cable from the negative (-) battery terminal and wait for 1 minute.

  1. Connect the cable to the negative (-) battery terminal.

  1. Connect the intelligent tester to the DLC3.

  1. Turn the engine switch on (IG) and turn the tester on.

  1. Wait 16 seconds or more.

  1. Enter the following menus: Powertrain / Engine and ECT / DTC.

  1. Check for DTCs.

    OK:
    P060E is not output.
