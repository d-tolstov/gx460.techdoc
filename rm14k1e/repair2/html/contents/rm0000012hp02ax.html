RELAY (for Magnet Clutch Control) > ON-VEHICLE INSPECTION

for Preparation Click here



1. REMOVE MAGNET CLUTCH RELAY



  1. Remove the magnet clutch relay from the engine room relay block.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Magnet Clutch Relay



2. INSPECT MAGNET CLUTCH RELAY



  1. Measure the resistance according to the value(s) in the table below.

    Standard Resistance:
    Tester Connection
    		Condition
    		Specified Condition
    3 - 5
    		Battery voltage not applied between terminals 1 and 2
    		10 kΩ or higher
    Battery voltage applied to terminals 1 and 2
    		Below 1 Ω

    1. If the result is not as specified, replace the magnet clutch relay.



3. INSTALL MAGNET CLUTCH RELAY

  1. Install the magnet clutch relay to the engine room relay block.
