1. INSPECT SEPARATE TYPE FRONT SEAT CUSHION SPRING ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Check the operation of the front seat cushion spring (slide motor).
    Check that the front seat cushion spring moves smoothly when the battery is connected to the slide motor connector terminals.

    OK:
    Measurement Condition
    		Specified Condition
    Battery positive (+) → 4
    Battery negative (-) → 1
    		Forward
    Battery positive (+) → 1
    Battery negative (-) → 4
    		Backward

    If the result is not as specified, replace the front seat cushion spring assembly.
    Text in Illustration 
    *a
    		Component without harness connected
    (Front Seat Cushion Spring Assembly (Slide Motor))
