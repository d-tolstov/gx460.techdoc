REAR CRANKSHAFT OIL SEAL > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here



1. REMOVE AUTOMATIC TRANSMISSION ASSEMBLY

  1. Remove the automatic transmission assembly (Click here).



2. REMOVE DRIVE PLATE AND RING GEAR SUB-ASSEMBLY



  1. Using SST, hold the crankshaft.

    SST
    09213-70011   (09213-70020)
    09330-00021  




  1. Remove the 10 bolts, the rear drive plate spacer, the drive plate and ring gear, and the crankshaft angle sensor rotor.



3. REMOVE REAR CRANKSHAFT OIL SEAL



  1. Using a knife, cut off the lip of the rear crankshaft oil seal.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Protective Tape
    *2
    		Cut Position

  1. Using a screwdriver, pry out the rear crankshaft oil seal.

    HINT:
    Tape the screwdriver tip before use.

    NOTICE:
    Do not damage the surface of the rear crankshaft oil seal press fit hole or crankshaft.
