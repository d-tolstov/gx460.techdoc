DTC    CB-13    USB Over Current Detection

for Preparation Click here


DESCRIPTION


DTC Code
DTC Detection Condition
Trouble Area
CB-13
An "iPod" or USB device overcurrent malfunction.
  1. "iPod" or USB device
  2. Harness or connector
  3. No. 1 stereo jack adapter assembly
  4. Multi-media interface ECU

WIRING DIAGRAM




INSPECTION PROCEDURE

1.REPLACE USB Device OR "iPod"

  1. Disconnect the USB device or "iPod" from the stereo jack adapter.

  1. Turn the engine switch off.

    HINT:
    When this DTC has been stored, it is necessary to turn off the engine switch to make it possible for the vehicle to recognize a new device when it is connected.

  1. Turn the engine switch on (ACC).

  1. Connect a known good USB device or "iPod" to the stereo jack adapter.


NEXT


2.CHECK FOR DTC

  1. Clear the DTCs (Click here).

  1. Check for DTCs and check if the same DTC occurs again.

    HINT:
    If DTCs are output frequently, replace the multi-media interface ECU.

    OK:
    Malfunction disappears.
