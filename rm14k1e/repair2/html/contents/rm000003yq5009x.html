CURTAIN SHIELD AIRBAG ASSEMBLY > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here


HINT:
  1. Use the same procedure for the RH and LH sides.
  2. The procedure listed below is for the LH side.



1. REMOVE ROOF HEADLINING ASSEMBLY

  1. Remove the roof headlining assembly (Click here).



2. REMOVE NO. 2 AIR DUCT REAR SIDE



  1. Detach the 2 clips.

  1. Detach the clamp and remove the duct.



3. REMOVE NO. 1 AIR DUCT REAR SIDE



  1. Detach the 2 claws and remove the cooler plate.




  1. Swing the bottom of the duct sideways and remove the duct.



4. REMOVE CURTAIN SHIELD AIRBAG ASSEMBLY LH

  1. Disconnect the connector.

    NOTICE:
    When handling the airbag connector, take care not to damage the airbag wire harness.



  1. Remove the 17 bolts and curtain shield airbag.
