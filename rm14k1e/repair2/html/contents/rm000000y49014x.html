POWER WINDOW CONTROL SYSTEM > Rear Power Window RH Auto Up / Down Function does not Operate with Rear Power Window Switch RH

for Preparation Click here


DESCRIPTION

  1. If the auto up/down function does not operate, the cause may be one or more of the following:
  1. The ECU in the power window regulator motor determines that the power window regulator motor has not been initialized.
  2. The rear power window regulator switch has a malfunction.
  3. The Hall IC in the power window regulator motor has a malfunction.
  4. There is an open or short in the wiring between the rear power window regulator switch and power window regulator motor.
