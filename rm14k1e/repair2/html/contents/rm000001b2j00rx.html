FRONT SPEED SENSOR > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here


HINT:
  1. The procedure listed below is for the LH side.
  2. Other than areas where instructions are provided, use the same procedures for the RH and LH sides.



1. REMOVE FRONT WHEEL


2. REMOVE FRONT SKID CONTROL SENSOR WIRE



  1. Disconnect the connector from the front speed sensor.

  1. Remove the 2 bolts and 2 harness clamps.

  1. Detach the clip.




  1. Remove the 2 bolts and 2 harness clamps.



  1. Disconnect the connector as follows.

      3. Detach the clip.
      4. Remove the bolt and skid control sensor clamp.



3. REMOVE SKID CONTROL SENSOR CLAMP
