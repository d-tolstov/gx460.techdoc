CONTROL VALVE ASSEMBLY > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here


NOTICE:
  1. Before removal, be sure to disable vehicle height control by pressing the height control OFF switch and confirming that the height control OFF indication is displayed on the meter.
  2. Check the safety of your surroundings and use caution when disconnecting each height control valve air tube as the air in each pneumatic cylinder will be discharged and the vehicle body will lower.



1. DISCONNECT CABLE FROM NEGATIVE BATTERY TERMINAL

CAUTION:
Wait at least 90 seconds after disconnecting the cable from the negative (-) battery terminal to disable the SRS system.

NOTICE:
  1. w/ Navigation System (for HDD):
    After the engine switch is turned off, the display and navigation module display (HDD navigation system) records various types of memory and settings. As a result, after turning the engine switch off, be sure to wait for the time specified in the following table before disconnecting the cable from the negative (-) battery terminal.

    Waiting Time before Disconnecting Cable from Negative (-) Battery Terminal:

    Condition
    		Waiting Time
    Vehicle enrolled in G-BOOK system
    		6 minutes
    Vehicle not enrolled in G-BOOK system
    		1 minute

  2. When disconnecting the cable, some systems need to be initialized after the cable is reconnected (Click here).



2. REMOVE FUEL PUMP CONTROL ECU ASSEMBLY



  1. Disconnect the 2 connectors.

  1. Remove the 2 bolts and fuel pump ECU.

    NOTICE:
    Make sure the fuel pump ECU is not subjected to physical shocks, such as being dropped, etc.



3. DISCONNECT HEIGHT CONTROL TUBE



  1. Disconnect the connector.

  1. Disconnect the 3 height control tubes (Click here).

    HINT:
    For the disconnection procedure of the tubes (type 1 and type 2), refer to Precaution of the Suspension Control System section.



4. REMOVE NO. 2 HEIGHT CONTROL VALVE SUB-ASSEMBLY



  1. Remove the 2 bolts and No. 2 height control valve.
