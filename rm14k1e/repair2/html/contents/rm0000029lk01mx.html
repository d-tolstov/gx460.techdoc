1. INSPECT PURGE VSV



  1. Measure the resistance according to the value(s) in the table below.

    Standard Resistance:
    Tester Connection
    		Condition
    		Specified Condition
    1 - 2
    		20°C (68°F)
    		23 to 26 Ω
    1 - Body ground
    		Always
    		10 MΩ or higher
    2 - Body ground

    HINT:
    When measuring the coil resistance, make sure that the surface temperature of the purge VSV is 20°C (68°F).



  1. Check the operation of the purge VSV.




    1. Check that air does not flow from port E to port F.
      Text in Illustration

      		Specified Condition
      Battery positive (+) → terminal 1
      Battery negative (-) → terminal 2
