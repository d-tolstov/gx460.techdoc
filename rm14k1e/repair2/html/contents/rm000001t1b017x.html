STEERING LOCK SYSTEM > DTC CHECK / CLEAR

for Preparation Click here


NOTICE:
  1. The steering lock ECU does not store past DTCs. If any DTCs are output, confirm and record them as soon as possible. Do not turn the engine switch off or clear the DTCs until the DTCs are confirmed and recorded.
  2. DTCs may have been stored due to problems unrelated to the steering lock ECU system. Be sure to confirm and record any DTCs output for other systems.
  3. When using the intelligent tester with the engine switch off, perform either of the following: 1) Turn a courtesy light switch on and off at intervals of 1.5 seconds or less until communication between the intelligent tester and vehicle begins, or 2) connect the intelligent tester to the vehicle and select "MANUAL" from the initial screen on the intelligent tester, and then select "KEY REGIST" under Model Code.


CHECK FOR DTC (MAIN BODY ECU)

  1. Connect the intelligent tester to the DLC3.

  2. Turn the engine switch on (IG).

  3. Turn the intelligent tester on.

  4. Enter the following menus: Body / Main Body / DTC.

  5. Check for DTCs.


CHECK FOR DTC (CERTIFICATION ECU)

HINT:
The steering lock system DTCs are output from the steering lock ECU via the certification ECU.

  1. Connect the intelligent tester to the DLC3.

  2. Turn the engine switch on (IG).

  3. Turn the intelligent tester on.

  4. Enter the following menus: Body / Entry&Start / DTC.

  5. Check for DTCs.


CLEAR DTC (MAIN BODY ECU)

  1. Connect the intelligent tester to the DLC3.

  2. Turn the engine switch on (IG).

  3. Turn the intelligent tester on.

  4. Enter the following menus: Body / Main Body / DTC.

  5. Clear the DTCs.


CLEAR DTC (CERTIFICATION ECU)

  1. Connect the intelligent tester to the DLC3.

  2. Turn the engine switch on (IG).

  3. Turn the intelligent tester on.

  4. Enter the following menus: Body / Entry&Start / DTC.

  5. Clear the DTCs.
