DTC    U0100    Lost Communication with ECM / PCM "A"

DTC    U0129    Lost Communication with Skid Control ECU

DTC    U0142    Lost Communication with Main Body ECU

DTC    U0151    Lost Communication with Airbag ECU

for Preparation Click here


DESCRIPTION

The combination meter assembly communicates with the ECM via the CAN communication system (CAN No. 1 Bus).

DTC No.
DTC Detection Condition
Trouble Area
U0100
When the vehicle speed is 5 km/h (3.1 mph) or more, the IG voltage is 9.5 V or higher and the following condition is met:
  1. No communication with the ECM continues for 2 seconds or more.
  1. ECM
  2. CAN communication system
  3. Combination meter assembly
U0129
When 15 seconds have elapsed after the engine is started, the IG voltage is 9.5 V or higher and the following condition is met:
  1. No communication with the skid control ECU continues for 3 seconds or more.
  1. Skid control ECU
  2. CAN communication system
  3. Combination meter assembly
U0142
When the vehicle speed is 5 km/h (3.1 mph) or more, the IG voltage is 9.5 V or higher and the following condition is met:
  1. No communication with the main body ECU (multiplex network body ECU) continues for 3 seconds or more.
  1. Main body ECU (Multiplex network body ECU)
  2. CAN communication system
  3. Combination meter assembly
U0151
When the vehicle speed is 5 km/h (3.1 mph) or more, the IG voltage is 9.5 V or higher and the following condition is met:
  1. No communication with the center airbag sensor assembly continues for 10 seconds or more.
  1. Center airbag sensor assembly
  2. CAN communication system
  3. Combination meter assembly

INSPECTION PROCEDURE

1.CONFIRM DTC OUTPUT

  1. Connect the intelligent tester to the DLC3.

  1. Turn the engine switch on (IG).

  1. Turn the intelligent tester on.

  1. Enter the following menus: Body Electrical / Trouble Codes.

  1. Clear the stored DTCs.

  1. Drive the vehicle at more than 5 km/h (3.1 mph) for at least 60 seconds.

  1. Stop the vehicle.

  1. Check for DTCs (Click here).
