FRONT CONSOLE BOX > DISASSEMBLY

for Preparation Click here



1. REMOVE CONSOLE COMPARTMENT DOOR SUB-ASSEMBLY



  1. Remove the 6 screws and console compartment door.



2. REMOVE REAR CONSOLE BOX HOLDER CUP

  1. Remove the 2 screws.



  1. Detach the 4 claws and remove the rear console box holder cup and rear upper console panel.




    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Rear Console Box Holder Cup
    		*2
    		Rear Upper Console Panel



3. REMOVE SHIFTING HOLE COVER SUB-ASSEMBLY



  1. Detach the 7 claws and 2 guides and remove the shifting hole cover.



4. REMOVE COMBINATION SWITCH ASSEMBLY



  1. Remove the 4 screws and combination switch (absorber control switch) from the upper console panel.



5. REMOVE SHIFT POSITION INDICATOR



  1. Detach the connector clamp.

  1. Remove the screw and shift position indicator from the upper console panel.



6. REMOVE REFRESHING SEAT SWITCH



  1. Disconnect the connector.

  1. Using a screwdriver, detach the 4 claws and remove the switch.

    HINT:
    Use the same procedure for both switches.



7. REMOVE INSTRUMENT PANEL CUP HOLDER ASSEMBLY



  1. Slide the cup holder and remove the 2 screws.




  1. Disconnect the connector and detach the 3 clamps.

  1. Remove the 4 screws.




  1. Tilt the instrument panel cup holder as shown in the illustration and pull it out in the direction of the arrow to remove it.
